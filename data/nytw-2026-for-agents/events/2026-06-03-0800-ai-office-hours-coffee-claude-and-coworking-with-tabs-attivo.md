## Description

Join Tabs and Attivo Partners during #NYTechWeek for a casual, high-signal day built around actually using AI in your finance and accounting workflows.

We're taking over a coffee shop in SoHo to create an intimate space where you can drop in, grab a seat, and get work done — whether you're visiting NYC and need a break from a hotel, or just want a change of scenery.

While you're there, spend time with the Tabs and Attivo teams for Claude Code in the Office of the CFO — hands-on office hours focused on how AI applies to finance and accounting, specifically:

- New to Claude Code? The Tabs team will get you set up and show you how to start using it in real workflows
- Already using AI but not sure where it fits? The Attivo team will help you think through where to actually build, where to buy, and how to prioritize automation across your order-to-cash, revenue, and accounting operations
- Have a specific order-to-cash or revenue challenge? Bring it. We'll help you identify root causes and map out practical next steps — not a sales pitch, just a real conversation

This isn't a lecture or a demo. It's one-on-one time with practitioners who work in and around these tools every day.

Drop in for a focused working session, stay to co-work, or just grab coffee and connect with other finance leaders building in the same direction.

Come for the coffee, stay for the conversations — or stay and get real work done.

Who should attend: Founders, CFOs, Controllers, and finance & accounting leaders at high-growth B2B companies who want to understand how AI actually applies to their function — not just talk about it.
