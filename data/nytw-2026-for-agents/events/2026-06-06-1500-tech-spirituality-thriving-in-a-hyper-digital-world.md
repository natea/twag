## Description

Tech & Spirituality: Thriving in a Hyper-Digital World explores how we can remain grounded, present, and spiritually connected in an age of constant notifications, algorithms, and digital noise. As technology increasingly shapes how we think, feel, and relate to one another, this event invites participants to reflect on how their inner life intersects with their online world. Through thoughtful conversation and shared insight, we’ll examine how technology can either fragment our attention, or become a tool for deeper awareness and intentional living.

This gathering brings together seekers, creatives, and professionals to explore practical ways to cultivate balance, meaning, and mindfulness in a hyper-connected world. From developing daily rituals that anchor your attention to reimagining your digital habits through a spiritual lens, attendees will leave with both inspiration and actionable tools. Whether you're rooted in a specific tradition or simply curious about living with greater presence, this event offers space to reconnect with yourself beyond the screen.
