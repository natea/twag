## Description

MVP to Launch: Content Credentials for Creators, Builders, and Brands 

Join Adobe for a special gathering that goes beyond the AI conversation to explore what meaningful transparency looks like across the creative ecosystem: who made something, how it was made, and why that matters, whether you're an illustrator, a builder, or a startup founder scaling to enterprise. 

Content Credentials is the global standard for digital provenance, securely attaching verifiable information to content, including photos, videos, audio, and generative AI, proving the origins of media and how it was created, composited, and edited. This information travels with the content and can be surfaced across apps, platforms, and websites.
