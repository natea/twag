## Description

AI agents are already running inside some of the world's largest banks, asset managers, and insurers. But deployment without governance creates real exposure: regulatory violations, failed audits, and gaps nobody saw until it was too late.

Valliappa Lakshmanan, Cofounder & CTO of Obin AI, will moderate an expert panel with: 
– Andrew Lang, Global Chief Technology Officer at JP Morgan Chase
– Virender Bedi, Managing Director, Client & Product Solutions Technology at Apollo 
– Toby Kovacs, Financial Services Account Director, at Google

Questions we'll tackle:

– Why do most enterprise AI agent deployments fail, and what separates the ones that survive?
– How do you build agents your board can defend and your regulators can audit?
– What governance frameworks are institutions actually putting in place right now?
– How do you prove ROI on AI investments when most of the value is hard to measure and the costs are easy to see?
– Who owns AI agent failures when something goes wrong: the vendor, the internal team, or the exec who signed off?
