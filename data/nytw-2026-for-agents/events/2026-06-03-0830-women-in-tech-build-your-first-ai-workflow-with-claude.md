## Description

There are only so many hours in a day, and if you've been using ChatGPT or Claude mostly like a Google Search, you're missing out on your personal AI Chief of Staff! 

In this workshop, we'll demo how to setup your first AI workflow with Claude and then you'll have time to break out and do it yourself. You'll also have time to meet other women in tech to discuss how they're using AI in and outside of work. 

If you're intimidated with how technical AI can feel, we'll break it down and make it fun!

Maggie Blackburn is a portfolio career strategist, coach, marketer, and community builder who helps women build portfolio careers that map to their values, interests, and lifestyle. She has 10+ years experience working in Product Marketing at Slack, Asana, and high-growth startups.

Paige Kehoe is a product leader at Webflow who transforms ideas into exceptional products through product-led growth, 0-1 development, and AI-native integration.
