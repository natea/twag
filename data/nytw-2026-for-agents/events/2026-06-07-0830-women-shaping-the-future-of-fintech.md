## Description

Women Shaping the Future of Fintech is a gathering by WomenX that brings together women working across finance, technology, startups, investing, and fintech.

As fintech continues to evolve, we believe women already play a critical role in shaping this space. We hope their voices can be heard, their experiences can be shared, and that they can build a community where they feel supported and connected.

At this event, we will invite women from companies such as Stripe, Goldman Sachs, PayPal, Apollo, Mastercard, and fintech startups. They will share how they work at the intersection of finance and technology, and how they see fintech evolving across payments, banking, investing, private markets, and AI-driven financial products.

After the talks, there will be a networking session where speakers and attendees can connect more freely and build meaningful relationships.

To keep the community thoughtful and relevant, attendance will be curated. We ask applicants to share materials such as a resume, portfolio, LinkedIn, personal website, or any platform that reflects their work or interests.

We hope this can be a space to meet, learn, and support one another.

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
