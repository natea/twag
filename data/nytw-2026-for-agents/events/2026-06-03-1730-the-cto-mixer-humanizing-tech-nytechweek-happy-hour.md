The CTO Mixer: Humanizing Tech | #NYTechWeek Happy Hour Co-hosted by Devlane & Merge Conflict

AI is rewriting the rules of how engineering teams are built, led, and scaled. But behind every architecture decision and technical strategy is a human, and that human side is more important than ever.

That's why we're bringing together CTOs, VPs of Engineering, and technical leaders for an evening of real talk, strong drinks, and connections that actually matter.

This isn't a panel. Just a curated room of senior technical leaders navigating the same pressures you are, hiring in a competitive market, adapting to AI-driven change, rethinking how to evaluate and interview talent in a world where not every candidate is what they seem, scaling teams without losing culture, and having the space for honest conversations about it.

If you're a CTO or engineering leader, whether you're deep in a scaling challenge, curious about how your peers are solving it, or just want to be in a room where the conversation is worth having, this is where you want to be on June 3rd.
