## Description

Join us in New York City to discuss one of the hottest topics in healthcare + tech: egg freezing.  

Presented by AI startup Sunfish and the Cooley Healthcare Team, at the Sunfish offices, this event will bring together medical expertise, patient testimonials, and analytical leaders for a conversation about how new data can help provide more clarity. 

Learn how your fertility intelligence (through Sunfish) can help define a target number of eggs to freeze, inform the right timeline, and support more personalized guidance, along with new financial protection models like Sunfish’s “second cycle free” package.

Attendees will leave with a clearer understanding of what fertility data can (and can’t) tell you, and how AI is being used in real life for egg freezing. 

What You'll Get:
- Meet others in the New York health tech / fertility community 
- Connect with a leading reproductive endocrinologist and fertility specialist, Dr. Canon of RMA of New York
- Hear a real patient story about Egg Freezing
- Learn about Sunfish's new model and how a second cycle is included if you don't freeze your target number of eggs from Sunfish's CEO Angela Rastegar
- Leave with discounts and swag
