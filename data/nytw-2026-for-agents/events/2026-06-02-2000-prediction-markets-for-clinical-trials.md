## Description

Prediction markets are reshaping how people forecast elections and sports. But what happens when we apply them to science?

At this event, we’ll share new findings from EndpointArena.com on how prediction markets and AI models forecast clinical trial outcomes.

We’ll explore how markets can generate real time signals around drug development, what this means for investors and researchers, and why forecasting may become a core layer of the biotech ecosystem.
