## Description

This is the longevity room at TechWeek. Capital is flooding the space, science is moving faster than expected, and Fortune 500 companies are rebuilding employee health programs around it. Come spend two hours with the people deciding where it goes next.

Panel I: Longevity Science and Business
Moderator:
Dylan Livingston — Longevity policy and biotech leader
Speakers:
Raghav Sehgal, PhD — Faculty at Yale (Computational Biology); Forbes 30 Under 30 in Healthcare; developer of SYMPHONYAge, a biomarker measuring aging across 11 organ systems
Dr. Priyanka Joshi — Assistant Professor, Georgetown University; Forbes 30 Under 30 in Science & Healthcare
Dmitrii Chebanov, PhD — CSO, The Reason; formerly Memorial Sloan Kettering; leads AI and genomics research mapping aging trajectories

Panel II: Health as a Performance Catalyst
Today, organizations are playing a bigger role in supporting employees' overall health. Work is where individuals spend 50%+ of their day, and companies have incredible leverage to influence how society manages its preventive health. This panel brings together leaders who are using innovative tactics to improve employee health, productivity, and retention.

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com
