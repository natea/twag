## Description

This is the longevity room at TechWeek. Capital is flooding the space, science is moving faster than expected, and companies are rebuilding health programs around it. Come spend two hours with the people deciding where it goes next.

Panel I: Health as a Performance Catalyst
Speakers:
Dylan Gambardella - Founder of Different Health
Dr. Jonathan Leary - Founder of Remedy Place
Sam Perkins - Founder of Nourish
Cody Levine - Founder of Smile House

Panel II: Longevity Science and Business
Moderator:
Dylan Livingston — Longevity policy and biotech leader
Speakers:
Raghav Sehgal, PhD — Faculty at Yale (Computational Biology); developer of SYMPHONYAge
Dr. Priyanka Joshi — Assistant Professor, Georgetown University
Dmitrii Chebanov, PhD — CSO, The Reason; formerly Memorial Sloan Kettering

Influencer Beat: meet Manissa Montour, the Founder & CEO of Loune, an AI-powered wellness platform for women of color. Loune combines cycle phase intelligence, AI coaching, and a clinically-informed sugar reset method to help women optimize their health across every hormonal phase. Before Loune, Manissa built NissaFit into a seven-figure wellness brand with a community of over 1M across platforms.
