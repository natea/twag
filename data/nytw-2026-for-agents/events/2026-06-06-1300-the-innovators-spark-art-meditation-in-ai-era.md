## Description

At a time when AI, automation, and constant acceleration shape how we work and think, The Innovator’s Spark offers a rare invitation to step out of the city and reconnect with the source of creativity, clarity, and original insight.

Hosted at Sunning Hill Retreat, a secluded 28-acre estate in Old Brookville, Long Island, this immersive experience invites participants into a setting that is itself part of the experience. Surrounded by open lawns, wooded paths, historic architecture, and a quieter rhythm of life, Sunning Hill offers something increasingly rare during Tech Week: space to slow down, breathe, reflect, and reset.

Centered around A Hymn to Life, the session introduces Art Meditation — a gentle practice that blends visual awareness, mindfulness, and intuitive reflection. Rather than focusing on artistic skill, participants are invited to engage with color, form, and texture as pathways into deeper presence and inner awareness.

The afternoon will be offered in two intimate small-group sessions, each designed for approximately 10–15 participants to preserve quiet, focus, and depth. Each group will include a guided Art Meditation experience followed by time for networking and sharing.

Beyond the exhibition itself, guests are invited to enjoy the designated retreat areas under the guidance of the Sunning Hill Retreat team, take in the natural beauty, and feel the contrast between the intensity of the city and the stillness of this historic retreat environment. For everyone’s safety and to help preserve the retreat environment, guests are kindly asked not to enter restricted, wooded, or unmarked areas, or explore the property without guidance from our team.

In a world where machines increasingly replicate logic and execution, this experience reconnects participants with what remains deeply human: intuition, reflection, emotional clarity, and the ability to generate truly original ideas.

This session also offers a glimpse into Sunning Hill Retreat’s broader Art of Mindful Living programs, with future opportunities to return for deeper experiences and gatherings.

This experience is hosted by Sunning Hill Retreat and co-hosted by New York Youth Roundtable and Youth Civic Service, bringing together mindfulness, creativity, youth leadership, civic engagement, and meaningful community connection.

This event is part of #NYTechWeek — a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
