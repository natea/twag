## Description

*Event to be covered by People, Inc, home to People, Health.com, Verywell Health, EatingWell, and Byrdie. 
*Light bites and beverages by Mid-Day Squares, Slate Milk, Smearcase, and WAWA water.

The doctor’s office is no longer the default entry point into healthcare.

GLP-1s are prescribed over text. Blood panels are ordered by consumers before symptoms appear. Gyms are evolving into longevity clinics. AI is becoming a first-line triage tool. A new health stack is forming outside traditional institutions, and clinicians, payers, and patients are all trying to understand where they fit.

This panel explores what that emerging stack actually looks like, who gets to participate in it, and what is lost and gained when health and the human body become part of a consumer-driven system.
