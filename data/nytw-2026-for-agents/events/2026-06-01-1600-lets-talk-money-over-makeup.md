## Description

Come kick off NY Tech Week with Verve & In Between with a fun afternoon talking money and doing makeup. 

Join us 4:30 - 5:30pm to sit down with financial experts from Morgan Stanley and the founder of Neptune, Sol Lee for a real conversation on wealth building, financial tools, mistakes to avoid, and more.

We'll have a makeup artist in our vanity room upstairs for you to learn everyday makeup and get your touchups and headshots before you head out for your next event for the night!

Verve supports ambitious women in tech, including founders, operators, and investors, to help build meaningful connections that turn into employment opportunities, mentorship, friendship, and new sources of support and capital. 

In Between is a NYC-based private community and residency for those in transition—post exits, between jobs, or careers. It offers a soft landing to pause, explore new directions, and redefine what comes next alongside a multi-hyphenate community that values curiosity and reinvention. 

Special thanks to our partners at Cooley.

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
