## Description

Transitioning from military service to the tech industry requires adaptability, leadership, and resilience — qualities veterans already possess in abundance. This panel brings together veteran leaders from Google, Microsoft, and Amazon Web Services to share how their military backgrounds shaped their careers in technology and innovation. Attendees will gain firsthand insights into navigating career transitions, building leadership skills in corporate environments, and leveraging military experience to succeed in today’s fast-evolving tech landscape.

Panelists 
- Victoria Sherwood (U.S. Marine Corps Veteran; Global Program Manager, Private Equity at Amazon Web Services)
- Justin Law (U.S. Space Force Veteran; Deployment Engineer, OpenAI) 
- Brian Tien (West Point Graduate & U.S. Army Veteran; Account Strategist at Google)
- Justin Swinsky (U.S. Air Force Veteran; AWS Sales Strategy and Operations Lead at Amazon Web Services)
- William Wang (West Point Graduate & U.S. Army Veteran; Former Lead Product Manager of Multimodal ML Analytics at the Pentagon; Senior Product Manager, Agent Reasoning & Knowledge Graph at Microsoft)
