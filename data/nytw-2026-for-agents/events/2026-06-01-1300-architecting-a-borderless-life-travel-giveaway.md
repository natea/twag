## Description

If traveling or moving abroad has been on your mind, join this interactive session if you're aspiring-to-be a traveler or expat in tech.

Register & attend to win a travel giveaway in Spain or Morocco.

From 'wild Uber' to E.V. escapades, travel and life abroad can be filled with unknown surprises, puzzles to solve, and gifts that keep giving.

Travel can be a great way to "refuel" and particularly fulfilling for creatives, product people, profssionals in transition, and entrepreneurs. But it's important to figure out:
- when working remotely can work, when it can't
- how to travel both prepared and open to adventure
- what to consider if planning a permanent move

 
Come with questions and concerns and leave inspired to take the leap!

---

In 2025, a record-breaking number of Americans reported wanting to move abroad permanently (40% women, 19% men), and the U.S. experienced its first net negative migration since the 1930s.

Throughout 2026, Mary Beth Snodgrass, the founder and Managing Director of the PAC, has traveled abroad in Portugal, Spain and Morocco, exploring life as a 'senior' digital nomad.

Previously, Mary Beth traveled to dozens of countries in Latin America, Africa, Asia and Europe and lived in Spain for a year.  

Taking cues from the audience on what topics to cover, Mary Beth will lead a Q&A and share research on why so many people and women want to leave the U.S. along with tips and tactics for months-long travels.
