## Description

Who actually holds the pen on AI decisions — and what does responsible deployment really look like from the inside? Join us for a cross sector panel exploring how women are shaping the next phase of AI across platforms, capital, safety, and public infrastructure. 
 
Melanie Jolson and Adine Mitrani from Fenwick will moderate the panel, including: 
•	Dr. Allison Mishkin, Child Safety + Wellbeing Product Policy Lead at OpenAI
•	Dr. Gal Noyman-Veksler, Partner at Lionbird Ventures
•	Stacey Matlen, Sr. Vice President of Innovation at Partnership for New York City
 
This discussion will explore how today’s leaders are navigating the gap between AI capability and responsible deployment, shaping decision-making and risk frameworks, and influencing the future of AI products, policy, and public infrastructure. The panel will also examine the trends and shifts likely to define the next 6–12 months across the AI ecosystem.
