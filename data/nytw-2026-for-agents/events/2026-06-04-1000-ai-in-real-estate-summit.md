## Description

THIS EVENT REQUIRES YOU TO PURCHASE A TICKET VIA OUR EVENTBRITE PAGE - https://www.eventbrite.com/e/ai-in-real-estate-summit-at-nytechweek-tickets-1985474224854

The AI in Real Estate Summit sponsored by Intapp, Lerner Associates, Facilimax, Regions Bank, Real Estate Technology Transformation Center, and Revolution RE brings together senior commercial real estate executives — REIT CEOs, CIOs, fund managers, and institutional operators — for an exclusive four-hour evening forum on artificial intelligence in the built environment. Co-located with NY Tech Week, Nareit's REITWeek and hosted by the US Proptech Council, the Summit features a curated startup showcase, two executive panels on AI adoption and responsible AI governance, and an open networking reception. This is not a general technology conference. It is a peer-level conversation among the decision-makers who are actively deploying AI across portfolios, operations, and underwriting — and who are accountable for doing it responsibly.
