## Description

What separates a product people *love* from one they tolerate? What makes design feel human and where does AI fit into all of this without flattening the things that matter?

Slingshot AI is bringing together a panel of designers and design leaders from tech and tech-adjacent companies for a night of honest conversation about craft, taste, and the unspoken rules that quietly make or break a quality product.

Open to designers, PMs, and curious creatives who are fascinated by AI — or skeptical of it. Both are welcome. Come for the panel, stay for the debate.
