## Description

New York's Empire AI is the country's most ambitious bet on public AI infrastructure — a $500M consortium of leading universities building world-class supercomputing for the public interest. Empire AI is already accelerating 130+ research projects with impact on industries ranging from healthcare to advanced materials to climate and culture. Join us to hear from the architects of Empire AI on how it came together, what it's producing, where it goes next, and how you can get involved.

The event features:
- Carolyn Dee, Assistant Secretary for Technology and Financial Services, Office of New York Governor Kathy Hochul
- Kiran Keshav, Director of Research Computing, Empire AI
- Julie Samuels, President and CEO of Tech:NYC
- Jeannette M. Wing, Executive Vice President for Research and Professor of Computer Science, Columbia University
- Giacomo Bagarella, Principal, HR&A Advisors (moderator)
