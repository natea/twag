## Description

AI APOCALYPSE OR SALVATION? THE CLOCK IS TICKING.

You might be feeling a knot in your stomach lately; the same feeling you get thinking about climate change, watching democratic norms fall like so many dominoes, or seeing kids glued to their screens, frying their attention spans, having algorithms permanently impact their brain development—a feeling of helplessness. 

We post. We protest. We panic. But nothing changes. 

What if there were a solution? 

This goes beyond a presentation - it's an invitation to reclaim power.

Spartacus.app is a weapon that ordinary people like us have been waiting for.  Drawing on insights from Game Theory, it combines conditional pledges and temporary anonymity to create something revolutionary: a means for the powerless to gain power. 

Think about it: Why don't people speak up? Because it's not "worth it." The risk is too high, the reward too uncertain. 

But what if you knew you weren't alone? What if you knew exactly how many others would stand with you? 

That's what Spartacus does. It builds critical mass. And numbers equal leverage, protection, and impact. 

Whether you're building communities, organizing unions, gathering signatures, exposing corruption, fighting bullies, or starting a revolution within your organization, Spartacus makes taking action worth it. 

Join us in NYC on June 4.  Bring your frustrations, your dreams, and your most provocative ideas about politics, activism, and tech. 

We'll play a coordination game that will blow your mind, demo the platform that could change everything, and talk about the collective action problems facing humanity, including the ones YOU care about. 

The powerful don't want you to have leverage. The status quo is betting you'll stay home. Your future self is begging you to be there. 

We’re in, if you’re in.
