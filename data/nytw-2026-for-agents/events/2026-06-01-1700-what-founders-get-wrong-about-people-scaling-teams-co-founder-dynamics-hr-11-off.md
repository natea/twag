## Description

Some of the biggest risks startups face are people-related — hiring the wrong talent, co-founder misalignment, disorganized teams, burnout, turnover, weak employer brand, or payroll and compliance gaps — yet they stay invisible until it’s too late, too costly, and too painful to fix.

Reserve your spot for a 30-minute 1:1 office hours session. We will discuss your specific people and HR related concern. You will walk away with clear, actionable guidance tailored to your business goals.

Spots are limited, and selected participants will receive a formal invitation via email. Priority will be given to VC-backed startups.

Book a time and apply here: https://calendly.com/jc-referred/connect-with-jc. 

ABOUT ME: People advisor to multiple startups and VCs, mentor at Techstars. Former HR Tech founder, Global People Partner at a Fortune 500 and Head of People at a biotech startup. I have managed HR initiatives in highly matrixed global organizations and built the entire people functions from 0 to 1 including hiring strong early talent, managing retention, payroll, performance, compliance, and cofounder dynamics. Certified executive coach helping founders elevate executive presence, leadership effectiveness, and stakeholder influence.
