## Description

Most organizations have deployed AI in some way, but far fewer have translated it into measurable business impact. 

Our curated panel of AI leaders and practitioners from Anthropic, Akamai, Cisco, and Jobbrex will explore what happens after deployment, using real-world use cases and on-the-ground insights to examine where AI underperforms, where costs rise, and what it takes to drive business impact and achieve ROI.

Designed for team leaders, founders, and operators moving past the experimentation phase, this session offers practical guidance on AI transformation, adoption, workflow integration, and how to apply AI where it creates the most value.
