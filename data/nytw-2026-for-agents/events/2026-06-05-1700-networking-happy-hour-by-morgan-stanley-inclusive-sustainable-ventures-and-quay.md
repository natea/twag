## Description

Morgan Stanley Inclusive & Sustainable Ventures (MSISV) and QUAY Acceleration are excited to host a Happy Hour to connect founders, investors and major ecosystem players of the NYC tech ecosystem during NY #TechWeek, following the success of the 2024 and 2025 editions.

Whether you are a #founder looking to learn more about MSISV, an #investor looking for the next groundbreaking idea to invest in, or a relevant #ecosystem player wishing to get involved with brilliant founders, we invite you to join us for a night filled with meaningful conversations while overseeing the Manhattan skyline, sipping drinks and eating tasty bites.

Spots are limited so please register at your earliest convenience if you wish to attend. 

We look forward to seeing you there! 

Please note: by registering, you acknowledge that photos and videos may be taken by the event organizers.

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.

About Morgan Stanley Inclusive & Sustainable Ventures (MSISV):
Morgan Stanley Inclusive & Sustainable Ventures provides early-stage innovators with access to capital and resources to help them develop and scale. Our mission is to catalyze innovation and impact by supporting early-stage startups and nonprofits that are building solutions for a more inclusive and sustainable future. Learn more at www.morganstanley.com/ventures

About QUAY Acceleration:
QUAY Acceleration supports US and international economic development groups to generate business outcomes through our accelerator programs. Our goal is to assist innovators in exploring new markets and achieving business growth by providing exceptional knowledge, connections, and resources. We collaborate with a network of public and private partners to deliver customized programs for startups across the globe. Learn more at https://www.quay.co/
