## Description

Everyone is suddenly putting AI into everything. The more important question is what that actually means for learning.

During New York Tech Week, the Future of Higher Education and Zo Computer are bringing together educators, founders, operators, and investors for a focused conversation on how AI is changing learning, teaching, and the role of higher education itself.

We’ll discuss questions that are becoming impossible to ignore: What should students actually be learning now? How should teaching evolve when AI is part of the classroom and workflow? What happens to
instructional design, credentials, and the value of the institution itself?

The evening will begin with a short panel featuring leaders across education and technology, followed by a mixer for deeper conversation with people building the future of learning.

If you care about the future of how we’re going to be teaching,
learning, and growing in the decades to come, this is the panel for
you.

Zo is your intelligent home. We help you kill SaaS you don't actually need and build a system that's truly yours. You get a 24/7 agent in a personal cloud computer. With Zo, you can create simple automations through iMessage or build complex apps to run your business.

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
