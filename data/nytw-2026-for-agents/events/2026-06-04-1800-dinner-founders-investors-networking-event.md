## Description

This private dinner experience brings together high-caliber founders, investors, strategic partners, and professionals shaping the future with food technology innovations, artificial intelligence, consumer innovation, and social impact initiatives.

The goal is simple: create the kind of environment where meaningful relationships, strategic conversations, investment opportunities, and long-term collaborations naturally emerge. This is because the best conversations during Tech Week rarely happen on stage.
They happen in the right room, with the right people, over dinner.

A Curated Room of Builders & Decision-Makers

This event is intentionally curated to ensure strong representation across: 
- Venture-backed and emerging founders
- Angel investors and venture capital professionals
- Corporate innovation and strategy leaders
- Professionals interested in the future of innovation and impact

Whether you are actively deploying capital, building transformative technology, exploring partnerships, or looking to connect with exceptional people working on meaningful problems, this evening is designed for high-quality engagement.

Featured Dinner Conversation
Non-Dilutive Funding Strategies for High-Impact Companies
As dinner is served, attendees will join an expert-led discussion exploring one of the most underleveraged growth opportunities available to innovative companies today:
How mission-driven startups are accessing grants, research funding, government-backed programs, strategic partnerships, and other non-dilutive capital opportunities to accelerate growth without immediately sacrificing equity.

While the conversation will include examples from FoodTech, HealthTech, sustainability, and community impact innovation, the insights are highly relevant to founders and innovators across sectors.
