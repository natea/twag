## Description

NET WORTH
A private padel morning on the East River. Hosted by Lyzr during NY Tech Week.
Compound your social interest.
Before Tech Week's last day starts, a small group is heading to Domino Park for three hours on a private padel court — Manhattan skyline on one side, East River on the other, and the kind of crowd that makes the warm-up conversations as good as the game.
Mixed pairs, rotating doubles. No tournament, no rankings, no one keeping score too hard. If you haven't played padel before, you'll pick it up in ten minutes — it's easier than tennis and twice as fun. If you have played, you already know.
Post-game: coffee and a slow debrief in the lounge. 


This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
