## Description

Great products run on reliable infrastructure — and AI is changing how that infrastructure gets built.

Join Intuit Mailchimp for a casual #NYTechWeek dev happy hour in the Meatpacking District, bringing together builders, engineers, founders, product leaders, and AI-curious teams for an evening of cocktails, light bites, live demos, and conversation.

We’ll explore how AI is reshaping the way teams build, test, troubleshoot, and scale the systems behind modern customer experiences — from product notifications and alerts to real-time email, SMS/MMS, and other critical touchpoints.
Expect:
 - Short talks from engineers and product leaders
 - Live demos, including Mailchimp Transactional and our recent MCP launch
 - Insightful customer panel on how teams are thinking about AI, developer workflows, and infrastructure
 - Conversations with builders, product folks, and Mailchimp teams
 - Cocktails, light bites, good company, and exclusive swag

Whether you’re building with APIs, experimenting with AI agents, rethinking your dev workflow, or just looking to meet other people building in NYC, this is a chance to connect with the community and get a closer look at what’s next.

Full lineup of speakers coming soon.

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
