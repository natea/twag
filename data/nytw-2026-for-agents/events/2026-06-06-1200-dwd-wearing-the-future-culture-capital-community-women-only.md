## Description

What if culture was the gateway to capital?

During NY Tech Week, Mothers to Daughters (M2D) invites you into a highly curated gathering for our Date with Daughters signature initiative at an upscale
restaurant in Manhattan for brunch. During this gathering women from some of the most exclusive rooms across capital, culture, and community will come together to explore how these forces intersect to shape the future of entrepreneurship.

This experience brings together founders, investors, and cultural leaders in a setting designed for both connection and clarity. Guests are invited to dress in a way that reflects their highest expression of identity, power, and ownership.

This is not a traditional brunch. It is a room built with intention.

What to expect:

- A highly curated guest list spanning founders, investors, and cultural leaders
- Real stories (the bad and the ugly) on capital, access, and scaling what you’re building
- Investor and operator insights on navigating growth and opportunity
- Thoughtfully facilitated introductions designed to spark meaningful, high-value connections
- A signature cultural moment honoring identity, leadership, and collective power
- Elevated hospitality, including bubbles, light fare, and a refined experience throughout
- Curated gift bags from aligned partners and featured designers

Guests will be encouraged to support our charitable mission through direct contributions, and featured designers will be contributing a portion of proceeds from sales to Mothers to Daughters.

Mothers to Daughters’ vision is to help launch 100,000 women entrepreneurs by 2035 through mentorship, access, and ecosystem building.

For inquiries, please contact: francine@motherstodaughters.org

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
