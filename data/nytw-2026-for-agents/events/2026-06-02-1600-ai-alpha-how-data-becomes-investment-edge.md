## Description

This session explores how AI is transforming the way investors turn raw data into actionable signals. We will walk through real workflows across research, portfolio management, and trading, and discuss how leading institutions are leveraging AI to extract insights, improve efficiency, and generate edge.

Topics include:
• Turning alternative and traditional data into signals
• AI-assisted research and idea generation
• From data pipelines to decision-making frameworks
• Real-world use cases from hedge funds and quant teams

Whether you are a discretionary investor or a systematic trader, this session will offer a practical view on how AI is reshaping the investment process.
