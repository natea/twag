## Description

Come connect with the NYTW community through real, honest conversation.

Join us for a supportive, guided experience where founders, business owners, and tech leaders can connect through small-group conversations, share personal stories and tools, and learn from one another.

Most events focus on what people are building. The Unplug creates space to explore the emotional side of building and leadership. Together, we’ll explore everything from burnout to work-life balance, self-awareness, and personal growth.

Expect:

🧘 A short guided meditation and breathwork session
💬 Facilitated small-group conversations
🧠 Closing insights from a mental health facilitator
🥂 Beverages and light bites

This is a space to connect beyond the surface, meet the humans behind the businesses, and leave with new perspectives, practical tools, and meaningful connections.

The space is intimate so spots will be limited!

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com!
