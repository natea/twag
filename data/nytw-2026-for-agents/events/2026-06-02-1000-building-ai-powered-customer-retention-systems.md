## Description

Sponsored by Wooxy, this hands-on workshop dives into how modern teams are using AI to design, launch, and scale customer retention systems—without massive engineering resources.

If you’re curious how startups turn user data into personalized journeys across email, SMS, and in-product experiences, this session will show you exactly how it’s done.

What to Expect

In this interactive session, we’ll break down how leading companies build retention loops powered by real-time data, segmentation, and AI-driven personalization—and how you can apply the same principles immediately.

You’ll get:

* A clear framework for designing AI-powered retention systems
* A practical checklist to implement omnichannel strategies
* Hands-on exposure to AI agents and marketing automation workflows

Then, you’ll put theory into practice.

Working in small groups, you’ll analyze real use cases, uncover hidden retention opportunities, and define one actionable experiment you can launch right away using existing AI tools.

💡 What You’ll Learn
* How to design retention loops using real-time data and AI personalization
* How to activate users across email, SMS, and product touchpoints
* How to identify and test high-impact retention opportunities quickly
* How startups leverage AI to compete with larger teams

👥 Who Should Attend

This workshop is designed for:
* Students in computer science or AI
* Recent graduates exploring AI startups
* Developers interested in generative or conversational AI
* Entrepreneurs building AI-driven products
* Marketing specialists looking to boost retention with AI
