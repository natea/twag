## Description

Step away from the buzz of conferences and code for a moment of peace and connection.

Join us for Tech & Tranquility, a special event that blends mindfulness with the fast-paced world of tech and engage in conscious tech conversation.

What to Expect:
Guided Mindfulness Meditation: Start your experience with a soothing meditation session led by mindfulness & yoga expert, Paola Cruz.
You'll leave with a simple yet powerful practice that you can incorporate into your daily routine to stay grounded amidst the hustle of tech life.

Conscious Tech Conversation: After the meditation, you are invited to participate in an interactive conversation about ongoing challenges, limitations, and opportunity regarding 'Conscious Tech'.

Mingle, Connect & Meet Your Hosts: The floor is open to connecting with each other and with your hosts!

Your Hosts:
Akash Chander is a Strategy Manager with Deloitte Consulting and he has advised governments globally on their digital transformation. He is also a World Economic Forum Global Shaper and has worked with several social enterprises and non-profits to develop innovative tech-enabled business models.

Paola Cruz is born and raised in Honduras. She is an internationally recognized 850HR E-RYT Yoga & Meditation instructor. She has been invited to teach at The NBA, Hilton Hotels, DHL, AVON, Nestle, etc.

Vidhi Chander: Vidhi spent several years working with Amazon as a software engineer. She is now completing her Tech MBA at Cornell. Vidhi is working on building two apps that are focused on spirituality and education.

Sahil Jain: Sahil Jain will be moderating the evening. He has worked in Uber as a strategist and at Walmart as an e-commerce director for sales strategy. He is a builder and operator and is currently studying spirituality as a way of life and integration into the modern head space. 
The Venue: The Chai Spot is one of NYC's secret gems located in the vibrant neighborhood of LES. It is a cozy and unique tea shop that invited relaxation and has an extensive menu of traditional Chai! They also offer homemade snacks and cookies.

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
