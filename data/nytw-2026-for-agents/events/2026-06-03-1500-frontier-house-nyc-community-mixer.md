## Description

Join us for the Frontier House NYC - Community Mixer on Wednesday, June 3rd, from 03:00 PM to 06:00 PM for notable speakers, amazing food & drink, and the best local DJs, on a beautiful Midtown rooftop.

​Frontier House is the premier digital community and event series for Frontier Innovators - investors, founders, ecosystem builders, and service providers boldly building the future outside of the traditional tech hubs. Our mission is to celebrate Frontier Innovation and democratize venture capital by drawing capital to underfunded markets across the US.

A special thanks to our Hosts: Eleven Wall Ventures, Ohio Startup Network, OneSixOne Ventures and Tech Week.

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
