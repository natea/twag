## Description

Join us for Frontier House NYC - a curated gathering bringing together Frontier Innovators, Investors and Ecosystem leaders during NY Tech Week.

The event will feature a fireside chat with Paige Craig (Outlander VC), early investor in Airbnb, Lyft, Scale AI, Gusto, and AngelList, alongside high-signal networking, cocktails, food, and music.

Frontier House is a national community and event platform focused on connecting innovators building outside traditional tech hubs and creating greater access to venture capital, talent, and opportunity across emerging ecosystems.
