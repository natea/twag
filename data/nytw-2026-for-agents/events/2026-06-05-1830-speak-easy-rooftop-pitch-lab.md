Your ideas are good. The question is: do they land?

Tech Week is full of conversations that matter, the ones that open doors, shape decisions, and move things forward.

This is your practice room.

Speak Easy: Pitch Lab is a hands-on evening for founders and ambitious professionals who want to sharpen how they pitch themselves, their ideas, or their next big move.

No lectures. No awkward spotlight. Real skill-building in a setting that doesn’t feel like a workshop.

How it works: Order a drink and pick up your Pitch Lab Passport, a self-guided practice card developed by Dr. Sumeyra Ozturk, executive communication expert and founder of Speech & Mind. As you mingle, experiment with techniques like Strategic Pausing, Vocal Authority, and Grounded Presence. Complete your Passport and walk away with sharper communication, stronger presence, and practical tools you’ll use the very next day.

WHAT TO EXPECT
