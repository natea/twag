## Description

Tech Week can be chaotic. Running between panels, pitching, networking, and feeling overstimulated. The Oasis is a daily retreat: a place to belong, breathe, and actually co-create.

We're building a persistent home base across the week where builders, founders, and creatives show up every day to flow together, have real conversations around meaningful topics, and leave refreshed and inspired. The series blends daily 'co-flow' drop-in sessions with curated collaborative programming: roundtable discussions, fireside chats, and mentor office hours. Each day seeds Flowstate Sparks, structured artifacts that keep the conversation going long after the week ends.

Day 3: Ikigai, Finding Purpose and Meaning

Today we slow down and turn inward. Using the Japanese concept of ikigai, the intersection of what you love, what you're good at, what the world needs, and what you can be paid for, we'll explore the questions that often get drowned out in the noise of building. Expect honest conversation, guided reflection, and the kind of company that helps you hear your own thinking more clearly. Whether you're between chapters, deep in one, or just sensing it's time to recalibrate, this is a space to sit with the bigger why.

With thanks to our venue partner, BELONG NYC

The Oasis is hosted at BELONG NYC (@belong.nyc), a hospitality-driven workspace and urban sanctuary in the heart of SoHo. Think airport lounge meets coworking: a high-design, high-service environment purpose-built for the in-between moments of life in motion. Beyond hosting our series, BELONG offers Flowstate members on-demand access to private meeting rooms, phone booths, and hot desks, plus showers and changing facilities, secure luggage storage, and rentable lockers throughout Tech Week. If you're juggling flights, workouts, dinners, and back-to-back meetings, it's the place to reset and keep going.

This event is part of #NYTechWeek, a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem.
