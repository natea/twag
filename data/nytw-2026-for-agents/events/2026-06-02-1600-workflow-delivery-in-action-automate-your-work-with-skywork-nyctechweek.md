## Description

Join Skywork at Tech Week 2026 for a hands-on workshop where we dive into the real-world application of AI, moving from concepts to execution-driven workflows. This session will be presented by Skywork’s Head of Operations and will include product presentations and real-world case studies showing how Skywork works in real production environments.

You will get to experience the capabilities of SkyBot, our brand-new 24/7 cloud-native AI assistant, along with a powerful multi-agent system designed to plan, coordinate, and autonomously execute structured workflows.

This workshop will be followed by a small hackathon session where attendees can work through their own workflows, and the Skywork team will be available for real-time support and guidance. This workshop is for founders, operators, educators, consultants, and anyone else who wants to learn about the application of AI for real-world use cases. Unlike other product demos, we’re talking about the actual breakdown of entire workflows, not individual features, so attendees can really experience AI for themselves!

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
