## Description

Rock Yard Ventures' FUTURE TITANS presents the first-of-its-kind, exclusive private screening of The Big Short, featuring live commentary from Greg Lippmann — the Deutsche Bank trader portrayed by Ryan Gosling in the film.

The Big Short tells the story of those who saw what others missed and had the conviction to act when the world thought they were crazy.

During the film, for the first time ever, Greg will provide live commentary on how events actually unfolded, offering firsthand insight into a trade that defined an era. Following the screening, he will share lessons from building LibreMax Capital, which now manages over $13 billion in assets, and discuss conviction, risk, and navigating asymmetric opportunities.

And to make it even cooler, each attendee gets a signed copy of the book to take home!
