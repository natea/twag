## Description

​A curated group of founders, investors, operators, technologists, creatives, and executives will gather at a private residence near Union Square for an intimate evening of cocktails, conversation, and connection.

​Hosted by Norma Chu, the Founder, Chairwoman & CEO of DDC Enterprise (NYSE: DDC), a global consumer platform and top-30 public Bitcoin treasury company and Peter Zhang (Chairman of quantitative fund Alphataraxia Capital), the evening brings together many of the people shaping what comes next across AI, consumer, crypto, media, infrastructure, energy, culture, and technology.

​The guest list is intentionally curated to include female founders, senior operators, investors, builders, and ambitious creatives who value genuine relationships, thoughtful conversation, and shared curiosity over transactional networking.

​Expect a warm, low-key, and high-signal atmosphere — the kind of night where conversations naturally move from startups and investing to culture, philosophy, media, music, and the future of technology.

​The evening will unfold throughout the residence and its two open terraces, with cocktails, wine and heavy hors d'oeuvres throughout the night.

​No panels. No pitches. No forced networking.
Just exceptional people in a beautiful New York setting.

This is a curated gathering with limited capacity, so attendance is subject to approval.

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
