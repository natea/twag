## Description

For our 4th year running, Mango NYC is running back our annual Tech Week mixer for South Asian professionals in the city! Expect great drinks, conversations, chai, and a ton of mango bites.

Join the WA to plan Tech Week moves and hear about the best events: https://chat.whatsapp.com/HXfCaaXwPAGBKod2pNd0hB?mode=gi_t. Request access to the Tech Week 2026 group and DM the admin with your name for entry

This year, we’re bringing NYC’s largest South Asian community (Mango), NYC’s US-India startup network (CorridorX), and the bank that has been investing in south asian founders for decades.

A Note from SVB: For more than four decades, Silicon Valley Bank has been at the center of the innovation economy, supporting founders, startups, and investors as they build what’s next. SVB goes beyond traditional banking—connecting clients to capital, insights, events, and a global network to help them grow and succeed at every stage. To learn more or get started with SVB, connect with the team at the event or visit svb.com.


This event is a part of #NYTechWeek - a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
