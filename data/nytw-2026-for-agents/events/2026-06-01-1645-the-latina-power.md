## Description

Last year we hosted the Latina Tech Mixer. This year, we’re being more intentional.

Latina Power brings together women founders, investors, and community leaders for an evening of honest conversations, practical insights, and intentional networking during Latino Tech Week.

The event will feature two sharp panels: first, a conversation with women founders on what it really takes to build from zero to one— the decisions, challenges, and lessons behind the journey. Then, a conversation with investors on how founders can better understand the current tech and funding landscape, approach VCs, and stand out when raising capital.

We’ll close the evening with networking, food, and drinks— creating space to connect with founders, investors, operators, and others shaping the Latino and women-led tech ecosystem.

Same energy, sharper focus, bigger platform.

Have a question for our women founders or investors? Please share it with us!
