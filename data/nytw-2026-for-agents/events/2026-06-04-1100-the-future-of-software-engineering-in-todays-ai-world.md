## Description

The conversation about whether AI will replace software developers has gone from contrarian to maintsream in the past year. The loudest voices disagree on what comes next. Some say coding is ‘practically solved’ and the title of software engineer will disappear. Others say developer roles will only become more solidified with AI. This panel will explore these ideas first-hand. 

Moderated by The Deep View’s Senior Reporter, Sabrina Ortiz. 
Panelists:
-John Allspaw, founder, Adaptive Capacity Labs and former CTO, Etsy
-Baishakhi Ray, Associate Professor, AI and Software Engineering, Columbia University
-Carrol Chang, CEO, Andela
-Neel Sundaresan, GM of Automation and AI, IBM
