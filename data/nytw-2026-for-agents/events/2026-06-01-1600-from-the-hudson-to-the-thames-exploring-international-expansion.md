## Description

Request to join us on Monday 1 June (4–6pm) for an exclusive panel bringing together founders, operators, and advisors to unpack the realities of scaling across the US–UK corridor. The discussion will cut through the noise on international expansion with a dive into market entry strategy, product and operations localization, regulatory and data considerations, plus the culture factors that make cross border growth work.

Featuring a headline speaker from Duolingo and advisors with deep experience supporting venture backed companies, the session offers grounded, actionable insight for teams navigating transatlantic growth.

Hosted by EisnerAmper, Blick Rothenberg, Osborne Clarke, and the British Consulate General New York’s Tech Team, the event convenes a strong network of operators and specialists across the ecosystem.

A networking reception will follow, connecting founders, investors, and advisors active in the US–UK tech landscape.

Location will be shared with approved attendees.  Please note - entry will not be permitted unless attendees have registered in advance.

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
