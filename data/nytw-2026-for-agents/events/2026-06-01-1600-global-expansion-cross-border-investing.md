A curated gathering focused on fundraising, scaling companies, and deploying capital across borders.

Hosted by Yorkseed, with the introduction of Valois — our private relationship and capital layer designed for high-trust connections within the global venture ecosystem.

→ Expanding into new markets and ecosystems
→ Building globally from day one
