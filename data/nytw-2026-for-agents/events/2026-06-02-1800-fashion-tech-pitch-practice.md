## Description

In the startup world, your pitch is your lifeline to turn a week like NYTechWeek into your company's big break.

So, we're giving 10 Fashion, Retail and Beauty Tech startups 60 seconds (no slides!) to convince a room full of investors, advisors, and potential team members that they're the next big thing.

Following each pitch, our expert panel of investors will give immediate, actionable feedback to help the founders refine their pitch to go out and make the most of TechWeek.
