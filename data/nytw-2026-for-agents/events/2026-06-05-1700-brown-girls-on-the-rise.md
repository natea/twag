## Description

Brown Girls on the Rise is an evening conversation centering the voices, journeys, and leadership of South Asian and Brown women across tech, startups, and innovation.

Bringing together founders, operators, and leaders, this panel goes beyond surface-level wins to explore the real stories behind growth, visibility, and navigating the ecosystem. Designed to feel personal and grounded, the evening creates space for honest dialogue, shared experiences, and meaningful connection within the community.

** Who Should Attend

- South Asian and Brown women across tech, startups, and innovation
- Founders, operators, investors, and emerging leaders
- Allies committed to supporting women-led growth and leadership

** What to Expect

- A thoughtfully curated panel of Brown women leaders
- Honest, close-to-home conversations on growth, leadership, and resilience
- Audience Q&A with space for real dialogue
