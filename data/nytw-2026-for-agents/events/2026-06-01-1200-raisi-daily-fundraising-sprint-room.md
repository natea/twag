## Description

Raisi Fundraising Sprint
Daily | Daytime Sessions

Walk in any weekday during Tech Week. Walk out with a custom built investor list, tightened outreach, and emails sent - all powered by Raisi

This is a daily working room for founders who are actively raising. You can drop in, plug into your raise, and make real progress alongside the Raisi team.

No panels. No presentations. This is execution time.

How it works

Each session is structured around focused execution blocks so you’re constantly moving:

- Build and refine your investor list using live market signals from Raisi
- Sharpen outreach so it actually gets replies
- Pressure test your materials where needed
- Send outreach during the session

You’re working inside your raise the entire time, with the team there to unblock you as you go.

What you get

- Immediate work product every time you attend 
- Live investor list teardown and refinement
- Real-time outreach feedback and iteration
- Structured checkpoints to keep momentum
- Entry into a drawing for a free month of Raisi if you send outreach during the session
- Rotating office hours with operators, angels, and partners

Who it’s for

- Founders actively raising or about to start
- People who want to compress weeks of fundraising work into a few focused sessions
- Founders trying to map out which investors they should focus on meeting during Tech Week 

Show up. Work your raise. Leave with progress.

About Raisi: 
Raisi is a fundraising operating system built to help founders run their raise end to end. From identifying the right investors to executing outreach and managing conversations, it replaces fragmented fundraising workflows with one system.

Since 2024, once every 50.1 minutes, a founder has met with an investor interested in their company through Raisi. The platform has facilitated 21,000+ positive connections between founders and investors and is powered by up-to-date signals from millions of live interactions across the private markets.
