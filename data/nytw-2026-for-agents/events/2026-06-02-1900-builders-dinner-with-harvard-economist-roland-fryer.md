## Description

Join Harvard economist Roland Fryer — founding partner at EO Ventures, MacArthur "Genius" Fellow, and John Bates Clark Medal winner (best American economist under 40) — for a small private dinner. 

The room: technical builders shipping at the applied AI companies defining this generation, plus engineers and operators thinking seriously about what they'll build next. 

Salon-style conversation with Roland. Off-the-record, no pitches. Once you submit to join, our team will be in touch. 

EO Ventures is an early-stage fund backing companies that increase economic mobility in America, founded by Roland Fryer and Bill Helman, former Managing Partner at Greylock.
