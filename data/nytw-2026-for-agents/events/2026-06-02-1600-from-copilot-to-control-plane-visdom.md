## Description

From Copilot to Control Plane: Building an AI-Native SDLC with VISDOM 

AI SDLC with VISDOM 2.0

Many teams start their AI journey the same way: they introduce AI coding assistants, see early excitement… and then watch usage drop and ROI disappear. 

In this live session, together with guest speakers from leading companies, we’ll share real experiences from AI adoption - what works, what breaks, and how teams are pushing through to real results. We’ll walk through how to assess where you stand today and what it takes to make your SDLC actually ready for AI with a practical demo of the VISDOM platform.


Who should join?
CTOs, VPs of Engineering, Heads of Platform, and senior engineers responsible for developer productivity and AI adoption strategy as well as senior engineers frustrated that their AI tools work in demos but not in their CI/CD pipeline.

Why it’s worth your time?
- See how an AI-ready SDLC looks in practice (live demo)
- Understand how to assess your AI maturity step by step
- Learn how to start small, validate quickly, and evolve without big upfront risk.

What to expect?
Visdom live session & platform walkthrough
A hands-on session showing how to evaluate your AI maturity and define next steps without a big-bang transformation.
- Deep repository scanning that builds the semantic context fabric AI agents actually need - not just open files, but the full dependency and intent graph across the codebase
- Blast radius scoring on an AI-generated PR before it touches CI - catching what code review misses when your team merges 3x more than last quarter
- Governed sandbox execution with full audit trails - the missing layer between "the AI suggested it" and "it's running in production"
- A live maturity assessment on a real delivery pipeline - where it stands today and the smallest concrete move that unlocks the next level

A live demo of VISDOM showing how it removes friction across context, execution, and validation, turning your SDLC into a system AI agents can reliably operate on

Speakers
Practitioners working hands-on with AI-driven delivery, developer tooling, and real-world implementation.

Bonus
We’ll be giving away signed copies of “Vibe Engineering: Best Practices, Mistakes, and Tradeoffs.”
