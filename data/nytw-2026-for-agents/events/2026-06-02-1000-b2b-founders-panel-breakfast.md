## Description

Join us for a morning with B2B founders, investors and operators during #NYTechWeek.

Panel: The Enterprise Buyer Has Changed. Has Your Sales Motion?
How to close deals when customers know more than they used to

The rise of AI has created a new kind of enterprise buyer. One who can evaluate your product, stress-test your claims, and map your competitive landscape before you've even booked the intro call.

This session brings together operators and investors who've rethought how B2B software gets sold in an era of informed, skeptical buyers, and what it actually takes to build a sales motion that wins in 2026.

We'll dig into what's changed at the top of the funnel, how the best GTM teams are adapting, and what founders consistently get wrong when they try to close at scale.
