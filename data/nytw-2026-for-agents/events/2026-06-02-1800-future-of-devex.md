## Description

The way we build software is changing faster than most of us can keep up with. If you want to see where it's headed, this event is for you.

Future of DevEx series is coming back to New York for Tech Week!
Join us to explore how AI is reshaping the daily reality of building software. Not just the tools, but the workflows, the rituals, the way teams write, review, ship, and maintain code. Some of it is genuinely better. Some of it is overhyped. We're here to have that honest conversation.

The evening features a series of tech talks from builders and toolmakers who are experiencing this transformation firsthand, followed by open networking with a room full of engineers and technical founders who are navigating the same thing.

Come for the conversation, stay for the drinks.
