## Description

Hosted by Relm, in partnership with Metropolis Group, JuuNoo, and Proptimal.

Commercial real estate is at an inflection point. AI is no longer a concept on a conference slide — it's being deployed across the deal lifecycle, from underwriting and diligence to permitting, financial modeling, and physical buildout. The question isn't whether the industry will change. It's who's leading it, and how.

During NYC Tech Week, join us for an intimate evening of candid conversation and high-signal networking with the operators, founders, and investors who are building the future of CRE — right now.

We'll kick off with a focused panel — AI, Capital & CRE: What's Actually Working in the Built Environment — featuring practitioners from across the real estate stack, followed by open networking in Juunoo's NYC showroom. No fluff. No keynotes. Just real talk on what's working, what's not, and where the industry is headed.

Speakers:

Moderated by Jackson Reiter, Co-Founder @ Relm

Evan Robinson — CEO & Co-Founder, Relm | Evan Robinson is the CEO and Co-Founder of Relm, an AI platform purpose-built for real estate intelligence and deal analysis. His career began in public policy — working on zoning and housing reform at LA City Hall and on U.S. Congressional campaigns — before moving into growth strategy consulting for Fortune 100 firms at MediaLink. At Relm, he focuses on unifying the real estate ecosystem through automation and data intelligence. Evan holds a B.A. in Political Science from New York University.

Kristen Fortino — VP, Metropolis Group | Kristen Fortino is the VP of Business Strategy at Metropolis Group, a specialized consulting firm that acts as a liaison between real estate developers, architects, and city agencies on permitting, zoning, and code compliance. She also serves as the Founding Head of Sales & Partnerships at Relm and is the Founder of OnePoint Real Estate Group, a woman-owned firm focused on multi-family investment and code consulting. Her work sits at the intersection of real estate operations, investment strategy, and technology adoption — giving her a uniquely cross-functional view of where the industry is heading.

Maxime De Scheemaeker — CEO, Juunoo | Maxime De Scheemaeker is the Co-CEO of Juunoo's USA division, leading the company's North American expansion of its circular, demountable interior wall systems for commercial offices, schools, and healthcare spaces. Originally from Belgium, Maxime joined Juunoo in 2022 as VP of Sales to spearhead the company's US launch — building a local team from the ground up and reaching early sales targets before being elevated to Co-CEO. His background spans sales leadership and product expansion across multiple office and commercial real estate companies in Europe and the US.

Lilian Chen — CEO & Founder, Proptimal | Lilian Chen is the Founder and CEO of Proptimal, a software platform that enables any real estate team to build institutional-quality financial models without a single Excel formula. Drawing on her background in both commercial real estate and the Silicon Valley tech ecosystem, she is on a mission to put frontier AI tools in the hands of everyday operators and investors. She also writes and speaks widely on proptech as the author of The Proptech Girl, with appearances across Bisnow, NAIOP, Thesis Driven, and numerous industry podcasts and keynotes.
