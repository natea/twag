## Description

AI in Go-to-Market Masterclass: From Single-Player GTM to Multiplayer GTM

AI has made it easier than ever to hack GTM alone with Claude Code, Clay, Cursor, agents, and automations. But real go-to-market is still a team sport - inside the company and across customers, partners, creators, communities, and investors.

This hands-on Masterclass will focus on the shift from single-player GTM to multiplayer GTM: how teams and networks can use AI to learn faster, coordinate better, and build growth systems that compound.

Join us on Wednesday, June 3, 2026 at Insight Partners for the next AI in Go-to-Market Master Class, hosted by the NYC GTM community and supported by Swan.

This event's special lineup will feature world-class GTM experts Eric Nowoslawski, Sangram Vajre, Nimo Shkedy and more - who will share their thoughts, strategies, and methods with the community. It's an event you won't want to miss!
