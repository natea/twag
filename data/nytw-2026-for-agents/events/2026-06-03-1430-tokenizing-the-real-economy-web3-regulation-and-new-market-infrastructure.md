## Description

Blockchain and Web3 are moving past the crypto-only conversation. Tokenization is becoming real market infrastructure for private capital — reshaping how real estate, energy, fintech and other traditional industries issue, manage and distribute assets. This panel brings together a technology, legal and market perspective to explore what's actually being built, what the new U.S. regulatory landscape enables, and where the real opportunities sit for founders, investors and corporates.

Hosted by Wootic (Argentina-based product and tokenization studio) together with ALVK Law (U.S. fintech and regulatory counsel), the conversation will feature Jorge Atala (CEO, Wootic), Agustín Iraola (Attorney, ALVK Law) and a third guest from the blockchain, fintech and investment ecosystem (TBA).

Designed for investors, founders, corporates, legal and fintech professionals, and anyone working at the intersection of private markets and Web3 infrastructure.
