## Description

Join CieloVision and WillDom to see how AI-powered signage turns passive screens into real-time revenue drivers delivering a measured 25.7% lift over static content in real deployments. We'll walk through the "screens that see" thesis: what changes when a display understands who's in front of it, and how privacy-first edge processing makes it possible at scale.

You'll see real results from active deployments across retail, QSR, fitness, hospitality, and family entertainment, plus the architecture behind it, real-time audience detection running on Samsung VXT, with no personal data leaving the device. 

Imre Szenttornyay, CieloVision CEO
Beatriz Burgos, WillDom SVP of Marketing  & Managing Director


Who should attend
This session is built for retail, QSR, hospitality, fitness, and entertainment operators rethinking the role of in-store screens, as well as brand and marketing leaders looking for measurable lift from physical media. It will also be valuable for CIOs, CTOs, and digital transformation leads evaluating edge AI architectures, and for investors tracking where intelligent signage is heading next.
