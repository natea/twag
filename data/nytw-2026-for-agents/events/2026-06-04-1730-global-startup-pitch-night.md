## Description

Founder Social Club and Manifest are teaming up for an International Founder & Investor Pitch Night during NY Tech Week. 

This one brings together founders, investors, operators, and corporates all in one room. 

We'll kick off with a founder panel and live Q&A, then hand the floor to a lineup of founders pitching in front of a room full of people you'll actually want to know, capped off with a long reception built for the kind of conversations that turn into your next round, hire, or partnership. 

Come see who's building and meet the investors backing them.
