## Description

Google for Developers and Build with AI present: Create your own AI Agent

Hosted at the Google for Startups Hub!

Learn how to use Google's Agent Development Kit (ADK) to build and deploy AI agents and multi-agent systems. Follow along live as Google Developer Experts take you step by step through building a working example that connects into real AI workflows.

This year's codelab at NYC Tech Week will be presented in real-time, so you can hear tips and tricks straight from Google Developer Experts at the forefront of building with AI. 

We’ll support you to troubleshoot and master the techniques and tools being taught.

​To participate, you must bring your own laptop and power cable. The activities are intended for laptops and cannot be completed on a tablet or phone.

Make sure to check out other virtual AI Learning Labs at https://ailearninglab.live/ 

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.

We expect these sessions to fill up quickly and be in high demand.  Space is limited and sessions are first-come, first-served, even as an approved guest.  Please be sure to arrive by the check in time listed on your session agenda.  Spots will be given away to waitlist beginning 10 minutes before each session.  

Guests will be permitted in the Google for Startups Hub only during the time slot they are approved for.  

We invite all guests to return for the Google For Developers Happy Hour at 4p.  Only those with valid badges will be permitted, space allowing.
