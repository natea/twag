## Description

Innovation at the Regulatory Frontier: A Digital Assets Cocktail Evening

The digital assets space moves fast — and the regulatory landscape is evolving to keep pace. Whether you're launching infrastructure, deploying capital, or navigating compliance as new frameworks take shape, the next chapter belongs to the builders and investors who move boldly and stay ahead of the curve.

Join Fox Rothschild and EisnerAmper for an evening of conversation and connection with the founders, executives, senior industry leaders, investors, and operators shaping the future of digital assets, fintech and blockchain. The evening will feature a brief fireside chat on the forces reshaping crypto regulation — but mostly open networking over cocktails with the builders, thought leaders and backers driving the space forward.

Leave the pitch decks at home — this one's about good drinks, sharp minds, and the kind of conversation that actually moves deals and ideas forward.

Date: Thursday, June 4th 

Time: 5:00 PM – 7:00 PM

Location: Manhattan, New York, NY. The venue address will be shared with confirmed attendees only.

Hosted by: Tamar Gubins and Stephen A. Aschettino, Partners at Fox Rothschild LLP and John Delalio and Chris Brodersen from EisnerAmper. 

This is a curated gathering for founders, executives, senior industry leaders, and investors in the digital assets ecosystem. Space is limited and registration is required.

Fox Rothschild is a national law firm that delivers strategic and practical solutions for clients. Our Fintech & Digital Assets attorneys advise clients navigating the rapidly evolving landscape of financial technology, providing guidance on regulatory compliance, transactions, and emerging legal frameworks.

Tamar Gubins is a business-oriented attorney who advises emerging and technology companies on formation, financing, corporate governance, and M&A throughout the full lifecycle of a technology company. 

Stephen Aschettino is the Chair of Fox Rothschild's Fintech & Digital Assets practice, with over 30 years of experience at the intersection of law, finance, and technology. A former Chief Legal Officer of a leading payment processor, he is a frequent speaker and author on payments, blockchain, and cybersecurity issues. 

EisnerAmper is one of the largest business consulting firms in the world, with a dedicated Blockchain and Digital Assets Group providing tax, accounting, assurance, advisory, forensics, and outsourcing services to the bitcoin, blockchain, and Web3 community. Our clients rely on us to help them navigate regulatory requirements and emerging risks in a space at the forefront of innovation. 

John Delalio is a Partner at EisnerAmper and member of the firm's Blockchain & Digital Asset Services Group, with over 25 years of experience modernizing financial operations through cloud-based technologies. He serves on the Board of Directors of NY Tech Alliance, New York's leading tech nonprofit.

Chris Brodersen is a Managing Director at EisnerAmper specializing in blockchain and digital asset solutions, with nearly 40 years of cross-industry experience. Formerly a Blockchain Lab Leader at a Big 4 firm, he now leads the firm's blockchain and digital asset practice.

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
