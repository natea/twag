## Description

Trans talent is building the future of tech — and this is the room where we talk about it.

Join Cool Trans Girl, Avid Society, and NYC Pride for an evening of real conversations, high-signal connections, and a night you’ll actually want to be at.

We’re bringing together 100+ trans founders, creators, and professionals at 7 World Trade Center, hosted by MSCI Pride & Allies, for a panel on building in the digital economy, a fireside chat on AI and the future of visibility, and curated networking with drinks, refreshments, and a live DJ set.

Featured speakers include Darie Santana, Allesandra Miller, Aishani Majumdar, and Zo Touloupis, hosted by Aria Brooks.

This is Tech Week. This is Pride Month. And this is the only event that sits credibly at the intersection of both.

Free to attend. Invite-only guestlist.

This event is part of #NYTechWeek — a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. 

Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
