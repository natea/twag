Join us for the 2026 NY K-Tech Demo Day, an exciting showcase of 12 innovative Korean tech-service startups — SeoulDynamics, RNA Analytics, EMCT, VOINOSIS, SpeedFloor, ANIAI, AISUM, Crosshub, TELADIA, PulseAd, Gadget Korea, and SPLAB — ready to expand into the U.S. market!

Experience startup pitches, VC networking, investor meetings, expert panels, and high-value connections — all focused on Korean innovation and global market growth.
