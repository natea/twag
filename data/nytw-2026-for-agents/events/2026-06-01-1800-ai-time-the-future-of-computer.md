---Speakers---

Ang Li is CEO and Co-Founder of Simular, creating AI agents that use computers like humans, including sai.work. He earned his Ph.D. from University of Maryland (2017) and led applied research at Google DeepMind (2017-2021). He has published 42+ papers in top-tier AI conferences.

Christopher Fong (Berkeley-Columbia MBA '10) is the founder of Key, an AI startup unlocking professional networks. He founded Xoogler.co, Google's alumni community with 28,000 members. An angel investor in 100+ startups, Fong spent 8+ years at Google on the Project Sand Hill team working with VCs and startups.
