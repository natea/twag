## Description

50–70% of private equity deals fail to create value. The reason is almost never financial. 

It's Stakeholder Risk .

From regulators and employees to local communities and strategic partners, mismanaging stakeholder dynamics can erode value just as quickly as strong alignment can accelerate it. 

As AI rapidly reshapes industries, a new layer of risk is emerging making stakeholder alignment more critical than ever. 

This session explores how leading firms identify, quantify, and proactively manage stakeholder risk, including AI-related exposures, as a core lever of value creation.

Featuring: 

Jennifer G. Bates, founder & CEO of HumanFactor Enterprises, a stakeholder risk Intelligence platform.
 
Known for her ability to see what others cannot, Jennifer Baytes transforms Stakeholder Risk and decision-making into structured, data-driven frameworks. Her work has contributed to outcomes including valuation increases of up to 800%, $140M+ in M&A readiness and transaction positioning, multi-million-dollar exits, and over $600M in enterprise value creation.

Key discussion points:

Why stakeholder risk is often overlooked in traditional PE due diligence

How misalignment with regulators, employees, or communities can destroy deal value

The rise of AI-related stakeholder risks: bias, transparency, data privacy, and regulatory backlash

Frameworks for identifying and mapping key stakeholders across the investment lifecycle

Real-world case studies: where stakeholder dynamics drove unexpected outcomes

Integrating stakeholder and AI risk into value creation plans and exit strategies

Preparing portfolio companies for AI governance
