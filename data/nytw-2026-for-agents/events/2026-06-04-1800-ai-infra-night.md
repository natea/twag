## Description

AI compute has become one of the most valuable and contested resources in the economy. Understanding what goes into a GPU hour — the chips, power, land, capital structure, and operational expertise — is essential to understanding where AI goes next.

Join us for an evening with cloud operators, investors, and infrastructure leaders exploring the full value chain behind AI compute. The conversation will cover today’s deployment bottlenecks, how the financing and capital stack shape what actually gets built, and how the ecosystem can evolve to unlock compute for a broader range of offtakers — from frontier labs to enterprises, startups, and researchers.
