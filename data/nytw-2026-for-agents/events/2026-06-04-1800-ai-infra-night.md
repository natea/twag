## Description

AI compute has become one of the most valuable and contested resources in the economy. Understanding what goes into a GPU hour — the chips, power, land, capital structure, and operational expertise — is essential to understanding where AI goes next. But understanding the stack is only half the story. The other half is how that capacity actually gets priced, contracted, and delivered to the teams building on top of it.

Join us for an evening with cloud operators, investors, infrastructure leaders, and the marketplace and procurement layer that connects them. The conversation will cover today's deployment bottlenecks, how the financing and capital stack shape what actually gets built, and how the ecosystem can evolve to unlock compute for a broader range of offtakers — from frontier labs to enterprises, startups, and researchers.

Co-hosted by TowersTeam, EverGrid.ai, and Compute Exchange — bringing together the operators, the capital, and the procurement marketplace behind today's GPU economy.

This is an intimate, invite-only gathering kept small to keep the conversation real. Please RSVP — admission is subject to host approval.

Thank you Wix, we're thrilled to host this event at their stunning New York headquarters!

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek — a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
