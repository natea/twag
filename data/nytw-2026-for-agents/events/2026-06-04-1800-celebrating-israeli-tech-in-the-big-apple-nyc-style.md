## Description

Join 97212 Ventures for a special NY Tech Week event celebrating the growth of the Israeli tech scene in NYC. With over 600 Israeli startups based in the Big Apple (among them 30+ unicorns!), NYC has become the 2nd largest tech hub for Israeli startups outside of Tel-Aviv. 

Join an intimate group of founders, VC investors, LPs and friends of the fund as we celebrate NY Tech Week in style at one of NYC's most amazing rooftops. 

The event is in collaboration with our partners - IDB Bank, Bereisheet Tech, Pearl Cohen, and Deel. 

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
