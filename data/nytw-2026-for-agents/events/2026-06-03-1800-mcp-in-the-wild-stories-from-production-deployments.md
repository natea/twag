## Description

A lot of MCP content right now is conceptual, but not this event!

"MCP in the Wild" brings together engineers, product leaders, and business builders shipping MCP in production, for a demo session followed by an honest roundtable on where the ecosystem stands.

- Stefan Lederer, Co-founder & CEO of Bitmovin: converting a video infrastructure and analytics suite into MCP servers, and what monetizing it looks like
- Chris Korhonen, Principle Engineer AI at OpenSea: building the internal AI platform that lets OpenSea employees own their own agentic workflows
- Michael Yaroshefsky, VP of AI at Usercentrics: running an internal MCP gateway in production
- Pierre-Louis Theron, Co-founder & CEO of Alpic: hosting and deploying MCP Apps with Alpic Cloud and Skybridge, Alpic's open-source framework

After showing us what they've built, we'll sit down together and have an honest look at how MCP is changing both internal enterprise workflows and how customers discover and use software. We look at adoption, growing pains and where gaps still exist.

MCP in the Wild is part of #NYTechWeek, a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem.
