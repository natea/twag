## Description

PwC is hosting a business focused executive forum for CEOs, CFOs, and senior leaders from high growth unicorn startups preparing for the transition to public markets.

The program will focus on the critical decisions, strategic inflection points, and common pitfalls that can shape IPO readiness, public company operating discipline, governance readiness, forecast credibility, disclosure readiness, institutional demand, revenue quality, and related capital markets considerations.

The morning will include a business focused breakfast discussion with perspectives from experienced public company operators, governance specialists, PwC leaders, capital markets participants, and an exchange representative. Attendees may also observe the New York Stock Exchange opening bell ceremony as part of the venue’s morning program.

Through candid discussion and practical perspectives, attendees will gain insights to help position their companies for the next stage of maturity as they evaluate the path to becoming public companies.

Location: New York Stock Exchange, 11 Wall Street, New York, NY

This event is part of #NYTechWeek, a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
