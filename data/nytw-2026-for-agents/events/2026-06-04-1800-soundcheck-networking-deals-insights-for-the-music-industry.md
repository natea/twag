## Description

Join PwC Deals for Soundcheck: Networking & Deals Insights for the Music Industry, an event bringing together companies and leaders across the music ecosystem, including publishers, streaming platforms, start-ups and other emerging players.

This networking-focused event is designed to foster meaningful connections across the music value chain while exploring the deal trends and transaction dynamics shaping the sector. We hope you’ll join us for an engaging evening of insights, conversation, light bites and networking with peers, PwC practitioners and industry participants
