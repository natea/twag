## Description

Most pitch events train founders to impress VCs. This one trains you to close customers.

C10 Labs, NY Robotics, and Wells Fargo are hosting a live pitch workshop for deep tech and robotics founders who sell to blue-collar and industrial buyers — plant managers, procurement teams, logistics operators, and regional decision-makers across Middle America.

Five selected founders will pitch live and receive a real-time teardown from a panel of experts: a marketing SME, a VC, and operators who actually buy this stuff. Feedback focuses on whether your story resonates with people who run shifts, manage crews, and keep systems online — not just people who write term sheets.

You may present:

A sales deck used with operators, plant managers, or procurement teams

An investor deck, if it's the primary tool driving pilots or enterprise adoption

Each selected founder gets:

A live pitch opportunity + deck walkthrough

Direct, candid feedback from a potential buyer, a marketing agency SME, and an investor

Practical guidance to make your story clearer and more credible in the field

Open application — submit your pitch to be considered. 3 slots reserved for C10 Labs cohort companies, 2 open to the broader community. Presenters announced onsite.

Breakfast and coffee provided by Wells Fargo.

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
