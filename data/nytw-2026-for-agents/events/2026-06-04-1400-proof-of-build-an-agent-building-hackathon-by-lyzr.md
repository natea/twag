## Description

PROOF OF BUILD
->An agent hackathon by Lyzr, during NY Tech Week.
->Six hours to build. One yard party to close it out.

Tech Week is full of panels about the future of AI. This isn't one of them.

Proof of Build is a working afternoon for people who'd rather spend six hours writing code than sitting through another keynote about code.

We're bringing together ~150 founding engineers, ML builders, and product engineers to build agents that solve something real - not a demo, not a slideware prototype, something you'd actually use on Monday. 

Lyzr is hosting, our infra is available if you want to use it, and our team will be in the room building alongside you.

Then we close the day properly. Once the builds land and the demos wrap, the yard opens - DJ, drinks, food, and the kind of crowd that'll still be talking about your agent at midnight.

Come with a laptop, a teammate (or find one here), and a problem you've been meaning to solve. Leave with a working agent, a small crowd that saw you ship it, and - if your build clears the bar - a prize.

Prize to be announced soon.

Who this is for: founding engineers, ML/AI engineers, senior software engineers, and technical founders currently building with agents or planning to. This is a hands-on build day, not a meetup - please only RSVP if you plan to actually code.

Format: Arrive 2 PM. Team up or fly solo. Build until 8 PM. Five-minute demos. Yard party runs until late. Light judging, real prizes, zero pitch decks.

Lyzr Events will confirm your spot within a few days of RSVP. Address shared with approved guests closer to the date.

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
