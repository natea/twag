## Description

Building a company, growing your personal wealth, and maintaining your sanity at the same time is easier said than done.

This brunch is a chance to step out of the "having it all together" mode and into the "what's really on your mind" conversation.

Join us for a candid and engaging conversation where we'll explore:
-- First hires, first big spend, first investor
-- Paying yourself vs. re-investing in the business
-- Equity vs. liquidity trade-offs
-- Building your personal balance sheet: how much of your capital to put into your biz and what to keep for you?
-- Concentration risk
-- Navigating pressure and creating little wins


--- About your hosts:
Lauren Humphreys is a Financial Advisor at UBS and passionate about making money conversations less complicated and stuffy. She's part of a NYC and Boston based Wealth Management team that works with entrepreneurs to help them navigate the personal side of building and scaling a business. 

Naseem Sayani is the Director of The Innovator’s Circle at WHAM (Women’s Health Access Matters), a member of the Milken Institute's Women's Health Network, and hosts The Capital Flex podcast. She was a founding General Partner at Emmeline Ventures, and currently holds Venture Partner roles at The Sparrow Fund and Zero Limits Capital. As a seasoned VC investor, Naseem is focused on backing ambitious female founders across healthtech and fintech, and has made 40+ investments from Pre-seed through Series A over the last five years. Naseem believes strongly in breaking cycles and redefining baselines, and brings 25+ years of experience in market-making, business-building, and operations to every conversation, cup of coffee, and whiteboard to help ambitious founders deliver outsized outcomes and impact.
