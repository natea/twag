## Description

GIDE Hackathon @ a16z NYC Tech Week

An elite, in-person hackathon hosted at the downtown Manhattan offices of Perkins Coie LLP (1155 6th Ave, 22nd Floor) during a16z NYC Tech Week.

Who Can Compete:
1. Up to 50 participants. (Limited Participants)
2. Developers, students and founders.
3. Prior GIDE hackathon winners auto-qualify (must confirm by Sunday, one week before).


Format:
30-min orientation → 5 hrs of in-building competition → 30-min judging → live presentations → awards for top 3

Platform Requirements:
Core solution must be built in GIDE and reviewable in a locally downloaded copy. MCPs, APIs, and external services are fair game, but teams must demo live to judges.

Judging:
30% Technical merit
30% Inventive use of GIDE
30% Effectiveness in solving the problem
10% Creativity

Schedule:
8:00 – 9:00 AM : Breakfast & Check-in
9:00 – 9:30 AM : Kickoff, rules, challenge reveal
10:00 AM : 3:00 PM : Hack
12:00 PM : Lunch 
3:00 PM : Judging begins
4:00 PM : Awards ceremony

Prizes announced live at the event.

Hosted in Perkins Coie LLP office

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com
