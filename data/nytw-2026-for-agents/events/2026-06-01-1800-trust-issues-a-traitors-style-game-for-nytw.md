## Description

Trust Issues is a live social deduction game - part party, part strategy, part social experiment/game (think Mafia meets the Traitors).

You’ll join a room of founders, builders, investors, and operators and get assigned a secret role that you'll play across a series of challenges.

Some of you will be trying to build the pot and trust, while others will be secretly working against you to try and break it.

No panels or pitch decks.

Just one question - "who can I trust?"

Come ready to play.
