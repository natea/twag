## Description

Stepping into NY Tech Week with one of the world’s most resilient and uncovered founder ecosystems.

Join us for an evening of impact, investment, and inspiration co-hosted by the American University of Beirut’s iPark and Impersonas. 

The event will feature American University of Beirut (AUB) Chief Innovation and Transformation Officer Dr. Yousif Asfour, Talal and Madiha Zein AUB Innovation Park (Zein AUB iPark) Director Maha Zouwayhed, and seven iPark-incubated entrepreneurs succeeding across the fields of AI, biotech, fintech, media, cybersecurity, and connectivity.   

Learn more about the approach behind iPark’s outsized success rates, network with angel investors, operators, and innovation leaders, and interact with a live AI avatar host that offers a glimpse into the future.
