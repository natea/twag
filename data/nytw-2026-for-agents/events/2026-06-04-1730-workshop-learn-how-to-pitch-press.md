## Description

Hosted by: Rock Yard Ventures, SutherlandGold, and Forum Ventures.

This is a tactical, hands-on workshop for venture-backed founders to execute on their communications and positioning strategy. This is not a panel; it is an intimate working session of highly curated venture-backed founders. They'll get hands-on support from PR experts.

At this session, you will work through:

Press Strategy: Drafting an actionable press release for an upcoming milestone.

Media Outreach: The mechanics of pitching and building relationships with journalists.

Market Mapping: Building a visual market map to define your competitive category.

The Outcome:
You will leave the event with a completed press release, an outreach plan, and a refined market map ready for immediate use

About the hosts: 

​​​​​​​Rock Yard Ventures is an early-stage venture capital firm backing exceptional founders redefining technology across core and foundational industries.

Sutherland Gold is a communications agency that has spent 20 years shaping the narratives of companies like Cloudflare, Circle, Brex, and Superhuman. They specialize in helping venture-backed startups move past the noise to secure top-tier media and own their category. 

Forum Ventures is the leading early-stage fund, accelerator, and venture studio for B2B tech startups. Founded in 2014 and based in New York, San Francisco, and Toronto, we’re on a mission to make the zero-to-one journey easier, more accessible, and successful for founders. Forum is investing in 65+ companies this year.

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
