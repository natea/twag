## Description

The best ideas won't come from a boardroom — they come from a room full of builders from diverse fields who show up with laptops and leave with something worth pitching. 

VibeHack: Health x AI is a hackathon & pitch event during NY Tech Week where teams vibe-code health-AI projects, get mentored by experts, and pitch to judges for actual prizes followed by a reception. 

If you vibe-code, this is your regular weekend. If you don’t just yet, come and learn with us!

📆 June 7th, 2026 
💻 8:30am-4pm Build all day 
⏱️ 4:30-7pm Pitch Competition 
🤑 7-8:30pm Awards Reception
🌸 Everyone may attend the pitch | Must RSVP 

Co-hosted by Girls Into VC NYC, Fierce Foundry, Scalpels & Spreadsheets, and NYTechWeek, this hackathon is intentionally built around gender inclusivity because the future of health innovation should reflect the people it's meant to serve.

Whether you're a dev, designer, PM, founder, operator, or someone who just can't stop thinking about the future of healthcare — grab your team and show up.

No deck required. Just bring your laptop, your idea, and the energy to build. 

**SPOTS ARE FULL FOR HACKERS** We will reach out via email to confirm selected hackers and your teams. All attendees welcome to come to both the pitch and reception, but must RSVP due to capacity limit.

Girls Into VC is the first non-profit organization dedicated to empowering the next generation of women in Venture Capital.

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek —a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
