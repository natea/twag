## Description

This panel (hosted by Topline Pro) brings together accomplished female leaders and investors who have navigated the highest levels of the tech ecosystem. Panelists will share candid perspectives on what it takes to lead with conviction, build influence in male-dominated rooms, and make strategic bets — both on companies and on themselves. Ideal for founders, operators, and aspiring leaders looking for actionable insight on scaling their careers and their impact. 

Panelists include:
 - Molly Alter, Partner at Northzone 
- Shannon Kay, COO and Co-Founder of Topline Pro
- Susan Lyne, Managing Partner at BBG Ventures
- Elaine Page, Chief People and Strategy Officer at Zinnia, former CPO at TaxJar (a Stripe company)



This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com
