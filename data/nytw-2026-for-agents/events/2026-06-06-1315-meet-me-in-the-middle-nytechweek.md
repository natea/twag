## Description

Meet Me in the Middle

Volley & Vibe × Growing the Middle

You know what's better than networking? Networking where you're actually doing something.

Meet Me in the Middle is a pickleball social for mid-career professionals who are done with stiff networking events. We're going to keep it simple — a few quick pointers on how to actually work a room, hit some balls, and then close it out with a real networking session where you can put it into practice.

Come to play. Leave with connections that actually go somewhere.

Here's how the day flows:
🏓 Quick networking tips from GTM founder Mariah Bayne — practical, no fluff
🎾 Pickleball session led by the crew at Volley & Vibe — they'll get you up to speed whether you've played 100 times or never touched a paddle
🤝 Networking session to close it out

All skill levels welcome. Volley & Vibe has you covered on the court — just show up.

Hosted by Growing the Middle & Volley & Vibe
