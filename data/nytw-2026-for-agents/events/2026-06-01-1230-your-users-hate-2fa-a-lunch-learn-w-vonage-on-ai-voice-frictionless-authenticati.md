## Description

Two of the biggest friction points in any product: getting users through your login flow, and getting them to actually pick up the phone. 

Join Vonage for a founder-focused lunch where we'll demo how Vonage's Voice API and Silent Auth features tackle both. You'll see an AI voice agent handle a real inbound call, and watch a user log in without ever touching their phone. Bring your questions.
