## Description

Most workshops leave you with notes. This one leaves you with a shipped plan, an app that keeps you on track, and a date your future attendees can already RSVP to.

For 2 hours, 100 people log in together and plan their first tech event, live. Not in a Google Doc you'll forget about. In a custom app built for exactly this, with your own profile, your own plan, and automated follow-ups that won't let you ghost yourself.

How it works:

Before the session, you get access to your profile in the app. During the session, I walk you through each module on Zoom and you fill it in live: your city, your niche, your date, your venue shortlist, your first invite list, your sponsor ask. Peer breakouts between modules to pressure-test each piece.

After the session, the app does what most workshops can't. It emails you on day 3, day 7, day 14, day 30. Reminds you to contact venues. Hands you the templates when you need them. Tracks your progress against your first edition date. Shows you a live map of where the other 99 operators are shipping.

Why do this with me:

I run Tech Wednesdays, 157 editions in. Every Wednesday for three years. The app is built from the exact checklist and templates I've used to keep it alive that long. You get the tools, not just the talk.

What you leave with:

- A profile in the app with your plan filled in
- A first edition date on the calendar
- Venue outreach emails auto-drafted, ready to send
- Your first 20 invites named, with message templates
- A sponsor ask in your voice
- Automated follow-ups for the next 90 days so you don't drift
- A public dashboard of 99 other operators doing the same thing
- A 6-month check-in to see who actually ran one

Who it's for:

Anyone who has ever thought someone should run a tech event in my city and suspected that someone is them.

Format:

2 hours. Virtual. 100 seats. Cameras encouraged. App access (free!) for 6 months.

You don't leave with inspiration. You leave with an event that's half-built before you close the laptop.
