AI-native competitors are moving fast, but if you’re a Series A+ company, you’re not starting from scratch. You have real users, established workflows, and systems that can’t afford to break.

This panel is built for teams navigating that reality.

We’ll dig into how leading health tech companies are layering AI into live products, without disrupting what already works. From prioritizing use cases to managing risk in production environments, this session focuses on what actually happens beyond the hype.

Expect an honest look at what’s working, what’s breaking, and how teams are sequencing AI integration while keeping their core product stable.

If you're building in healthcare and thinking about AI beyond prototypes, this is a room you want to be in.
