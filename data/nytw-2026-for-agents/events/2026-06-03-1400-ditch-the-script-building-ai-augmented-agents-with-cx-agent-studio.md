## Description

Let’s be honest, we all know how frustrating those rigid, scripted chatbots are for users, but hiring a massive 24/7 support team isn't exactly an option when you're managing a lean burn rate.

The future of customer experience isn't about writing better logic trees; it’s about giving AI the right instructions and the right data. In this session, we’ll show you how to deliver enterprise-grade CX without the enterprise-level headcount using CX Agent Studio. Discover how to build "Playbook-based" agents that use natural language to navigate complex, unpredictable customer journeys with the nuance of a human and the flawless factual accuracy of a machine.
