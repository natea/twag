## Description

Most AI tools are great at generating outputs.
Few are built for real design workflows in commercial-grade.

Join us for a curated happy hour with professional designers, creative leads (and wines🍷) exploring how AI can support commercial-grade, consistent design — not just one-off results.

We’re bringing together a small group of brand, marketing, industrial and visual designers and teams in NYC who care about systems, quality, and scaling their creative work.

Throughout the evening, we’ll be hosting live, lightweight demos of Virse — an AI-native infrastructure that enables professional design teams to scale the production of high-quality visual assets in a systematic and automated way.

No formal presentations. No long talks.
Just good conversations, thoughtful creatives, and a chance to see how AI can actually fit into real design pipelines.

Who this is for:
- Brand, marketing, industrial and visual designers working on shipped work
- Creative directors and design leads
- Designers at studios, and in-house teams
- You are excited about creative AI!!
