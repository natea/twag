## Description

In a city built on data, why do we still sign leases based on a 5-minute walkthrough and a "gut feeling"? We call this "The Zero Data Decision," and it’s the most expensive mistake a New Yorker can make.

Houstory is hosting a tactical workshop designed to bridge the gap between physical reality and digital history. We’ll teach you how to "debug" an apartment before you sign the lease. This isn't just about aesthetics; it's about infrastructure, accountability, and your right to a safe home.

What We’ll Cover:

The "Hardware" Audit: A deep dive into the Houstory Visitor Checklist. We’ll show you how to spot "Landlord Specials," identify hidden pest mitigation (the "Steel Wool" check), and test the vitals of a unit (water, heat, and signal strength).

The Digital Trace: A live demo of the Houstory app. We will take real NYC addresses from the audience and pull their "Hidden History"—including open violations, past complaints, and owner transparency.

HR to Housing: Why vetting your next landlord should look exactly like vetting your next employer.

Goal: To equip the next wave of NYC residents with the tools to demand transparency and move with confidence.
