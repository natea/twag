## Description

AI is not just a technological shift - it’s a restructuring of the labor market.

White-collar roles are compressing. Entry-level pathways are breaking. Entire categories of work are being reorganized faster than new ones are being created.

At the same time, demand is rising for real, task-based capability - often in areas the market has historically overlooked or misunderstood.
We’re entering a moment where:
> Output is becoming abundant
> But signal on human capability is increasingly scarce

The structure of work is changing.
Capital hasn’t fully caught up.

This salon brings together a curated group of founders, investors, and operators to explore how labor markets are evolving - and where capital is likely to flow next.

We’ll discuss:
> Where displaced talent goes - and where new demand is actually emerging
> What replaces traditional entry pathways - and who controls access to opportunity
> Which sectors expand vs. contract - and why the market may be mispricing both
> Where talent is being undervalued - and how that creates asymmetric opportunity
> What new infrastructure is needed - and which companies could define it

This is not a surface-level “future of work” panel.
It’s a focused, high-signal conversation on labor, capital, and emerging opportunity - designed for those building and investing in what comes next.

Join Light Dao, and conscious investors and entrepreneurs for an evening filled with connection and deep conversations with Jenny Fielding, Co-Founder & Managing Partner of Everywhere Ventures, and Shrinidhi Rao, Director of Workforce Innovation at The Burning Glass Institute.

Guest Speaker: Jenny Fielding
Jenny Fielding is one of the most active global pre-seed investors, having been the first money into 300+ startups across a portfolio worth over $10B. As Co-Founder & Managing Partner of Everywhere Ventures, she leads a thriving community of 500+ founders and operators investing across money, health, and work. Formerly Managing Director at Techstars, Jenny is a 2x founder, lawyer by training, and an Adjunct Professor at Columbia University and Cornell Tech. Her forthcoming book, Venture Everywhere, distills her global perspective on building purpose-driven companies. More info: https://www.jennyfielding.com/venture-everywhere

Guest Speaker: Shrinidhi Rao
Shrinidhi Rao is Director of Workforce Innovation at The Burning Glass Institute - a leading research organization focused on the future of work, skills-based hiring, and labor market transformation. His work sits at the intersection of workforce strategy, labor market intelligence, and economic mobility, helping organizations understand how AI, shifting workforce demands, and evolving hiring systems are reshaping opportunity and talent allocation across industries.
