## Description

Join us for a focused morning of real case studies, investor perspective, and hands-on building — limited to 30 founders and executives. Breakfast included. 

The companies winning in the AI era have changed who builds. Enabling non-technical teams to build the frontend is how the fastest companies are expanding velocity - and it's more practical than it sounds.    
 
What we'll cover?
🔹 How mature companies are restructuring who builds what to accelerate time-to-market
🔹 Intro to frontend vibe-coding and best practices
🔹 In-product building in practice: a hands-on workshop, going from PRD to production-ready component
😀 The Lineup: 
→ Jacob Kitchen (Associate, Radian Capital) — how AI is changing the scaling playbook

→ Keren Fanan (Co-Founder & CEO, Myop — ex CRO at Gett) — the org change behind 5x faster time-to-market: real case studies from teams where product, design, and marketing all ship

→ Hadar Geva (Co-Founder & CTO, Myop — ex Wix) — hands-on session: practice it live, don't just hear about it


Who is this for: 

🔹 Founders, who want to move faster and improve time-to-market
🔹 CPOs, CMOs, and VPs of Product and Marketing, at companies with mature products 

🧑‍💻 We’ll be working hands-on, so make sure to bring your laptop and charger.
