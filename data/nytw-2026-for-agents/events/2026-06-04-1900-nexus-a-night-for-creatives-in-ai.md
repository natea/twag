## Description

FLORA is bringing NEXUS back to Brooklyn for one night during NY Tech Week. An evening for the creatives who are shaping what it means to create in the generative era.

NEXUS is where creatives, technology, and ideas converge. Experience how forward-thinking practitioners are integrating AI into their workflows, join a conversation on the future of creative work, and move through an evening of art x tech installations and live DJ sets. Food and cocktails will be served.

Hosted by FLORA at the Domino Sugar Factory in Williamsburg. Capacity is limited.
