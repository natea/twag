​About Alpha Square Group:

​Alpha Square Group is a ~$2B AUM multi family office based in NYC. ASG has an extensive network which has been a differentiated value-add for our founders. Notable investments include OpenAI, Anthropic, Xai, SpaceX, World Labs, Mercor, Crusoe, Scale, Deel, Figure Technologies, Netskope, Digital Ocean.

​About AGI House:

​At AGI House, our mission is to support humanity's journey toward AGI by fostering collaboration among the brightest minds in the AI community. Through a diverse range of events, including hackathons, dinners, salons, and mini-conferences, we create opportunities for insightful dialogues and meaningful partnerships. We honor the greatest AI founders and researchers of our time!

​Our events have featured distinguished guests like Sergey Brin, Andrej Karpathy, Grimes, Eric Schmidt, Sebastian Thrun, founders from OpenAI, Anthropic, Huggingface, and other top AI startups, alongside renowned investors, researchers and engineers from various institutions.

​About Backcountry Ventures:

​Backcountry Ventures is an early stage venture firm led by Caitlin Dullanty, Ben Daniels, and Thomas Whiteaker, investing in enterprise AI, cybersecurity and quantum computing. The team spun out of IBM Ventures, where they built and ran the investment program for seven years, including investments in Hidden Layer, Hugging Face, Gem Security, Quantinuum and several others. They invest at seed and Series A, primarily co-leading and participating alongside tier one funds.
