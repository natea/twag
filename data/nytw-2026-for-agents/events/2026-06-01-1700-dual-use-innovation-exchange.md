## Description

Campus Deep Tech, Morrison Foerster, and the ​Defense Entrepreneurs Forum (DEF) are bringing together a curated group of startups, investors, industry leaders, and academics to explore the next wave of dual-use technologies with meaningful applications for defense and other industries.

Sign up here or on our main Luma page: https://luma.com/qd5qjuyy

​Federal funding for national security continues to expand across agencies, creating critical opportunities for startups. Many areas of climate tech and deep tech, including energy, advanced materials, robotics, geosciences, and others map directly to defense applications, making dual-use an important path to attract investment and accelerate commercialization for technologies with the potential to ultimately deliver value across other industries & for people.

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
