## Description

🏃‍♂️ Central Park, 5 km run before the city wakes up — for those who take care of both mind and body as part of how they operate.

An intentionally small, selected group of founders and investors running together during NYC Tech Week. Hosted by Swiss-based founders. The rule: if you can explain and defend your idea while running, you can do it anywhere. With a pitch deck, it's easy, holding a clear thought and pitching at kilometre five is the real test.

Who this is for:
- Founders in AI, AI robotics, defence tech, deep tech, and infrastructure.
- Investors looking at any of the above.

👩‍💻Founders are reviewed and selected in advance by profile - the list of attendees stays small and relevant on purpose.

Format: 5 km run in Central Park. Exact meeting point and route shared with confirmed participants only.

✍️ About the organiser: LatentWorlds AI is building the data infrastructure layer for physical AI focused on robotics and real-world systems. At its core, the company develops a backend that captures, organises, and processes complex multi-sensor data from robot fleets, turning raw operational data into structured, usable datasets for training and debugging AI systems. The bigger vision: give robotics and physical AI teams the same level of data infrastructure maturity that web and software teams already have, so they can focus on building intelligence, not pipelines.

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
