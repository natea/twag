## Description

A Female Tech Founder’s Story 

Join us for an inspiring evening during NYC Tech Week as we explore a question many of us are quietly asking:
•What if AI could help us live more intentionally — not just work more efficiently?
This event is part founder story, part product demo, and part conversation about the future of human-centered AI.

You’ll hear the journey behind Banjo, an AI-powered platform designed to help people align their time, energy, and daily choices with the life they actually want to live. While much of the AI world is focused on productivity and automation, Banjo was built around a different idea:
Can technology help people feel more fulfilled, connected, and intentional in everyday life?

The evening will include:
•The personal story behind building Banjo
•Lessons learned from building an AI startup 
• A live product demo of Banjo
• Reflections on AI, longevity, and intentional living
• Networking with founders, creatives, technologists, and curious minds across NYC Tech Week
