Software for Hardware: The Tools Powering Robotics & Embodied AI

As robotics and embodied AI move from research into the real world, a new generation of software is emerging to power how machines are built, deployed, and scaled.

Join New York Tech Alliance and New York Robotics for an evening exploring the digital infrastructure behind physical systems — from simulation and autonomy stacks to developer tools, data pipelines, and deployment platforms.

With an amazing lineup of demo's and talks from:

Xavier Chi, co-Founder @ mBodi
Harry Mellsop, co-Founder @ Antioch
Ayna Arora, CEO @ MorphoAI
Ryan Eppley, CEO @ Root Access
Yang Zhou, NYU PhD Candidate
Tyler Raciti, Founder @ Volumes
Brandon Shrewsbury, Viam
Kit Gilbert, Head of Strategic Partnerships @ Niantic Spatial
Pim de Witte, CEO @ General Intuition

This event brings together founders, engineers, researchers, and industry leaders building at the intersection of software and hardware, with a focus on real-world applications across robotics and embodied AI.

Expect:
- Talks from founders and technical leaders shaping the robotics software stack
- Live demos of tools and systems powering real-world robotics deployments
- Conversations with teams building and scaling embodied AI companies
- Networking with the NYC robotics, AI, and broader tech ecosystem

Whether you’re building in robotics, exploring the space, or looking to connect with the teams shaping the future of physical AI, this is a chance to get an inside look at the tools driving the next wave of innovation.
