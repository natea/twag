Event Details: 

Dive into the future of creative coding at Vibe Code Live, a collaborative micro-hack for NY Tech Week.

The evening opens with several rapid demos to highlight new workflows and integrations, followed by open co-building time for you to experiment, collaborate, or just watch and learn, and ending with time to share what you built! Lovable is providing 100 credits to each attendee to vibe code live while at the event. 

We'll save time for casual networking alongside local founders, designers, and developers.

Demos include:
- moneymouth.club
- everhome
- plus more!

This event is co-hosted by WiMDLS (Women in Machine Learning and Data Science) x NYAI (New York Artificial Intelligence) x BrainStation.

Since 2016, NYAI has hosted high-caliber events, from technical talks to curated gatherings - designed to spark meaningful conversations, share cutting-edge ideas, and connect the people shaping the future of AI. Learn more at nyai.co or say hello@nyai.co
