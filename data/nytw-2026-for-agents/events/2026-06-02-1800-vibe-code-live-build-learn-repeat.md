## Description

All attendees will get FREE credits from Lovable for use during the event.

Dive into the future of creative coding at Vibe Code Live, a collaborative micro-hack for NY Tech Week.

We’ll explore the evolving world of vibe coding, intuitive, fast, and expressive building across today’s emerging tools and no-code/low-code platforms.

The evening opens with several rapid demos (see below) to highlight new workflows and integrations, followed by open co-building time for you to experiment, collaborate, or just watch and learn. Lovable is providing 100 credits to each attendee to vibe code live while at the event. 

Wrap up with short community showcases and casual networking alongside local founders, designers, and developers.

Demos include:
- moneymouth.club
- myflytplan.com
- plus more!

This event is co-hosted by WiMDLS (Women in Machine Learning and Data Science) x NYAI (New York Artificial Intelligence) x BrainStation. Sponsored by Lovable.

The mission of Women in Machine Learning & Data Science (WiMLDS) is to support and promote women and gender minorities who are practicing, studying, or are interested in the fields of machine learning and data science.

NYAI is a global community of AI builders, founders, and investors exploring the frontier of artificial intelligence. Since 2016, NYAI has hosted high-caliber events, from technical talks to curated gatherings - designed to spark meaningful conversations, share cutting-edge ideas, and connect the people shaping the future of AI. Learn more at nyai.co or say hello@nyai.co 

BrainStation is the global leader in digital skills learning and workforce transformation. They empower businesses and professionals to succeed in the digital age by equipping them with the necessary skills to create value in fields such as AI, data, design, marketing and product.

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com
