## Description

Global talent plays a critical role in driving innovation, entrepreneurship, and economic growth in the United States. Yet for many international students, professionals, and founders, building a long-term career in the U.S. can be complex and difficult to navigate.

This panel brings together industry leaders, career strategists, immigration experts, and startup founders to discuss how international talent can successfully transition from education to employment, leadership, and entrepreneurship in the U.S. We’ll explore career pathways, workforce mobility, and the evolving opportunities available to global professionals across industries.
