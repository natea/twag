## Description

NY Tech Week is nonstop. Between panels, pitches, and meetups, it can be hard to find a moment to breathe—let alone actually recharge.

Our lounge is your low-key escape in the middle of it all. Join Primary Venture Partners, Supermomos, and Catalyze, Gunderson Dettmer’s client platform for founders and investors, for a come-and-go space designed to give you a quick break between a packed schedule of events.

Drop in anytime between 11 am and 2 pm to:

- Relax: Unwind with a short chair massage between events
- Recharge: Refuel with coffee, drinks, and light snacks
- Reconnect: Work (and network!) in a Wi‑Fi‑enabled space

There’s no stage, no panels, no formal programming—just a comfortable place to land, catch up on email, trade stories with other attendees, and reset between your Tech Week events.

---

Gunderson Dettmer is the preeminent international law firm exclusively focused on the innovation economy, partnering with visionary founders, disruptive startups, and pioneering investors to build companies that reshape how we work, live, and connect. Building on this deep commitment to the startup ecosystem, Catalyze is Gunderson Dettmer’s client platform that enhances the startup journey through thoughtfully curated events, deeper connectivity with our legal team and your peers, and expanded access to resources.

Supermomos is NYC's premier platform for connecting with professional communities in technology, business, and finance. We're dedicated to bringing people together in real life to foster meaningful connections and knowledge exchange, all while facilitating the participation of female and underrepresented minority groups.

Primary is the largest dedicated seed firm in the country, with $1.6B in AUM, a 60-person team, and sector-specialized partners across Compute, Healthcare, GTM, Enterprise AI, Fintech, Cybersecurity, Vertical AI, Industrials, and Consumer. The Impact team outnumbers investors 2:1 and runs at full operating intensity year-round: 2,500 hours of embedded fractional leadership, 3,000 candidate interviews, a live pool of 5,000+ job seekers, and 250 events for the NYC tech community.

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
