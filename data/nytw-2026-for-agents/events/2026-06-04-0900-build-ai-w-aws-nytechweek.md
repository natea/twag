## Description

New York Startups! ​
For this year's NYC Tech Week, AWS invites you to join us for an exclusive, hands-on workshop where AWS Solutions Architects work directly with you to bring your AI use case to life.

​​In this intensive, full-day session, we'll take your specific AI challenge and transform it into a working proof-of-concept (POC) solution in your AWS environment. Our team of AI Subject Matter Experts will guide you through architecture decisions, implementation best practices, and help you build a functional prototype – all within your own AWS account.

​
​​Agenda:
​​8:30 AM - Check in & breakfast
​​9:00 AM - Opening & Introductions
​​9:15 AM - Overview of AWS AI Toolings & Frameworks
​​9:30 AM - Hands on workshop (w. working lunch)
​​4:00 PM - Closing + (optional) Demo

​​What to Bring:
​​An AI use case
​​One or two engineers with their laptops
​​Your AWS Account

​​Key Benefits:
​​Walk away with a working MVP implemented in your AWS account - not just slides or theory, but actual code and infrastructure running your use case

​​Get direct, hands-on guidance from AWS Solutions Architects and selected AWS partner who will help scope, architect, and build your solution using AWS AI/ML services

​​Seamless transition from POC to production through our AWS Partners, who will provide continued support and expertise to scale your solution
