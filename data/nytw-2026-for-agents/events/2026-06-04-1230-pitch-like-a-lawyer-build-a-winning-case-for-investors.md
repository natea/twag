## Description

Have you ever thought about how a pitch is like a mini-trial? Learn how to build a case for your startup the way that lawyers build a case for trial.

In this workshop, led by a former US Department of Justice attorney, you'll learn the frameworks lawyers use to build clear and compelling arguments, and walk away ready to present yours. 

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
