## Description

What if the next breakthrough sustainability or climate solution started as a simple prototype?

Hosted by Green Project Management (GPM) and MiGSO-PCUBED (MP), this session brings together perspectives across implementation, transformation and sustainable delivery to explore how emerging technologies can move from ideation to real-world impact. 

Participants will explore how AI can support sustainability-focused challenges: from climate intelligence and circular economy systems to operational workflows and supply-chain transparency… through hands-on experimentation, prompting, research exploration, and no/low-code tools.

The session is followed by a panel discussion on what it takes to move ideas beyond the prototype stage.

Participants are encouraged to come with a sustainability-related challenge, question, frustration, or idea they’d like to explore during the session, either independently or collaboratively with others in the room. Please bring your laptop.

The goal is not to build a polished product, but to explore ideas, test concepts, and turn sustainability challenges into rough prototypes.

Spots are limited ✨
