## Description

The Living Space is an off-the-record evening for Series A+ founders, investors, and operators. Built around an honest conversation with a special guest sharing sharing what actually happens behind the scenes of building and scaling.

The format is simple: a small-group conversation - no decks, no pitches - followed by curated networking. The kind of conversation you'd have in your own living room, not on a stage: the hard calls, the inflection points, and the things you don't say on a podcast.

Hosted by Lazo, Ramp & AlleyCorp.

About Lazo 
Lazo provides incorporation, tax, bookkeeping, fractional CFO and fundraising solutions for startups, powered by AI Agents and Human Experts.  Through our Lazo One platform, we streamline essential operations, enabling founders to focus on growth while we manage the complexities. We help startups scale with confidence.

About ​AlleyCorp
AlleyCorp is a New York-based venture capital firm and venture studio that incubates and invests in transformative technology companies. AlleyCorp focuses on backing founders building in sectors such as AI, digital infrastructure, healthcare, enterprise software, and marketplaces. Beyond capital, the firm partners closely with early-stage teams to help launch, scale, and build enduring businesses with global ambition.
