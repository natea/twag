## Description

Join Women of Customer Success and Notion for one of NYC Tech Week's must-attend evenings for women working in post-sales.

The CS role is evolving fast. New titles like "Customer Success Engineer" and "Forward Deployed Engineer" signal a shift toward more technically fluent, strategic professionals who operate closer to the product and the business. AI is accelerating that shift - and the teams pulling ahead aren't just using it, they've built a system around it.

This event tackles both sides of what that looks like in practice.

First, an "Under the Hood" session: 3 CS leaders will demo how they are using AI to build their own Chiefs of Staff - the workflows and tools that handle the cognitive overhead of the role. 

Meet your "show & tell" presenters: 

- Jamie Wernet, Director Enterprise Success & Services, Greenhouse
- Melina Giakas, GTM Enablement, Clay 
- Denise King, CSM, and Nida Noorani, AI Solutions, Notion 

Then we'll move to a fireside chat with CS leaders from across the industry on the skills that matter most once AI handles the routine: consulting instincts, technical fluency, and the strategic thinking that separates CSMs who manage accounts from those who drive outcomes. 

Meet your panel: 

- Christina Parra, Head of CS, Notion
- Sam Medina, Snr Director Customer Success & Growth, Bitly 
- Sarah Benussi, CS Leader, Anthropic
- Cassie Vaughn, Global Head of Growth Strategy, Clay.

75 seats. Notion's NYC office. Drinks, food, and a room full of CS professionals serious about what comes next.

Please note this event is for women only.

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek - a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
