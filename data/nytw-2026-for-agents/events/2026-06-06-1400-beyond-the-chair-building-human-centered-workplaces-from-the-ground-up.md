## Description

The workplace of the future isn't built in the cloud — it starts where people do: the chair they sit in.
Hosted by Libernovo, pioneers in intelligent ergonomic solutions, this half-day experience brings together workplace leaders, technologists, and people-experience professionals to explore a simple but powerful idea: that smart, human-centered furniture is the foundation of a high-performing workplace.
From dynamic support to data-driven wellbeing, join us to discover how the intersection of technology, health, and comfort is reshaping the future of work — one workspace at a time.
