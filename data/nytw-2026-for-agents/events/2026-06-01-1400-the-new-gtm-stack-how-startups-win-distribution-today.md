## Description

During NY Tech Week, EchoHer is bringing together founders, operators, and investors for a focused conversation on how startups are winning distribution today.

We’ll break down the new GTM stack — from product-led growth and AI tools to community, partnerships, and modern channels — through the lens of what’s actually driving traction right now.

The discussion is grounded in real experience: how teams are getting their first customers, what’s scaling, what’s not worth the time, and where early-stage founders should focus.

Expect practical insights, honest perspectives, and a room of people actively building and investing in this space.

EchoHer is a global community of 9,000+ founders, investors, and operators supporting women and non-binary builders through events, connections, and opportunities.

Witti Marketing is a NYC-based growth marketing agency specializing in go-to-market strategy, performance marketing, and scalable growth systems for startups and high-growth brands.
Rather than operating as a traditional outsourced marketing team,  the team has supported both startups and established brands across industries including AI, robotics, consumer tech, ecommerce, and lifestyle.
Core capabilities include:
GTM strategy and positioning
Performance marketing
Content systems and creative testing
GEO / AI-native search visibility
Conversion optimization
Multi-channel growth strategy
