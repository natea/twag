## Description

Trust Issues is a live compatibility experiment inspired by the Black Mirror episode "Hang the DJ." 

Here's how it works:
→ You fill out a short questionnaire
→ An algorithm pairs you with someone in the room
→ Then.. like a metal detector for human connection, your devices pull you toward each other. It beeps. It gets faster the closer you get.

When you find your match, you'll move through a few rounds of questions and challenges (think Traitors meets Love Island) designed to reveal how well you actually click. 

Each round receives a compatibility score, and at the end, the system reveals who in the room you're most compatible with, plus a few people you should really go find before you leave.

No name tags or "what do you do" questions. Just a slightly unhinged, very fun way to meet the right people.

We make no promises about what kind of compatible. 👀

🔬 Spots are limited. Come be a test subject. The algorithm needs you.
