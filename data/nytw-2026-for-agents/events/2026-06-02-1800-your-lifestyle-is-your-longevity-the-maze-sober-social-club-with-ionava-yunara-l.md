## Description

You already know the truth. Longevity isn't built in a clinic. It's built in your kitchen, your calendar, your friendships, and yes, your bank account. 

For one evening during NY Tech Week, ionava, Yunara Life, and The Maze are teaming up to ask what it looks like when the way you fund your health, the way you navigate care, and the way you live every day finally line up. 

Three perspectives. One evening. One model.

- How you fund your health (ionava - https://ionava.com)
- How you navigate care and self-care (Yunara - https://yunara.life)
- How you live day-to-day (The Maze - https://www.themazenyc.com/)

The night includes a fireside chat, an open house at The Maze, and showcases from partners working across wellness and health innovation. Food will be provided.

Come for the conversation. Stay for the community.

About the Hosts:

Ionava is a financial platform designed around health and wellness, enabling individuals to direct resources toward everyday well-being while reserving liquidity for major life events. (Not health insurance.)

Yunara is rebuilding the first layer of the healthcare by transforming prevention into a lifestyle, while helping people cut through the noise of the longevity industry. 

The Maze is New York City's first alcohol-free members club, built around something rarer than access - it's built around belonging. Every element of the community is designed to foster genuine, meaningful connection among members who show up with intention.
