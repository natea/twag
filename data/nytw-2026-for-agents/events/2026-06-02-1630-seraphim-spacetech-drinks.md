## Description

Join Seraphim Space during NY Tech Week for an evening bringing together founders, investors, and friends of the SpaceTech ecosystem.

The event comes at a major moment for the sector, with SpaceTech recording its strongest quarter on record in Q1 2026 and significant momentum across the Seraphim ecosystem, including the NYSE listing of HawkEye 360, Xona Space Systems’s $170m Series C, and Seraphim Space Investment Trust completing a £137m equity raise.

The evening will include a short market overview, networking with the wider Seraphim community, and an update from LambdaVision, Seraphim accelerator alumni and portfolio company developing the first protein-based artificial retina designed to restore meaningful vision for blind patients.

Members of the current Seraphim x Space Florida cohort will also be joining us alongside alumni and partners from across the network.

Space is limited and registrations will be reviewed and approved.


This event is a part of NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
