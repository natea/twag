## Description

Relationality and DefinedTalent are coming together for a special Lunch & Learn experience designed to hold space for founders, cofounders, and scaling organizations ready to grow with intention.

Through DefinedTalent’s ReDefine T.H.I.S. framework, Talent. Human Resources. Innovation. Strategy. alongside Relationality’s groundbreaking culture and compatibility platform, we’ll explore what it truly means to scale people, culture, leadership, and alignment in a sustainable way.
Relationality helps scaling companies diagnose their CULTURE TYPE, identifying one of 9 unique organizational culture profiles that shape communication, leadership, decision-making, hiring success, and long-term team dynamics.

As you begin vetting new employees, the Relationality Test provides measurable compatibility insights between candidates and your company’s culture  helping organizations move beyond resumes and instinct-based hiring into intentional, aligned team building.

Join us for lunch, conversation, connection, and an interactive live demo showcasing how recruiting with measurable compatibility may be the game-changing strategy your organization has been missing.

This is more than a hiring conversation.
It’s a conversation about scaling humans well.
