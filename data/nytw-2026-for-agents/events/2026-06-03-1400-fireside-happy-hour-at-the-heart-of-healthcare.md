## Description

Join one of North America’s fastest-growing healthtech communities for a networking lunch with founders, investors, and operators across healthtech and life sciences.

We will be joined by the team at AccurKardia for a fireside chat, moderated by Ambreen Mohamed of Heartbeat Health. 

AccurKardia leverages AI to interpret the visible and hidden signals within ECGs, transforming them into a more powerful diagnostic and broad biomarker that improves outcomes and saves lives. 

Heartbeat Health is on a mission to deliver high-quality heart care through early detection, virtual access, and proactive management, improving lives and lowering costs.
