## Description

Early-stage founders don’t need more theory; they need access to operators who have actually scaled from zero to growth and can help them solve real problems in the room.

As part of NY Tech Week, Karcic Partners, in collaboration with The Front Yard at Pace University, HiBob, and Mark Wellness, is hosting an interactive, small-group working session for Seed and Series A founders and executives. Join experienced GTM, product, engineering, finance, legal, and people leaders for hands-on “clinics” focused on your company’s current challenges.

This is not a panel. It’s a working session designed for teams actively navigating scaling, product execution, fundraising, hiring, go-to-market, AI adoption, and operational structure.

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com
