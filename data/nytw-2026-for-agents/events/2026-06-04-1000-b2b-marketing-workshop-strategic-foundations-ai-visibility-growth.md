## Description

Growth oriented B2B tech start-ups and scale-ups require a shift from intuitive guesswork to a go-to-market approach built on strategic marketing rigor and AI-driven visibility. In this joint workshop, Boon Auditing and BeonAI provide a comprehensive framework to accelerate both brand awareness and lead generation.


The session begins by grounding attendees in the core strategic pillars of Boon’s B2B marketing framework, including branding, targeting, messaging, positioning, understanding your buyers and more. BeonAI then introduces the emerging discipline of AI visibility, showing how brands are discovered, evaluated, and recommended across generative platforms (i.e. ChatGPT, Gemini, etc.) rather than traditional search and showcase their agentic solution. 


Participants will walk away with a blueprint to transform their marketing into a predictable, scalable, and investor-ready revenue engine.
