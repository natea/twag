## Description

Shabbat State of Mind: A Wednesday Gathering for Israeli Founders, Executives & Investors

As Tech Week NYC kicks into high gear, join us for an evening that brings the best of Shabbat — community, reflection, and rest — to a Wednesday night in Manhattan. Step away from the noise of the conference circuit, settle in with good wine and great food, and make space for the kind of honest conversation that built the Startup Nation.
Fox Rothschild invites Israeli founders, company leaders, and investors building, scaling and backing the next generation of technology companies to come together for a night that trades the pace of the work week for something deeper — real community and genuine connection. Whether you're forming your first U.S. entity, scaling a team through hypergrowth, navigating a growth round, or deploying capital, this is a room built for you. Expect Israeli wines, Mediterranean-inspired bites, and a room full of people who understand what it takes to build something from scratch — no panels, no pitches — just the kind of unhurried, meaningful conversation that Shabbat was made for, shared with fellow founders, executives and investors shaping the Israeli tech ecosystem in New York.


Date: Wednesday, June 3rd

Time: 5:30 PM – 7:30 PM

Location: Manhattan. Venue will be shared with confirmed attendees only 

Hosted by: Tamar Gubins and Sarah Biser at Fox Rothschild

Tamar is a business-oriented attorney who advises emerging and technology companies on formation, financing, corporate governance, and M&A throughout the full lifecycle of a technology company. Sarah Biser is co-chair of the firm’s Israel Practice Group, International Arbitration Practice Group, and Construction Law Practice Group. With more than 25 years of experience in construction and infrastructure, Sarah drafted and negotiated Technion University’s joint venture agreement with Cornell University to develop and construct a premier, state-of-the-art applied science graduate school and research facility in New York. Fox Rothschild's dedicated Israel Practice Group — recognized for six consecutive years on the Legally Israel 100 list — helps Israeli companies from startup to market leader navigate expansion into the US market. 

This is an intimate, curated gathering. Space is limited and registration is required. Priority is given to founders, executives and investors active in the Israeli and Israeli-American technology ecosystem.

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
