## Description

Step into The Retro: a cocktail experience surrounded by artifacts from companies that didn't make it. Each piece tells a story about what was built, what mattered, and what comes next.  

This isn't a cautionary tale or a roast. It's a room that brings order to an ending most of the industry avoids talking about. Because what was built still matters—even when it ends. 

Enjoy custom cocktails and appetizers, explore curated displays throughout Pearl Box's first floor, and join the conversation about the full lifecycle of building, including closure.

Raise a glass to what didn't make it—and what comes next.

About SimpleClosure: 
SimpleClosure is the shutdown partner for founders and startups navigating the less-talked-about side of entrepreneurship. When it’s time to properly close a company, SimpleClosure handles the paperwork, legal filings, compliance requirements, and final steps founders are often left to figure out on their own. What is traditionally a confusing, months-long process becomes a clearer path forward, so founders can close cleanly, and get back to building. To learn more, visit SimpleClosure.com.
