Max Her Potential — by AI Valley × MiniMax

Max Her Potential is a curated gathering of women founders, builders, and operators shaping the future of AI and technology.

Hosted by AI Valley in partnership with MiniMax during NYC Tech Week, this evening brings together a high-signal room of students, alumnae founders, and early- to growth-stage operators across the New York tech ecosystem.
