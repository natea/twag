## Description

Tech Week drinks for experts who want in on the AI training data gold rush, primary research market, and the future of expert work.

Frontier labs are quietly paying domain experts $100-300/hr to train their models. Mercor, Surge, and Scale have built billion-dollar businesses on this. At the same time, consulting firms, hedge funds, and PE shops pay experts $300-1,500/hr through networks like GLG, AlphaSights, and Guidepoint for primary research calls. Most experts have no idea how to break into either market, or which platforms are worth their time.

Come for a drink, meet other experts, and trade notes.

Topics of conversation will be around:
- How the AI training data and expert network markets actually work
- Who's paying, what they're paying for, and how to get on their lists
- What separates a $50/hr task from a $300/hr engagement (and a $1,500/hr expert call)
- The platforms worth joining vs. the ones wasting your time
- How to position your expertise to maximize rate and deal flow across both markets

This event is a part of #NYCTechWeek, a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
