## Description

Innovation builds companies. RE: WEALTH explores how more of us can participate in ownership, investing, and the future of wealth.

​Join founders, investors, artists, and corporate leaders for a cross-sector conversation on reclaiming wealth and expanding participation in the tech ecosystem.

​Overview
​While much of the tech ecosystem focuses on building companies, RE: WEALTH explores how more of us can meaningfully participate in building and moving wealth.

​At its core, RE: WEALTH invites us to reconsider our relationship to wealth and resources — to reclaim, rethink, and remember that participation in shaping wealth is not reserved for a select few.

​This is a space for all of us — including those who may not traditionally see themselves as part of the “tech economy.”

​Our perspectives, resources, and participation are essential to shaping our future of wealth.
