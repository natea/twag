## Description

GNOMI Gen Z Growth Hackathon — NYC HQ | June 5–6
During a16z Tech Week

We are opening GNOMI’s New York HQ to a small group of exceptional growth minds, product thinkers, creators, creative strategists, and culture-native builders.

GNOMI has raised $30M+ to build the world’s first AI News Agent — turning global news, markets, filings, earnings calls, and live events into real-time intelligence across 189 countries and 17+ languages.

On June 5–6, participants will compete to make GNOMI impossible to ignore for the next generation.

The defining question:
How do we make GNOMI the intelligence layer for a generation that wants to understand the world before the world tells them what to think?

Participants will compete across two tracks:

Best Content Creation
Create the most compelling, original, and culturally relevant content concept for GNOMI. This may include short-form video, social campaigns, creator-led formats, memes, storytelling concepts, launch moments, or content franchises that make GNOMI instantly understandable, shareable, and impossible to ignore.

Best Growth Strategy
Build the smartest and most scalable strategy to drive GNOMI adoption among Gen Z and millennials. This may include viral loops, ambassador programs, campus growth, referral mechanics, creator partnerships, community strategy, product-led growth, or entirely new distribution ideas.

This is not a traditional marketing brainstorm.

We are looking for executable ideas that can move culture, drive adoption, and turn GNOMI into a product people talk about, share, and use daily.

Prizes
Each track will have its own winners:
🥇 1st Place: $4,000 + interview opportunity at GNOMI
🥈 2nd Place: $2,000 + interview opportunity at GNOMI
🥉 3rd Place: surprise awards and recognition

We are looking for rare builders and creators who understand the intersection of AI, finance, media, culture, behavior, product, and growth — people who can turn insight into strategy, strategy into execution, and execution into measurable adoption.

Content. Growth. Product. Creative. Social. Community. Campus. Finance. Data. AI. Culture.

The next frontier is not more information.

It is building the intelligence layer the next generation trusts, uses, and shares.


This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
