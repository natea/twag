## Description

Part of the Global Connect Show event series, GCSx NYC 2026 is a curated, invitation-only networking event during NYC Tech Week, designed for one purpose: putting the right people in the same room and letting real conversations happen. 

No panels for the sake of panels. No badge-scanning. Just 80+ founders, operators, investors, media professionals, and ecosystem builders, all there to connect, exchange, and build.

The event is produced by the team behind Global Connect Show (GCS), a series of global events that connects hundreds of innovative brands to business stakeholders in North America, Europe, MENA, and Southeast Asia. 

GCSx NYC 2026 brings that same results-first philosophy to New York's most energized week of the year. For brands, it's a direct line to the US market stakeholders that take months to reach through conventional means. For media, it's hands-on access to some of the most exciting global tech launches of 2026, with the founders and engineers behind them in the room. For investors and partners, it's a concentrated look at where global innovation is heading next.

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
