## Description

We are entering a new era of AI. Today, artificial intelligence is the central driving force in every facet of the economy, and the time to build is right now.

Fuel up for the future with a delicious pancake breakfast before kicking off this exclusive two-part Techweek session with Google DeepMind. Get the latest insights straight from the team building the products and discover the high-level trends that will shape the next generation of startups.

From there, we transition from vision to execution with our "Stacks on Stacks" series. We’re not just talking about the breakfast buffet! You will have the opportunity to establish your startup's technical foundation using Google's latest AI innovations. In this segment, you will be introduced to the newest tools and infrastructure solutions from Google Cloud. 

Discover how to strategically layer your tech stack to quickly turn your AI breakthroughs into tangible outcomes and real-world impact using state-of-the-art Gemini tools and models.

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.

We expect these sessions to fill up quickly and be in high demand.  Space is limited and sessions are first-come, first-served, even as an approved guest.  Please be sure to arrive by the check in time listed on your session agenda.  Spots will be given away to waitlist beginning 10 minutes before each session.

Guests will be permitted in the Google for Startups Hub only during the time slot they are approved for.  

We invite all guests to return for the Google For Developers Happy Hour at 4p.  Only those with valid badges will be permitted, space allowing.
