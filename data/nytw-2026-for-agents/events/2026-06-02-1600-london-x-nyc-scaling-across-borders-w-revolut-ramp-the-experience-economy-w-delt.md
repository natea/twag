## Description

Team London invites you to an exclusive gathering during New York Tech Week bringing together the founders, investors, and city leaders shaping the future of global growth.

As AI, Fintech, Climate Tech, and Frontier Technologies, reshape the global economy, London and New York remain two of the world’s most influential launchpads for ambitious companies, breakthrough research, and international growth. This event will explore how the world’s leading cities attract talent, capital, and the technologies that will define the next decade.  

Hosted at the London Stock Exchange and sponsored by Virgin Atlantic and Delta, the evening will convene a curated audience of top founders, VCs, institutional investors, real estate innovators, and academic leaders for high-value conversations and meaningful connections across the Atlantic.  There will be a civic delegation led by Opportunity London who are keen to meet with innovators at this event.  

What To Expect

•	London & Partners’ CEO, Laura Citron, sharing London’s vision for growing its tech economy in a period of rapid change.
•	Leadership at Ramp and Revolut will share their insights on launching and scaling in new international markets.
•	Virgin Atlantic & Delta sharing how they are utilizing AI to enhance the transatlantic travel experience.  Plus a chance to win a flight to London!
•	Tangible networking opportunities with corporate buyers, VCs, startup founders, universities, real estate giants, and political leaders from London and NYC.  

Expect candid conversations, actionable insights, and the opportunity to meet the people driving the next generation of transatlantic growth and innovation.

Who Should Attend

•	Founders and operators planning international expansion or fundraising. 
•	Venture capital and institutional investors seeking early access to high-growth ecosystems and emerging technologies. 
•	Real estate and infrastructure leaders building the future of innovation campuses, labs, and mixed-use tech districts. 
•	University and research leaders focused on commercialisation, talent, and industry partnerships. 
•	Executives and ecosystem builders shaping the future of global cities. 

Why Attend

NY Tech Week is crowded with events. This one is intentionally different.

Alongside the impactful panels, there will be curated networking opportunities enabling you to connect with senior decision-makers across venture, government, academia, and industry, the people influencing where capital flows, where companies scale, and where the future economy gets built.

If you care about the future of AI, innovation ecosystems, global expansion, and the next generation of world-leading cities, this is the room to be in.

This event is part of New York Tech Week, a week of events bringing together founders, investors, and leaders from across the global technology ecosystem.
