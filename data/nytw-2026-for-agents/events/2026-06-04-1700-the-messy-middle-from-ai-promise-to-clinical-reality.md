## Description

We are convening a highly curated group of healthcare leaders, pharma executives, clinical innovation leaders, provider organizations, investors, operators, founders, and ecosystem stakeholders for an honest and practical conversation on the realities of scaling AI from pilot projects to organization-wide implementation across healthcare.

The discussion will focus far less on hype and far more on the operational realities behind adoption — including validation, workflow integration, governance, organizational buy-in, change management, patient impact, and what it actually takes to move beyond experimentation toward meaningful implementation and scale.

The event is designed to surface real-world lessons from organizations actively deploying AI across healthcare environments — including providers, pharma, clinical development, and broader care delivery systems. Together, we will explore what healthcare organizations actually need from AI today, where implementation friction exists, and what separates successful enterprise adoption from pilots that never scale.

The conversation will address practical challenges such as integration into clinical and operational workflows, cross-functional alignment, evidence generation, responsible governance, and scaling AI initiatives across complex healthcare organizations.

We will also feature a select group of companies working directly with healthcare organizations, sharing implementation lessons and insights from real-world deployments.
