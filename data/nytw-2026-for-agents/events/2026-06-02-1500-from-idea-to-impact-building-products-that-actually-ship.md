## Description

You don’t need more ideas.
You need a way to actually build and ship them.

Most builders get stuck not because they lack creativity, but because they overthink, overbuild, or don’t know what to build first. So nothing ships.

This is a hands-on working session led by AI product leader Nia Joseph.

It’s designed for founders, product operators, and builders who are past the “I have an idea” stage and stuck on:

How do I turn this into something real?

You’ll work through a simple, practical build loop used to go from idea → MVP → real user feedback.

💡 What You’ll Do
• Define a real problem worth solving
• Identify your core product assumption
• Scope the smallest possible version of your idea (MVP)
• Apply a clear build → test → iterate loop
• Turn your idea into something you can actually validate

📌 What You’ll Walk Away With
• A clear path from idea to MVP
• A structured way to stop overthinking and start building
• A working framework you can reuse for future products

🛠 Workshop Format
Not a demo. Not a panel.

A small-group working session (30 people max) focused on execution.
You’ll actively work on your own idea during the session.

Bring your laptop.

👥 Who This Is For
• Founders (pre-seed to $1M raised)
• Product builders
• Operators working on 0 → 1 ideas
• Ideal for: aspiring entrepreneurs, early-stage founders, indie builders, product managers, and operators ready to move from idea to MVP
