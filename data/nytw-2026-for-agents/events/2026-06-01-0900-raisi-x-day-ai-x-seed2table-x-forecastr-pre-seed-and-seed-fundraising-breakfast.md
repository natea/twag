About Raisi

Raisi is a fundraising operating system built to help founders run their raise end to end. From identifying the right investors to executing outreach and managing conversations, Raisi replaces fragmented fundraising workflows with one integrated system.

Since 2024, once every 50.1 minutes, a founder has met with an investor interested in their company through Raisi. The platform has facilitated 21,000+ positive connections between founders and investors and is powered by up-to-date signals from millions of live interactions across the private markets.

About Day AI

Day AI is an AI-native relationship management platform designed to help founders, operators, and investors manage conversations, follow-ups, and workflows more intelligently. The company recently raised a $20M Series A led by Sequoia and is building tools that turn meetings and communication into actionable systems for teams and businesses.

About Seed2Table

Seed2Table is a founder and investor community platform focused on creating meaningful relationships across the startup ecosystem through curated experiences, conversations, and events. The organization brings together founders, operators, investors, and ecosystem leaders to create high-quality connections that extend beyond traditional networking.

About Forecastr

Forecastr helps founders build investor-ready financial models and projections. From early-stage planning to growth-stage forecasting, Forecastr gives you the clarity and structure investors expect, so you can walk into conversations with confidence and back up your story with real numbers.
