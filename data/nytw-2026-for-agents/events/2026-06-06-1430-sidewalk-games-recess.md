## Description

You've made it through a week of panels, meetings, and happy hours. Sidewalk Games: Recess is your Saturday afternoon escape — an outdoor city-exploration game in FiDi to close out NY Tech Week.

Teams of founders and operators complete interactive challenges, visit iconic landmarks, and turn everyday city streets into something spectacular. The game runs on our own software, built for making connections.

-- What To Expect --
Your game host will meet you on-site to form teams, explain the rules, and kick-off the competition. Then, you and your team will explore the city, completing immersive challenges while outwitting the competition. At the end, we'll regroup to crown the victors and take some photos.

-- Who It's For --
Founders, operators, and anyone in tech who wants to meet people somewhere other than a rooftop bar.

This is a free event hosted by Urban Origins, makers of Sidewalk Games — outdoor, team-building experiences.

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
