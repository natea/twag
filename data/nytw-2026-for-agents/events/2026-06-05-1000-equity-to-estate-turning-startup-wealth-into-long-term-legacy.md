## Description

In the startup world, years of hard work often translate into concentrated, event-driven equity—but without a plan, value creation doesn’t always become lasting wealth. This session focuses on how founders, early employees, and operators can turn equity into a coordinated long-term strategy—optimizing for liquidity events, minimizing tax drag, and building structures that protect and extend wealth. The goal isn’t just a successful exit, but creating economic freedom and a durable financial legacy that compounds across generations.
