## Description

What happens when every second counts, and the systems we rely on are pushed to their limits?

Join us for Signals in the Storm, an immersive public safety event bringing together government, industry, and technology leaders to explore how innovation is advancing public safety and emergency response. Through a dynamic, story-driven experience inspired by real events during Superstorm Sandy, we’ll trace how moments of crisis have shaped today’s tools for communication, coordination, and decision-making.

The program features a panel discussion, interactive technology demos and exhibits, and dedicated time to connect with peers across the public safety community. Light refreshments will be provided.

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
