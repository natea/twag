## Description

Join us for a special breakfast event with like-minded pre-seed stage founders in NYC, hosted by Jenny, Scott, Anna and Michael from Everywhere Ventures in partnership with Ayo from Microsoft for Startups. The morning will feature light programming followed by a hearty sit-down breakfast and time for meaningful conversation.

​This event is exclusively for founders of product focused companies at the pre-seed stage and space is limited to keep it intimate. 

​​​Everywhere Ventures is a global founder collective and early-stage venture fund investing across vertical AI. Over the last 8 years, we’ve invested into 300 pre-seed companies, making us one of the most active early stage funds around. Our capital comes from hundreds of limited partners who are all founders themselves and help to support our growing portfolio.

https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/startups

Microsoft for Startups is a global startup program designed to help founders build, scale, and succeed by providing access to technology, cloud infrastructure, and go-to-market support. Through technical enablement, Azure credits, and direct connections to Microsoft’s product teams, partners, and customers, the program supports startups from idea to growth. Microsoft for Startups works with thousands of founders worldwide, helping them accelerate product development and bring innovative solutions to market.

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek - a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
