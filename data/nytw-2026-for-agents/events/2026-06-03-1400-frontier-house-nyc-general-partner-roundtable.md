## Description

Join us for the Frontier House NYC - General Partner Roundtable on Wednesday, June 3rd, from 2:00 to 03:30 PM on a beautiful Midtown rooftop with amazing food & drink.

​We a convening a select group of General Partner from local and national venture capital firms for a roundtable discuss on the state of VC and to identify specific actions we can take to support each others success.

​This event will be immediately followed by the Frontier House NYC - Community Mixer.

​Frontier House is the premier digital community and event series for Frontier Innovators - investors, founders, ecosystem builders, and service providers boldly building the future outside of the traditional tech hubs. 

​Our mission is to celebrate Frontier Innovation and democratize venture capital by drawing capital to underfunded markets across the US. 

​A special thanks to our Hosts: Eleven Wall Ventures, Ohio Startup Network, OneSixOne Ventures and Tech Week.

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
