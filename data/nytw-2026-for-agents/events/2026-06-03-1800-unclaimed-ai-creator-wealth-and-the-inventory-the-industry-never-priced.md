## Description

The creator economy made a lot of people rich. Just not always the creators.

Join us during NY Tech Week 2026 for an evening that takes that problem seriously. The Overlooked Economy is a live demonstration and conversation built around a simple premise: the most valuable advertising inventory in digital media has been sitting inside creator content for years, unpriced and unclaimed, and the creators who built it have never had a mechanism to access what it was worth.

FullScale brings its AI-powered product placement technology into the room and runs it live, on real content, in front of the audience. You will watch it surface placement opportunities and attach dollar values to inventory that existing ad infrastructure has never been able to see. Then a curated panel of creators, brand marketers, and investors will have the honest conversation about why that gap existed, who it cost the most, and what changes now that the infrastructure has caught up.

Expect a focused guest mix, a room shaped with intention, and a conversation with something real to say. No pitch competition. No keynote theater. Just a live demonstration and the kind of discussion that tends to keep going after the event ends.
