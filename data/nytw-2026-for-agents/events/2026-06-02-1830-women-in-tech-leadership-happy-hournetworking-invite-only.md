## Description

Join a curated group of women leaders and underrepresented founders across tech for an intimate happy hour during NYC Tech Week.

This is an invite-only gathering for women in leadership, immigrant founders, and underrepresented builders in tech — no pitches, no panels — just a thoughtfully designed space to connect, unwind, and meet your people.

Expect founders, executives, operators, and creators from companies like Miro, Chief, Nvidia, and more — women who are leading teams, building companies, and breaking barriers.

We're keeping this intentionally small with a capped guest list and a private room to ensure meaningful conversations, real connections, and the kind of energy you don't get at larger events.

If you're a woman in leadership, an immigrant founder, or an underrepresented builder looking for your circle — this is your room.

A sponsored open bar will be available for guests during the first half of the event. Following that, the bar will transition to regular service for the remainder of the evening.
