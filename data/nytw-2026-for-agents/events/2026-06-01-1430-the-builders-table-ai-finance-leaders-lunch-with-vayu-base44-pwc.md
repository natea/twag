## Description

Join Vayu, Base44, and PwC for a private, invite-only lunch with CFOs and finance leaders during NY Tech Week — focused on how modern finance teams are moving from waiting on systems and engineering teams to building, automating, and acting faster with AI.

AI is changing what finance teams can build, automate, and understand on their own. As revenue models become more usage-based and data becomes more fragmented across billing, CRM, ERP, and spreadsheets, finance leaders need faster ways to see what is moving, why it is moving, and what to do next.

Base44 will lead an interactive working session: “Build a Finance Superagent in 60 Seconds.” The audience will choose the focus — revenue movement, executive visibility, or cash — and see how a finance-focused AI superagent can be shaped live to answer executive-level questions across fragmented systems.

Vayu
Vayu provides revenue infrastructure for modern finance and operations teams, helping companies connect the path from selling to billing, revenue visibility, and business performance.

PwC
PwC brings a transformation and advisory perspective from working with leading enterprises on finance modernization, automation, operating models, and AI adoption.

Base44
Base44 enables teams to build production-ready applications and AI-powered workflows from natural-language prompts, helping operators turn ideas into working tools without waiting on engineering.

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
