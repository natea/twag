## Description

Why do brilliant innovations often fail to get funded? Because the investor’s brain is wired to "tune out" what it cannot quickly categorize. For founders in Biotech, MedTech, and Deep Science, the greatest hurdle isn't the technology—it’s the Cognitive Load placed on the listener.

In this intensive 90-minute clinic, neuroscience-based executive coach Jennifer Pawlitschek teaches you how to bridge the gap between technical brilliance and investor-ready communication. Using the Technical Translation Matrix, you will learn how to explain high-stakes science without triggering "mental fatigue."

Key Takeaways:

    -The Neuro-Audit: A direct diagnostic of your most complex slide to identify "friction points."

    -Neural Anchoring: Techniques to ensure your breakthrough stays "top of mind."

    -The Action-First Protocol: Lead with value so the brain stays engaged through the data.
