## Description

South Asians in Finance brings together South Asian leaders across finance, fintech, investing, and adjacent sectors for a focused, high-signal conversation during New York Tech Week. From traditional finance to emerging platforms, the discussion explores how careers are being built, capital is being deployed, and where the industry is heading next.

​Designed to be thoughtful and practical, this session creates space for candid insights, shared experiences, and meaningful connections within the ASAN community.
