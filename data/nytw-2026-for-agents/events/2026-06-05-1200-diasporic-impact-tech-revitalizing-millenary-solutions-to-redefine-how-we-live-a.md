## Description

Humanity is on the move, there have never been more people living outside of the country of origin in modern history. Between the world's oldest regions from Greece to the Amazon Rainforest and the Himalayas, existing solutions can meet modern technology to ensure proper longevity. Today, amidst this very special period of transition, most of the people may not have access to their bank account, but they have a connection. A digital relay framework makes it possible to scale these initiatives in order to be implemented across the world. Solutions are disseminated more rapidly than ever through diasporic impact across regions and continents. The next major arena facing humanity is the digital and physical architecture of a regenerative Diaspora and its design, leveraging modern-day technologies to enable significant and scalable contributions redefining how we live and interact, regardless of where you are from and where you are going. This Tech Week 2026, the Global Portal Institute and The Port Global invite you to another session exploring vital millenary intelligence brought to our lives through technology.
