## Description

Join Databricks Ventures and VC Friends for a founders and investors meetup!

Amidst the chaos of NY Tech Week, we’re carving out a focused, curated space for people building and backing data and AI startups. No panels, no pitches on stage, no sitting around—just sharp conversations, real connections, and space to talk honestly about what’s working (and what isn’t).

Some of our friends include: Antler, Capital One Ventures, General Atlantic, Insight Partners, JPM Growth Equity, Lightspeed Ventures, NEA, and SignalFire.

About Databricks:
​​Databricks helps startups accelerate innovation and time-to-market by allowing them to focus on building their core products instead of managing complex data infrastructure. 

About Databricks Ventures:
Databricks Ventures invests in innovative companies that share our view of the future for data, analytics and AI. Our focus is on supporting early- and growth-stage companies that are empowering AI in innovative ways on top of or alongside the Databricks Data Intelligence Platform.

About Fenwick: 
Fenwick is a leading law firm, purpose-built to guide visionary tech and life sciences companies and their investors through every stage of growth, from startups securing their first round of funding to leading publicly traded global enterprises. As one of Silicon Valley’s original legal practices, today we have over 600 lawyers, patent agents, engineers, and scientists serving clients all over the world. Visit www.fenwick.com to learn more.

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
