## Description

Most AI deployments don't deliver what the demo promised.

The question is whether that's the vendor's problem, your infrastructure's problem, or a decision that was made six months before you signed anything.

On June 4, the vibeFORWARD team is convening 30 engineering and product leaders, operators, and the founders building the infrastructure they're betting on — for an off-the-record panel and dinner during NYC Tech Week.

Speakers: Presented shortly.
