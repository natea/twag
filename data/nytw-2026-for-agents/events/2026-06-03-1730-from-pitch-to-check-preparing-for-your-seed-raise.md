## Description

Join us for Pitch to Check: Preparing for Your Seed Raise at New York Tech Week, hosted by Brown Girl Angels, Powered by Brown Girl Magazine and NineF. 

This is an intimate, community-driven evening designed to unpack how investors actually make decisions and assess investments. 
We’re bringing together a candid conversation between operators and investors to explore what happens behind the check:

• How conviction is built — beyond the pitch
• The balance between instinct and financial analysis
• What makes a founder (and a market) feel investable
• How investors think about risk, loss, and long-term potential

This is a behind-the-scenes look at how capital moves and how to position yourself when you’re in the room.

Learn From: 
Raeva Sayed — Founder & CEO, Tavia Health
Maya Bakhai — Founder & General Partner, Spice Capital
Bhargavi Varma -Founder, Brown Girl Angels

Moderated by Sravani Kallepalli & Nikhila Kulukuru - Cofounders, NineF

Following the conversation, join us for an intimate reception to continue the dialogue with founders and investors over food and drinks.
