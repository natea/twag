Madeline & Co. and Excelsior Edge Ventures return with our annual opening night gathering, now a four-year Tech Week tradition. Last year, our opening party at Gosepl NYC became one of the most talked-about nights of the week. This year, we’re bringing the same energy with a sharper founder/investor crowd, music and a room built for real collisions.

Come to connect. Stay to dance. Leave with three new friends, two potential investors, and at least one conversation you’ll still remember in six months.

​Excelsior Edge Ventures
​Excelsior Edge Ventures is a founder and investor community based in New York City. Over the past two years, we’ve hosted 48 events across strategy, innovation, fundraising, culture, and community building, bringing together founders, operators, investors, and creatives across the city. ​ 

Excelsior Edge is the community behind 11-11 Sprints, a live venture-building campaign designed for people at the very beginning, even pre-idea, but with real domain insight, strong instincts, and a clear sense that something in their world is broken.

Cohorts consist of 11 aspiring and early founders with real domain insight, strong instincts, and something worth building, whether that means a raw idea, a loose concept, or simply a problem they understand deeply enough to attack. Over 11 days, we’ll work with one founder per day to help shape the strategic backbone of their startup in public.
