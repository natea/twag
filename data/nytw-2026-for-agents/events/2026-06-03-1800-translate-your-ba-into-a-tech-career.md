## Description

There’s never been a greater time to break into tech with a BA degree.

In the age of AI, companies don’t just need coders. They need people who can think critically, understand audiences, and communicate clearly.

Business Insider recently named communications the hottest job in tech. These are the exact skills BA majors develop—but the path into tech isn’t always obvious.

This workshop is designed to change that.

If you’ve ever thought: “I’m not technical enough for tech” or simply “What can I even do with my degree?” This session is for you.
