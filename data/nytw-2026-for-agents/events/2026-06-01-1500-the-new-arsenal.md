## Description

FORGE presents: The New Arsenal — Venture Capital and the Dual-Use Economy

Hosted by VC in DC during NY Tech Week.

Join us for a moderated panel and networking reception spotlighting how venture capital is reshaping the dual-use and defense technology landscape. Our panelists span corporate venture, institutional venture, and crossover funds with deep experience across AI, autonomy, space, cyber, and reindustrialization. The conversation will explore how investors evaluate emerging dual-use technologies, navigate the federal market, and align capital with mission priorities.
Panelists:

Brian Maccarthy, Managing Partner, Booz Allen Ventures
Alex Poulin, Partner, Lavrock Ventures
Steve Ryan, Partner, Pillsbury (moderator)

FORGE is VC in DC's event series focused on the companies, capital, and people building critical national infrastructure.

Curated audience. Intimate format. Limited capacity.
Approval required.

Thank you to our partners: Pillsbury, Betts Recruiting, Sift Search, Finstrat Management, and Stifel Venture Banking.

Stifel Financial (NYSE: SF) is a global investment bank, wealth management, and commercial banking institution with $400B+ in AUM. Stifel Bank offers tailored venture and fund banking solutions, including no-fee accounts for fund clients, ICS accounts with up to $250MM in FDIC insurance, capital call and management company credit lines, and venture lending.

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
