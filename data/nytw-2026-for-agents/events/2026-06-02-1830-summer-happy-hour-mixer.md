## Description

Hosted by United Women in Business in partnership with NY #TechWeek, this is the perfect chance to expand your circle, swap ideas, and make meaningful connections—without the awkward small talk.

Our friendly UWIB volunteers will be there to help break the ice, so whether you come solo or with friends, you’ll always have someone to chat with.   🍷 Enjoy extended happy hour specials until 8:30pm and light appetizers while they last—we can’t wait to see you there! 🎉

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
