## Description

iHeartMedia Ventures, Alumni Ventures, and Coefficient Capital invite you to pull back the curtain at iHeartRadio HQ. We're bringing together sharp minds in tech, media, and venture for an evening of honest conversation moderated by Oz Woloshyn — Peabody and Emmy Award-winning journalist and host of iHeart's TechStuff.
 
We'll get into:
•	The attention economy is broken — so what comes next? Algorithms flattened culture. Feeds got saturated. Digital burnout is real. Oz covers this weekly on TechStuff — and our investors are living it in their portfolio companies. What does it mean for consumer brands when people are simultaneously more online and more checked out than ever?
•	The consumer story nobody's telling Every week on TechStuff, Oz finds the angle everyone's missing. Tonight, we ask our panel the same question: what's the most important consumer shift happening right now that isn't getting nearly enough airtime?
 
Then John Stessel, NYC magician and mentalist, takes the stage — a fitting close to an evening spent separating signal from noise.
 
Stay after for cocktails, networking, and music. The conversations that matter most usually start after the mics go off.


About Alumni Ventures: AV is a venture co-investor managing over $1.5B AUM and backing 1,600+ portfolio companies from Pre-Seed through Series D. Consumer investments include Oura, Quince, Goodles, Sleeper, Gametime, Bluesky, and more.

About iHeartMedia Ventures: iHeartMedia Corporate Development & Ventures invests in companies across all consumer industries and companies strategic to iHeart’s business and growth.

About Coefficient Capital: Coefficient Capital leads venture and growth stage investments into businesses powering modern transformational consumer shifts.
