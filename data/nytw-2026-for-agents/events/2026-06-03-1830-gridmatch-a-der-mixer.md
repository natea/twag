## Description

NY Tech Week, but make it electric⚡️

Anchors: Michael Lee, Peyton Boswell, Andrew Gordon, Emily Fisher 

GridMatch is a fast-paced networking experience where the people building, financing, and operating the electric grid come together in a lightning-session style event to brainstorm with as many people as possible.

We're all working on various silos on the grid, it's time to open some doors and see the system from each other's eyes. 

You’ll  solution wild card questions with experts across the utility, project development, and asset management space. And hopefully the new perspectives can shed some light on new solutions. 

Format:
- Round Robin Brief by Anchors: biggest challenges
- Lightning Brainstorm Sessions led by anchors: rotate across 10-15 minutes each
- Debrief by Anchors: promising holistic solutions and new ideas from sessions
- Open Mixer 

This a rapid matchmaking system for the GRID. Open Bar and food provided. 

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
