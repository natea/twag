## Description

Swap keys. Talk shop. Leave with a keyboard. Join us for a hands-on build night where every attendee assembles their own premium mechanical keyboard and takes it home.

Every attendee will receive a keyboard builder kit and choose between two custom keyboard designs before building their board. You’ll also get to pick from three switch types: linear, tactile, or clicky, and take home a custom switch keychain. 

Once the keyboards are built, attendees can optionally join our engineers for Type Racer battles or LAN games. (Just bring your own device if you want to participate in the games.) 

We’ll have snacks, a fully stocked fridge with every drink you can dream of, and merch bags for everyone who attends.

We’ll also be raffling off a high-value gift for one lucky attendee who applies for our Member of Technical Staff role at www.anterior.com/careers

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
