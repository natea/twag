## Description

DeepTech Buildathon

NYC Tech Week just got its most interesting night.

We're bringing together the boldest deep tech founders in the city for a live showcase where they demo what they're building, get real feedback from the room, and compete for prizes. No pitch theater. No fluff. Just founders showing their work and a crowd that actually gets it.

5–10 selected founders will present. Judges + crowd vote. Winners take home tool credits, resources, and a fast-track introduction to Soma Capital for investment consideration.

Apply to present or come to watch — either way, you'll leave with new people in your corner.
