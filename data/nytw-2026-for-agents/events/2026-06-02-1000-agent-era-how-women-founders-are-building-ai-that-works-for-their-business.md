## Description

Description:
AI agents are the biggest unlock for resource-strapped founders — but most of the conversation is happening in rooms that don't look like us. Agent Era flips that. Hosted by Black Women Talk Tech, this half-day experience brings women founders together to cut through the AI noise, hear from operators who are already deploying agents in their businesses, and walk out with a working agent they built themselves. No jargon. No fluff. Just results.

Agenda:
10:00 AM – Check-in & Networking
10:30 AM – Opening: The Agent Opportunity (Why agents, why now, why us)
10:45 AM – Panel: "Agents in Action" — Women founders share how they're using AI agents for sales, ops, customer support, and content. What's working, what flopped, and where the real ROI lives.
11:45 AM – Break
12:00 PM – Workshop: "Build Your First Business Agent" — Guided, laptops-open session. Attendees choose their use case (outreach, scheduling, customer service, content) and build a functioning agent with step-by-step instruction.
1:30 PM – Show & Tell: Attendees demo what they built
1:50 PM – Closing & Next Steps
2:00 PM – End
