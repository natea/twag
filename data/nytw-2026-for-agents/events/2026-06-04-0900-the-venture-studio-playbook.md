## Description

Join us during NY Tech Week for a casual fireside chat exploring the rise of venture studios and how they’re reshaping company creation, early-stage investing, and founder support. Featuring Lauren Cooks Levitan of Root alongside Byron Dailey and Jonathan Sagot of Fenwick, the conversation will dive into what makes the venture studio model work, how studios identify and build winning companies, and what the future of startup formation looks like in today’s market.

Expect candid insights, practical lessons for founders and investors, and the opportunity to connect with NYC’s startup ecosystem over networking and drinks following the discussion.

Root is a venture studio and company-building platform focused on creating and scaling next-generation consumer businesses. Founded by longtime consumer and marketplace operator Lauren Cooks Levitan, Root partners with founders, creators, and operators to help turn emerging ideas into enduring brands through hands-on operational support, strategic guidance, and company-building expertise.
