## Description

Age and Identity verification is full of deepfakes. It is getting harder and harder to distinguish honest actors from fraudsters. 

The solution isn't that we use better AI tools to detect deepfakes - that is playing a losing battle. Rather we should be using authoritative sources and cryptography to verify user's identity. 

That is what Reclaim Protocol does (https://reclaimprotocol.org) - an open source verification system that uses zero knowledge proofs to verify users' identity. 

Come learn more, and discuss with others solving the same problem. Each participant will be hand picked, to maintain high density of like minded people.
