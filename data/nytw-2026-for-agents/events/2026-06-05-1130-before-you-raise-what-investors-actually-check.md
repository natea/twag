## Description

This event is designed for tech founders and investors navigating fundraising and growth. Most companies enter investor conversations assuming their structure is solid and find out during diligence that it’s not.

We’ll break down what tech investors actually verify, where structural issues tend to surface, and how to approach ownership, equity, and governance early so they hold when capital is deployed.

Who this is for:
- Tech founders preparing to raise or actively in investor conversations
- Teams making decisions around equity, ownership, and early structure
- Tech or AI Investors evaluating early-stage companies

What we'll cover:
- What investors actually verify during diligence in high-growth tech companies
- Where cap tables and legal records diverge and how that impacts ownership and control
- How equity, governance, and decision-making authority evolve as companies raise and scale
- The structural issues that tend to surface when companies move from building to raising
