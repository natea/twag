## Description

LinkedIn GTM Hackathon

think you can build a brand's LinkedIn presence in 2.5 hours? come prove it.

you'll get matched with a real early-stage startup, given their brand brief, and challenged to build a 3-post content series live — from hook to value to CTA. then you'll pitch your strategy to a panel of founders and creators who judge you on execution, creativity, and how well you got the brand.

the format:
🔹 Act 1 — prompt + brand reveal, quick panel lightning round
🔹 Act 2 — the sprint (3 posts: hook, value, CTA)
🔹 Act 3 — pitch your strategy, judges vote, networking

how you'll be judged:
best hook · best strategy · most engagement · founder's choice

who's this for:
students, early-career professionals, founders, and creators who want to practice real marketing storytelling — not fake case studies.

what you'll walk away with:
real posts. real feedback. real connections. 

are you a startup that wants your brand featured? email natalie@thegenztea.com
