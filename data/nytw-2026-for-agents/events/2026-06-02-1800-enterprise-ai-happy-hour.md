## Description

Building AI at the enterprise level? Come meeting your people.

Credal AI and the AI Platform Collective are hosting a happy hour for the practitioners actually shipping agents into production: the people navigating agent sprawl, designing governance frameworks, securing data pipelines, and building the internal platforms that enterprises run on.

There exists a large gap between the capabilities of AI and harnessing it at enterprise scale. This is a room for the people who know that gap intimately.

Come ready to talk about what's working, what's not, and what you're thinking about next.

Space is limited and the guest list is curated. RSVP to secure your spot!

About Credal AI

Credal.ai is the control plane for enterprise AI: a platform for building, deploying, and governing AI agents at scale. Credal is built around a security-first philosophy: agents that automatically mirror existing access controls, full audit trails, and org-wide governance policies that enforce across every team, every agent, and every chat surface. Customers like MongoDB, Wise, Checkr, and Comcast use Credal to move past proof-of-concept and into production, all without sacrificing compliance or control.
