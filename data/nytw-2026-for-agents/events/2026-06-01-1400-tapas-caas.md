## Description

New York Tech Week 2026 starts here.

Every June, thousands of founders, investors, and operators descend on New York for NY Tech Week. 

Tapas & Cañas is how we open the week with FJ Labs. Spanish-style tapas, cañas, and the people shaping today's tech ecosystem, all at a Michelin-start restaurant before the programming kicks off. No panels, no slides. Just the room you want to be in on day one.

This is an invite-only event for 7- and 8- figure founders, and the VCs backing them.
