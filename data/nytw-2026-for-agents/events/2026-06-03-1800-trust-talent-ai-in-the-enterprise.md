## Description

AI is fast changing how we work - team structures, leadership, performance, recruitment and more. Some organizations can't keep up, or know where to start.

Join Lucy Schott, Andrea Henderson and Devanshu Mehrotra for a fireside chat on what the future of work actually looks like now that AI is embedded across the enterprise. 

Lucy spent 10+ years in global HR, Culture & Capability for Fortune 500 companies, but started her career as a games designer. She now is a founder of a self service AI Adoption Platform, advises companies on AI workforce strategy and teaches AI to leaders at General Assembly. 

Andrea is a Partner at DHR Global (top 6 exec search firm), a Perplexity Fellow, and has 25+ years matching talent to value across life sciences, healthcare, and tech.

Devanshu is a Senior Director at Gartner in the sectors of Digital Assurance, Audit Analysis and AI and has for the last 20+ years worked in technical auditing at some of the biggest consulting firms in the world. 

Real talk on what's shifting, what's hype, the big buzz word of trust and what leaders need to do now.

Drinks + networking to follow.
