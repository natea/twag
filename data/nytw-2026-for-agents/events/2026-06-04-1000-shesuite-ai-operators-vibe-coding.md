## Description

For women who build, and the community that shows up for them.

This NY Tech Week, Sparx Labs and FemBuildCollective are bringing together women operators and builders for a full afternoon of learning, building, and connecting.

SheSuite is part panel, part hands-on building, and all community. Whether you're a founder scaling your first product or an engineer stepping into leadership, this is your room.

What to expect:

🎤 Women AI Operator Panel — Real conversations with women leading teams in AI. Topics include building AI products at scale, the reality of technical leadership, and navigating career paths in a fast-moving industry.

🛠 Ship It Vibe Coding — Walk away with a live, working AI agent. In two hands-on hours, you'll build a public relations agent, working around the clock for you to find you the best exposure for your personal brand, complete with automated follow-up emails. No prior AI experience required.

🤝 Community + Networking — Meet the people building alongside you.
