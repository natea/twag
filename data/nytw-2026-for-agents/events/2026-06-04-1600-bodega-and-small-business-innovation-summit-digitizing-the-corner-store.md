## Description

The Bodega and Small Business Association, representing neighborhood businesses across New York City, invites you to join a conversation on how we can better support and strengthen small businesses in our communities.

There are 15,000+ bodegas in New York City. They move billions in commerce—and almost none of them run on modern infrastructure.

That’s not a problem. It’s a massive opportunity.

The Bodega and Small Business Innovation Summit brings together founders, operators, investors, and technologists to explore how media tech, AI, fintech, and security can transform the future of small business in urban communities.

Now featuring partners including Negozee and Fiado, expanding the conversation around financial tools and infrastructure for independent operators.

This isn’t about adding gadgets—it’s about building integrated systems that allow small businesses to unlock new revenue streams, compete with larger players, and scale on their own terms. Modernization without displacement. Tech without erasure.

This will be a free event, with refreshments provided and ample opportunity to network, connect, and engage with fellow founders, operators, and community leaders throughout the evening.

Hosted by the Bodega and Small Business Association, Tech Ranch, Bodegas TV, and Angeles Investors as part of #NYTechWeek, this is where community-rooted commerce meets institutional capital.

If you're building, investing in, or supporting the future of local commerce—this is a room you want to be in.
