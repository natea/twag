## Description

Women represent nearly half of all business owners in America, yet only 1% will ever exit. That's not just a founder problem. It's a $3.3T market opportunity that fintech has yet to fully build for.

The resources to start and scale are available. Meanwhile, the infrastructure to exit is still being built.
What's holding women back isn't just financial literacy. It's the psychosocial, emotional, and identity dimensions of the female exit that make her experience categorically different and categorically underserved. Most women have never seen the process clearly enough to picture themselves in it.
This session exists to bring together the female founder and the fintech world to have a conversation about the gap and the opportunity. 

Co-hosted with Baton, the modern business brokerage platform, we walk through what selling actually looks like — step by step, in plain language — so the platform feels approachable and the female founder can see herself in the process.

For the fintech builders and investors in the room: you leave with a clear picture of what this market needs, what it's worth, and where the white space is still wide open.
