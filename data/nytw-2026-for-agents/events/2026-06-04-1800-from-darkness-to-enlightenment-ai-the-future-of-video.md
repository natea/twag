## Description

Join TwelveLabs, AWS, and industry leaders as we explore how AI-powered video understanding is ushering media into its age of enlightenment.

This NY Tech Week panel unpacks what it means when AI can finally see and understand video the way humans do, and what that transformation means for publishers, broadcasters, creators, brands, and the technologies connecting them.

You’ll hear from leaders across media and AI on where the real progress is happening, how new measurement and monetization models are emerging, and what a fully “enlightened” video ecosystem might look like over the next three years.

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek - a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
