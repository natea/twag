## Description

A room for the conversations we can’t afford to avoid.

AI is changing how we work, create, lead, and understand our own value. Beneath the speed of innovation is a deeper question:

How do we build a future we actually want to be part of?

The Impact Room is a raw, intimate, and interactive conversation that moves beyond AI hype and into the human, cultural, and organizational challenges shaping adoption.

Together, we’ll address the elephants in the room: Who is AI really impacting? What futures are being sold to us? What bottlenecks are preventing real adoption? How do we build cultures people can trust? And how do we lead ourselves and others through this shift?

Our panel brings together experts across ethics, governance, technology, and adoption to explore how we move from hype to real capacity through experimentation, human-centered guardrails, and coalition-building.

After the discussion, join us for the Impact Room Mixer with great drinks, great food, and the chance to connect with some of the most innovative minds at NY Tech Week.

Because the future is not inevitable. It is built by the people willing to shape it.

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com
