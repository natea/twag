## Description

In a market characterized by volatility, conservative investment strategies from family offices and institutions, and extended fundraising timelines, this event aims to create a collaborative space to openly discuss the challenges and solutions emerging venture capital managers face when raising capital. 

The event will bring together key limited partners and emerging managers for a constructive dialogue on how the fundraising landscape is evolving and how managers can position themselves for long-term success. Notably, we will feature 3 emerging managers and 1 limited partner who have recently achieved significant milestones in their fundraising journey. These speakers will share their insights, strategies, and advice for navigating today's fundraising environment. 

We also hope to see a few family offices and family office event organizers attend, as their perspectives and potential involvement are crucial to fostering deeper connections and understanding in the fundraising ecosystem.  This event promises to foster meaningful discussions and actionable takeaways for those looking to thrive in the venture capital space.

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
