## Description

AI’s impact continues to reshape how companies hire, operate, and grow. Join Vanguarde Digital--NYC's AI Workforce Accelerator for one of Tech-Week NYC's most timely conversations hosted at Adelphi University's Manhattan campus. Featuring panels and networking with practitioners from GOOGLE, NVIDIA, and ACCENTURE as they tackle the questions top of mind for job-seekers: Which roles in tech are being restructured or eliminated because of AI?  What are tech employers actually hiring for right now? What does AI fluency look like across non AI companies? This will be a frank, insider-led dialogue designed to give attendees a clear-eyed view of AI’s impact on the world of work (in NYC) and how to stay ahead of it. Every attendee walks away with Vanguarde's exclusive AI Skills Audit Toolkit, a curated self-assessment framework to identify an individual’s AI readiness to position themselves for roles being created now.

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
