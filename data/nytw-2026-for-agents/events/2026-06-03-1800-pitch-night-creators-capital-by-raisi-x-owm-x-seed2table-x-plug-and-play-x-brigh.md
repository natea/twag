## Description

New York’s most impactful founders, most active investors, and largest corporate innovators - all on one roof. Run by Plug and Play, Raisi, OWM, and Seed2Table.

What to expect

Live pitch competition consisting of ten rapid fire 90-second advanced AI startup elevator pitches. Four esteemed VC judges will rank the startups and winners will receive prizes including a Mac Mini, Delta Airlines Status, 3-months of Raisi campaigns, and office space in Civic Hall through Plug and Play.

You’ll be in a room with:

- Founders who are shaping the future of technology and the way businesses operate
- Top investors from New York City and around the world that are actively deploying capital 
- Corporate innovators searching for advanced solutions to their problems 
- Creators who are shaping the way startups go to market and brand themselves

After the pitches, the room opens up:

- Founders and investors connect for potential investment opportunities 
- Founders meet corporations to be early adopters of their solutions
- Founders meet creators who can amplify their brand
- Creators find companies they actually want to be part of


About Plug and Play

Plug and Play is a global innovation platform that connects startups, corporations, and investors. With programs across major industries and offices around the world, they help founders scale through partnerships, pilot opportunities, and access to capital.

Plug and Play has accelerated thousands of startups and built one of the largest innovation ecosystems globally, bringing together early-stage companies and some of the most influential enterprises and investors.

---
About Raisi

Raisi is a fundraising operating system built to help founders run their raise end to end. From identifying the right investors to executing outreach and managing conversations, it replaces fragmented fundraising workflows with one system.

Since 2024, once every 50.1 minutes, a founder has met with an investor interested in their company through Raisi. The platform has facilitated 21,000+ positive connections between founders and investors and is powered by up-to-date signals from millions of live interactions across the private markets.

---
About OWM

OWM is a creator equity platform for brands and creators looking for long-term, invested partnerships. 

We help founders find the creators who actually influence their customers, and provide the equity infrastructure like a standard Influence for Equity agreement and the CSOP (Creator Stock Option Pool) with performance tracking for "earned equity”. With over 10,000+ creators looking to invest their social capital, creator ownership is our wedge in, but we’re building ownership management for all.
