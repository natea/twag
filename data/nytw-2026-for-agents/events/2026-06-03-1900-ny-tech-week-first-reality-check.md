## Description

For The Firsts presents: First (Reality) Check

​Most NY Tech Week events follow the same script: Someone pitches their startup, or announces they're raising a new round. AI is mentioned about 11 times. And QR codes are passed around like hors d'oeuvres.

​This isn't that.

​First (Reality) Check is a candid, lighthearted panel conversation about what it's actually like to work in tech, and how pointing out the obvious can lead to building a business that rivals a traditional startup. Our panel will include:

​Alberta Devor: Alberta Tech breaks down the world of tech and AI through sketch comedy and sharp commentary.

​Varun Rana: Varun Rana turns the emotional rollercoaster of corporate life into clever, relatable storytelling.

​Come for the honest (and funny) conversation, and stay for the mixer and cocktails.

​This is a NY Tech Week event with no pitch decks, no raise announcements, and of course - no job titles.

​Hosted as part of NY Tech Week.
