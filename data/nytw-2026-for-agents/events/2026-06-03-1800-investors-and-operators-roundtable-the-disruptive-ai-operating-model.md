## Description

StartOut NYC and Out in Tech NYC present 'Investors and Operators Roundtable: The Disruptive AI Operating Model', a private, invite-only roundtable, bringing together a select group of LGBTQ+ and allied Venture Capital, Private Equity operating partners, late-stage founders, CFOs, and AI leaders.

The session will focus on a core question facing leadership teams today: 

Where is AI actually creating enterprise value and how are organizations structuring to make it work?

This is a closed-door, operator and executive level discussion centered on real AI execution, measurable outcomes, and practical lessons from deploying AI across portfolio companies and enterprise environments.

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
