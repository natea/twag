## Description

How We AI: NYC, Inaugural Edition · #NYTechWeek

We're in the middle of a Cambrian explosion. Not the one from half a billion years ago - the one happening right now, in every team trying to figure out what AI actually means for the way they work.

The creatures that survived the first one weren't the biggest or the fastest. They were the most adaptive. The octopus is still here because two-thirds of its neurons live in its arms, not its brain. Each arm senses, decides, acts. That's the model - not just for how AI should work inside a company, but for how companies themselves need to evolve.

How We AI: NYC is a room of forty people figuring this out in real time. Operators, founders, marketers, and business leaders - some inside large enterprises, some building startups, some growing emerging brands - all already in the work, all with skin in the game.

The evening is built around candid conversation: a panel with people who've rewired how their teams operate, live demos of the workflows actually running, and time to compare notes with the others in the room.

Intelligence is human.

This is the first gathering of the people building what that looks like.

Forty seats. The founding cohort.

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com
