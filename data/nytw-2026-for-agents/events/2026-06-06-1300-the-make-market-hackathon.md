## Description

You have 3 hours. An engineer. A creator. A stack that can actually ship something. Go.

Make & Market is a special-edition hackathon for builders and creators who want to make something real — not just talk about it. No all-nighters. No decks. No judges in suits. Just a curated afternoon, good drinks, and the quiet thrill of putting something into the world.

Here's how it works: engineers and creators are paired. 
The engineer builds a consumer app using Lovable, ElevenLabs, and Tavily. 
The creator shapes the story and makes it land. 

By 5pm, your product is live and your marketing is posted. The room votes. One week later, we check real traction. Most downloads, clicks, views - that team wins.

Come hungry. Leave with something shipped.

→ Spots are capped at 50. Apply with your role and a link to your work. 

We'll suggest your match - you have the first 30 minutes to swap if the vibe's off.

The Make & Market Hackathon is part of a16z Tech Week, and brought to you by The FOMO Group, the builder community behind FOMO Hack and FOMO Club, and Verci, NYC's home for the creator economy. Two communities, one room, one afternoon.

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
