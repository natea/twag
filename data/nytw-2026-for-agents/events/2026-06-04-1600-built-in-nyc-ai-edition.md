## Description

New York is quickly becoming one of the most exciting places in the world to build AI companies, and the founders driving that momentum are doing it right here in NYC.

Join us for a panel discussion featuring AI founders building and scaling companies across the New York tech ecosystem. We’ll dive into what makes NYC uniquely positioned for the next wave of AI innovation, the realities of building in today’s market, lessons from scaling early-stage companies, and where the future of AI is headed.

Kristine Di Bacco, Partner at Fenwick, will moderate a panel including: 
- Michael Rubenstein – Co-Founder & Co-CEO at Firsthand
- Jonathan Frankle – Chief AI Scientist at Databricks
- Glen Wise - CEO at Cinder
- Vin Sachidananda - Partner at Radical Ventures

Expect candid conversations, founder insights, and the opportunity to connect with fellow entrepreneurs, operators, investors, and members of the NYC tech community over networking and drinks following the panel.
