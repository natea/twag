## Description

What if founders could identify compatibility before building a company together?

One of the leading reasons startups fail is cofounder incompatibility yet most entrepreneurs don’t discover misalignment until stress, growth, and decision-making pressure begin impacting the business.

That’s why Relationality and DefinedTalent’s visionary impact are coming together for a special networking and launch event focused on redefining how we build teams, partnerships, leadership, and culture from the very beginning.

During this collaborative event, DefinedTalent will officially introduce the ReDefine T.H.I.S. framework a new people-centered approach built around:
Talent. Human Resources. Innovation. Strategy.

Alongside the launch of Relationality where attendees will experience the power of of the company’s groundbreaking compatibility platform, designed to identify decision-making styles and predict long-term working compatibility between founders, collaborators, and leadership teams.

As part of the experience, participants will receive FREE access to the Relationality Test prior to the event giving attendees the unique opportunity to better understand themselves and immediately identify whether a potential cofounder, collaborator, or strategic connection they meet at the event may truly be aligned…or not.

This is more than a networking event.
It’s a conversation around alignment, leadership, culture, innovation, and building relationships that actually last.

Whether you’re a founder, entrepreneur, leader, creative, investor, or community builder — this event is designed to help you connect with intention, purpose, and compatibility.

Come network.
Come connect.
Come ReDefine what alignment can look like in business and beyond.

Learn more at www.relationalityforwork.com and www.definedtalent.com

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com
