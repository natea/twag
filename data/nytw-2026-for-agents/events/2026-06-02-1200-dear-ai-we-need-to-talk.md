## Description

Dear AI, We Need To Talk.

An “Agony Aunt/Uncle” Session for AI Projects Going Sideways…

Your AI project looked promising. Then reality showed up.

This session is for people whose AI projects are flailing in some form or fashion. Or for people who want to start an AI project and avoid issues such as:
* inconsistent answers to the same question, when the answer needs to be precise & repeatable
* outputs that are plausible but wrong
* prototypes that worked in demo, then failed in real workflows
* unclear ROI despite time, tools, and budget already spent
* workflows that still need too much human checking and correction
* no clear decision on whether this should be AI, automation, better data, or simply a process fix

Your panel slash "Agony Aunts/Uncles" include:
1/ Darlene Newman (Ivy Cap Tech)
2/ Anurag Banerjee (Axioned)
3/ Confirming soon!

Three people who have been hands-on working with:
* AI implementation inside real businesses
* operational workflows and automation
* product and engineering delivery
* data, accuracy, and reliability challenges
* scaling AI projects beyond the prototype stage
* rescuing projects that stalled after leadership bought into the hype
