## Description

As an alternative to typical NY Tech Week events, we ​will host a walk as a chance to connect with fellow neurotech enthusiasts in a relaxed setting! Previous events included attendees from: OpenBCI, Synchron, Sharper Sense, Mount Sinai, etc.
