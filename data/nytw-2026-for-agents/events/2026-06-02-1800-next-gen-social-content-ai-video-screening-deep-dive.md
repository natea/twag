Join us for an exclusive AI video showcase and deep dive into the next generation of creator tools. Hosted by StoryFlow AI, this event is built specifically for social media creators and creative teams ready to move beyond short, disconnected AI clips.

We will showcase the Martini platform, demonstrating our node-based, canvas-centric workflow designed for crafting consistent, longer-form video narratives. During the live demo, we'll explore the technical nuances of directing "intent"—focusing on the emotional performance and specific creative goals of a scene, rather than just tweaking general metadata. Stick around after the showcase for a creator panel discussing the latest in Social Media Content Creation and AI influencers, followed by networking.

(Plus, complimentary drinks and popcorn will be provided throughout the entire event!) 🍿🥂

6:00 PM – 6:30 PM | Doors Open & Welcome

Settle in, grab a drink and some popcorn, and meet fellow forward-thinking creators.

6:30 PM – 7:00 PM | AI Short Film Screening

Sit back and enjoy a curated selection of AI-generated short films, showcasing the cutting-edge of synthetic media storytelling.

Discover the Martini platform's canvas-centric workflow. We will walk through a live demonstration on how to direct "intent" for cohesive, long-form narratives.

A deep dive into social media content creation, featuring insights from creative partners like Elena Savlokhova and Cat Jin. We'll discuss actionable playbooks for building an audience with AI.

Connect with the StoryFlow AI team, discuss the screening, and brainstorm your next project over drinks.
