## Description

If you are a Believer working in Tech, you know how isolating the industry can feel. The grind is real, the spaces are secular, and finding community with people who share your faith and understand you is rare. This brunch was made for you. Come as you are, bring a colleague, and be reminded that you are not doing this alone.

Together, we can advance the Kingdom of God in Tech.
