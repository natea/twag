## Description

PoC Fest is the only hackathon at NYC Tech Week where winning teams walk away with letters of intent from sponsors, not just prize money. This is a one-day, application-gated event for early-stage founders and builders who want to ship something that actually matters, across any stack: AI, crypto, fintech, CPG, whatever you're building.
No panels. No fireside chats. No pitch tourists. Just a curated group of founders and engineers in a room, building something stupid-good with real commercial stakes on the line for the best teams.
Spots are limited and attendees are screened. Apply with your GitHub and LinkedIn; we'll confirm qualified builders on a rolling basis.

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
