## Description

Join Maersk and partners for an intimate, invite-only breakfast and discussion on how global trade moves, the innovation behind it, and how you and your startup/team can be positioned to win. Maersk's innovation team is opening the door for a candid conversation on where supply chain technology is headed — the infrastructure being built, the bets being made, and what the next decade looks like for global logistics.

Expect sharp conversation, real perspectives from operators and investors, and no fluff.

Note: Recording is not allowed at the event.

Speakers include:
- Aruna Hussain, Global Head of Landside + CRM Product Development & Innovation @ Maersk
- Caroline Duffy, Partner @ Cowboy Ventures
- Raj Aggarwal, Founder @ ex-GM GenAI at AWS

Moderated by:
- Naomi Goez, Partner @ Forum Ventures

What to expect:
8:30-9:00: arrive and mingle
9:00-9:45: panel and Q&A
9:45-10:30: open networking 

Food and drinks provided.

Space is limited.

Maersk is a global leader in integrated logistics, currently spearheading a digital revolution in the supply chain with a dedicated team of 100,000+ employees operating in more than 130 countries.

​​​​​​​Rock Yard Ventures is an early-stage venture capital firm backing exceptional founders redefining technology across core and foundational industries.

Forum Ventures is the leading early-stage fund, accelerator, and venture studio for B2B tech startups. Founded in 2014 and based in New York, San Francisco, and Toronto, we’re on a mission to make the zero-to-one journey easier, more accessible, and successful for founders. Forum is investing in 65+ companies this year.

Cowboy Ventures is a high-conviction, seed-stage venture capital firm based in the San Francisco Bay Area. Founded in 2012 by Aileen Lee, the veteran investor who famously coined the term "unicorn", the firm is known for its "founder-obsessed" philosophy and its focus on being a deeply hands-on partner during a startup's earliest days.

The event is sponsored by Stripe and Rho.

Stripe is a global financial infrastructure platform that helps businesses accept payments, facilitate multi-party payouts, manage revenue, and scale worldwide. Stripe’s software and APIs power payments, subscriptions, billing, fraud prevention, and global commerce, helping businesses grow faster and operate more efficiently around the world.

Rho is the all-in-one banking platform for startups. With banking set up in minutes, 2% cashback cards, and powerful tools for managing spend, paying bills, and closing the books, Rho helps high-growth startups move fast and stay focused—with support that goes to the ends of the earth to help you win.

Rho is a fintech company, not a bank. Checking and card services are provided by Webster Bank, N.A., member FDIC; savings account services are provided by American Deposit Management Co. and its partner banks.
