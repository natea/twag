## Description

Most founders do not struggle with building product. They struggle with turning early traction into repeatable, scalable pipeline.

This session is for seed to Series A founders who already have customers and need a go-to-market system that does not depend entirely on the founder’s personal network. 

We will break down what a strong 90-day GTM roadmap actually looks like, covering positioning, messaging, channel priorities, and how to use AI to support execution without losing strategic direction.

You will see how to go from “we need marketing” to a focused plan your lean team can actually execute. 

No beginner overviews. No generic AI tool demos. 

Just the frameworks, decisions, and practical tactics founders need to build a more repeatable pipeline.

Best fit for founders with paying customers who are ready to build a more structured GTM engine but are not yet ready to hire a full-time CMO.
