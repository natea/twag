## Description

High-growth companies rarely stall because of lack of talent or opportunity. More often, momentum slows because decisions stay psychologically open—creating hidden drag across teams, priorities, and execution. This session reveals the often-invisible leadership bottleneck that causes capable organizations to loop, delay, or split responsibility at critical moments.

In this talk, executive decision strategist Kim Groshek introduces the concept of UN-DECISION and the Decision Containment framework—showing how leaders restore clarity, reclaim authority, and create calm momentum across their organizations. You’ll leave with a practical lens for identifying decision drag in real time and a clear method for closing loops so your team can move forward with confidence.

Perfect for founders, executives, and operators navigating growth, transition, or complex decision environments. Expect sharp insight, practical frameworks, and a new way to think about leadership momentum.
