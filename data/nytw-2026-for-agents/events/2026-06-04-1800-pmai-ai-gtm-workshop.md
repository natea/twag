## Description

Scaling Intelligence: The AI-Native GTM Workshop
Moving Beyond "Spray and Pray": Paid Acquisition for Seed to Series A Founders.

​The traditional GTM playbook needs updating. In 2026, winning the attention of enterprise buyers and consumers requires more than a standard ad account. An autonomous growth engine perhaps?

​This workshop is designed for the founders or growth leads who views marketing as a data-driven problem. Join us for an intensive, in-person GTM deep dive specifically for AI startups. We’re getting into the weeds of how to leverage AI-powered paid acquisition to scale from initial traction to Series A.

​What we’re covering:

​The AI-Native Stack: Practical setup for automated creative testing and real-time bid optimization.

​Measurement that Matters: Moving from vanity metrics to "Cost Per Inference" and predictive LTV.

​The Trapdoor: Common pitfalls that bleed Seed-stage budgets (and how to avoid them).

​Live Audit: Bring your current CAC data, we’ll break down 2-3 accounts live in the room.

​Who Should Attend

​Seed to Series A Founders: Who are ready to move from founder-led sales to a scalable, automated engine.

​Growth Leads & Operators: Those responsible for hitting CAC targets and scaling performance budgets.

​Technical Product Managers: Who want to understand how growth loops should be integrated directly into the AI product architecture.

​VC Platform Leads: Looking for tactical playbooks to bring back to their portfolio companies.
