## Description

On Wednesday, June 3, WeWard and RevenueCat will co-host an intimate, invite-only dinner in the heart of Flatiron, bringing together 20–25 leaders across the mobile app ecosystem for meaningful connection and candid conversation. The curated guest list will include a mix of C-suite, product, growth, and tech executives from leading mobile app companies.

Hosted over a thoughtfully curated three-course dinner, the experience will be structured to spark high-value dialogue. Seating rotates between each course, giving every guest the opportunity to connect with new peers, exchange insights, and build relationships beyond surface-level networking.

During the evening, diners will also hear from a distinguished industry guest speaker who will share insights on leveraging AI for scaling, retention, product innovation, and navigating today’s technology ecosystem.
