## Description

Step away from the rush of Tech Week and into a room designed for better conversations.

Join Tea Drunk for Steeped in Tech, a tea reception for founders, investors, builders, and curious minds interested in the intersection of AI, culture, education, and the senses. Over exceptional Chinese top-terroir teas brewed gong fu style, we’ll gather for thoughtful networking, guided tasting, and open conversation around one central question:

"What role can technology play in helping us understand, teach, and articulate deeply human experiences — like taste, aroma, and sensory memory?"

Tea Drunk comes to this conversation with curiosity rather than conclusions. As a traditional craft business rooted in sensory education, cultural heritage, and human connection, we’re using this evening as a living case study: how might AI and emerging tools support growth, learning, and discovery without flattening what makes the experience meaningful?

Expect beautiful tea, light conversation prompts, and plenty of space to meet founders, investors, operators, AI builders, product thinkers, educators, and fellow sensory nerds.

This is a different kind of Tech Week room: slower, more human, and more memorable.

No alcohol. No hard pitch. Just exceptional tea, sharp minds, and real conversation.
