## Description

Billions of people are asking ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini before before making a buying decision. The brands showing up in those answers aren't there by accident, and the work it takes to get there looks very different from traditional SEO.

Join Goodie for an exclusive evening on what's actually working in AI Search right now, broken into three focused sessions:
- Agentic Commerce: what brand visibility looks like when the buyer is an AI agent, not a human, and what determines who gets picked.
- Social as Infrastructure for AI Search: why Reddit, LinkedIn, and YouTube have quietly become some of the most-cited sources in LLM answers, and how to show up there.
- How to Win AEO/GEO Today: the signals, content moves, and technical fundamentals driving citations across ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude and Google's AI Overviews.

We're also bringing in a curated lineup of mystery guests: operators driving AI Search visibility at some of the most recognizable brands in tech, retail, and media. Names revealed closer to the event.

Complimentary food and drinks courtesy of Carbone and Levain Bakery.

Designed for founders, search nerds, senior marketing, comms, PR, and growth leaders who want to leave with a clear, current view of how AI Search works, and what to do Monday morning. Space is limited; registrations will be reviewed to ensure a relevant peer group.
