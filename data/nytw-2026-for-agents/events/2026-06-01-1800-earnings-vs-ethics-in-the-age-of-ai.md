## Description

The Fordham AI Business Hub presents Earnings vs. Ethics in the Age of AI, a NY Tech Week panel bringing together senior leaders from venture capital, the startup ecosystem, corporate America, law, and academia to explore the defining trade-offs of the AI era: how organizations balance speed and accountability, shareholder returns and societal impact, competitive advantage and the public good.

Networking reception with a selection of appetizers, empanadas and signature cocktail to follow the panel discussion. 

--
Featured speakers include:

Anthony Ferrante, Senior Managing Director, Global Head of Cybersecurity, FTI Consulting | Former Director for Cyber Incident Response at U.S. National Security Council and Chief of Staff for FBI's Cyber Division

Dana Rao, Former General Counsel and Executive Vice President, Adobe | Former IP General Counsel, Microsoft 

Lerzan Aksoy, Dean and George N. Jean Ph.D. Chair, Fordham Gabelli School of Business

--
The Fordham AI Business Hub is New York City’s new home for ethical, applied AI grounded in discipline-specific expertise, academic rigor, and experiential learning. The Hub connects companies, entrepreneurs, policymakers, students, and community partners to harness AI responsibly for growth, innovation, and meaningful impact.

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
