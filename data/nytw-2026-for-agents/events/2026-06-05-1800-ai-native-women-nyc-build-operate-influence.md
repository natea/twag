A curated gathering for women becoming AI-native — founders, operators, and creators learning how to turn AI from a tool into a system for work, products, careers, and influence.

​In every category — operations, marketing, design, product — the women who go AI-native this year will hold leverage for the next ten.

​Capital, careers, and influence are moving toward the people who can credibly say "I am confident with AI."

​It's not a future shift. It's happening this quarter.

​The men in this industry already have their AI rooms.

​Just real founders and operators sharing what they actually use, what's working, what's not, and how to skip the embarrassment of asking questions in public.
