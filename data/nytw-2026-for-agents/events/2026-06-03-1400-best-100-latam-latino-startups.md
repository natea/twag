## Description

For the first time ever, we're mapping the 100 best early-stage startups led by Latinos — 50 from the US and 50 from Latin America — into one unified list.

This is a small, curated ceremony at Soho House to unveil the report and celebrate the founders who made the list. We'll showcase the Best 100 mapping, spotlight the startups, and bring together the investors and ecosystem leaders who helped make it happen.

After the ceremony, we head straight to the Founder Speakeasy — keep the night going.
