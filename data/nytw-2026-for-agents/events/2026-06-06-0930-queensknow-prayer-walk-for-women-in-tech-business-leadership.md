## Description

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com

QueensKnow for a faith-filled morning of reflection, connection, and community during Tech Week New York.

This prayer walk is for women in tech, business, leadership, and creative industries who want to begin the day grounded, intentional, and connected. We’ll gather at Battery Park for a peaceful walk, meaningful conversation, and a moment to reset before heading into the day.

After the walk, we’ll continue with Coffee & Conversations for Women Building in New York — a casual space to connect with inspiring women building purpose-driven lives, brands, businesses, and careers.

Come as you are. Leave refreshed.
