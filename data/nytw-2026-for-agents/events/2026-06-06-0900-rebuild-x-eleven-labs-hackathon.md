## Description

**IN PERSON ONLY**

Rebuild is teaming up with ElevenLabs for a full-day hackathon where the challenge is simple: make something kick-ass. Whether that means a talking AI assistant, an audio-driven app, a voice-powered game, or something nobody has thought of yet, the weirder and more ambitious the better.

Every participant gets (FOR FREE)
- 1 month of ElevenLabs access
- Rebuild notebook/sticket
- breakfast/lunch/dinner/snacks/drinks

Prizes:

- 3 months Pro tier of 11 Labs (~$300 value)

Eleven Labs Best overall project
- 6 months scale tier (~$2k value)



Fuel is covered all day: breakfast, lunch, and dinner are all on us, so you can keep your head down and keep building without worrying about anything other than shipping something awesome. 

- Bagels and coffee in the morning
- Sandwiches for lunch
- Pizza for dinner
- Drinks and snacks all day long (energy drinks, beer, pop, water)

The event runs for a full day, and teams will have time to build, test, and put the finishing touches on their projects before presenting.

Whether you come in with a fully formed idea or just a vague feeling that voice interfaces are underrated, you are welcome here. Register, show up, eat good food, and build something worth talking about.
