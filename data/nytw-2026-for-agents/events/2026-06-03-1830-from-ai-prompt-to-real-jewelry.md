## Description

What happens when AI moves beyond the screen and into the physical world?

Join founders, creators, artists, designers, and investors during NY Tech Week for an interactive evening exploring the future of AI-powered creation through jewelry, fashion, and personal expression.

Hosted by Skyveil AI, the company building AI tools for physical creation, the evening will feature live experiences with Aura Matter, Skyveil’s consumer platform that allows anyone to design custom jewelry using AI, no design background required.

Throughout the night, guests will be able to try Aura Matter, generating personalized jewelry concepts in real time and seeing how ideas and prompts can become real pieces.

The evening will feature:
• Hands-on AI jewelry creation experiences  
• Live demos of Aura Matter by Skyveil AI  
• Conversations around AI, creativity, fashion, and the future of personalization  
• Curated networking with founders, creatives, and the NYC tech community  
• Drinks, light bites, and beautiful people building cool things ✨

Guests will also have the opportunity to participate in a live AI jewelry design challenge. The winning concept of the night will receive a $2,000 creation coupon toward producing a fully custom jewelry piece.

Whether you work in AI, consumer tech, fashion, design, or simply want to experience the next generation of creative tools, we’d love to have you there.
