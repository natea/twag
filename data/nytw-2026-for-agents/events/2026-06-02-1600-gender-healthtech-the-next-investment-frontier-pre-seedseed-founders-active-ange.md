## Description

Technology built to improve access, delivery, financing, and outcomes across women's and LGBTQ+ health is one of the most undercapitalized and highest-potential sectors in healthcare today.  This invitation-only salon brings together the founders building it, the investors funding it, and the health system and healthcare leaders who are deciding where it gets adopted next.

We'll skip the awareness conversation and get into the work: where the 2026 market opportunity actually is, where capital is moving and where it's stuck, and how founders, angels, VCs, payers, and healthcare leaders can stop operating in silos and start coordinating. Then we'll keep the conversation going, over structured networking and a salsa class.

Our afternoon together will look like this:
• Panel (30 min): CEOs and founders of leading gender healthtech companies in conversation with active investors, sharing what it actually takes to build and fund category-defining companies & hearing about investors' scars and triumphs in this space 
• Structured networking (30 min): A curated room of partner-level VCs, active angels with recent deployment history, and executives from ecosystem partners designed to make introductions that matter
