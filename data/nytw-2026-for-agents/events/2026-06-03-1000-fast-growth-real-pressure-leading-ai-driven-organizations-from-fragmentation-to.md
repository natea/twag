## Description

Fast Growth, Real Pressure: Leading AI-Driven Organizations to Alignment and Performance

AI is accelerating everything: product cycles, expectations, growth and competition. The pressure to grow fast and deliver results is constant.

But here’s the reality:
Most companies don’t break because of bad strategy. They break because their teams can’t stay aligned as they scale up.

As organizations grow, execution slows, priorities clash, and leaders become bottlenecks. What looks like a technical problem is often a leadership one.

In this interactive workshop, Ashveena Gajeelee (Founder, The Adaptive Leader; Harvard-trained leadership educator) will help you see what’s really happening inside your organization and what to do about it.

Through real-world tech scenarios, you’ll learn how to:
- Identify the unseen obstacles that emerge with fast growth
- Navigate resistance, misalignment, and competing priorities
- Lead under pressure: when authority, data, or AI alone aren’t enough

Built for founders, product leaders, and VCs navigating high-velocity environments where performance must hold, even without clear answers.

If your organization is growing but execution is getting harder, this session will show you why and how to fix it.
