## Description

🌸 Picnic Social Sunday, June 7th. Part of NYC Tech Week :) experiential event! 

​"A social for burnt out people who want to romanticize life again, a tech and non-tech community picnic"-Ruben

​🌸 Why: I choose to be relentlessly kind and ruthlessly compassionate, that is why I fight to make this simple moment happen.

​🌸 Where: NYC° at Pepsi cola Sign Park - By the East River. Overlooking the views of Manhattan.

​🌸 When: Social picnic starts at 1PM until 8PM (I'll be there early to setup for anyone who wants to help too).

​🌸 What: This is a community event that is free to attend! I will be bringing Mimosas and tea to serve, along with a picnic setup including low picnic tables and blankets (Donations accepted to help cover costs of tea and mimosas).

​🌸 Who: For you silly! The burnt out toast. The tech people who are losing their jobs and need a break. People looking for support and even someone to listen to them, in this crazy world. For the person who just craves something simple, fun.

🌸 optional: feel free to bring a picnic dish too! I’ll be bringing basics like tea, mimosas, crackers, charcuterie, fruit. More of those or any shareable items are welcome!
