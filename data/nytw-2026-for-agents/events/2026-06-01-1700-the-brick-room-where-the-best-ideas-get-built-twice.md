## Description

You've pitched your idea a hundred times.

But have you ever built it?

The Brick Room is a one-night-only experience during NYC Tech Week where founders, investors, and builders come together to connect in a way that no rooftop happy hour ever pulled off.
Using LEGO® Serious Play® - a research-backed methodology trusted by teams at Google, Spotify, and NASA - you'll build with your hands, share what you made, and meet the people in the room on a level that actually means something. No slides. No stages. No "so what do you do?"

Just bricks, builders, and conversations worth having.

Here's how the evening flows:

🧱 Walk in. Find your seat at the table. Get your hands on some LEGOs.
🧱 Build. Respond to facilitated prompts that reveal how you think, not just what you do.
🧱 Share. Your build tells your story better than any elevator pitch ever could.
🧱 Connect. Founders meet investors who are actually curious. Investors meet founders who are actually building.

This isn't a networking event with a gimmick. It's a different kind of room — intentionally small, carefully curated, and genuinely unlike anything else happening at Tech Week this June.

Space is limited. If you're here, you're meant to be here.

Hosted by Saad Bin Tariq, certified LEGO® Serious Play® facilitator and Founder of Fine Line Consultants - the experiential learning studio behind programs for Google, Spotify, RO and Rolls-Royce.

The Brick Room is an official NYC Tech Week 2026 event.

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
