## Description

Prediction markets are often seen as niche financial tools. We think they are the foundation of a new intelligence layer.

This session explores how prediction markets can evolve into predictive intelligence systems, where markets generate signals, agents interpret them, and liquidity enables real-world execution.

Hosted by Daedalus Research, we’ll share early prototypes and ideas at the intersection of markets, liquidity, and infrastructure.

The session will include short talks, demos, and open discussion with builders working on AI, crypto, and decision systems.
