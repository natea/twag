## Description

Taiwan Founder Night brings together Taiwan-linked founders, investors, operators, and ecosystem partners for a curated networking experience built around real conversations, not pitch decks. 

Most startup events make founders perform while everyone else sits and listens. Taiwan Founder Night is designed differently: we give guests just enough context to know who is in the room, then create the space for meaningful introductions to happen naturally.

The evening will open with remarks from Taipei Economic and Cultural Office (TECO) in NY and LANDED, followed by a concise look at Taiwan’s startup ecosystem and why Taiwan is increasingly becoming a world-class startup lab: rapid prototyping, advanced manufacturing, AIoT hardware-software integration, deep tech talent, and critical supply-chain capabilities.

We will feature startups in our event catalogue. At the start of the event, each featured startup will join a short Roll Call, where they introduce what they are building and who they hope to meet. No long pitches. No panels. No demo-day. Just enough signal for meaningful connections.

The rest of the night is dedicated to networking, giving founders visibility while keeping the focus on connection. Guests will also have access to a featured startup catalogue, making it easier to discover companies, follow up after the event, and make the right introductions.
