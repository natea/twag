## Description

Vibe Code & Sip is a guided, beginner-friendly building experience where creativity, community, and AI come together.

Think paint and sip, but instead of leaving with a canvas, you leave with your first app, prototype, or product idea brought to life.

Whether you’re a founder, creator, student, small business owner, or just AI-curious, this session is designed to help you learn how to use AI tools to turn ideas into something real. No coding background required.

We’ll walk through the basics of vibe coding, help you shape your idea, guide you through the building process, and create space for you to connect with other builders over drinks, conversation, and good energy.

Come with an idea or come with curiosity. Either way, you’ll leave with more confidence, more clarity, and something you actually built.
