## Description

Interwoven Ventures, Runway Growth Capital, and Alpha Partners are excited to invite you to the 3rd Annual VC Rooftop Happy Hour. Join us for an evening of drinks, networking, and great conversations with our VC NYC ecosystem.  

Interwoven Ventures is an early stage venture fund investing in the field of robotics and AI across multiple industries including manufacturing, logistics, transportation and healthcare.  

Runway Growth Capital provides loans of $30M to $150M to late and growth stage companies as a complement, supplement, or alternative to equity financing.

Alpha Partners invests in growth-stage technology companies with over $10 million in annual revenue, assisting early-stage investors and VCs in exercising their pro-rata rights while sharing carry in bite-sized investments ranging from $3 million to $20 million. 

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
