## Description

To close out the day during NYC Tech Week, Pollen8 and Die-Hard are hosting a session on storytelling as a competitive advantage for startups.

In a world where attention is everything, the startups that stand out are the ones that know how to tell a story people want to follow—and be part of.

This session explores how founders craft narratives that attract users, build trust, and create momentum over time. We’ll break down how storytelling drives distribution, how consistency compounds attention, and how to turn everyday progress into content that resonates.

The panel will feature founders and creatives who are actively building in public and using storytelling as part of their growth engine.

After the discussion, the event transitions into a more relaxed, social networking environment—bringing together builders, creatives, and operators for meaningful connections.

Light bites and drinks will be served.
