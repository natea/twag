## Description

Building a company is hard.
Turning it into real wealth is even harder.

During NYC Tech Week, we’re bringing together a small group of founders, operators, and builders for a candid conversation on what actually happens between scaling and exit—and what most people get wrong along the way.

This is not a surface-level panel.

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.

We’ll cover:
• What changes between Series A → B → Exit
• Mistakes founders make with equity, taxes, and liquidity
• How to think about personal wealth while still building
• What operators wish they did earlier

Featuring:
– A current Series B founder in the trenches
– A founder who has successfully exited
– Advanced planning specialist from UBS

Expect a high-signal room, honest conversation, and real connections.
