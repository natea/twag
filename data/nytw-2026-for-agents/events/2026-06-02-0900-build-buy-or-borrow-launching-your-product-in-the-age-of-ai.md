## Description

A 30-Minute Panel + Networking at Tavern Community Coworking.
As a founder, you're constantly deciding what to do yourself, what to hire for, and what to buy or build a tool for. But AI is changing that equation—fast.
Entire categories of work that used to require a hire, an agency, or a vendor can now be handled by AI tools. At the same time, the need for human judgment is getting higher, not lower. There’s a critical difference between outsourcing a task and outsourcing judgment. 
The risk isn’t just delegating work—it’s losing control of decisions that define your company. Get it wrong and you're either burning cash or burning out. 
This panel is about getting it right.
We'll dig into how to figure out which parts of your business need you, which are better handed off to an external specialist, and which problems already have a tech solution waiting for you. This discussion will help you move faster without losing control of the things that actually matter.
We're bringing together founders who've been in the weeds, operators who've built the systems, vendors who sell the solutions, and investors who've watched it play out to give you insights from experience you can use for your own discernment.  
After the panel, stick around for bagels and Coffee and to connect with fellow founders, potential partners and investors.
