## Description

Modus Salons are intimate, invite-only dinner events designed to gather senior leaders to network, learn about industry trends, and build community in this era of rapid technological change.

As AI evolves from chat interfaces to agentic systems that can reason, recommend, and act on our behalf, the role of digital experience is fundamentally changing. Instead of simply helping people find information, intelligent systems are increasingly helping them choose.

Join leaders from Amazon and Google DeepMind for a conversation on the emerging discipline of agentic design—how AI advisors are reshaping commerce, decision-making, and the relationship between brands and customers.

We’ll explore what happens when AI becomes a collaborator in complex choices, what it takes to design systems people trust, and how companies can remain indispensable as agents begin mediating the customer journey.

Featuring:
Matt Dellinger, Principal Conversation UX Design, Amazon (Rufus)
Nerjada Maksutaj Oflazer, Product Management, Google DeepMind

Hosted by Kristen Kulenych, Chief Experience Officer, Modus Digital.

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
