## Description

You don't need to be an AI expert to walk through this door. You just need to show up.

This is a speed networking event for women in tech who are curious about AI — whether you've never touched a tool or use one every day. We're bringing together women across industries for an evening of real conversations, purposeful connections, and light bites.

After you RSVP, you'll answer 5 quick questions — takes under 2 minutes. Your responses shape the conversation rounds so every pairing is intentional and no one feels out of their depth. On the night, you'll rotate through guided rounds, each with someone new and a fresh conversation starter. Come with what you know. Leave with what you didn't.
