## Description

Sponsored by Havas

Infinite Impact Lab is a hands-on workshop for professionals, operators, and team leaders who are doing meaningful work but struggling to clearly explain the value they create.

This workshop is for you at any stage of your career.

Whether you are getting back into the job market after a layoff, preparing for a promotion, trying to get stronger in your current role, pivoting into a new role or industry, returning after a career break, or getting ready for performance reviews and salary conversations, one thing is true:

Being capable is not enough if your impact is hard to see.

Many professionals are doing the work, solving problems, supporting teams, improving processes, driving outcomes, and creating value every day. But when it is time to talk about that work, they default to tasks:

“I manage projects.”
“I support customers.”
“I run reports.”
“I lead meetings.”
“I handle operations.”

The problem is, tasks do not always tell the full story.

Infinite Impact Lab helps attendees turn everyday execution into clear, measurable business value.

In this session, attendees will use GTM’s framework — Task → Skill → Impact → Proof → Transfer — alongside AI to uncover hidden skills, sharpen outcome language, identify stronger KPIs, and build proof around the work they already do.

Attendees will learn how to:

Translate daily work into strategic skill language
Use AI to strengthen impact statements and surface measurable proof
Connect execution to outcomes leaders, hiring teams, managers, and clients actually recognize
Identify transferable skills for promotions, pivots, interviews, and internal growth
Build an Impact Portfolio they can use for interviews, performance reviews, promotion conversations, salary conversations, career pivots, and leadership opportunities

This is not another “build your personal brand” talk.

It is a practical working session for people who need better language, stronger proof, and a clearer way to explain why their work matters.

Attendees leave with tangible outputs, stronger positioning, and a repeatable system for making their contribution undeniable in an economy where AI is changing how work is evaluated, communicated, and rewarded.
