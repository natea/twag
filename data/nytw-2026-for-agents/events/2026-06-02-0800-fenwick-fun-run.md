## Description

Start your day on the move with a casual 5K designed to be as welcoming as it is energizing. Whether you’re race-ready, an avid runner, or a light jogger, all paces are encouraged. This is about getting outside and starting the day together, not setting personal records.

Our run will begin and end in the Flatiron District. After the run, we’ll gather for a healthy breakfast for networking and conversation. Connect with fellow founders, investors, and operators over great food and even better conversation in a relaxed, post-run setting. Come solo or increase the fun by bringing a colleague!

The run will be led by professional running coaches Matt and Sammy McClintock!
