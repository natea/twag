## Description

The Low Latency Club is heading to NY TechWeek. Telnyx and Deepgram are teaming up for an evening of real conversations around voice AI with the people who are building it.

We're bringing together devs, founders, and product teams shipping voice AI in production, and creating space to share what's working, what's not, and what's been harder than expected.

Voice systems are more than speech. They're a chain of moving parts:

– Capturing audio
– Transcribing in real time
– Making decisions
– Responding fast enough to feel natural

Getting that full loop to work reliably is where things get interesting. That's what the evening is about.

What to expect: Cold drinks, great food, and time to connect. No pressure, no pitches. Just a room full of devs, founders, and product teams solving similar problems.

Why come? Most events talk about what voice AI could be. We show you what it actually looks like running.

Oh, and one of you is leaving with a brand new Mac Mini!!

Who should come– CEOs and founders building or scaling voice AI products– Developers building voice agents– Engineers working on real-time systems– Product teams exploring voice interfaces– Anyone curious how voice AI works beyond demos

Meet people solving the same problems. Leave with a clear sense of how to build and what to watch out for.

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
