## Description

When an AI agent makes the purchase for the customer, does your brand make the cut? 

AI is no longer just influencing discovery it’s starting to make decisions. As AI agents increasingly search, evaluate, and purchase on behalf of consumers, brands face a new question: will you even show up when it matters? 

Join us for an evening of drinks, light bites, and an interactive panel where we unpack how agentic commerce is reshaping visibility, preference, and ultimately revenue and what you can do today to stay ahead.
