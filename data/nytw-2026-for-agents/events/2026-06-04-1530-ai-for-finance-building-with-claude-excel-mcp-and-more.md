## Description

Finance leaders know they need to adopt AI but most don’t know where to start.

The space is filled with either hype or overly technical deep dives. This is neither.

Join us for a practical, hands-on working session where finance teams will learn how to actually apply AI to their day-to-day workflows. From forecasting to reporting to decision support, we will break down real use cases and show how to implement them using modern AI tools, agents, and workflows.

Bring your laptop. Bring your real problems.
We will build solutions together.

This is an interactive session, not a panel, where you will work alongside other operators to identify opportunities, test ideas, and walk away with workflows you can apply immediately.

Hosted by Pluvo & BMO, this session is designed to help finance teams cut manual work, move faster, and start operating in an AI-first way.
