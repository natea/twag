## Description

New York's primaries are heating up. 🔥

Sway (sway.co) is a voting group platform that shifts influence into actual votes. Sway helps people organize to become as powerful as unions and lobbyists. You can join groups on different topics — or start your own. At election time, you’ll get a voter guide from the groups you’re in. The larger a group, the more influence it will have with elected officials who want your votes.

Join us for drinks, bites, and spicy conversations about the digital organizing in the midterms and beyond.
