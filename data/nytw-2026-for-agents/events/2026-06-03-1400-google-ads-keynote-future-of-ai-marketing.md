## Description

Founders today are wearing multiple hats. The last thing you have time for is worrying about the day-to-day minutiae of digital marketing—but you still need to master user acquisition, and Google Ads plays a critical role in that growth. 

Join Google Managing Director Alicia Carey for an exclusive session that serves as your look into the future of search.  We will unpack exactly what you need to do as a startup to leverage the "AI Essentials"—the must-have technologies and solutions required to capture demand, stay visible in LLM-driven queries, and accelerate growth. Alicia will show you how to use AI tools that actively free up your time, from launching entire AI-driven campaigns to automated creative asset generation. 

Come learn how to implement these innovations as an extension of your team, so you can automate the heavy lifting and get back to building your company.

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
