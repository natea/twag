## Description

Hiring isn’t the problem — making the final decision is.

As teams scale, signals start conflicting across interviewers. The challenge is no longer collecting information — it’s structuring decisions under pressure.

This session explores how high-performing teams surface tradeoffs, align on priorities, and make clearer high-stakes hiring decisions.

We’ll cover:
• Why disagreement is often a sign of hidden tradeoffs
• How strong teams structure decisions under pressure
• A live breakdown of competing candidates and decision alignment
• The emerging category of “decision infrastructure”

~45 min session + optional Q&A

For founders, operators, and hiring managers scaling teams.
