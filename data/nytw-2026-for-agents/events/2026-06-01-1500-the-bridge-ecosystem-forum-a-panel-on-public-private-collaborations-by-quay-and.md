## Description

The Bridge is a curated bi-monthly strategic forum where corporate innovation leaders and government officials align priorities, remove blockers, and build a thriving innovation economy together - converting ecosystem connections into regional growth. Every seat at the table belongs to a strategic thinker and ecosystem builder—a leader with a track record of getting things done who is focused on understanding collaboration models, how decisions get made, and designing shared investment to cultivate warm relationships for when opportunities naturally arise.

For Tech Week this year, we are excited to host a broader gathering bringing together corporate innovation leaders, government officials, investors and founders working on ventures aligned with The Bridge’s mission. The event will feature a panel conversation on How the Public and Private Sectors Can Effectively Collaborate to Drive Innovation, followed by a networking reception.
