## Description

Walk into Tech Week knowing exactly how to raise and what to do next.

Seed & Pre-Seed Founders Breakfast
June 1 | Morning

A curated breakfast for early-stage founders who are actively raising or about to.

This is a high-intent room focused on helping you understand how investors are actually making decisions right now and what you should do next.

Free catered breakfast included.

What to expect

We’re bringing together seed and pre-seed founders, along with a group of active investors and accelerator leads, for a focused, conversation-driven session.

No panels. No presentations. Just real discussion, data, and direct feedback.

How it works

Curated Roundtable Conversations

- What investors are responding to right now
- Why deals are getting traction or getting ignored
- How to adjust your positioning and outreach

Live Discussion + Feedback

- Founders share where they are in their raise
- Investors and operators give direct input
- Open conversation across the room

Raisi Data Snapshot

- Key patterns from millions of live founder-investor interactions
- What’s actually working across outreach, meetings, and conversion

What you get

- A clear next step for your raise
- A curated room of founders and investors worth knowing
- Raisi data snapshot handout
- Free strategy session offered to attendees
- Entry into a raffle for a free month of Raisi (for founders who book a follow-up)

Who’s in the room

- Seed and pre-seed founders
- A small group of active seed investors
- Accelerator and ecosystem leads

This is the starting point for the week. You walk in early, get clarity, and carry that into every conversation that follows.

About Raisi: 
Raisi is a fundraising operating system built to help founders run their raise end to end. From identifying the right investors to executing outreach and managing conversations, it replaces fragmented fundraising workflows with one system.

Since 2024, once every 50.1 minutes, a founder has met with an investor interested in their company through Raisi. The platform has facilitated 21,000+ positive connections between founders and investors and is powered by up-to-date signals from millions of live interactions across the private markets.

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
