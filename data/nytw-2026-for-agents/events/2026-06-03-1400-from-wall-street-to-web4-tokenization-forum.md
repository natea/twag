## Description

Description

As capital markets evolve, the lines between traditional finance, blockchain infrastructure, and artificial intelligence are beginning to blur. Tokenization, stablecoins, and programmable financial systems are transforming how assets are issued, traded, and managed — laying the foundation for what many are beginning to call Web4.

During NY Tech Week, CoinBridge will host an evening forum exploring the transition from Wall Street to Web4, and what it means for the future of global capital markets.

The discussion will bring together investors, founders, and financial professionals to examine how tokenized assets, on-chain infrastructure, and emerging AI-driven systems could reshape the next generation of financial networks.

As a platform focused on bridging traditional finance with on-chain economies, CoinBridge works with institutions and asset issuers to bring real-world assets and capital flows onto blockchain infrastructure, helping build the foundations of next-generation financial markets.

The evening will feature a short panel discussion followed by networking with professionals across finance, Web3, and AI ecosystems.

Space is limited. RSVP required.
