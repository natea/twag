## Description

Founder Idol: One Minute. One Shot.

Apply now by joining the waitlist for your chance to pitch during NY Techweek 2026.

Can you win the crowd and the judges in just 60 seconds?

Founder Idol is the ultimate rapid-fire pitch event where ambitious founders take the stage during NY Techweek 2026 to deliver one-minute pitches to a live audience and a panel of four experienced judges—without a pitch deck or any visual aids.

This is not a demo day. It’s a test of clarity, charisma, and conviction. Whether you're pitching or attending, Founder Idol is an unmissable evening of startup energy and sharp ideas.

This is a highly limited & selective event: you must be accepted off the waitlist to attend. If you are seeking to pitch and you are accepted, you will be expected to attend. Do not join the waitlist if you cannot make it!

This event is a preliminary version of what is set to be a much larger production format in the near future. Joining now will put you on the best track to be involved in the evolution of Founder Idol as it grows!

Event Format:

10-15 founders pitch their startup or idea in a one-minute slot
A panel of expert judges provides fast, honest feedback
The audience votes for their favorite pitch
Judges select a standout winner based on clarity, originality, and potential
