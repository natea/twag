## Description

CREWASIS presents: Human in the Loop in the age of Agentic AI
https://crewasis.ai/

Cindy Cook, Chair of the New York Angels will be providing an interesting perspective on how Agentic AI investments are being looked at from a preseed/seed standpoint.  

Kelsey Deans will provide a perspective on implementing Agentic AI solutions in enterprise companies.

Sharon Joseph, CEO of CREWASIS, will provide a perspective in building an AI native company and adding in agentic AI. CREWASIS is a Techstars company.

Agentic AI has made it trivial to build.
It has made it effortless to generate.
It has made it faster than ever to launch and optimize.

It has not made it easier to stand out.
It has not made it easier to earn trust.

Enterprise teams are now operating in a sea of Agentic tools, each promising automation, each claiming intelligence.

But most are producing more noise, not better decisions.

So the real question is not how to use AI Agents and test them for success.

It’s how to use Agentic AI to actually improve marketing outcomes inside enterprise environments.

This session brings together operators and investors at the front lines of that shift to unpack what’s working, what’s not, and where human judgment still creates disproportionate advantage. What's hot in the space and working? What's not?

If you’re a B2B SaaS founder, investor, or enterprise operator navigating Agentic AI in your go-to-market, this conversation is designed for you.

We will be discussing how marketers, researchers, digital execs, and digital agencies are using Agentic AI in Enterprise companies. We'll also dig into how investors are looking at Martech AI companies and what differentiates them.
