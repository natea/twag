## Description

What is Actually Driving Growth in Sports Right Now

About the Event
Sports is evolving faster than ever but what is real vs. hype? From the rise of womens sports to the expansion of betting and digital platforms, join us for a conversation on where the biggest opportunities are emerging and how builders can position themselves to win. Our speakers bring decades of experience across sports betting, corporate strategy, and venture backed growth to break down what is actually driving the next wave.

Schedule
4:30 to 5pm: Guests Arrive
5 to 6pm: Panel
6 to 7:30pm: Networking Hour
Light refreshments will be served.

Presented by Laces
This event is presented by Laces, a sports education platform teaching women, casual fans, and beginners how to understand and enjoy sports through bite sized, judgment free learning. Join the waitlist and learn more at lacessports.com.

Panelists
- Yashreeka Z. Huq is a Partner at Perkins Coie, where she counsels technology and life sciences companies on corporate governance, venture capital financings, and complex mergers, acquisitions, and restructurings. She advises both public and private companies with an emphasis on early stage ventures and their investors.

- Jamie Shea is a senior iGaming and sports betting executive with more than 30 years of experience leading operations, VIP strategy, and growth across top real money gaming brands. She played a pivotal leadership role in building and scaling the sports betting business at DraftKings and now serves as Executive Vice President at SimWin, overseeing operations, monetization, and player lifecycle for the company's virtual sports ecosystem.

Moderator
- Kamini Manivannan is the CEO and Founder of Laces Sports, a sports education platform teaching women, casual fans, and beginners how to understand and enjoy sports through bite sized, judgment free learning. She previously served as a Data Science Manager at the NFL focused on fan engagement, with additional experience at the USTA and Brooklyn Nets, and holds an MS in Data Science from Northwestern University. Kamini is on the Board of Directors for the Fantasy Sports and Gaming Association, and was recently selected for TechRound's SportsTech22 for 2026. 

From the rise of women’s sports to the expansion of betting and digital platforms, we’ll cover where the biggest opportunities are emerging and how builders can position themselves to win. If you're a founder or operator looking to build in sports or just trying to understand where the industry is heading, you don't want to miss this!
