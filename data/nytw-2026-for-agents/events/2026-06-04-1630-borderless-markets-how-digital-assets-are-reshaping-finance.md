## Description

For decades, the most lucrative corners of financial markets have been gated by geography, regulation, and legacy infrastructure. Today, tokenization is opening access to private markets, stablecoins are reshaping the global payments stack, and alternative assets are moving firmly into the mainstream. Join industry leaders and infrastructure builders for a timely discussion on how digital assets are transforming the future of investing, capital formation, and financial access worldwide.
