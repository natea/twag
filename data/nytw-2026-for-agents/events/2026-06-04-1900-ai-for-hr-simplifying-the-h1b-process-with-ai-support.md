## Description

Immigration strategy shouldn't be opaque. But for most HR teams, it is. You submit paperwork, wait months for status updates, have no visibility into where things stand or what your options actually are. Meanwhile, your hiring managers are frustrated, your candidates are confused, and you're explaining delays you don't fully understand yourself. Manifest Legal built a tool to change that. They track H1B statuses in real time and, more importantly, they surface the alternative pathways available to you - the visa options, the timing strategies, the creative solutions that most HR teams never even know exist. This dinner brings together HR leaders who are ready to stop guessing and start understanding what immigration actually looks like for their organizations.

We'll talk through how visibility into your pipeline changes everything. What alternatives exist when H1B doesn't work. How to plan hiring strategy around visa timelines instead of being surprised by them. What conversations to have with your legal team, your hiring managers, and your candidates when you actually understand the landscape. Manifest will walk through their tool - how it tracks, how it communicates, how it opens up options. And we'll talk honestly about what this means for your organization's ability to compete for global talent.

You'll leave knowing what you're actually working with, what options you've been missing, and how to move immigration from a compliance headache to a strategic lever in your hiring. And you'll do it over dinner with HR leaders dealing with the exact same friction.
