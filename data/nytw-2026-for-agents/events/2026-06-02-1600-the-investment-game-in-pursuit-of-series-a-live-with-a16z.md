## Description

Which seed-stage founder will capture the heart—and conviction—of a top-tier investor?

At this live, dating game-inspired session, three seed-stage founders will take the hot seat to try and win the attention of Kenan Saleh, an a16z partner, and make the case for his (hypothetical) Series A backing.

Across multiple rounds, founders will respond to hot-button investment questions as Kenan evaluates first impressions, compatibility, and potential dealbreakers. After each round, he'll break down what’s sparking interest and what's raising red flags - all leading up to his final choice.

Along the way, the audience will weigh in on who’s making the strongest impression and who’s headed for a missed connection.

Seed-stage founders will leave with a clearer understanding of:
* What it takes to stand out in a competitive Series A market
* The most common gaps that hold seed-stage companies back
* How to tell a clearer, more compelling story to investors

Following the session, stick around and make your own connections over drinks, light bites, and relaxed networking.
