## Description

The best hospitality businesses are built on taste, instinct, and unreasonable attention to detail. The best AI products will be too.

Pull up a chair in La Cabra's reserved section for a casual morning with operators, builders, and investors working at that intersection. 

Free coffee, fresh cardamom buns (arguably the best in the city), and no agenda beyond meeting good people.

This is for hospitality operators, founders building AI for the physical world, and investors backing them.

Cohosted by:
- Ivan Romero | Founder @ Kintow 
- Avi Chad-Friedman | Founder @ Kintow 
- Megan Maloney | Founder & GP @ Dria Ventures 

Kintow automates back office workflows for restaurants: AI voice inventory counts, web browsing purchasing agents, and real-time visibility into food cost, labor, and cash flow. Built so one person can run ten locations.

Dria Ventures is a seed-stage fund backing technical teams bringing modern software to Main Street industries like food service, logistics, and auto repair.
