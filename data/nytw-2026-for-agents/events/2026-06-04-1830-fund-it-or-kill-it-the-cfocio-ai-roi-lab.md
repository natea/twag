## Description

AI ROI Lab is a hands-on workshop for CFOs, CIOs, and cross-functional leaders who need to decide where AI can create real business value - and where it won’t. Attendees will bring real workflows and pressure-test them live using a practical ROI framework focused on business value, implementation friction, repeatability, feasibility, and expected return.

This is not a panel or a theory session. It’s a live working lab for people who need to make the case, own the budget, evaluate implementation tradeoffs, or drive adoption across the business. Participants leave with a reusable framework to prioritize AI use cases with more rigor and less hype.

Hosted by Andra Vaduva, Founder and CEO of SafeSpace, a New York–based AI Adoption Lab with global clients, helping companies turn AI into practical workflows across operations, finance, and growth, in partnership with Catalin Briciu, CEO and Co-Founder of Linnify, a global technology partner that helps U.S. mid-market companies plan and execute digital and AI initiatives through proven, low-risk frameworks.

Together, they combine expertise in AI adoption and implementation to help U.S. mid-market companies move beyond the hype and deploy AI in ways that drive real, measurable business value.
