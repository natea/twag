About the Event

The 165 Global Launch Party is the marquee celebration of NYC Tech Week 2026, bringing together the city's most ambitious founders, investors, and innovators for an evening of meaningful connection, high energy, and community.

This is the official launch of 165 Global in New York, and we are doing it right. Expect a curated room of builders and backers who are shaping the next generation of companies, deals, and ideas. Whether you are raising your next round, deploying capital, or growing your network, this is the room you want to be in.

The evening will feature live performances from a violinist, saxophonist, and harpist, setting an elevated backdrop for open networking and the kind of conversations that turn into partnerships. The atmosphere is sophisticated, the energy is high, and every guest is here for a reason.
This is not a generic mixer. It is a celebration of what is being built, and the people building it.

Attendance is limited. The guest list is curated. Come ready to connect.

Live Entertainment

Experience an evening elevated by live musical performances featuring violin, saxophone, and harp. Each performance is designed to complement the atmosphere and create a memorable, immersive experience from the moment you walk in.

About 165 Global

165 Global is a dynamic investment firm backing visionary founders committed to building with purpose. They provide essential capital without bureaucracy, empowering leaders to stay true to their vision while creating ventures that elevate people and communities.

At 165 Global, the belief is that the greatest companies are built within strong, mission-aligned communities. Their focus is on empowering networks that uplift founders, expand influence, and unlock extraordinary opportunities across industries. They pursue dynamic investments with a global footprint, identifying ventures that transcend markets, cultures, and conventional thinking.

Learn more at www.165global.com
