## Description

Build that site you've been putting off. Get some help creating that automation you can't quite figure out.

Vibe Code & Sip is a guided, beginner-friendly building experience where creativity, community, and AI come together.

Think paint and sip, but instead of leaving with a canvas, you leave with a live site, app, or prototype.

This session is designed to help nontechnical people learn how to use AI to turn ideas into something real. No coding background required.

We’ll walk through the basics of vibe coding, help you shape your idea, guide you through the building process, and create space for you to connect with other builders over drinks and good conversation.

Come with an idea or come with curiosity. Either way, you’ll leave with more confidence and clarity.

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com
