## Description

In an era where 84% of healthcare VC exits fall below $650M and 42% fall below $150M, the pursuit of billion-dollar ‘unicorns’ increasingly resembles a lottery ticket strategy. 

​This salon confronts the reality that mid-market exits ($80M–$250M) now represent healthcare’s most replicable liquidity path. This gathering will bring together healthcare GPs and LPs for a thoughtful conversation around M&A trends, fund design, capital strategy, and alignment of LP and GP goals – and as a result better outcomes for LPs, GPs, and founders.

To apply to participate, you must currently be either the GP or MP of a healthtech focused fund or a current LP into the same.
