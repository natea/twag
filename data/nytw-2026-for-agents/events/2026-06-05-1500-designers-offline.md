## Description

In a week full of panels, talks, and packed schedules, this is your pause.

Designers Offline is where designers and people design-curious enough to crash the party, come to hang out like normal humans.

Drop by for designer-focused networking, light bites, live music and hot/cold brews.

Think of it like a house party for people with overlapping worlds: a room full of creative, thoughtful humans, a shared professional curiosity, and an easy vibe that makes meeting new people feel natural.

No panels. No pitches. No awkward “so what do you do?” energy.

Just good people, coffee, a few hot takes, and a little bit of chaos (the fun kind).

Expect:
🎟️ Light networking (with a twist) - we’ll make it less painful, promise
