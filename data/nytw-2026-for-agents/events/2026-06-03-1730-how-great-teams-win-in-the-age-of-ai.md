## Description

AI is accelerating but but team performance is not.

As organizations adopt new tools and technologies, many are running into the same challenges: unclear ownership, slower decision-making, and growing disconnects between strategy and execution.

This live webinar brings together experienced leaders to unpack what it actually takes for teams to perform in the age of AI.

This is not a passive panel, it is a guided, interactive conversation.

In this session, we will explore:

Why teams still matter in an AI-driven world
The core attributes of high-performing teams
The most common breakdowns teams experience today
How leaders can improve decision-making, alignment, and trust

The discussion will go beyond surface-level insights. Panelists will challenge assumptions, share real experiences, and work through practical scenarios that leaders are facing today.

Attendees will leave with clearer thinking, sharper questions, and a better understanding of how to strengthen their teams in an AI-driven environment.
