## Description

CFO Breakfast
Hosted by Concourse & a16z during NYC Tech Week

Join a select group of finance leaders for a focused breakfast on AI in the finance function. We'll explore moving past the AI hype cycle into the operational reality of deploying AI and agents across the finance function and encourage teams to come to share and learn form each other.

Coffee, bagels, and breakfast will be served in the Concourse HQ. We'll have ample time for networking and sharing as well as a panel on how CFOs have deployed AI into production (more details soon on speakers)

Ivan Makarov (Partner, a16z)
Matt Hafemeister (CEO & Co-Founder, Concourse)

The discussion will cover:
- How CFOs are rewiring close, FP&A, and treasury function with AI
- How AI actually driving ROI (and how it's being measured)
- Org design: who owns AI in finance, how teams are changing, where do agents sit in the organization
- Honest takes on vendors, build-vs-buy, and where the biggest wins have been

Seats are limited to keep the conversation candid and the room peer-level.

About Concourse:

Concourse is the platform for finance teams to build AI agents. Teams connect their core data systems, automate the work that slows them down, and deploy agents into production they can trust — turning finance into a faster, sharper decision engine.

Concourse is deployed in production at companies like Palo Alto Networks, Front, Tecovas. Concourse has raised over $17M from Standard Capital, Andreessen Horowitz, CRV, Y Combinator and leading operators from OpenAI, Vercel, and Cursor.

The Venue: Concourse HQ — Flatiron, NYC (full address shared upon RSVP)

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
