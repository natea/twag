## Description

Construction projects generate massive amounts of data, yet most teams still operate reactively instead of proactively. This NY Tech Week event brings together operators, investors, and AI builders to explore how “Construction Intelligence” is changing that - turning fragmented project data and on-site reality into real-time operational clarity.

Agenda:
3:30 PM — Networking + Check-In

4:00 PM — Opening Discussion | Rob Metz + Anita
Why construction teams still operate reactively — and why AI-native infrastructure is changing how projects are managed in the field.

4:20 PM — Product & Operator Perspectives | Jobotics AI + Bowser AI
How new systems are capturing on-site reality, connecting fragmented construction data, and enabling real-time operational visibility.

Topics include:
- field-first workflows
- fragmented systems and data reconciliation
- operational intelligence for construction teams
- moving from reactive execution to predictive operations

5:00 PM — Audience Q&A + Open Discussion

5:20 PM to 6.30 PM — Networking

If you care about applying AI beyond the screen - into the physical world - this conversation is for you.
