## Description

It all starts here, folks.  

On June 4, we’re bringing something truly different to NY Tech Week. Rocket Mixer® and Apollo Society are hosting the second edition of a one-of-a-kind event that redefines the tech experience—where wellness meets innovation in one of the most stunning venue in NYC: Edge observation deck.

The life of a founder is anything but linear. It’s lived at the edge—of pressure, uncertainty, sharp turns, and constant intensity. Instead of ignoring that reality, we’ve built an experience around it.

The day begins with guided breath-work led by a professional instructor, helping founders reset and find clarity. 

From there, the energy shifts. At the edge of the observation deck, eight top early-stage startups will step forward for a high-stakes elevator pitch competition, presenting to a leading VC fund for the opportunity to secure significant funding.

​The morning continues with an exclusive private breakfast alongside a curated group of founders, VCs, and operators.

​
​We’re bringing a single, high-impact experience built as a three-part package: immersive breathwork, a pitch competition, and an exclusive private breakfast with founders, VCs, and operators. A can’t-miss gathering.

​Startups will compete for the opportunity to secure up to $1M in funding for late-stage startups, or a first check and accelerator access for early-stage founders, led by LVLUP Ventures.
