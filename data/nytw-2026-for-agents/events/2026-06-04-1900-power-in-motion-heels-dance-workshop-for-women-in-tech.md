## Description

You won’t see any other event at New York Tech Week even a little bit close to this one. 

And that’s exactly why we need it. 

A magnetic presence. 
Confidence in self-advocacy.
An aura that says “I respect myself”, so they respect you back. 

As ambitious women in tech, we need these qualities to thrive in our careers.

Yet too many of us are trapped in our heads, for too long. 

Doubting our brilliance. 
Dimming our own light. 
Underselling ourselves. 

To get out of our own way and step into our power, we need embodied, authentic confidence and a presence that exudes authority, effortlessly. 

Power in Motion: Heels Dance Workshop for Women in Tech is an introduction to the dance style that has captivated women across the world for the way it emboldens and empowers us to reach our highest potential. 

Izzy Becker is a dancer, consultant in data & technology and coach from London, UK, based in NYC. 

In this unique event, you will:
🎤 Hear Izzy’s personal story of transformation
💃 Learn basic heels dance technique and a short piece of choreography
🫶 Get Izzy’s 101 cheat sheet for developing confidence through heels dance
💞 Have fun & move outside your comfort zone

Absolutely no dance experience necessary. Open to all levels. 

Come for the class, stay for the connections you’ll make: with other women just like you.

Limited spots available.
