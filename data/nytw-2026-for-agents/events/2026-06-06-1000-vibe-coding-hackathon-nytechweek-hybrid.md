## Description

About the Event
This is a focused, high-signal hackathon for early- to mid-career professionals in product, engineering, and design who want to build, experiment, and ship meaningful products — not just brainstorm.

Participants will receive free Lovable credits to build AI-powered apps and gain access to Miro's full collaborative workspace.

Goal of the Event: Build and ship a real AI-powered product in one day.

Schedule
9:00- 11:00: Registration and Networking
11:00-12:00: Kickoff and Speakers (Panel)
12:00: Prompt Reveal
12:00-2:30: Build Time
2:30-4:00: Networking
4:00: Top 10 Teams Announced
4:00-4:30: Final Preparation
4:30-6:00: Demos and Judging

Jun 7: Winners Announced

We'll be joined and mentored by Aj Yadav (TheVibeFounder) — Forbes 30 Under 30, serial founder (Simplified AI, 20M users), now shipping solo with AI agents. Aj builds live every Sunday, on camera, demonstrating how to ship products at team speed without the team. 

Our second judge is Alisha Beotra — strategy consultant advising CXOs at F500 companies and PE firms on growth and value creation | content creator for 100K+ professionals on careers and money | MBA (Tuck) | with a background in VC, consulting, and investment banking.
