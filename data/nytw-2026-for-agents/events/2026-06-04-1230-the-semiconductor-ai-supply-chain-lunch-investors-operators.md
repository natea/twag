## Description

A private NY Tech Week lunch for investors and operators focused on the AI supply chain.

AI, the supply chain behind it, and the public-market equities tied to that buildout have become part of the same conversation. This lunch brings that conversation into a private room with people investing in, building around, and tracking the space.

Hosted by Kerman Kohli, Founder of Silicon Frontier, a hedge fund focused on AI public-market equities. Join us for lunch and a private conversation on the AI supply chain opportunity, public markets, and how investors and operators are reading the space from here.

The room will bring together portfolio managers, allocators, venture investors, founders, operators, and technical builders across public markets, private markets, AI infrastructure, and semiconductors.

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
