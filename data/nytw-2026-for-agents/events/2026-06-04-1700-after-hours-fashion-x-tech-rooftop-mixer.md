All throughout Tech Week, it’s been nothing but panels, fireside chats, networking, and workshops — now it’s time to unwind.

Whether you're in fashion, tech, investing, or simply curious about where the future of fashion is headed — come hang out, network, and enjoy the night with good people, great conversations, DJ vibes, and an exclusive indoor rooftop atmosphere.
