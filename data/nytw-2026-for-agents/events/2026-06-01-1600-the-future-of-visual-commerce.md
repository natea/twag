## Description

Please note: Doors open at 4:00 PM. Space is limited and entry will be first come, first served. A valid ID will be required to enter the building. The fireside chat with Pauline Brown will begin promptly at 5:00 PM.

Visual content drives every purchase decision in e-commerce, yet the way brands create, communicate, and scale their visual identity is evolving quickly.

As AI and new digital tools reshape fashion and retail, the role of taste, aesthetics, brand intuition, and creative direction is becoming more important than ever.

This event will explore the future of visual commerce through two conversations: a fireside chat on aesthetic intelligence and brand intuition, followed by a panel discussion on how AI and technology are being applied across fashion and retail.

Event Schedule:

4:00–5:00 PM
Networking / Shopping

5:00–5:45 PM
Fireside Chat with Pauline Brown
Former Chair of LVMH USA | Adjunct Professor at Harvard & Columbia Business School | Author of Aesthetic Intelligence

Moderated by Zehra Soysal, founder of Savanah.ai, this conversation will explore the role of taste, aesthetics, and brand intuition in a technology-driven world. Pauline will share her perspective on why aesthetic intelligence still matters as AI and new digital tools reshape how brands create, communicate, and connect with customers.

5:45–6:30 PM
Panel Session
Featuring Maria Costa, Senior Director of Marketing at M.M.LaFleur and Anna Lombardo, Chief of Staff at Nordstrom

A discussion on how technology and AI are being applied across the retail and fashion industries, from creative workflows and content production to customer engagement, marketing, personalization, and operational efficiency. The panel will explore where innovation is already creating value and what the future of fashion and retail may look like.

6:30–8:00 PM
Enjoy light snacks and drinks while connecting with founders, operators, and brand leaders.

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
