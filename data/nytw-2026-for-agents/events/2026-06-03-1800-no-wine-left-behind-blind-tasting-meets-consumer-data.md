## Description

What if consumers actually decided what gets stocked?

Join No Wine Left Behind for an experiential blind tasting + live data demo featuring the winning wines from our Women in Wine Festival. You’ll taste a curated lineup of wines completely blind, submit your feedback in real time, and watch as your palate becomes data — revealing collective preferences, patterns, and top performers.

Then, we reveal everything.

After the reveal, you get to revisit the wines with full context, experiencing firsthand how perception shifts once labels, stories, and pricing are introduced.

This isn’t just a wine tasting — it’s a live demonstration of how consumer data can directly determine retail decisions. By turning individual opinions into actionable insights, we’re building a more transparent, democratized approach to wine buying — where the crowd, not gatekeepers, decides what earns shelf space.

Perks:
– Store credit for all attendees
– First access to winning wines
– A chance to help decide what gets stocked at Le Dû’s
