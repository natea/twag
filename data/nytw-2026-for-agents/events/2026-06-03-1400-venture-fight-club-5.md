## Description

Tired of boring speakers, mundane panels and inane chit-chat?  

Come join us for a No-Gi Jiu Jitsu Open Mat 2:00pm to 4:00pm on Wednesday, June 3rd in SoHo for TechWeek's weirdest event.

Venture Fight Club is a community for Investors and Entrepreneurs who grapple (jiu jitsu, judo, wrestling, etc).

Open and free to any investors or entrepreneurs with previous grappling experience. You don't have to be good, but you can't be day 1 new. We don't want anyone getting hurt.
