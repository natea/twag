## Description

Bring Your Product. We'll Break It.

Live adversarial testing for 10 AI-native founders. 60 minutes. Real vulnerabilities. Coffee provided.

Your product ships fast. Your attackers move faster.

Most early-stage companies think security is something to solve later, after scale, after growth, after fundraising. But modern products are being probed from day one by bots, automated scripts, AI-assisted attackers, and increasingly sophisticated threat actors.

In this 60-minute live session, we’ll simulate how real attackers evaluate a product and show you where your system breaks before the internet does.

Using controlled offensive security methods, the Tequity Security Ops team will:

— Map your exposed attack surface across product, infrastructure, workflows, and user behavior
— Demonstrate how seemingly small vulnerabilities compound into major security risks
— Identify trust and security gaps across authentication, permissions, APIs, AI systems, internal tooling, and operational workflows
— Show how attackers think, move, escalate access, and exploit assumptions inside modern products
— Help you understand what security maturity actually looks like for a zero-to-one company

This is not a theoretical webinar. It is a live working session.

Bring your product, staging environment, prototype, or architecture. We’ll walk through real-world attack scenarios and show you where your company is most vulnerable today.

You’ll leave with:

* A high-level risk assessment of your current product ecosystem
* A practical understanding of your most critical vulnerabilities
* A security prioritization framework for your current stage
* Recommendations on what to fix immediately vs later
* A clearer picture of what modern Security Ops should look like inside a growing startup

Who this is for:
— Founders building AI-native or SaaS products
— Engineering teams moving quickly without dedicated security leadership
— Startups handling sensitive user, healthcare, fintech, or enterprise data
— Teams preparing for enterprise sales, compliance reviews, or due diligence
— Companies that have never undergone adversarial security testing before

Limited to 10 companies so each team gets hands-on attention.

Led by Tequity, an applied AI company, working with early-stage startups across AI, health-tech, fintech, and enterprise software. Our teams work closely with zero-to-one founders to build products that are not only usable and scalable, but resilient under real-world conditions.

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com
