## Description

2026 is shaping up to be the year AI agents move from concept to workflow. In finance, that shift matters: the work is knowledge-intensive, regulated, and dependent on judgment and trust.

Hosted by Women Leading AI, this NY Tech Week panel will explore how agentic AI is beginning to reshape institutional financial services – from banking, portfolio management, investment research, wealth management, risk, compliance, operations, client service, and capital allocation to the broader governance questions raised by increasingly autonomous workflows.

This will be a dynamic women-led speaker panel featuring startups and leaders building at the intersection of agentic AI and financial services. The conversation will focus on what is real today, where adoption is emerging, and what finance and AI leaders need to understand before agentic systems become more widely implemented.

Drawing on Women Leading AI’s institutional finance & AI lens, the event is designed for investors, finance and AI leaders seeking a practical, high-signal discussion of the next wave of AI adoption in financial services.

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek – a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com
