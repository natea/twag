## Description

You care about making healthy choices. You see doctors mostly for prevention. You spend on SoulCycle, the gym, therapy, acupuncture — and almost none of it counts for anything in the system you're paying into.

ionava is built for that. And this conversation with Melanie Griffith, SoulCycle Head of Brand Experience & Master Instructor, will tell you why the next generation of healthcare is about breaking vicious cycles and 

ionava is not health insurance. It's a financial platform designed for health and wellness.

With a Vault subscription, 65–75% of your fee returns to your Wallet — user-controlled cash on a debit card. Spend it on the things that actually support your health: PCPs, dentists, labs, therapy, acupuncture, Reiki, the gym, SoulCycle.

Plus you'll be your doctors' new favorite customer because they get paid on the spot. And you benefit from cash prices which are typically lower than insurance.

The other 25–35% activates your Vault — reserved personal liquidity of $300K, $700K, or $1M, available if a Major health event occurs (terminal, chronic, or critical).

What you don't spend stays yours and earns 1–3% interest.

Your subscription price never goes up. Rent control, but for your health.
