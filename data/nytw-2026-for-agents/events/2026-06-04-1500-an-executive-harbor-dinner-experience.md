## Description

AI Adoption vs. Risk Tolerance
An Executive Harbor Dinner Experience | NYC Tech Week

Join an exclusive gathering of C-level executives, board advisors, cybersecurity leaders, and AI decision-makers for a private executive experience during NYC Tech Week.

This curated evening is designed for leaders navigating the growing tension between innovation, governance, operational efficiency, and enterprise risk in the age of AI.

Featured executive voices include Teri Green alongside senior leaders across technology, cybersecurity, infrastructure, and enterprise operations.

Together, attendees will explore how organizations are balancing AI adoption with board expectations, security realities, and evolving risk tolerance models.

Executive Discussion Themes

◆ AI & Machine Learning Governance
◆ Operational Efficiency Through AI
◆ Data Management & Enterprise Trust
◆ Legacy Systems vs. AI-Integrated Technologies
◆ Cybersecurity & Critical Infrastructure Risk
◆ Boardroom Strategy, Risk Appetite & AI Adoption

Special discussion will examine how emerging threats, including the Mythos Vulnerability, are influencing executive and board-level conversations surrounding AI governance, resilience, and critical infrastructure protection.

This is intentionally not a traditional panel.

The evening is structured as a high-trust executive roundtable focused on authentic conversation, strategic insight sharing, and meaningful relationship building among leaders shaping the future of AI adoption.
