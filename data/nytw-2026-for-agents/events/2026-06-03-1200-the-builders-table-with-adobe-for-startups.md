## Description

We're hosting a small lunch in New York to talk about company building. Twenty founders, early-stage marketers, and operators. No agenda other than a real conversation about where AI actually fits into the way early teams work — and where it doesn't.  

We'll look at some of what Adobe is building for teams like yours, get into the actual friction points that slow companies down, and hear from a couple of partners who run creative and ops at a leading brand agency and venture studio.   

This is also something of a first conversation. Adobe for Startups is early, and we want to build it with the people who'd actually use it. So if you have opinions about what tools are useful, what's overhyped, and what you actually need, we want to hear them. 

This is part of #NYTechWeek — a week of events hosted by VCs and startups across New York's tech ecosystem. More at www.tech-week.com.
