## Description

You have optimized your calendar, your team, your systems. Have you thought about your mind?

Jiali Zhang is a computational neuroscientist from Oxford and the founder of Soma. She spent a decade mapping how the brain processes information, builds memory, and sustains attention. She left the lab with one conviction: the science of peak cognitive performance belongs in the hands of the people actually trying to do hard things.

Join founders, investors, and neuroscientists to learn the latest research in cognitive performance and discuss what it means to perform at your best.
