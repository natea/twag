## Description

The leap from simple client-server apps to intelligent, automated workflows is the next frontier for developers. Building these high-performance, AI-driven applications requires more than just a clever prompt—it demands seamless access to enterprise-grade infrastructure that bridges the gap between frontends and the full power of Google Cloud.

In this deep-dive technical session, we explore how Firebase has evolved into the ultimate platform for "vibe coding" and agentic development. Discover how to move beyond basic LLM integrations to build full-stack, production-ready apps that leverage relational data, instant identity verification, and collaborative AI agents.
