## Description

This event is a part of NY Tech Week. Tech Tribe is a curated evening experience at the intersection of culture, technology, and global entrepreneurship—bringing together founders, operators, and innovators building across the Caribbean diaspora and beyond.

Hosted by Irie Jam Media at the Jamaica Consulate, the event blends high-impact conversations on identity, capital, and the evolving Caribbean tech ecosystem with live demos, intentional networking, and cultural moments. Designed for both current and aspiring entrepreneurs, Tech Tribe creates space for meaningful connection, insight, and access—while highlighting how multicultural communities are shaping the future of business and innovation.
