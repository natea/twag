## Description

It's Tuesday. You could be at just another networking mixer. Or — you could build the project that changes your entire career trajectory. We vote for the second one.

The Career Hack is one electric evening built around a deceptively simple idea: stop building stuff for everyone else and build the thing that gets you where you're going. That side project you keep "meaning to start." The wild idea you pitch at parties but never ship. The portfolio piece that screams "hire me / fund me / partner with me." Tonight's the night it gets real.

No client specs. No boring prize categories. Just you, your laptop, good snacks, and a room full of people building toward their next big thing.

The vibe:

⚡ Pitch the project that represents your next move (60 seconds, make it spicy)
🛠️ Build a working prototype before the night's out
🧠 Trade feedback with mentors and fellow builders who get it
🏆 Walk away with something real — and bragging rights

Who's invited:
Builders, designers, engineers, founders, career-changers, and the chronically curious. Whether you're plotting a pivot or scratching a long-ignored itch, pull up a chair.

The best careers turn on something you actually made — not a resume line. So bring the idea. Bring the caffeine. Leave with proof.
