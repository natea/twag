## Description

We are bringing together NYC's LP community and select GPs with Brown Rudnick and those visiting for connecting before late evening events. This is the second year of this gathering. This event is open to individuals, CVCs, Fund of Funds, and Family Offices that have an active interest in LP opportunities in VC funds, who will be joined by a group of GPs that we love working with. We will have light bites and beverages for the gathering.

Attendees are allowed to join the LP Collective afterwards, which is a no-fee community solely for LPs to see compelling emerging managers who are raising, in case of serendipity.

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
