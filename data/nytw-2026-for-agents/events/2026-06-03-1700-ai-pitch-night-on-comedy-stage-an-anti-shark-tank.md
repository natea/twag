## Description

Room 52 is a prominent stand-up comedy club in NYC, where renowned stand up comedians run open mics and hone their skills before they run on national networks. This NY Teach week, Comedy meet venture – where laughter is the best due diligence.

Apply to Pitch: https://tally.so/r/kdkj8M

This New York Tech Week, UpScale X is linking up with Startup Intros, Room 52, and Ugly Talk to drop NYC's first-ever Anti-Shark Tank — live, on a real comedy stage. Founders pitch. Anti-sharks pounce. Comics roast. Everyone learns something (and at least one person gets humbled).

The night will be MC'd by Peter Ballmer, one of Room 52's regulars — a former tech PM who came up through the industry and now roasts it for a living.

Eight AI founders will take the mic for short, candid founder sets — what they're actually building, what's harder than it looks, and what they think the market is still sleeping on. Then the Anti-Sharks step in: live, on-the-record reactions from operators and builders who've been in the trenches.

Expect sharp founder stories, live "anti-shark" feedback, drinks, light bites, and curated networking with AI builders, operators, investors, and ecosystem partners working across agentic AI, commerce, finance, media, workflow automation, and applied AI.
