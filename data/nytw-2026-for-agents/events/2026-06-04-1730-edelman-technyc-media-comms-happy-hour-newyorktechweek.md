## Description

Join Edelman and Tech:NYC for an evening happy hour and panel on how tech storytelling is evolving as the pace of innovation accelerates.

From the rise of newsletters and long-form video to new audience engagement strategies, we’ll explore how media are reshaping their formats to keep up with constant change, and how AI is transforming the way stories are sourced, produced, and distributed.

Julie Samuels, President & CEO of Tech:NYC, will moderate a dynamic conversation on the shifting media landscape, what’s driving today’s most effective storytelling, and where communications leaders should focus to stay ahead in an increasingly complex ecosystem.

Confirmed panelists include: Fortune, John Kell; Semafor, Rachyl Jones; Deep View, Sabina Ortiz; WSJ CIO Journal, Steve Rosenbush; LinkedIn News, Tanya Dua; NBC News, Jared Perlo

This event is a part of #NewYorkTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
