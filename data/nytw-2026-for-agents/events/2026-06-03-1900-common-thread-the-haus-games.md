## Description

NY Tech Week's most intentional social event. Get sorted into Houses of 5 and compete your way to real connections.

Some of the best connections you'll ever make won't happen in a conference room. They happen when you're on the same team, figuring something out together, laughing at something that went sideways.

That's what Common Thread: The Haus Games is built around.

As an official NY Tech Week event, Fostr and Tera are bringing together the founders, builders, and creatives shaping what's next at the intersection of fashion, culture, and tech. Join us and the tastemakers defining NYC culture for a little friendly competition.

The Brief:

This isn't random. When you register you'll answer a few questions about what you bring to a room and what you are looking for in this event. We use those answers to sort you into a House of five people. No need to worry about working the room hoping to stumble onto the right person. We did that part for you.

Compete together, figure things out together, and by the end of the night you'll have met people you actually want to stay in touch with.

Beyond that, we'll let the night speak for itself. Curated, competitive, and a little unexpected.

Come dressed to impress.

Spots are limited.

Agenda:

7:00pm — Doors open. Grab goodies, find your house, and start chatting.
8:00pm — Welcome from Fostr & Tera. 
8:30pm — Games begin. May the best team win!
9:30pm — Prizes and an unexpected ending to the night.

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem.
