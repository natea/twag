## Description

Sponsored by Wooxy, this fast-paced hackathon challenges you to design and build real AI-powered solutions to customer retention problems—in just a few hours.

Whether you're a developer, founder, or AI enthusiast, this is your chance to go from idea to working prototype using cutting-edge AI tools.

What to Expect

This is not just brainstorming—you’ll actually build.

The session is structured in three focused stages:

1. Methodology
We kick off with Wooxy’s specification-driven coding approach—showing how to rapidly build high-quality MVPs using AI-assisted development.

2. Ideation
Form teams, identify high-impact retention challenges, and validate your ideas with guidance from Wooxy experts.

3. Build & Demo
Bring your idea to life using AI tools and APIs. At the end, present your working MVP and get feedback from engineers and fellow participants.

💡 What You’ll Gain
A hands-on understanding of building with AI under real constraints
Experience applying AI to customer retention challenges
A working MVP you can continue developing after the event
Feedback from experienced builders and peers
Exposure to modern AI workflows and rapid prototyping techniques
👥 Who Should Join

This hackathon is ideal for:

Developers and engineers experimenting with AI tools
Startup founders and builders
Students and recent grads in tech or AI
Product thinkers interested in real-world applications
Anyone excited to build something real, fast
🛠 What You’ll Leave With

By the end of the session, your team will have:

A functional prototype (MVP)
A validated idea targeting a real retention problem
A clear next step to turn your hack into a live experiment or product

🎁 All participants will receive 3 months of free access to Wooxy, so you can keep building after the hackathon.
