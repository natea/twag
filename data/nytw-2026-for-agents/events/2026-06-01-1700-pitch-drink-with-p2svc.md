## Description

🔥 Venture Speed Dating
📣 Calling all founders looking for investment opportunities!
We're excited to bring you Pitch&Drink with P2S.vc - a night in New York where founders and investors meet under one roof.

🚀 Event Format
Venture (founders & investors) speed dating. You'll have 2–3 minutes to present your elevator pitch (no presentation) directly to the investor, followed by a 6-7 minute Q&A session. After 10 minutes the gong sounds and  the founders change their place.

❗️ Don’t forget to submit this form to participate in pitching https://forms.gle/gFhwcwMPrHA3iMf8A

⭐️ 5pm- 7pm - exclusively reserved for investors and approved startups to showcase their elevator pitches
🕰️ After 7pm - the doors open to welcome all other approved guests

‼️If you are selected to pitch, you will receive an email with full instructions. Otherwise, just come at 7 pm for networking (for approved guests)

Why You Should Join:
👉 Benefit from live pitch reviews and align your startup with investor expectations.
👉 Don't miss this opportunity to make valuable connections and explore funding possibilities!
👉 Connect with a community of like-minded individuals and VCs and gain insights into your next fundraising venture in a relaxed and fun environment.

​p2s.vc 
Pre-seed to Succeed (P2S) is helping young and ambitious companies raise their first funding round. It's an investment program launched by AltaIR Capital, Yellow Rocks, Smart Partnership Capital and I2BF Global Ventures. The P2S is designed for startups in the early stages with MVP and first traction and focuses on various industries. It offers initial funding from participating venture companies, ongoing support from seasoned experts and mentors, and 50% of the next round for proven startups.

If you have any questions please contact us by email: raise@p2s.vc

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
