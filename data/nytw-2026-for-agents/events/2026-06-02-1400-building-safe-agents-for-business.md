## Description

AI agents are moving from demos to production. They're executing payments, writing code, accessing sensitive data, and making decisions autonomously. But the safety infrastructure hasn't kept up. Guardrails are probabilistic. Failures are silent. Prompt injections can hijack agent behavior. And enterprises are stuck between "move fast" and "don't blow up."

The missing piece is cryptographic guarantees. Not better prompts. Not more filters. Mathematical proof that an agent action complies with your policy before it executes. See docs.icme.io

This workshop teaches you how to build agents that enforce policies with formal verification, resist prompt injection and hijacking, and prove compliance before every action. Hands-on, technical, and applicable to any agent framework.
