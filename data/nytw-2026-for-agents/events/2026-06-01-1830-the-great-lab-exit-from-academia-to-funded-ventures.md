## Description

Many aspiring scientists wonder how they can take the leap into building their own company. At the same time, many entrepreneurs are looking for ways to tap into the deep knowledge, IP, and research emerging from academia to build their next venture.

This event brings together ex-Nucleate founders who have successfully made that transition from academia into entrepreneurship. They will share how they started, how they turned research into companies, and what it actually took to raise capital and scale beyond the lab.

The panel will be followed by networking and drinks.
