## Description

Join us for an invite-only evening celebrating the launch of Augment’s mobile app. Get access to private companies, real-time pricing, and early allocation opportunities for the first time. Be among the first to experience the app, meet the team, and get early access to upcoming deals before they go live.

The Evening

7:00 PM | Doors Open - Cocktails and networking with fellow investors, founders, and the Augment team.

7:45 PM | The Launch - Augment CEO unveils the new iPhone app and what it means for individual investors accessing private markets. 

8:00 PM | The Floor Is Yours - Download the app, explore 300+ pre-IPO companies with real-time pricing, and talk one-on-one with the Augment team. Complete your sign-up on the spot to claim your priority Collective allocation.
