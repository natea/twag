## Description

Join LinkedIn for an exclusive gathering of B2B startup founders and marketing leaders during #NYTechWeek.

We are bringing together a curated group of high-growth startups for an evening focused on how B2B teams are evolving their go-to-market in a fast-changing landscape.

Speakers include: 
• Ruby James, Senior Manager, Startups & Tech, LinkedIn
• Carilu Dietrich, Former CMO / Head of Marketing at Atlassian; now advisor to CEOs and CMOs of high-growth tech companies
• Craig Rosenberg, Chief Platform Officer, Scale Venture Partners
• Nick Lafferty, Founding Marketing Engineer at Profound
• Eliott Lee, Director of Partnerships at Profound

What to expect:
• New LinkedIn insights on the key shifts shaping B2B startup marketing today
• A practical conversation with peers on how teams are navigating AI-driven discovery, using creative and human-led content to stand out, and measuring what drives pipeline
• Networking with founders, marketers, and operators over drinks and light bites

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
