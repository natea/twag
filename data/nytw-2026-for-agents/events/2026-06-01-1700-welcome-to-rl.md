## Description

Prompting was chapter one. Fine-tuning was chapter two. Reinforcement learning is chapter three, and the teams shipping it in production are quietly rewriting what enterprise AI looks like at scale.

Models that learn from their own outputs. Optimize continuously. Compound into company assets instead of API subscriptions.

Welcome to RL is an evening for the people actually building this: VPs and Heads of AI, ML practitioners, infra engineers, and applied researchers. 

A short panel with builders running production RL, an off-the-record conversation about what's working and what's not, and drinks.

If you're running LLMs in production, at a bank, an insurer, a telco, or any high-throughput consumer platform, this is your room.
