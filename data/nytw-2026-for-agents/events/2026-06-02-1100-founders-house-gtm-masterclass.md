## Description

Founders House is a members-only home base in Manahattan where founders work, recover, and build alongside others going through the same thing.

​It's a workspace (phone booths, conference room and hot desks), a wellness club, and a community in one - designed around how founders actually spend their days. Apply for membership: www.foundershouse.nyc

12pm - 1pm: Lunch & Tour Founders House (approval required) 

Grab lunch & experience the future of founder coworking and wellness at our open house. Tour Founders House's working spaces, lounges, and wellness amenities. 

1pm - 2pm: GTM Masterclass by Cristina Ciaravalli

Learn tactical tips and tricks from our very own Founding Advisor on how to scale your startup.

Cristina Ciaravalli is the Founder & CEO of Valli Ventures, a GTM advisory and community platform helping early-stage founders scale through sales strategy, recruiting, BD partnerships, and curated events.
Prior to launching Valli Ventures, she was the #1 salesperson at two fintech unicorns and also helped grow a fintech community from 9,000 to 150,000 people, that was recently acquired by Plaid.
Today, Valli Ventures works with leading startups and VC-backed founders across fintech, AI, and B2B tech, while also building a fitness-founder community called Founder League, rooted in the belief that founder health and relationships drive company success.
Cristina has worked with companies like Cursor, Lithic, Ramp, Rillet, Taxbit, and more.

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
