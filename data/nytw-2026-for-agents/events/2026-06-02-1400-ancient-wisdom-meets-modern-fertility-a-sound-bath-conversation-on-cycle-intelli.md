## Description

Join Lotus Fertility, NJ Fast, and The Rewilding Collective for a special sound reset and conversation on fertility wisdom in the modern age, during NY Tech Week.

This intimate gathering for female investors, founders, and corporate leaders, brings together fertility wisdom, cycle awareness, and the emerging intersection of femtech and data privacy. 

We'll open with a sound bath to settle the nervous system and create space. Then we'll move into a facilitated conversation about cycle intelligence: what it means to understand your body's internal seasons, how to honor where you are in your cycle, and how technology, when we can trust it, can deepen that self-knowledge rather than extract it.

This is a space to rest, reset, and reconnect with a kind of intelligence that isn't always talked about.

About Lotus Fertility 

Lotus bridges the gap between proactive wellness and financial security in reproductive health. By partnering with major life insurance carriers, Lotus embeds comprehensive fertility protection as a living benefit directly into term life insurance policies. This approach replaces unpredictable out-of-pocket medical expenses with a reliable financial safety net, empowering individuals to take control of their fertility and build their families on their own terms

About NJ Fast

NJ FAST is a New Jersey-based fintech accelerator founded by NJEDA, Prudential Financial, and Stevens Institute of Technology in partnership with Plug and Play. Designed to help high-potential startups scale through strategic connections, pilot opportunities, and industry-informed support, this non-dilutive program strengthens New Jersey’s position as a leading hub for financial innovation. Since inception, MetLife and Transact Capital have joined as NJ FAST partners, further expanding the program’s reach across the financial services ecosystem. Together, NJ FAST creates a powerful platform for collaboration between startups and established industry leaders to accelerate the future of fintech.

About The Rewilding Collective

The Rewilding Collective designs experiential gatherings that help people reconnect with themselves through movement, play, creativity, reflection, and a deeper connection to nature. Founded by Dina Samra, the Collective creates space for people to pause, reset, and reconnect with who they are beneath the noise of modern life.
