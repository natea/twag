## Description

NYC has always been the proving ground for location-based play. From Come Out & Play Festival to Pac-Manhattan, from Pokemon GO's Central Park stampedes to Tribeca Immersive — this city turns its streets into game boards.

Join 20Week Festival and Stoop Sets for a conversation about locamotive games and microtourism: experiences that transform neighborhoods into playable spaces. We'll explore how AR, mixed reality, and location-based design are creating a new kind of urban exploration — one that drives foot traffic, cultural discovery, and real economic impact at the hyperlocal level.

Featuring speakers from the frontier of location-based entertainment, interactive storytelling, and neighborhood-scale experience design.

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
