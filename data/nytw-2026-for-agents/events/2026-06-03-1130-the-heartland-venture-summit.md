## Description

Why the Next Great Venture Opportunity Is in the Midwest

​During New York Tech Week, the Ohio Startup Network will host a private gathering of venture capitalists, founders, and ecosystem leaders aboard the Hornblower Lexington to explore one of the most important shifts happening in the startup landscape.

​Across the American Midwest and broader Heartland, a powerful innovation corridor is emerging. Cities like Columbus, Cleveland, Cincinnati, Detroit, Pittsburgh, Chicago, Indianapolis, and St. Louis are producing world-class research, ambitious founders, and globally competitive startups—often operating with exceptional capital efficiency and deep technical talent. As infrastructure investment, manufacturing resurgence, and frontier technologies converge, the region is becoming one of the most compelling new frontiers for venture capital in the United States.

​The question facing investors is no longer if venture will expand beyond the coasts.

​The question is who will recognize the opportunity first.

​This curated event brings together national investors and Heartland ecosystem leaders to discuss the structural advantages of the region, highlight emerging venture opportunities, and build new relationships between coastal capital and Midwest innovation.

​Set against the backdrop of the New York skyline, this gathering offers a rare chance to meet the people building—and funding—the next generation of companies across America’s fastest-rising tech corridor.

​What to Expect

​🚀 High-signal networking with venture investors and founders
🌎 Insight into the Midwest’s emerging innovation corridor
💡 Conversations on AI, frontier tech, and capital-efficient startups
🛥️ A private New York harbor experience during Tech Week

​Come celebrate and explore the incredible venture opportunities coming to the heartland of the United States.

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
