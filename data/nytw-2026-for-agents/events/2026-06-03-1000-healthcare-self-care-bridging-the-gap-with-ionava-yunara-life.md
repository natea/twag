## Description

We’ve never had more access to health information, yet taking care of ourselves has never felt more complicated.

Join us during NY Tech Week to cut through the noise of the health and longevity industries and learn how to best invest in your health now to extend your happiest and healthiest years later.

Across a series of short, high-impact conversations, our clinical team will cover:

-Why behavior change is so hard and how to actually make it stick
-A smarter, more sustainable approach to weight loss
-Honest conversations about peptides, hormones, and what’s real vs. hype in the biohacking and longevity space
-The evolution of the aesthetics industry - from looking better to aging better
-What it could look like if the healthcare industry actually supported you in staying healthy

In partnership with Ionava, we’ll also share how the future of healthcare may begin to align around prevention, incentives, and long-term outcomes. https://ionava.com

Explore the space, get advice from trusted experts, and take a first look at what we’re building.
