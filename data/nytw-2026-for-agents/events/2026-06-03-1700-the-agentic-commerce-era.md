## Description

AI agents aren't just answering questions — they're shopping. They're browsing, comparing, and buying on behalf of consumers before a browser ever opens. This intimate happy hour and panel brings together builders and operators working at the frontier of agentic commerce to talk about what's actually shipping, what's breaking, and what it means for brands, platforms, and consumers.

Hosted by Glance, which delivers AI-powered shopping experiences to tens of millions of devices through OEM partnerships with Samsung, Motorola, Verizon and more. Panelists include leaders building at the intersection of AI, commerce, and consumer products.

Expect a 30-minute moderated panel followed by drinks, conversation, and real talk about what happens when AI agents become your best customers.

Who should come: founders, product leaders, marketers, and investors working in AI, commerce, retail tech, or consumer products
