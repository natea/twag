## Description

Join FoundHer House for an intimate afternoon conversation spotlighting the women shaping the future of startup funding.

We are excited to present "The Female Funding Floor," an all-female panel event featuring investors and founder-operators from Pace Capital, Precursor Ventures, Lightspeed Venture Partners, and Soma Capital. Join us for a conversation on fundraising, building investable companies, navigating venture, expanding access to capital for women-led startups, and what it means to be a woman in the broader startup and VC ecosystem.

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
