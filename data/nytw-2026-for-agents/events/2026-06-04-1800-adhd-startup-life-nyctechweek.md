## Description

ADHD is everywhere in tech — but rarely talked about openly.

Join M13 for an intimate, off-the-record evening exploring how founders, investors, and technical leaders navigate building ambitious careers with ADHD. Moderated by M13 Partner & Head of Talent Matt Hoffman and featuring Rho founder and CEO Everett Cook and ADHD coach Rita Ramakrishnan,, the conversation will cover executive functioning, time management, leadership, and practical strategies for designing work around how your brain actually operates.

Expect candid conversation, actionable insights, and a highly curated room of peers navigating similar challenges.

Space is limited and attendance is by application only. We’re prioritizing founders and technical leaders actively building and operating at scale to keep the conversation thoughtful, honest, and high-signal.

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
