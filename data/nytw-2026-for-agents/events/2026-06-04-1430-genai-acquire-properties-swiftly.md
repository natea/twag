## Description

1) Site Selection & Feasibility: 6 months to 2 years (includes market analysis and locating land).
2) Due Diligence & Land Acquisition: 6–9 months (includes soil testing and environmental checks).
3) Investment Scoring: 1 to 6+ months (varies by NOI, property value, and cap rate).

With Project REDS, the current predevelopment timeline is:
1) Site Selection & Feasibility: 10 minutes (includes market analysis and locating land).
