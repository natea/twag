## Description

Join Us for an Evening of Insight and Connection

Citizens Bank, GrayHair Venture Partners, and Fox Rothschild LLP invite startup founders, investors, and legal innovation enthusiasts to an exclusive NY Tech Week event exploring the rapidly evolving world of LegalTech.

The global LegalTech market is experiencing unprecedented growth and capital inflows (topping $4.5 billion in 2025 and on a trajectory to surpass that amount in 2026), driven in large part by the transformative impact of artificial intelligence. AI-powered tools are reshaping how legal services are delivered, from contract analysis and due diligence to litigation prediction and regulatory compliance. 

Panel Discussion: Recent Developments and Challenges in LegalTech

Our expert panel will examine the forces shaping LegalTech today, including:
•	The rise of AI in legal practice and how it is redefining workflows across the industry.
•	Regulatory and ethical considerations surrounding AI-driven legal tools, including questions of accuracy, bias, privilege and the unauthorized practice of law.
•	Investment trends and market dynamics, including where venture capital is flowing and what differentiates the startups that succeed.
•	The evolving relationship between law firms, in-house legal teams, and technology providers as adoption accelerates.

Panelists will include:

•	Lev Loukhton, Founder and Managing Partner at GrayHair Venture Partners
•	Otto Zastrow, Founder of Midpage.AI
•	Nick Baughan, Managing Director at Marks Baughan
 •	Arina Shulga, partner at Fox Rothschild LLP

Following the panel, join fellow founders, investors, and legal professionals for a relaxed reception. This is your opportunity to make meaningful connections, exchange ideas, and explore potential collaborations in one of tech's most dynamic verticals.

This event is designed for startup founders building in the LegalTech space, venture capital and angel investors evaluating legal technology opportunities, and anyone passionate about the intersection of law and technology.

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
