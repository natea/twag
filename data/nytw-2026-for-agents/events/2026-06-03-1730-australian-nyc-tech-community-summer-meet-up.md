## Description

Austrade and the American Australian Association are delighted to once again invite you to the annual Australian NYC Tech Community Summer Meet-up. 

​Join us for a fun night of networking, drinks and refreshments with fellow Aussies and our friends from the New York tech ecosystem.

​As always, we will be highlighting a few of our Aussie founders in the room with 1-2 minute introductions on their companies, featuring some of the best sunset views over Manhattan.

​Please bring photo ID to check in with building security. 

​Looking forward to seeing you all there!

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
