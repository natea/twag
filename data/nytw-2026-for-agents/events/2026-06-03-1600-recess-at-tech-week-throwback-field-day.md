## Description

Tech Week is here! Which means a full week of non-stop panels, pitches, and hackathons, oh my! But amidst the marathon, you're gonna need to take a real break — not just a drink in hand at a "networking happy hour," but an actual, moving-around, playing-with-friends kind of break.

On Wednesday during Tech Week, we're teaming up with Throwback Fitness to bring you a real Field Day. Not a pickup game. Not unstructured hang time. A produced, coached, team-vs-team field day with rotating game stations, bandanas, and a live leaderboard tracking every point.

Here's how it works: show up, get assigned a team, and compete across a rotation of field day games — think egg & spoon race, logic puzzles, and a Family Feud-style trivia mashup. Throwback coaches run everything. You just show up and play. Games are designed for all fitness levels — think brainpower and teamwork, not wind sprints and pushups.

Throwback has been running field days like this for companies like Google, Netflix, Uber, and many others since 2014. Tech Week attendees get a taste for free.

See what a Throwback experience looks like here: https://youtu.be/X5JFLSZzPlQ
