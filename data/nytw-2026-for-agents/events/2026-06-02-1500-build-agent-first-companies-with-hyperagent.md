## Description

Agent Battle Night: Build an agent. Pitch to your peers. 

You'll spin up an agent on Hyperagent, Airtable's platform for building and deploying fleets of agents. Your agent will pitch to your peers in the room. The format is open. Have your agent send emails, voicemails, a custom landing page, whatever you can ship in the time you've got. Your three targets are real people, not seed prompts. They'll get the pitch live, and the whole room will watch it land.

The room votes. Winner takes an additional grand prize.

Everyone leaves with $10K in Hyperagent credits to spin up their own agent team.
