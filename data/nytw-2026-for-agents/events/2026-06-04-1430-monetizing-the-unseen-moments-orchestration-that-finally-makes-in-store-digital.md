## Description

Monetizing the unseen moments.

Every day, billions of moments pass in front of screens that aren't paying attention. A customer walks in. A family browses the menu. A guest waits in the lobby. The screen plays the same loop it played an hour ago, a day ago, a week ago — regardless of who's standing there. That's wasted inventory, and most of the industry has accepted it.

Join CieloVision and WillDom for a fireside conversation at NY Tech Week on how a US founder and an Argentine engineering partner built a new category of intelligent signage — and what it took to get there.

In 18 months, CieloVision went from a services company to a platform redefining how physical screens generate revenue. WillDom, the Argentine engineering and brand partner behind the build, was the reason it held together. Together, they've built a system that turns the screens retailers, restaurants, hotels, gyms, and entertainment venues already own into responsive environments — with a measured 25.7% lift over static content in active deployments.

We'll cover:

• The pivot. What it takes to rebuild a company, a product, a brand, and a team — all at once — and survive it.
• The market timing. Why screens that have been deployed for years are suddenly the most valuable real estate nobody is monetizing.
• The category shift. Why digital signage as we know it is broken, and what changes when a display understands who's in front of it — in real time, without storing a single piece of personal data.
• The partnership model. What US–Argentina engineering partnerships look like when they actually work, and why the next wave of AI products will be built this way.
