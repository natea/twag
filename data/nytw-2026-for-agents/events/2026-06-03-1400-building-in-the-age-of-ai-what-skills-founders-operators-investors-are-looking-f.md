## Description

Featuring Bryan Kim (BK) @a16z & Mattan Griffel @CBS

Columbia Business School presents a 90-minute panel featuring top AI investor, Bryan Kim (BK) @a16z, Mattan Griffel, a two-time Y Combinator-backed entrepreneur, Co-Founder of Ophelia, and recipient of the CBS Dean's Award for Teaching Excellence, and Jacqueline Deprey, CBS MBAxMS student and co-founder. The conversation, facilitated by Gracy Sarkissian, Associate Dean, will explore the question every aspiring founder and operator asks: *what does it take to build and lead in the age of AI?* 

From the skills investors are funding, to the curriculum being built for the next generation, to what it feels like to ship a product from a dorm room, this conversation is designed to be honest, practical, and directly useful.

Open to the CBS community first, then the public.

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com
