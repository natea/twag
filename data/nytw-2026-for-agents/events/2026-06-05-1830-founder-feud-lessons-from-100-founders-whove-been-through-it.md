## Description

👨🏻‍💻 vs 👨🏻‍💻
Two teams of founders go ⚡️head to head⚡️ on stage in a Family Feud-style game show but every question is about the real challenges of building a company.

We surveyed 100+ founders on the things that actually matter when you're scaling from $1M to $10M+. The questions hit on topics like runway management, hiring mistakes, fundraising red flags, and the decisions that make or break your company at the growth stage.

The answers aren't made up. They're what founders actually think (not what they post on LinkedIn). Some are funny. Some are uncomfortable. Most are things you'll wish you knew earlier.

🧠 Why come?
Whether you're watching from the crowd or competing on stage, you'll walk away with a real look at how founders at your stage are thinking about the same problems you're facing. It's founder journey disguised as a game show fast, competitive, and way more useful than another panel.

🍸 Drinks and networking before and after. Come meet the people building alongside you.
