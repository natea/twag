## Description

The Morning Bell: CFO Conversations – maintaining a high-growth mindset, before, during, and after an IPO

This session moves beyond the technicalities to share the real-world stories—the wins, the misses, and the "grind" of getting a tech company IPO-ready. We’ve curated a powerhouse panel featuring experienced CFO's to provide perspectives and give you a 360-degree view of the public market transition.

Whether you are navigating an upcoming S-1 or building the long-term infrastructure for a future exit, come for the caffeine and stay for the high-level networking with fellow CFOs and Controllers.

Doors open at 8:30am for sign-in and breakfast. The panel will begin promptly at 9am. We encourage all to arrive early so you don't miss any portion of the discussion.  This event is taking place steps from Bryant Park's vibrant lawns so enjoy the view while you network. 

Speakers Include: 
David Borecky, Partner - a16z
Jonathan Kearl, Managing Partner - Connor Group
Adrian Mitchell, Chief Financial Officer - Warby Parker

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
