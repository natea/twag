## Description

Still Human: AI x EQ
Hosted by Lora, a new consumer app rooted in self-discovery founded by the former CPO of Hinge.

Everyone’s building AI. Very few are building for humans.

As products get more powerful, they’re also getting flatter-optimized for output, not experience.

We’re bringing together builders and product leaders working at the edge of AI to talk about:

- Emotional intelligence as a product advantage
- Designing for feeling, not just function
- Where AI is replacing connection—and where it could actually deepen it
- How AI can prompt more emotional depth from a user in products
- What the next generation of human-centered products looks like

If you care about building things in the age of AI that actually deepen connection, meaning, and belonging, this one's for you!
