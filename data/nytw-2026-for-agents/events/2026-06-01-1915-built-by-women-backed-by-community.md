## Description

Woman Owned: A Space for Women in Tech—and Those Who Champion Them


This NY Tech Week, we’re creating a space for women in tech who are building, scaling, designing, investing—and doing it all with vision. Woman Owned is for the founders, creatives, leaders, and advocates shaping the future of tech, and for those who understand the impact of women-led innovation.

This event is all about connection—with each other, with great ideas, and with the resources that help us grow. You’ll meet fellow innovators, discover organizations like NAWBO and global partner networks, and walk away with new energy and real-world support.

Come as you are. And hopefully leave more connected than you came. 🤗

Who It’s For:
	•	Women in tech—founders, operators, creatives, builders
	•	Allies and advocates who champion equity and leadership
	•	Anyone who values powerful community and good conversation

No panels. Just smart people, connection, and great energy. 

Beer, wine and snacks will be provided with many thanks to our sponsors.

Full bar available for purchase.

Sponsored by Woman Owned NYC and SocializeLA.com.

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
