​Traction is the only metric that matters. This time, you're paying for it.

​The Conversion Playbook: How to turn a cold lead into an early adopter, and an early adopter into a vocal evangelist.

​Bring your computers, landing pages, and outreach drafts. This is a hands-on workshop. At most, I will be speaking for the first 20 minutes to lay out the framework. After that, we are rolling up our sleeves and building your early growth engine together. You will leave this room with a concrete plan to hit 1,000 users.

​What you get when you register:

​The No BS Startup Guide ($179). Free. All of it.

​Over 10,000 founders have downloaded this guide. It covers idea validation, customer discovery, early fundraising, and your first 90 days of growth. It's the foundation everything in this workshop builds on. Yours the moment you sign up.

​You're not paying for a lecture. You're paying for 2 hours of focused work with someone who has done this at the pre-seed stage and helped 500+ founders do the same. The guide alone is worth the price of admission.
