## Description

An invite-only VR showroom tour for select companies and VIPs in the architecture, real estate, hospitality, attraction, live experience, and spatial storytelling industries.

Walk through the gen 2 Four Seasons Holodeck (responsible for over $500M in presales) and get hands-on with mixed reality and XR demos from Agile Lens, StorySplat, and WanderLens Lab. Experience immersive tech delivering blue-chip value in 2026.

• Now celebrating 10 years of excellence in spatial design, Agile Lens was born from a mission to redefine storytelling through immersive technology, merging design, architecture, and XR to create transformative experiences that delight and surprise audiences around the world. https://www.agilelens.com/

• DBOX is an international creative communications agency that develops strategic campaigns for luxury real estate and hospitality, combining global expertise and local insight to build brands, visualize unbuilt projects, and drive sales across the full project lifecycle. https://dbox.com/

• WanderLens Lab is a NYC/LA-based creative tech startup focusing on the application of Gaussian splatting and physical AI in real estate, and how Snap Spectacles are shaping the next generation of location-based entertainment. https://www.wanderlenslab.com/
