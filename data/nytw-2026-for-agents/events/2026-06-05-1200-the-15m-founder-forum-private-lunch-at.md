## Description

Most founder events focus on the early hustle. This lunch forum is for the later phase where the systems and models you built need to evolve. 

This is strictly for those above $15M ARR or $15M raised. It's a sector-agnostic environment in a private, members-only space at Gramercy Park that provides a closed-door environment where you can speak honestly with fellow CEOs facing the same challenges.

We skip the panels and speeches to focus on real growth. This is a dedicated space for leaders navigating the messy middle: the transition from builder to architect where you are now managing managers rather than just direct reports.

Details:
24 seats (strict cap)
No pitches. No panels.
$100 buy-in (includes lunch and bar)
Business casual dress
🚨 If accepted, attendees will be directed to Eventbrite to complete registration

Hard Requirements:
↳ Scale: $15M+ ARR (or equivalent GMV/run rate) or $15M raised from institutional investors.
↳ Leadership: You manage managers, not just direct reports.
↳ Commitment: Full-time founders only. This is your career, not an experiment.

What You Walk Away With:
↳ Specific solutions: Identify one current operational bottleneck for the group to solve through direct advice or introductions.
↳ A peer network: Connect with founders who understand the isolation of scaling and navigating board dynamics or organizational health.
↳ Strategic clarity: Permission to reject standard management advice and build systems that actually work for execution risk.

Space is strictly limited to 24 seats to ensure high-quality interaction.
