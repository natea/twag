## Description

Justworks is bringing together some of NYC's most thoughtful managers in Tech for an evening of meaningful conversation at London & Martin in the Financial District.

The night will start with a 30 minute panel discussing what it really means for leaders to give their teams true ownership. Our panel will explore how high-growth companies can flatten hierarchy, unlock accountability, and build cultures where engineering, data, design and product builders think and act like founders!

Happy Hour will immediately follow - 

This is an opportunity to connect directly with Justworks leaders — the people who shape how we build, how we ship, and how we think about the future of our product. Expect real talk about tech culture, product philosophy, AI and what it means to do your best work.

We're actively hiring! Justworks is looking for our next great technologists across engineering, product, data and design— and we expect to meet them at events like this one. If you're open to exploring what's possible, come ready for great conversations with leaders who are ready to hire!

Drinks and light bites will be provided!
