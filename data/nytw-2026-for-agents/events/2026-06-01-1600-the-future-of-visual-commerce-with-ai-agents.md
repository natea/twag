## Description

The Future of Visual Commerce with AI Agents

Visual content drives every purchase decision in e-commerce, yet the way it’s produced remains slow, manual, and fragmented.

A new paradigm is emerging: AI agents that generate, deploy, and continuously optimize visual content across channels.

This session brings together leaders across fashion, commerce, and technology to explore how agentic AI is transforming visual production, from static assets into dynamic, performance-driven systems.

We’ll cover:
	•	Why traditional visual workflows break at scale
	•	What it means to have AI agents handling content creation and iteration
	•	How brands maintain consistency while generating content across hundreds of SKUs
	•	How visual content becomes a continuous, data-driven growth engine

The session will include a live demo showcasing how Savanah AI agents can generate full product catalogs and campaign visuals, deploy them across channels, and iterate based on performance.

Followed by curated networking with founders, operators, and brand leaders.
