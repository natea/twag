## Description

A private sunset sail on the Hudson for 12 senior AI leaders building real agent systems in production. 

Hosted by Siva Surendira, founder of Lyzr.ai, who has spent the past year sitting across from leadership teams at Pepsi, WTW, Prophet, and 200+ enterprises deploying AI agents. 

This is a small, curated gathering for candid conversation about what is actually working in enterprise AI — what is moving beyond pilots, where agents are creating value, what is still breaking, and where the market is heading next.

A deliberate mid-week reset away from the crowded streets and panel rooms. No slides. No pitches. One boat, a small room of peers, and the skyline at golden hour.
