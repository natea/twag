## Description

An intimate happy hour with food and drinks for people who started their careers in politics — Hill staff, campaign operators, administration alumni, advocacy leaders — and have transitioned into tech. A gathering of builders, investors, and operators with a shared history, looking to drive impact through a different lever. Come share stories and meet new friends who made the same jump. 

Hosted by Kate Cummings, Partner at EO Ventures. 

EO Ventures was founded by Bill Helman (ex-Greylock) and Roland Fryer (Harvard economist) to back companies that increase economic mobility in America.

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com
