## Description

AI is no longer just a technology issue, it’s a workforce and operating model challenge. 

This session gives people leaders a practical framework to define what AI capability looks like in real roles and workflows, breaking it into clear, assessable skill areas that can be developed and embedded into talent systems. It shows how to apply this framework across the employee lifecycle, linking AI capability to workforce planning, hiring, performance, learning, succession, governance, and compensation. 

The focus is on turning AI ambition into practical infrastructure: clearer roles, better assessments, targeted development, and stronger organizational readiness.

Who Should Attend:

Featured Speakers:
Kenneth Matos, Ph.D., is Director, Insights Lab at HiBob.  He leads HiBob’s research on global trends in employee experience and HR best practices. Dr. Matos uses that research to educate the HiBob and media communities on the strategic impact of emerging trends in human capital management and people analytics. A former consultant to global organizations, he has published on inclusion, wellbeing, leadership, and culture. He holds degrees from Amherst, Cornell, and The George Washington University, and is a frequent speaker on engagement, flexibility, and workplace culture.
