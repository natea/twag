## Description

You keep acquiring customers. But net revenue isn't moving the way it should.

You're not sure if it's the product, the team, or the process. And you don't have time to figure it out.

This is a small, curated breakfast for founders who are in it - or can see it coming. We'll spend 20 minutes on the framework, then open it up for real conversation.

We'll cover:
- Why retention breaks down as you scale
- The customer engine every growing company needs before it's urgent
- When to hire your first CS leader - and what to build before that
