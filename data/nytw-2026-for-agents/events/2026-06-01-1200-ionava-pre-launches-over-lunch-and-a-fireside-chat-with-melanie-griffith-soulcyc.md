## Description

No ROI on your current health insurance? We see you! You care about making healthy choices. You see doctors mostly for prevention. You spend on SoulCycle, the gym, therapy, acupuncture—and almost none of it counts for anything in the system you're paying into.

ionava is designed for health. Join us for a conversation with Melanie Griffith, SoulCycle Head of Brand Experience & Master Instructor, who will tell you why the next generation of healthcare is about breaking vicious cycles and building virtuous ones.

ionava is not health insurance. It's a financial platform designed for health and wellness.

With a Vault subscription, 65–75% of your fee returns to your Wallet — user-controlled cash on a debit card. Spend it on the things that actually support your health: PCPs, dentists, labs, therapy, acupuncture, Reiki, the gym, SoulCycle...

Plus, you'll be your doctors' new favorite customer because they get paid on the spot. And you benefit from their cash prices.

The other 25–35% activates your Vault—reserved personal liquidity of $300K, $700K, or $1M, available if a Major health event occurs (terminal, chronic, or critical).

What you don't spend is yours and earns 1–3% interest in your Wallet.

Your subscription price never goes up. It's like rent control, but for your health.

Join us for a healthy lunch, open house category discovery discussions with the team that's building ionava, and a fireside chat with SoulCycle's Head of Brand Experience and Master Instructor Melanie Griffith about Virtuous Cycles vs Vicious Spirals.

This is the New York launch of ionava's Founding Membership & Early Access Program with SoulCycle. Come if you care about health, wealth, fitness, finance, longevity, or how those things might finally start working together.
