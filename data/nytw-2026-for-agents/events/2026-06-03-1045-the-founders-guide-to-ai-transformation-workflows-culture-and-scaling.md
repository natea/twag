## Description

Join us for an interactive workshop on how Google scales with AI—and how you can apply those same frameworks to your startup architecture.

As the startup landscape in 2026 shifts toward hyper-efficiency, the goal is no longer just "integrating AI"—it's about becoming an AI-native organization. In this session, we’re opening the hood on Google’s internal operations. We’ll explore how we use our own AI tools to accelerate code generation, automate complex sales triage, and drive market intelligence, and how these internal wins serve as the roadmap for your growth.

This is a workshop-style session designed for high-potential AI founders. We will move beyond the slides to discuss real-world implementation, agentic workflows, and the specific technical hurdles of scaling in 2026.

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
