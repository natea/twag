## Description

Flowstate x Leave Normal Behind Present: Human Flourishing in the Age of AI

NY Tech Week moves fast. The Oasis is designed as a place to slow down, reconnect, and have the conversations that actually matter.

For the opening gathering of the series, we're bringing together founders, builders, creatives, and ecosystem leaders to explore one central question: How do we ensure technology advances human flourishing rather than diminishes it?
This event is part of a16z #NYTechWeek, a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem.

Thank you to our partners at Vibe Space, Boardy, The AI Collective, and Leave Normal Behind, and to our venue partner BELONG NYC for hosting the Oasis throughout the week.

About our venue partner, BELONG NYC

BELONG NYC (@belong.nyc) is a hospitality-driven workspace and urban sanctuary in the heart of SoHo. Think airport lounge meets coworking: a high-design, high-service environment purpose-built for the in-between moments of life in motion. Beyond hosting our series, BELONG offers Flowstate members on-demand access to private meeting rooms, phone booths, and hot desks, plus showers and changing facilities, secure luggage storage, and rentable lockers throughout Tech Week. If you're juggling flights, workouts, dinners, and back-to-back meetings, it's the place to reset and keep going.
