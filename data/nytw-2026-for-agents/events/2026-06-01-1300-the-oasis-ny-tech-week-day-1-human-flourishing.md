## Description

Flowstate x Leave Normal Behind Present: Human Flourishing in the Age of AI

NY Tech Week moves fast. The Oasis is designed as a place to slow down, reconnect, and have the conversations that actually matter.

For the opening gathering of the series, we are bringing together founders, builders, creatives, and ecosystem leaders to explore one central question:

How do we ensure technology advances human flourishing rather than diminish it?

The Programming:

Panel I: Technology & Human Flourishing

A conversation exploring the evolving boundary between human intelligence and artificial intelligence: what should technology automate, and what should remain deeply human? As AI increasingly shapes how we think, create, work, and connect, this panel examines the tradeoffs between convenience and capability, efficiency and meaning, augmentation and replacement. Together, we’ll explore how to use AI to enhance human flourishing without losing the curiosity, judgment, creativity, and emotional depth that make us human in the first place

Moderated by Claire Beluga (@clairexplains)

Panel II: AI Trust & Deepfakes

As AI lowers the barrier to building, creating, and shipping faster than ever before, it also introduces a new wave of vulnerabilities that many founders, creators, and teams are unprepared for. This panel explores the hidden security, privacy, and trust risks emerging in the age of AI, from data exposure and insecure AI-generated code to deepfakes, synthetic identity, and overreliance on systems we don’t fully understand. A practical conversation on how to use AI thoughtfully, securely, and responsibly while navigating an increasingly automated world.

Moderated by Rob Fajardo (@rob_fajardo)
