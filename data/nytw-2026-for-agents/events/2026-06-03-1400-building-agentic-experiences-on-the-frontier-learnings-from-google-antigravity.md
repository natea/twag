## Description

Every month brings new frontier model capabilities, agent extensibility points, standards, and more. These can often be overwhelming - what will last the test of time vs just momentary fads? what should you build that will scale with future improvements at the model level? what moves the needle the most for the end user experience?

In this deep-dive presentation straddling technical and product, we will share the tenets that we’ve anchored around while we have developed AI products for the last few years, most latest being Google Antigravity, Google’s agent-first platform. We will share the decisions that we made while designing and shipping Antigravity, grounded in real examples and demonstrations of today’s frontier capabilities, and how the thinking could be applied to agentic products more generally.

The frontier constantly changes, and you’ll learn how to innovate on this constant change, not just how to maximize today’s fleeting frontier.
