## Description

Mandarin × Markets is a gathering for globally minded professionals with demonstrated professional and/or academic engagement with China, its markets, technology, culture, and role in the global economy.

China is a global leader in technology and artificial intelligence. During a week dedicated to what's next in tech, that conversation belongs in the room and we're bringing it here.

No panels. No pitches. Just good conversation and a curated room of people who think seriously about China; whether you work in tech, finance, policy, media, or somewhere in between.

We'll also come with prompt questions and conversation starters so you leave not just with new contacts, but with a sharper read on China's rising presence in global business.

Mandarin speakers will find people to practice with. Non-Mandarin speakers will find people worth knowing.

Come curious. Leave connected.
