## Description

Most events celebrate the raise.

This one is about what comes after.
The Exit Breakfast is for founders who've built something real and now they're thinking about what's next. Exits. Secondaries. M&A. Strategic moves. The path from here to there.

No keynotes. No pitches. Just honest conversation over breakfast about how to navigate acquisitions, strategic opportunities, IPOs, secondary sales, and what the exit process actually looks like from the inside.

Sexy Soho venue. Good coffee. The right people.
