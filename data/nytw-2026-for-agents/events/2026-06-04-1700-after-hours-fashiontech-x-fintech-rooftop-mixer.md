## Description

Throughout Tech Week, it’s been nonstop panels, fireside chats, networking events, and workshops — now it’s time to unwind.
​Join 99 YARDS and Breakline Society for After Hours: FashionTech X FinTech Rooftop Mixer, an exclusive indoor rooftop gathering where fashion, technology, finance, and culture collide. Step away from the formal conversations, grab a drink, enjoy the DJ vibes, and connect with an incredible mix of FashionTech founders, FinTech innovators, investors, creatives, models, and industry leaders in a more relaxed setting.

​About 99 YARDS & Breakline Society:
​99 YARDS is an AI-powered fashion ecosystem connecting brands, creatives, agencies, manufacturers, and fashion professionals into one streamlined network. We’re building the future of fashion industry collaboration, talent discovery, service booking, and idea execution — all in one place.

​Breakline Society, a community bringing together forward-thinking founders, operators, creatives, and innovators across emerging industries, has designed this mixer to spark authentic conversations and meaningful connections across both FashionTech and FinTech.

​With confirmed models, startup founders, investors, fashion executives, and creatives attending, this is the perfect opportunity to network organically while enjoying an elevated rooftop atmosphere.

​Whether you're in fashion, tech, finance, investing, or simply curious about where these industries are headed next — come hang out, connect, and enjoy a night of good people, great energy, and unforgettable conversations.

​Hosed by Tony Henry, Founder, & CEO of 99 YARDS

​is a fashion producer and entrepreneur with over 10 years of experience in the fashion industry, producing fashion shows and creative experiences across major platforms, including New York Fashion Week (NYFW) and Miami Swim Week. Throughout his career, he has worked closely with designers, models, production teams, and creative talent to bring fashion concepts to life on the runway.

​

​Lucia Lu, Founder & CEO Of Breakline Society

In 2025, she founded Breakline Society, a curated network connecting Chinese founders, investors, and creatives across AI, FinTech, and fashion. Within its first year, Breakline hosted 10+ private salons and founder roundtables across New York, bringing together hundreds of entrepreneurs, operators, and capital partners.
