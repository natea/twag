## Description

Come spend an evening building with Shaped, a retrieval API that sits on top of your data and returns ranked, minimal context. 

Why? Because the best agents in the world are backed by good retrieval. They don't drown in 200 search results for every query, they get the top, ranked ones. 

We'll guide you on setting up your first retrieval engine and wiring it to your agent.  Feel free to bring a project you've been sitting on, or just show up with a rough idea. 

You'll get direct API access and real talk from people running this at scale.
