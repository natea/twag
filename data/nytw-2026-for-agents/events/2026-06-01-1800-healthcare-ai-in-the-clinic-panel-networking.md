## Description

The conversation around AI in healthcare has gotten loud. Most of it skips the hard parts: regulatory exposure, integration complexity, and whether the investment actually shows up in the numbers.

Confido Health is bringing together investors, operators, legal experts, and healthcare leaders for an honest panel on what is actually changing across outpatient healthcare.

The discussion covers three areas that keep coming up in boardrooms and operating committees: how AI is reshaping M&A strategy and investment theses in outpatient providers, where regulation is heading and what that means for organizations deploying AI today, and what it takes operationally to move from pilot to production across multi-location groups.

Whether you run operations at a DSO or MSO, advise PE-backed healthcare platforms, or are evaluating AI for your own organization, this panel is designed to be useful, not theoretical.

Moderated by Dr. Raihan Faroqui, VP of Partnerships at Confido Health, the panel brings together voices that rarely share a stage.

Confido Health has deployed across more than 1,000 sites, and those deployments inform the conversation throughout. But this is not a product pitch. The goal is to give PE operating partners, AI and technology advisors, MSO and DSO leaders, and healthcare operators a room where the questions are practical and the answers are grounded in what these groups are seeing right now.
