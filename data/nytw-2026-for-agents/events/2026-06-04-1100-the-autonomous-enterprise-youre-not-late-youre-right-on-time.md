## Description

Join JeenAI and WillDom to see how a unified AI foundation goes from AI-curious to autonomous in 24 months, connecting directly to your data, APIs, and SaaS tools, with no workarounds required. 

Walk away with the playbook for sequencing real wins on infrastructure you fully control. We'll unpack what "autonomous" really means, how AI moves into the decision-making layer of the business without losing oversight, and where companies typically land at 3, 6, 12, and 24 months in.
You'll see how Jeen's AI infrastructure connects directly to your enterprise systems, APIs, and data—on-prem or in the cloud, with full observability and control and how WillDom's senior delivery team turns it into production outcomes.
