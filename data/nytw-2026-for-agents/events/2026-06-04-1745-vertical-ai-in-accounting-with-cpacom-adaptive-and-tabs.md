## Description

Join us for an evening of drinks, networking, and a conversation the accounting profession is overdue for.

General-purpose AI is impressive. It can summarize, draft, and reason across an enormous range of tasks. But accounting and tax aren't general problems. They're governed by rules that change, edge cases that carry liability, and judgment calls that no model gets right just by being large. That gap, between what AI can do and what accounting actually demands, is where the most consequential work in the profession is happening right now.

This panel brings together thought leaders, firm leaders from the profession, and the builders working at that frontier to share what's working in practice, where the hype still outpaces reality, and what separates AI that holds up in an accounting environment from AI that doesn't.

If you're leading a firm or advising one, this is a conversation worth being in the room for.

Joining us for the panel:

Seema Amble, Partner at a16z (moderator)
Richard Kopelman, CEO of Aprio
Erik Asgeirsson, President & CEO of CPA.com
Matt Calvano, CEO & Co-Founder of Adaptive
Ali Hussain, CEO & Co-Founder of Tabs

Hosted at CPA.com headquarters, with views overlooking Central Park.
