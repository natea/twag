#NYCTECHWEEK: The Founders & Funders Plus-One Party | Presented by MongoDB Ventures & Met Through Friends

Skip the cold outreach and awkward intros - it’s time to meet your next founder or funder, through friends.

On Tuesday evening of #NYCTechWeek, we’re bringing together a hand-selected group of leading AI builders and backers for an intimate night of high-intent connection, designed so you can actually meet, engage with, and learn from everyone in the room.

The twist: you don’t come alone, you bring a friend. If you’re a founder, bring a VC with exceptional vision. If you’re a VC, bring a founder with a compelling idea. Every attendee is vouched for and trusted by this intentionally crafted community. 

Here’s what you can expect: 
-A curated guest list, including some of NYC’s top tech founders and investors 
-Structured “mini-dates,” led by the talented team at Met Through Friends, to ensure you meet, connect with, and learn from everyone
-A closing reception with an open bar, passed apps, and more

Spots are extremely limited, and this is a plus-one-only experience. To be considered, you must apply with the person you plan to bring.

If you’re building, funding, or looking for your next big bet - this is where you meet them. Apply to be considered now!

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.

P.S. You may know Met Through Friends from their viral dating events--please note that this is a professional networking event and NOT for dating. Met Through Friends is taking their expertise in facilitating high-quality connections to help great founders and funders meet each other and take their ideas to the next level.
