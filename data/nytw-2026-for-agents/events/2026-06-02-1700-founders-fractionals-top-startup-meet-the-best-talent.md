## Description

Are you a founder trying to move faster without hiring full-time too early?

Are you a senior operator looking to work with high-potential startups in a more flexible way?

Founders & Fractionals brings NYC's most ambitious startups and exceptional senior operators into one curated room for meaningful connections, rapid-fire talent pitches, and real team-building opportunities.

For founders, it’s a chance to meet proven fractional talent across GTM, growth, sales, engineering, design, HR, legal, finance, ops, AI, and beyond. The kind of senior support that helps you move faster without over-hiring.

For talent, it’s a chance to get in front of early and growth-stage companies, sharpen your fractional positioning, and connect with founders actively looking for flexible senior support.

Launched in Los Angeles, Founders & Fractionals has already brought hundreds of founders and senior operators together, creating meaningful founder-talent connections across key startup functions.

The evening will bring together top founders and senior operators for a curated mixer, rapid-fire expert pitches, founder-talent matchmaking, and practical conversations on building and joining elite fractional teams.
