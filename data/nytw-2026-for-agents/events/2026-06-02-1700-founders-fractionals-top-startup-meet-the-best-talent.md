## Description

Are you a founder trying to move faster without hiring full-time too early?

Are you a senior operator looking to work with high-potential startups in a more flexible way?

Founders & Fractionals, Build Momentum, and Miro are bringing NYC’s most ambitious startups and exceptional senior operators into one curated room for meaningful connections, rapid-fire talent pitches, and real team-building opportunities.

Together, we’ll explore how startups are building faster, leaner, and more flexible teams, and how Miro is helping founders accelerate growth as the AI-native operating system for startups.

For founders, this is a chance to meet vetted senior operators across GTM, growth, sales, engineering, design, HR, legal, finance, ops, AI, and more, and find flexible support before hiring full-time.

For fractional operators, this is a chance to get in front of founders actively looking for senior support, sharpen your positioning, and build relationships that can lead to advisory, consulting, fractional, or long-term startup roles.

The evening will include a curated mixer, rapid-fire expert pitches, founder-talent matchmaking, and practical conversations on how elite startup teams are being built today.

The first 50 attendees will receive a complimentary drink ticket, thanks to Miro.

Learn more at https://foundersandfractionals.com/

Register to be eligible for a complimentary 15-minute post-event strategy call with Founders & Fractionals.

About Miro

Miro is the AI-native operating system for startups. It makes AI multiplayer, helping founders, teams, and agents work together in one shared space. In Miro, the canvas becomes the prompt: diagrams, notes, journeys, workflows, and prototypes become context for AI to summarize, prioritize, and turn into docs, slides, prototypes, and even code.

About Build Momentum

Build Momentum helps technical founders, domain experts, and emerging companies turn strong ideas into real companies through product, go-to-market, fundraising readiness, U.S. market entry, and team-building support.

About Founders & Fractionals

Founders & Fractionals connects high-potential startups with exceptional senior operators across GTM, growth, product, engineering, design, finance, legal, HR, AI, operations, and strategy.
