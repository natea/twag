## Description

Join the Unique AI team for an evening of Old World versus New World wines, paired with bites and great conversation. We'll be tasting our way through a curated selection of European and American wines side by side, same grapes and different worlds, in a nod to Unique AI's own journey: founded in Zurich and now making our mark in the US market.

This event brings together two kinds of people: those who invest in companies like ours and those who would use what we build at financial institutions. Expect a lively mix of structured tasting and open discussion on the trends, challenges, and opportunities shaping AI adoption across the industry.

Come ready to taste, compare, and debate, whether that's Burgundy versus Willamette Valley or what agentic AI actually means for your firm in 2026. We build secure AI made specifically for hedge funds, asset managers, and private wealth, and there's no better way to get to know us than over a good glass of wine.

Learn more about our enterprise platform and orchestration layer powering end-to-end workflows at https://www.unique.ai/.

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
