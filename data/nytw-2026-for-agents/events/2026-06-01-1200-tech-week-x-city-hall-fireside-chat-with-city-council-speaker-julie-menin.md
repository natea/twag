## Description

Join us for a New York Tech Week first: a conversation inside City Hall, with NYC Council Speaker Julie Menin, on how the world's most dynamic city can maintain its edge in talent, tech, and innovation during the biggest economic and societal transformation since the Industrial Revolution.

As industries and institutions are reshaped, New York faces an extraordinary opportunity: to cement itself as the city where the next generation of great companies is built. How can NYC continue to attract the founders, investors, and talent defining the AI economy? What makes New York's startup ecosystem distinct — and how do we keep it that way? And which sectors and use cases are positioned to lead?

Speaker Menin joins a16z General Partner David Haber in City Hall's legendary Red Room for a conversation on building a culture of innovation in New York — from the neighborhoods and industries that make this city unique, to the ambition required to stay ahead. This discussion will explore what it looks like to make NYC the defining tech hub of this era.

This event is a part of #NewYorkTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
