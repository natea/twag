## Description

Join us for the Next-Gen Innovation Summit — Future Builders: From Vision to Impact, a dynamic gathering that will bring together global leaders across technology, capital, policy, and social impact.

This year’s inaugural Summit will explore how the next generation of innovators, investors, and public leaders are turning bold ideas into scalable, real-world solutions. Convening voices from industry, academia, and government, the program will highlight the power of cross-sector collaboration in shaping the future of cities and global innovation ecosystems.

Together, we will explore critical questions at the intersection of innovation and impact:
-How can emerging technologies like AI accelerate inclusive growth and opportunity?
-What does it take to translate innovation into scalable solutions across education, healthcare, and communities?
-How can capital, policy, and talent align to build more equitable and future-ready societies?

Expect thought-provoking keynotes, fireside chats, and interactive panels featuring leaders from high-impact organizations, alongside visionary founders and next-generation changemakers.

The program will also feature an invite-only “Designing for the Future” workshop, where intergenerational leadership teams will collaborate on actionable solutions to advance innovation with purpose.

Whether you are an executive, investor, technologist, policymaker, or emerging leader, this Summit will offer a unique opportunity to connect, learn, and help shape what’s next.

Space is limited. RSVP required.
