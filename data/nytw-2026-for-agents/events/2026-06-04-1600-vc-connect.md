## Description

VC Connect is an exclusive and carefully curated gathering designed to break away from the traditional pitch format.

The evening will begin with a reverse pitch session, where selected VCs will share their fund focus, investment thesis, and what they look for in founders — offering a unique opportunity to understand the ecosystem from the investor’s perspective.

Following the conversation, attendees will have the opportunity to connect directly with participating investors in a more relaxed setting designed to encourage meaningful conversations, strategic networking, and potential opportunities.

The participating funds invest in early-stage startups across Latin America and the United States, from pre-seed to Series A.

This edition will feature investors from leading funds across the region, including:
