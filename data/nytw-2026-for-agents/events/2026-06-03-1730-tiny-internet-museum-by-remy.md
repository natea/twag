## Description

The internet is full of almost-finished things.

- Half-built apps. 
- Abandoned Notion docs. 
- Figma files nobody has opened in 2 years. 
- Notes app ramblings that were definitely going to be a startup.

We're putting them on a wall and bringing them back to life.

Tiny Internet Museum is a gallery of "almosts" — an exhibit of abandoned ideas, forgotten prototypes, and digital artifacts from builders and creatives. Wander the space museum-style, leave reactions, vote on your favorites, and connect with founders, almost-founders, operators, product managers, and investors who all share the same graveyard of ideas.

Just the honest, human side of building things.

Featuring a Q&A session from Dmitry Shapiro, CEO of MindStudio, who is also the ex-CTO of MySpace, ex-PM Lead at Google, 3x founder with $140M+ raised, on what he's learned about product lifecycle across decades of building, scaling, and starting over.

https://www.linkedin.com/in/dmitry-shapiro-a2b1/

Building a product used to require a team, a budget, and months of runway. AI has changed that and the gap between idea and shipped product has never been smaller. Remy is proof. 

The world's first product agent, Remy turns your abandoned spec into a real, full-stack application: backend, frontend, database, auth, integrations, and deployment. Your idea didn't fail because it was bad. It failed because building was hard. That excuse is gone.

🎁 Submit your most abandoned ideas for a chance to win raffle prizes! Prizes include: Meta Ray-Bans, free credits, and access to Remy:
https://forms.gle/7KcJDVafUTtcZQhD7

Come wander. Come remember. Come build.

Co-hosted by LvlUp Ventures and Sip & Scale.
