## Description

Investors. Founders. Operators. One studio

Finding Harmony, the musical networking experience, is bringing a jam session to NY Tech Week. 

The concept is simple, when you play music with your peers, the relationship opens up, friendships are made, and business gets done. 

For this session, we all have the unified goal of playing Three Little Birds, Lovely Day, and Stand By Me.

Once we're finished, we’ll do our ask session, which is where the connections and intros get made. 

No need to travel in with your gear….

We’ll have drums, keys, guitars, basses, mics, and a whole percussion closet to raid. 

Make this the #NYTECHWEEK one to remember.

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek, a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
