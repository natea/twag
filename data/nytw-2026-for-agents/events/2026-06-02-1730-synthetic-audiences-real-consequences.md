## Description

Synthetic audiences and digital twins are changing how companies understand their customers. 

Used well, they close the gap between what people say and what they actually do. Used poorly, they make that gap invisible — and the decisions built on them more confident and more wrong.

Join Panoplai for an evening with founders, marketers and innovators who’ve been humbled by that gap — and who are using AI to close it. Honest conversation about what actually works, what’s overhyped, and what it looks like when you finally stop guessing about your audience.

Why come:
- Hear from practitioners who’ve built AI audience intelligence inside real companies — and hit the limits
- Get an honest take on where AI and digital twins create real value and where they produce confident nonsense
- Walk away with a clearer picture of how the best teams are making faster, better decisions about their customers

For: 
Insight professionals, marketers, innovators and strategists who make decisions about people they’re not always sure they understand.

The panel:
Neil Dixit — CEO, Panoplai
Andrea Harrison — Gallo. Brand-operator POV on making AI audience work usable inside a big consumer business.
Phil Ahad — Managing Director of Data, Cint. The data-infrastructure POV on what synthetic audiences and digital twins need to actually hold up.

Moderated by Josh Ingram, TheMostWanted.

Panel discussion followed by happy hour at Pianos NYC.
