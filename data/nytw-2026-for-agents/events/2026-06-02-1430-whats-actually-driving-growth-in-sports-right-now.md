About the Event
Sports is evolving faster than ever but what is real vs. hype? From the rise of womens sports to the expansion of betting and digital platforms, join us for a conversation on where the biggest opportunities are emerging and how builders can position themselves to win. Our speakers bring decades of experience across sports, corporate strategy, and venture backed growth to break down what is actually driving the next wave.
