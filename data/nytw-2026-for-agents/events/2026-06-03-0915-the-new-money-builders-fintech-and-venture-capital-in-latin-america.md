## Description

A look into the new generation of companies redefining how money moves, invests, and is built across Latin America. From fintech infrastructure and venture capital to payments, crypto, and embedded finance, this panel will bring together founders and ecosystem leaders to discuss why the region has become one of the most attractive markets for financial innovation.

What are investors looking for today? Where are the next opportunities? How do you build scalable companies in highly volatile environments? A conversation about capital, technology, and the financial future of the region.
