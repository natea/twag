## Description

What is HuCapital?

​​​​​​HuCapital is an AI social platform uniting entrepreneurial creatives and professionals through 1:1, peer group and prompted matches; powered by our Al Engine Huey. Learn more at hucapital.co.

About This Event

HuCapital is teaming up with New York Tech Week to bring together founders, builders, and tech professionals for a hands-on experience exploring AI and the Future of Work. This event blends insight and execution, starting with a fireside chat featuring investors and operators shaping the future of work, followed by a hands-on vibe coding session where attendees will actively build AI-powered solutions.

Attendees will collaborate in real time using tools like Lovable to prototype solutions that address real challenges across hiring, productivity, and access to opportunity in an evolving workforce.

This experience is designed to bring together people who are not only thinking about the future of work, but actively building it!

Key Takeaways

Understand What’s Fundable:
Learn what VCs are actually looking for in the future of work space and how to position AI-driven ideas to be investable and scalable.

See How AI Is Being Applied:
Gain real insight into how founders are using AI to solve challenges across hiring, productivity, and skill development.

Navigate the Shift in Hiring & Talent:
Understand how Talent and People leaders are adapting to AI-driven changes in workforce strategy, recruiting, and evaluation.

Leverage Community as a Competitive Advantage:
Explore how social capital, networks, and ecosystems are becoming key drivers of opportunity and career mobility.

Go From Idea to Prototype:
Learn how to quickly build and test ideas using vibe coding and AI tools, and walk away with something tangible.
