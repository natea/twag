## Description

💡 What’s the Ugly Truth

Beauty tech is booming, but growth has made the category louder, not clearer. AI diagnostics, AR try-ons, personalization, and smart devices are pulling more founders into the space, but more innovation also means more hype, more copycats, and more brands fighting for the same customer attention.

For founders, a strong product is just the entry fee. The real edge comes from knowing what actually drives trust, repeat purchase, and loyalty in a market where branding and novelty can fake momentum. This room is for people who want to separate signal from noise and figure out which trends are truly shaping the future of beauty tech.

🎯 Why You Should Attend

- Hear from a pioneer in beauty tech and understand what they are building, how they got there, and what they are paying attention to now.
- Connect with other beauty tech founders who are relentlessly innovating and building through the noise.
- Understand shifting consumer behavior, changing demand, and what customers are looking for now.

🙋 Who's Getting In This Room

- Beauty tech founders
- Beauty business owners
- Consumer and brand experts
- Investors interested in beauty tech and consumer companies

🎤  Who’s Taking the Stage
Zehra Soysal - Founder & CEO of Savanah.ai

Zehra's career journey began at Google in business strategy, followed by impactful roles at BCG, where she worked across diverse sectors in Europe, the Middle East, and Asia. She played a key role in establishing a tech joint venture with Turkey's largest conglomerate, igniting her passion for innovative business technologies. She earned her MBA from Harvard Business School in 2022. Post-MBA, she joined Metaphysic.ai in New York, where she was instrumental in scaling operations and driving strategic growth through its $1.4B acquisition. She is now the founder and CEO of Savanah.ai, a New York-based visual AI platform helping brands create e-commerce imagery at scale and optimize conversion.


Yarden Horwitz - Co-Founder of Spate

Yarden Horwitz is the Co-founder of Spate, an AI-driven platform used by 250+ beauty brands like Estée Lauder, L’Oréal, and P&G to make data-driven decisions. Before founding Spate in 2018, Yarden worked at Google, launching the first Google Trends Report and co-founding the trendspotting division. She’s been named a top female leader by Entrepreneur Magazine and sits on the boards of CEW and NewBeauty Brain Trust. Yarden is a frequent speaker at beauty events like CEW’s State of the Beauty Industry and her work has been featured in publications including WWD, Vogue Business, and the New York Times.


Bonnie Young - Founder & Chief Creative at BY. Bonnie Young

Bonnie Young is founder of the luxury brand BY. Bonnie Young, known for its timeless, artful, and purposeful design that merge modern couture with effortless ease. She began her career at Ralph Lauren and Donna Karan before launching her own label. Her work has appeared in leading publications including Vogue, Forbes, and The New York Times, and has been worn by celebrities and Middle Eastern royalty. Young has collaborated with Adidas, Swarovski, and The Hunger Games: Mockingjay Part 2. She is also an author, photographer, philanthropist, CFDA member, and recipient of the 2025 FGI Ready-to-Wear Award for excellence.
