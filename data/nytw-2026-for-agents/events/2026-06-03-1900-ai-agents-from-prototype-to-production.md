## Description

📋 Event Description

Everyone's building AI demos. This is where you build AI agents that actually work.

AI Agents: From Prototype to Production is a one-evening, in-person sprint for builders who want to go beyond the ChatGPT wrapper and ship something real. Powered by Phinite and GMI, this is your chance to build a production-ready multi-agent application — with real infrastructure, real guardrails, and real stakes.

The top three teams get to pitch their project to a VC.

The Challenge
Build a real-world solution using AI agents. Not a demo. Not a proof-of-concept vibe. Something that could actually run in production — hitting real APIs, talking to databases, handling real workflows.

You'll have access to:

Phinite is building the Multi Agent OS — the infrastructure layer for the multi-agent era. Every enterprise deploying AI agents needs identity, observability, and governance underneath them. That's what Phinite ships, out of the box.

GMI — an AI-native inference cloud built for production AI, combining serverless scaling and dedicated GPU infrastructure with predictable performance and cost.

What to Expect

- Sponsor intros + tool walkthroughs at kickoff so you hit the ground running
- 2+ hours of focused build time
- Team formation on-site or come with your crew
- Demos + judging at the end — top 3 pitch to a VC
- Food & drinks provided
