## Description

Join Retro Report, New Media Ventures and New Rising Ventures during New York Tech Week to explore how artificial intelligence and innovative technology are revolutionizing education and shaping civic participation. This event examines how educators and innovators are leveraging AI and tech tools to transform learning, foster civic awareness, and promote equity. Together, we’ll discuss the future of AI in education and its broader societal impact.

Moderated by Liz Willen, editor-in-chief of The Hechinger Report, the event features conversations with educators, entrepreneurs, and leaders at the forefront of AI, civic education, and public-interest technology. Panel discussions will explore how AI can spark civic action, nurture responsible tech practices, and prepare the next generation to tackle social challenges.

The event will include a networking reception with drinks and light bites, offering a unique chance to connect with professionals, foundations, investors and changemakers. Discover how education technology is shaping a more equitable and engaging future for all.

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
