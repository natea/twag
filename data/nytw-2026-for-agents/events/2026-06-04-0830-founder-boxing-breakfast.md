## Description

Join us for a boxing class hosted by Outlander VC, Draper, BOXCLUB, NEXT, & Rho!

​We're huge fans of boxing. Incredible workout that pushes you to your limits and challenges you to push past them. Kind of like being a founder at the pre-seed, seed, or Series A stage, right?

Let's have an awesome morning workout + breakfast after to kick off your day with a punch 🥊

Pre-seed to Series A founders and VCs welcome!

Meet the hosts:

​Draper Associates is an early-stage venture capital firm, funding the future first. We invest from pre-seed to Series A, backing ideas across AI, robotics, biotech, defense, crypto, space, and deep tech.

At BOXCLUB, we provide elite boxing fitness services to working professionals to ensure that they are fit, both in body and mind, to conquer their daily tasks and fulfill their life ambitions.

Outlander VC is an early-stage venture capital firm backing visionary founders at the pre-seed and seed stages. Outlander's GPs boast 18 unicorns as first or early checks (e.g., Scale, Lyft, Wish, Gusto, Postmates, SpaceX, and more).


NEXT powered by Shulman Rogers, voted the #1 Law Firm in the US for Enabling Startup Success, helps founders build the legal foundation investors expect. With fixed-fee startup packages, transparent pricing, and senior startup attorneys, NEXT delivers practical legal support built for founder budgets. From formation through financing and growth, NEXT gives startups the strategic counsel they need to move faster and scale with confidence.

​​Rho is the all-in-one banking platform for startups and the accountants supporting them. 

​​​​Rho is a fintech company, not a bank. Checking and card services are provided by Webster Bank, N.A., member FDIC; savings account services are provided by American Deposit Management Co. and its partner banks.

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
