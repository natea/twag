## Description

Why would investors put $14M behind faces that don’t exist yet, and make it one of Europe’s biggest pre-seed bets?

Because avatars are no longer just a visual layer. They’re becoming products, performers, and scalable businesses.

Join us for a look inside Europe’s biggest avatar studio and see what it actually takes to build real-time digital humans people want to watch, interact with, and come back to.

We’ll cover:

* the tech stack behind live, responsive avatars
* how they become products and revenue streams
* what it takes to move from prototype to deployment
* and what happens when these faces don’t start from zero, but already come with followers, fandom, and reach

No fluff. Just a talk about what’s already happening, what’s coming next, and why this space is moving faster than most people realize, delivered by our CEO & Founder, Vlastimil Venclik.
