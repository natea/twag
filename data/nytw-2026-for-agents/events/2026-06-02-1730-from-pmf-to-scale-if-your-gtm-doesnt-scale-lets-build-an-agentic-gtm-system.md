## Description

You closed your first customers on grit. The problem is grit doesn't compound.

Most founders hit the same wall: product-market fit is real, revenue is coming in, but growth feels like pushing a boulder uphill every single morning. Your GTM motion is a patchwork of one-off plays, manual outreach, and tribal knowledge locked in someone's head. You know it won't survive the next stage. You just haven't had time to fix it.

This workshop transforms that in real time. You will walk out with 1) clarity on the systems mindset you need to scale, 2) a working piece, and 3) a downloaded DragonflySkill for you to use during the workshop with Claude.

Where should you start? RSVP to receive via email a free GTM assessment that takes 15 minutes. This diagnostic assessment will help you determine your GTM stage.

Come ready to work!
