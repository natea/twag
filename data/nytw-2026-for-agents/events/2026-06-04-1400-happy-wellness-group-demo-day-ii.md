## Description

Demo Day II by Happy Wellness Group will bring together one of the most concentrated gatherings of wellness capital ever assembled in a single room. More than 600 curated investors, executives, and operators will gather for this flagship Tech Week event.

The main event, taking place in a gorgeous theater in midtown Manhattan, is a fast-paced, Shark Tank–style pitch competition featuring 10 innovative companies across wellness technology, consumer products, and science-driven performance. Each founder will have just three minutes to command the room. Top institutional and strategic investors will judge live, and the audience will vote in real time to crown the winner.

Every seat in the room holds real capital, and every pitch has the potential to change a company’s trajectory. This is where the next category-defining wellness brands may be discovered.
