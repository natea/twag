## Description

The grid is under pressure and is struggling to keep pace with the digital economy. Electricity demand is surging, utility bills are climbing, and the infrastructure we have wasn't built for the loads we're asking it to carry. The answer isn't just building more of the same. It's building smarter. PowerLines is bringing together leading gridtech companies to talk about what comes next.

Join us for a conversation with leading gridtech innovators to showcase the technologies reshaping how we move and manage power. 

The conversation will dig into what's blocking faster deployment, regulatory frameworks that haven't caught up, market structures that don't reward efficiency, and procurement bottlenecks slowing adoption, and what it takes to break through. The companies in this room are building the software layer, the hardware stack, and the financing models that will define the next generation of grid infrastructure.

The grid of the future won't be built from scratch. It'll be unlocked. Join us.

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
