## Description

You don't need to know how to code. You just need an idea and a laptop.  

Join Female Founder Collective and Vibe Code Mama for a hands-on morning exploring how founders can use AI to rapidly prototype tools, creative ideas, and products - live, together, in the room. No technical background required. Just curiosity, experimentation, and a willingness to build.

Vibe coding is the practice of building apps, tools, and products using AI conversationally - describing what you want in natural language and collaborating with AI systems to help bring ideas to life. It’s changing who gets to build and expanding what's possible for founders at every stage.

In this interactive lab, you'll get a crash course in vibe coding fundamentals, then open your laptop and start building - with real-time feedback from those who do this every day. We'll close with live demos where volunteers share what they built and get feedback from the room.  

The format: 
- Vibe Coding 101 
- Live build session with pro vibe coders 
- Founder demos + feedback 
- Networking  

For the event:
- Bring your laptop
- Sign up for a Claude account

During this session you will be hosted by CEO of Female Founder Collective, Alison Wyatt, along with the Founder of Vibe Code Mama, Jenna Cao, and  Head of Operations and Partnerships of Vibe Code Mama, Kendall Crocker - two builders who believe female founders have the opportunity to shape the next era of AI adoption and growth.  

Capacity is limited. Come ready to build.
