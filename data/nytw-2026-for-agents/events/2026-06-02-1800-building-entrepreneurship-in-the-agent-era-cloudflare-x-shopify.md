## Description

Join us for a fireside conversation exploring how AI agents are reshaping entrepreneurship, commerce, and the fundamental architecture of the internet.

Moderated by Fatima Yusuf (Sr. Director, Growth at Cloudflare), the panel features Stephanie Cohen (Chief Strategy Officer at Cloudflare), Brennan Loh (VP of Brand at Shopify),  Rita Kozlov (VP of Product at Cloudflare) and Nick Grossman (General Partner at Union Square Ventures). 

Together, they’ll discuss the next frontier of autonomous systems and what it means for developers, founders, merchants, and the future of online business.

Hosted at Shopify’s Soho space, this will be a relaxed evening with great conversations, food, drinks, and a chance to connect with founders, builders, and merchants across the NYC tech ecosystem.

This event is part of #NYTechWeek — a week of events hosted by VCs and startups bringing together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
