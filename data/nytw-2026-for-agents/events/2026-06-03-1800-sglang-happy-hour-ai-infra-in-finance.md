## Description

Join SGLang, HOF Capital, Crusoe, Cloudflare, and Arklex AI for an evening of lightning talks and conversation with the people building inference systems for finance.

​Inference is the next frontier in finance. LLMs are moving beyond experimental tooling into latency-sensitive production workflows across trading, research, compliance, and risk. The constraints that come with that — throughput, structured outputs, on-prem deployment, cost per token — are now first-class strategic concerns.

​What to expect
​5 lightning talks from inference engineers, infrastructure providers, founders, and researchers, followed by an open happy hour with drinks and food.
