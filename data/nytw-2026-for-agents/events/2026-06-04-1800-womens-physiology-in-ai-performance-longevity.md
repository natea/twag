## Description

The Hidden Variable: Women’s Physiology in AI, Performance & Longevity

For decades, health research, fitness programming, and even AI systems have been built on male baselines.

As personalized health, wearables, and data-driven optimization become mainstream, one critical variable remains under-modeled: women’s physiology.

This panel brings together founders, researchers, operators, and investors to explore:

• How hormonal fluctuation impacts performance, cognition, and recovery
• Why women are often misrepresented in health and training data
• The implications for AI, product design, and workplace performance
• Where the next wave of opportunity in women’s health innovation lives

Expect a sharp, science-forward conversation — not a wellness panel.

45-minute moderated discussion
15-minute audience Q&A
Curated networking to follow

Open to founders, engineers, clinicians, investors, and operators interested in the future of personalized health.
