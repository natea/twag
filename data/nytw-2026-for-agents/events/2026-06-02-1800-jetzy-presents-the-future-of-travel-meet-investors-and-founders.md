Speakers:

Shama Zehra
Shama Zehra is the Founder & CEO of JETZY which is the world’s first social platform to connect and reward people with passion for travel, adventure and unique lifestyle experiences.  On Jetzy you can connect with like-minded people and access VIP perks and up to 70% discounts for hotels, restaurants, car rentals, yachts, private jet and many other lifestyle brands and experiences. Jetzy is transforming travel by replacing fragmented search with a personalized, conversational AI experience. By combining AI, social discovery, and seamless booking, Jetzy will turn travel planning into an intuitive, end-to-end experience designed around each individual. 

Shama is a serial entrepreneur and has built several successful businesses in technology, financial services and fashion space. Shama began her career as one of the youngest female entrepreneurs in Pakistan when she built and sold a women apparel firm 12x at age 19. Shama later moved to financial services industry where she worked at Goldman Sachs and Morgan Stanley before starting her own investment advisory firm Aligned where she raised more than $2 billion. Shama’s entrepreneurial vision and success has been featured on the cover of Forbes Entrepreneurs and in Crains New York, Huffington Post, Nasdaq Entrepreneurs, Columbia University’s Core Magazine and several other publications. Shama holds two MBA degrees: one in Finance & Strategy from Michigan Ross School, and second in International Business from IBA, Karachi (formed under affiliation with Wharton, University of Pennsylvania). Shama lives in New York City and enjoys travel, skiing, yoga and spending time with her family (esp her niece and nephews).

Zain Jeewanji
Zain is a globally recognized pioneer in the Insuretech space, a visionary entrepreneur, investor, speaker, humanitarian, and an insurance industry veteran. Zain created the first true travel medical insurance product in the USA and online travel insurance in 1996. Zain is a mentor, investor and advisor to numerous startups, entrepreneurs all over Silicon Valley and sits on the board of advisory of several companies. He is also on the Board of advisory of Rand Corp’s Center for Asia Pacific Policy.

An avid cricket fan, you will find him on stadiums across the globe. When not watching cricket, he is busy helping startups in marketing and advising young CEO’s, founders, & entrepreneurs on challenges they are facing. Zain lives in Silicon Valley, California


Rob Mihalko
Rob is a tech executive, investor, and business educator with 20+ years of experience building and scaling software businesses. Rob is founder and managing director of Spectus Ventures. His background spans SAP/Ariba, Microsoft, and United Airlines, and he has a particular interest in travel technology. Rob developed and teaches a course on digital marketplaces through Stanford Continuing Studies. Rob is an avid traveler and resides in Silicon Valley, California
