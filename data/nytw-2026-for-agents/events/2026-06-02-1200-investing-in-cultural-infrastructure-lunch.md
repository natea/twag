Tuesday, June 2 · NYC Tech Week

Culture is evolving into something far more structural than expression alone—it is becoming a system layer for capital, media, and digital coordination.

This invitation-only Lunch & Learn brings together investors, founders, and operators for a focused discussion on how cultural activity is shifting from fragmented creative output into scalable, investable infrastructure.

We’ll explore how new coordination models, incentive structures, and platform architectures are enabling culture to function more like a networked system—capable of compounding value over time rather than relying on isolated, short-term cycles.

This is a high-signal, discussion-driven session designed for those building and investing at the intersection of technology, media, and next-generation platform design.
