## Description

Leave Tech Week with content worth posting.

You came to Tech Week with something to say. This is your chance to capture it.

This is not another panel, mixer, or pitch event. It’s a real sit-down conversation about what you’re building, where the industry is headed, and how your work fits into the bigger picture.
