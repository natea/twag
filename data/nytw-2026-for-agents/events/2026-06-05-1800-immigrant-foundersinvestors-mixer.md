## Description

Join us for an inspiring night of connection, conversation, and collaboration at the Immigrant Founders & Investors Mixer — an exclusive gathering designed to bring together immigrant entrepreneurs and investors who are driving innovation and building the future.

Whether you’re a founder scaling your startup, an investor seeking bold ideas, or a community builder championing diverse perspectives, this is your space to share stories, spark meaningful connections, and explore opportunities for collaboration.

Open Stage Pitch at 6:30 PM. Submit your Expression of Interest ahead of the event; a shortlist of founders will be invited to deliver a 5-minute elevator pitch — no slides, just your vision — followed by a 5-minute open-floor Q&A to engage the audience and ignite follow-up conversations.

Link to Open Stage Pitch Expression of Interest Form: https://form.typeform.com/to/TJmXY1d7

Agenda: 
6:00 – 6:20 PM | Check-in & Networking
6:20 – 6:30 PM | Opening Remarks
6:30 – 7:15 PM | Open Stage Pitching
7:15 – 8:00 PM | Networking

This event is made possible by OneRise and The Yard

​OneRise is a non-profit to support early-stage underrepresented founders in NYC. We focus on providing founders the knowledge, skills, and networks to succeed, through our community, incubator programs, and public events. https://onerise.nyc/

The Yard is a coworking space at the intersection of creativity, design, & play with locations in Manhattan, Brooklyn, Philly, Washington DC, and Chicago, with a variety of shared and private workspace options to meet your team's needs. Built to inspire and cultivate success, The Yard is here to help you win. Learn more at theyard.com

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
