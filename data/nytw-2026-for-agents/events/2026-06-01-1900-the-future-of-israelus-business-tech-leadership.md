## Description

Join TAMID Group, itrek, and Vision 2048 for an evening of networking and insightful discussion with leading voices at the forefront of Israel–U.S. business and tech leadership. This event is designed for post-grad emerging business leaders.

The evening will feature a short speaker segment with an accomplished group of leaders. We are honored to be joined by:
- Moshe Bellows, Partner, Maccabee Ventures
- Tal Hacohen, Partner, Holland & Knight
- Yael Shemer, Co-founder and CCO, TULU
- Ore Segal, Venture Development Lead, Base44 (acquired by Wix)

Drinks and food, including kosher and non-kosher options, will be served.

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
