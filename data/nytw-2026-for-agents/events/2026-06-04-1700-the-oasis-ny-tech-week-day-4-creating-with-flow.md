## Description

Tech Week can be chaotic. Running between panels, pitching, networking, and feeling overstimulated. The Oasis is a daily retreat—a place to belong, breathe, and actually co-create. 

We’re building a persistent home base across the week where builders, founders, and creatives show up every day to flow together, have real conversations around meaningful topics, and leave feeling refreshed and inspired.

The series blends daily ‘co-flow’ drop-in sessions with curated collaborative programming: roundtable discussions, fireside chats, and mentor office hours. Each day seeds Flowstate Sparks—structured artifacts that keep the conversation going long after the week ends.

This fourth day is focused on creating with Flow, with conversations guided towards the way that humans would make decisions when everyone would have access to the same tools.

This event is a part of #NY TechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
