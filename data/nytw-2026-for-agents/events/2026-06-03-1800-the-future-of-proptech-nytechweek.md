Sipho Simela  
Founder & CEO, Matrix Rental Solutions  
A first-generation American from a Zimbabwean immigrant family, Sipho built Matrix Rental Solutions from lived experience — knowing that access to housing is foundational to economic opportunity. Sipho currently advises the FHFA, Fannie Mae, and Freddie Mac on making housing access faster, fairer, and more human.
