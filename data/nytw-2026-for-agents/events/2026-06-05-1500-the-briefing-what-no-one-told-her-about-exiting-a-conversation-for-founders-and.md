## Description

Only 1.37% of businesses that sell a majority stake are female-founded. The women who do exit are navigating one of the most consequential financial and personal transitions of their lives — often without the advisory infrastructure to match.

This briefing brings together exited female founders and the wealth managers, RIAs, family offices, and exit advisory professionals who serve them, for an honest conversation about what the exit actually looks like from the inside. What worked. What was missing. What advisors were never trained to ask.

Moderated by Alisha Pennington, multi-seven figure exited founder and creator of Exette, this is not a panel about how to sell your business. It's a conversation about what happens when you do — and what the advisory world needs to understand to serve this client at the moment it matters most.
