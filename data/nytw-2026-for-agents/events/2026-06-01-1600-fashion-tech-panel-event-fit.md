## Description

This event is part of a series: New York (Fashion) Tech Week - Presented by The Pattern Maker. https://thepatternmaker.substack.com/

See the full list of fashion tech events here: https://tinyurl.com/NYFTW

-------
For the second year running, The Pattern Maker is partnering with the Fashion Institute of Technology to bring together the brands, builders, and innovators shaping fashion tech's next chapter and this year's lineup is not one to miss.

We've put together two panels getting at the most top of mind topics for fashion leaders and fashion tech founders, plus a tech talk to dive deep on how the technology reshaping fashion production actually works. 

Panel 1 — Beyond the Headlines: Why Getting AI Right for Fashion Is Harder Than It Looks and the Founders Fixing It

Founders and AI experts enabling AI across the fashion value chain will dig into what it really takes to build with this technology in a way that actually solves real problems, why the gap between the hype and the reality is wider than most people think, and what the path forward looks like for fashion brands and builders alike.

For full details about each panel and panelist, check out our latest post: https://thepatternmaker.substack.com/p/free-fashion-tech-panels-and-demo

Moderated by Rachel Sterling of The Pattern Maker, featuring:
✦ Franz Tschimben, Co-Founder & CEO, ALLSIDES
✦ Yusan Lin, Founder & CEO, Mirror Mirror AI
✦ Sreya Halder, Founder & CEO, The Mall
✦ Sophia Sterling, Founder & CEO, Paprika (Stealth)

Tech Talk, presented by Make the Dot

Between our two panels, Emilie Ho, Co-Founder & CEO of Make the Dot, takes us behind the scenes on how they've rethought the technology supporting every step of fashion production and beyond. 

Panel 2 — Fit Check: What It Actually Takes for Fashion and Tech to Partner Successfully

Getting to the heart of what every founder and fashion leader wants to know: What should fashion brands look for when choosing a tech partner? How do you stay ahead of constantly shifting technology? And what do fashion tech companies need to understand to land and keep major partnerships?

Moderated by Michael Ferraro, Executive Director of the FIT DTech Lab, featuring:
✦ Corey Moran, CMO, Michael Kors
✦ Diarra Bousso, Founder & Designer, DIARRABLU
✦ Emily Erkel Erkle, Co-Founder, LePrix

Doors open at 3:30 PM · Panels 4–6 PM · Open networking 6–7 PM

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
