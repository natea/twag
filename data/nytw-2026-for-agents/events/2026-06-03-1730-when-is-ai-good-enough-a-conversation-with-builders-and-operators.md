## Description

AI is moving out of the sandbox. As organizations shift from experimentation to deployment, leaders are facing the ultimate judgement call: “Should we ship it?”

Join us for a happy hour and panel conversation with leaders working across journalism, legal services, and civic technology. We’ll explore how teams set quality bars, manage risk, and decide when AI is ready for the real world.

Panelists:
- Justin Bleuel, OpenAI
- Teresa Mondría Terol, NPR
- Sanket Karuri, Code for America
- Zoë Egelman, Elego Law

Come for the conversation; stay to meet other people thinking seriously about what it takes to deploy AI responsibly.
