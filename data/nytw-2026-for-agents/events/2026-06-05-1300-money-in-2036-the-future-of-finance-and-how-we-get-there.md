## Description

In 2036, a repair shop owner in Nairobi, Kenya gets a $1,200 loan in 17 seconds. Three competing offers, best rate wins, money moves before he closes the tab. No human involved.

The infrastructure for that doesn't exist yet. Some of it is being built right now, in this city, by people in this room.

We're putting together a panel featuring leaders in traditional finance, DeFi, compliance, and the founders working on the underlying rails — to get into what's actually happening and where the gaps are. Networking after.
