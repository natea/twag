## Description

InTheRoom: From Match to Meeting

A conversation on trust, intention, and what would make digital connection worth using for real-life outcomes.


Join us during Tech Week 2026 in New York for a curated evening conversation exploring what makes people actually want to meet in real life. From dating apps to social platforms to professional networks, more and more of modern connection begins digitally, but the real test is whether it becomes something meaningful in person.

Built for thoughtful conversation, stronger signal, and more meaningful real-life outcomes than digital interaction alone can provide, this gathering is designed to make the room itself feel worth entering.

Expect strong perspectives, thoughtful chemistry, and the kind of evening that makes the gap between online connection and real-life connection feel newly visible.

With optional reset moments and curated connection suggestions in the InTheRoom app.

InTheRoom is a real-life social connection experience designed to help people arrive better, connect more meaningfully, and leave with more than chance alone would have produced.

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
