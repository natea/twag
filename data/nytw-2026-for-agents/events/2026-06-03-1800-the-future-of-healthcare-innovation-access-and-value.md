## Description

Join us for a dynamic panel discussion on how healthcare is evolving in today’s rapidly advancing health tech landscape. As the industry continues to shift toward more outcomes-driven and patient-centered models, this conversation will explore what it takes to drive innovation while improving access and delivering value at scale.

Our panel will bring together perspectives from insurers, investors, and founders across the health tech ecosystem. Together, they’ll discuss the role of emerging technologies, data infrastructure, incentive alignment, and execution in shaping the future of care delivery.

Attendees will gain insights into the key opportunities and challenges facing healthcare today — from the viewpoints of payors, founders, and investors.

This event is ideal for founders, clinicians, investors, and healthcare operators interested in the intersection of innovation, access, and value.

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek — a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
