## Description

Join Roebling for a Grow Everything Live: AI-Powered Engineering from Scratch podcast event.

How much capital does it take to build and operate at scale?

That question drives every major decision in deep tech (fundraising, site selection, go/no-go) and most teams are still answering it with spreadsheets, consultants, and months of waiting.

In this live recording of the Grow Everything podcast, hosts Erum Azeez Khan and Karl Schmieder will bring an industrial scale product idea to Roebling's CTO Brentan Alexander, Ph.D., who will use Roebling's AI-enabled platform to design, simulate, and cost it out on stage in real time from equipment sizing through capital costs and financial projections. Then they'll pressure-test the results live. The event will also be livestreamed and recorded for a future Grow Everything episode.

Speakers: Brentan Alexander (CTO, Roebling), Erum Azeez Khan and Karl Schmieder (Grow Everything podcast)
