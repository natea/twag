## Description

An Acoustic Bollywood Jam Night for Founders, Investors & Engineers

Hosted by Momo VC and The Brown Room, Bollywood & Builders is a curated, music-led gathering during NY Tech Week.

This is an intimate evening centered around live acoustic renditions of 90s and 2000s Bollywood classics — set in a warm, floor-seating environment designed for real conversation.

Expect:

- Live unplugged Bollywood music
- Chai + curated refreshments
- A thoughtfully selected mix of founders, investors, engineers, and operators
- Light-touch prompts to spark meaningful interaction
- A relaxed, no-pitch atmosphere

Whether you’re building a company, backing one, or engineering the future, this is a space to connect beyond formal networking structures.

Capacity is intentionally limited to maintain the quality of the room.

Additional details and programming elements will be announced soon.
