## Description

Founders House (www.foundershouse.nyc) is a members-only home base in Manhattan where founders work, recover, and build alongside others going through the same journey. It’s a workspace, wellness club, and community in one designed around how founders actually spend their days.

Join us for our weekly early morning run with founders, operators, and builders across the city. We’ll start with a 4-mile run, then hang out after for a coffee party with DJs, and real conversations with people across the startup community.

Whether you’re building a startup, raising a round, or scaling your company, this is how NYC’s most ambitious founders start their Tuesday. No pitching, no name tags — just movement, good energy, and community.
