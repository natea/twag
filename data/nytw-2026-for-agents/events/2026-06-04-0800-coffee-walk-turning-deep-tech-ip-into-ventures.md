## Description

Coffee & Walk: Turning Deep Tech IP into Ventures

Founders and builders in NYC for Tech Week, join an informal morning coffee and walk through Central Park. This meetup is an opportunity to connect with peers and explore how to build high-growth companies using established university intellectual property, clinical research, and translation facilities.

Organized by Koç University's Venture Studio, this event focuses on bridging the gap between academic innovation and commercial execution. Attendees will review a live case study of commercialization-ready deep tech and healthtech IP that can be worked on immediately. The gathering is also open to other academic researchers looking to share their translation pipelines with commercially minded entrepreneurs.

Expect practical discussions on accessing lab infrastructure, structuring venture spin-outs, and partnering with technical talent without heavy initial capital expenditure.

Meeting point: Sip and Co, 41 W 58th St, followed by a walk north into the park.

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
