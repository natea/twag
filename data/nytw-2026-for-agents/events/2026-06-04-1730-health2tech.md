## Description

On the Digital Health Inside Out YouTube channel (https://www.youtube.com/@DigitalHealthInsideOut), there are monthly series called Digital Health Vitals — where experts talk through the latest digital health news, grade recent fundraises and public company earnings, and share an honest take on what’s working and what’s not.
For this event, we’re bringing that same format to Health2Tech — live.
We’ll be recording an episode on stage with the lineup of experts, reacting to breaking news, debating a bit, playing a couple of games, and opening it up for Q&A with the audience.
If you want to hear real perspectives and get a feel for what’s happening in healthtech right now, this is the must-attend event.
