## Description

June 3rd is It's Global Running Day, what better way to celebrate than crossing the Brooklyn Bridge with NYC's tech community?

Join RemoFirst and Running Booth for a morning run or walk across the Brooklyn Bridge — one of the most iconic routes in the world. Meet us at the Oculus at the World Trade Center, lace up, and connect with NYC's tech community stride by stride. We'll bridge Manhattan and Brooklyn, and a few new relationships along the way. Start your day at NY Tech Week and celebrate Global Running Day the right way. 

Via PD will also be providing water, coffee, and post-run fuel so you can head into the rest of your day and NY Tech Week energized and ready.

The run will be approximately 3 miles with a shorter walk option. 

For every runner that joins us, we will be making a donation to Still I Run. Still I Run is a running-based mental health nonprofit focused on supporting, inspiring, and uniting people in their journey toward better mental health through running.

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com
