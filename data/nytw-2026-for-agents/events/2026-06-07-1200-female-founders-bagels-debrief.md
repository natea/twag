## Description

Building a company is exhilarating, exhausting, and often lonely. Join fellow female founders and entrepreneurs to step away from the inbox, share what's actually going on behind the scenes, and connect with women who get it.

No pitches. No panels. No agenda. Just good bagels, real conversation, and the kind of community that reminds you you're not building alone.

Whether you're navigating a fundraise, a tough hire, a pivot, or just need a moment to exhale with people who understand the journey, you'll find a supportive space here to debrief, ask questions, and trade notes with fellow female founders.

We can't wait to see you there.
