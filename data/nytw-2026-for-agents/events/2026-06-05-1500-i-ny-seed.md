## Description

I ❤️ NY Seed: Building the Next Great NYC Tech Company

A celebration, a masterclass, and a love letter to the greatest startup city in the world.

📅 When: June 5, 3-4pm (with happy hour to follow)
📍 Where: Crosby St, SoHo
👥 Who: Founders building in NYC or thinking about it

---

🗽 New York has produced some of the most iconic companies of the last two decades. That didn't happen by accident.

This panel features the NYC-based seed investors who were in the room early, wrote the first checks into companies you use every day, and have seen firsthand what separates the winners from the rest.

They're here to share what made those companies work, why NYC gave them an edge they couldn't have found anywhere else, and what it really takes to build here today.

🏆 Part celebration, part history lesson, part masterclass. Whether you're already building in NYC or deciding where to plant your flag, this is the room you want to be in.

Stick around after for happy hour with the people who built the NYC startup ecosystem from the ground up. 🍻
