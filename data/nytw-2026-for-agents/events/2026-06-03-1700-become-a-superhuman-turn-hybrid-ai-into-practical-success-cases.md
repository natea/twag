## Description

Become a Superhuman: Turn Hybrid-AI into Practical Success Cases

Join AxonJay and BelCham for an evening at the New York Tech Week exploring how to responsibly implement AI in B2B contexts. This event is designed for business decision-makers across industries, featuring real-world case studies, expert panels, and actionable insights — all centered around supercharging human capability with AI, not replacing it.

The Evening
5:00 PM – 5:10 PM · Welcome & Introductions by Jean-Philippe M.L. Schepens van Thiel

5:10 PM – 5:45 PM · Hybrid AI: Become a Superhuman in the B2B Market — How AxonJay's AI-Signal Agents augment human decision-making and what "hybrid AI" means in practice for B2B organizations.

5:50 PM – 6:20 PM · Responsible Usage of AI by Uvika Sharma — Lessons from real-world failures, governance frameworks, and what responsible adoption actually looks like beyond the buzzwords.

6:20 PM – 7:20 PM · AxonJay's Practical B2B Use Cases: Become a Superhuman — Real-world case studies showcasing how AxonJay's technology applies to organizational growth and human resource management.

· Werner Vergult (Remote): HR use case with AxonJay — how AI-driven signal intelligence transforms executive search and recruitment workflows.

· Yvan De Munck: Panel Discussion — Opening perspective on AI in capital markets and financial services, drawing on 10+ years of experience across two companies applying AI and ML to trading, asset management, and talent acquisition. 

Moderated by Jean-Philippe: Structured Q&A exploring hybrid AI in HR, competitive advantage through AI-powered recruiting, and real-world lessons from financial services.

7:20 PM – 8:00 PM · Drinks, Small Bites & Networking

8:00 PM · Optional Group Dinner for Continued Conversation
