## Description

Cabaka is hosting an official NY Tech Week panel discussion on June 4 at 6:00 PM at Savills’ New York headquarters, focused on the convergence of blockchain, stablecoins, digital-dollar banking, and AI in financial services.

The panel will bring together leaders from Glean, N3XT Bank, Nightfall Network, and NYU to discuss how blockchain and AI are beginning to reshape financial infrastructure, payments, banking, and enterprise operations. The conversation will explore topics including regulated digital-dollar banking, stablecoin use cases, programmable payments, enterprise AI adoption, AI-enabled knowledge management, and how financial institutions can prepare for a more real-time, automated, and data-driven future.

Confirmed panelists: 

> Jeffrey Wallis, CEO of N3XT Bank
>Paul Brody, CEO of Nightfall Network, former EY Global Blockchain Leader, and Consensus Miami speaker
>Glean, who will discuss the enterprise AI knowledge management.
>Frederic Siboulet, NYU professor of financial blockchain and former EY machine learning leader

The purpose of the event is to create an executive-level forum for discussing the future of financial services at the intersection of blockchain and AI, while giving participating partners direct visibility with a relevant audience of financial services and technology decision-makers. 

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
