SaaS is changing — the line between operators and developers is blurring, and RevOps, GTM, and ops teams are leading the charge. At Build Mode, HubSpot is bringing together the builders who are using AI coding tools, MCP servers, and low-code connectors to ship integrations, automate workflows, and extend platforms like HubSpot in ways that used to require a full engineering team.

Following our sold-out 2025 SF Tech Week event on Agentic AI + MCP, we're bringing this energy to New York with a sharper focus: how RevOps and go-to-market operators are becoming the new builders.

- A fast-paced but intimate panel on how non-traditional builders are solving real problems and unlocking unprecedented value with AI tools and MCP.
- Drinks, conversation, and a curated room of the people actually building the next wave of GTM infrastructure.
