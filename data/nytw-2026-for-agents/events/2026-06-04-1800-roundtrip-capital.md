## Description

Roundtrip Capital: The Missing Generation | Hosted by IncentivesPRO and ALPFA Puerto Rico

Capital doesn't move in one direction. The strongest economies; the ones that actually compound, run on a roundtrip: human capital, financial capital, and network capital flowing back and forth across borders, generations, and balance sheets until each one strengthens the next.

Puerto Rico has been running half that loop for a generation. People leave. Money comes in transactionally. The networks stay siloed. The roundtrip never closes. This event is about the founders, operators, and early employees who are closing it.

They came of age inside the turbulence — the 2008 collapse, Hurricane Maria, the political ousting of 2019, the COVID-19 pandemic — and chose to build anyway. Some stayed on the island and built from San Juan, Ponce, Mayagüez. Others left, learned the playbook in New York and beyond, and are routing capital, talent, and deal flow back home. They are the Missing Generation: raised in turmoil, now raising millions, employing hundreds, and quietly assembling the infrastructure the rest of the capital stack hasn't caught up to.

We're convening this conversation with six Puerto Rican founders who've each raised capital and built innovative solutions across the sectors that will define the next decade of the island's economy — Regulation Technology, FinTech, AI, HR, Energy, and Healthcare — alongside the institutions that hold the levers: the diaspora investors, operators, and allies already moving capital across the corridor.

This is a call to action. The producers are already here. The capital is already moving. The networks are already forming. What's missing is the room where we agree to close the loop on purpose.

We close with a networking reception. Salsa, plena, merengue; because we do business, and we dance.
