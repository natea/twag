## Description

Breakfast & Build: A Community Brunch for Diverse Founders & Execs

In collaboration with Accessible AI for All Angel Hive, and Cure, we’re bringing together diverse founders, investors, operators, and execs for a brunch gathering during Tech Week NYC.

This year, we’re moving from our usual evening happy hour to a morning format and creating space for the same kind of meaningful connection in a fresh setting.

Breakfast & Build is all about good coffee, brunch, real conversation, and meeting people who are building with AI for social impact. 

No panels. No formal programming. Just a room full of thoughtful people, honest conversations, and new connections.

If you joined us for Sip & Scale last year, we’d love to see you again.

Space is limited, so RSVP early.

Part of #NYTechWeek — a week of events hosted by VCs and startups across NYC.
