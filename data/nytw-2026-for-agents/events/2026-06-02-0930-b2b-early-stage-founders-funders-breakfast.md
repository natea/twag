## Description

In collaboration with Intercom and Cooley LLP,  Forum Ventures is hosting the Early-Stage B2B Founders & Funders Breakfast as part of New York Tech Week 2026. This is a curated, inclusive event specifically for entrepreneurs and VCs across B2B startup space to connect, get inspired, and have a great time! 

​Why Attend?

- Kick off New York TechWeek over breakfast and coffee, meet amazing people in a relaxed, low-pressure setting.

​- Engage with a community of peers and industry leaders who recognize the importance of diversity in tech. 

- Discover shared interests that unlock opportunities for partnerships, investments, and collaborations.

​- Gain insights & knowledge that could shape the trajectory of your startup or investment journey.

- Benefit from a balanced ratio of founders and funders—ensuring every conversation is mutual, engaging, and ripe with opportunity.

Who Should Attend?

​Pre-seed to Series A B2B founders, especially those who identify as BIPOC, Women+, disabled, and/or LGBTQIA.

Investors wanting access to diverse ideas and early opportunities in B2B software, SaaS, and AI.

Approved Guests

​Application does NOT guarantee attendance. ​​Spaces for this event are limited to maintain the quality of interactions and to ensure every participant receives optimum value.  Admitted guests will be notified via event approval.

​​NOTE: Invites are non-transferable. No +1s (space is very intimate, and capacity is strictly enforced on a first come first serve basis).


This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
