## Description

Join the Victorian Government and the American Australian Association (AAA) for an exclusive Tech Week New York networking event highlighting Melbourne and Victoria’s world‑class digital technologies ecosystem.

This event will bring together leading members of the New York tech ecosystem, Australian alumni in the US and a delegation of high‑growth Victorian digital technologies companies. 

Guests will have the opportunity to connect with founders, executives, investors, partners and customers, and hear from Brett Stevens, Victoria Commissioner to the Americas as to why Victoria is recognised as Australia’s leading technology hub and a preferred destination for global innovation, investment and expansion.

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
