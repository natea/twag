## Description

​Powered by Expert DOJO and Nightrush AI. For four days during NYC Tech Week, The Deal House brings together investors in Hudson Square for curated introductions, high-signal conversations, and early access to where smart capital is moving next.

​From 10AM–4PM daily, investors will rotate through 2-hour meeting windows designed to keep conversations intentional and the room highly relevant. Come ready to share the deal you're the most excited about, and meet allocators and capital partners you should already know.

​Everyone has a story about the deal they missed. This room is about being early to the next one.

​No founders. Strictly investors exchanging the best deals in the market. Select your 2-hour time slot when requesting to join. Please register for only one session.

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
