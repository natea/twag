## Description

AI is creating and concentrating wealth at an unprecedented pace. This carries enormous societal ramifications that could exacerbate existing inequities.
   
What could optimizing for justice and eudaimonia (human flourishing) look like? What institutions and groups are capable of and responsible for shaping that future?

There are no simple answers. Chris Russo (CEO of Savas Labs), will host a conversation spanning academia, business, nonprofit and policy perspectives. Audience questions encouraged in advance and live.
