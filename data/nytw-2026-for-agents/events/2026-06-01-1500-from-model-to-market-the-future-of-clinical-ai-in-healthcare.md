## Description

As health systems race to modernize, startups are increasingly shaping how hospitals operate—from core technology infrastructure to AI‑enabled workflows and new care models. But adoption doesn’t happen in a vacuum. Legal, regulatory, procurement and operational realities all influence what makes it from pilot to scale.

Join us for a candid conversation with founders, health system leaders, investors and advisors working at the intersection of healthcare delivery and emerging technology. We’ll explore how hospital–startup partnerships are evolving, what health systems are actually looking for today, and how companies can navigate the complexities that come with selling into—and partnering with—large providers.

We’ll cover topics including:

- Where hospitals are investing in technology and AI infrastructure today
- How health systems evaluate, adopt, and scale startup solutions
- Key legal, regulatory, and business considerations that shape partnerships
- What differentiates startups that move beyond pilots from those that stall

Panelists:
- Jared Augenstein, Manatt
- Ashley Beecy, Sutter Health
- Vig Chandramouli, Oak HC/FT
- Bailey Renger, Besound Health
- Sean Sondej, Vega Health

Expect a thoughtful, off‑the‑record discussion with time for audience Q&A and informal networking with fellow founders, operators, investors and health system innovators. Refreshments will be provided.
