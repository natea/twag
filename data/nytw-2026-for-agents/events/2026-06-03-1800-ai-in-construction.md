## Description

AI is everywhere in construction right now. But where is it actually working — and where is the hype still running ahead of reality?

Join founders from the companies building vertical AI across construction's most critical workflows for an honest, unscripted conversation about what's real, what's broken, and where this is all going.

We'll cover pre-construction and estimating, field operations, and construction finance — and we won't be pulling punches on where the industry is still getting this wrong.

Joining us for the panel:

Lisa Kelly, CEO & Founder of Radical Results (moderator)
Matt Calvano, CEO & Co-Founder of Adaptive
Sarah Buchner, CEO & Founder of Trunk Tools
Dustin DeVan, CEO of Ediphi


Panel followed by drinks, networking, and conversation with founders, operators, and investors building the future of construction.


This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
