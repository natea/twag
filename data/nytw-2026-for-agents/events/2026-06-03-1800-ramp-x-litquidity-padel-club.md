## Description

​Litquidity and Ramp are joining forces to start an exclusive padel club - a series of Miami and New York based padel socials and tournaments for the industries best founders, operators, and investors. This round will include two rooftop courts of padel clinics and one rooftop court overlooking the water used for wellness and social activations. 

We will be having pop-ups like compression boots, red light therapty, embroidery, a Spritz bar by Capital Call Vintners, and a Creatine Mocktail bar by Creia (Creia is NYC's first sparkling creatine beverage built for mental clarity and everyday strength).

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
