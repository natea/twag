## Description

If AI gives everyone access to the same knowledge, what actually separates top performers?

Join Atlas Primer for an invite-only dinner bringing together founders, operators, and researchers to explore how human performance is evolving as AI becomes foundational to work.

This is not a panel or presentation. It is a curated, small-group conversation focused on how individuals and organizations translate AI into real-world execution.

We will explore themes including:

* Why AI compresses advantage at the information layer
* How performance is shifting from knowledge to judgment and behavior
* What defines high performance in an AI-augmented world

Space is intentionally limited to foster meaningful discussion.
