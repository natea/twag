## Description

Join us at the Kaizen HQ for drinks, bites, demos, and a room full of people who believe beautifully designed software shouldn't be a luxury in government.

Essential public services have been stuck in 1998 for too long. Americans deserve better than the digital experiences they're handed for renewing a passport, filing taxes, or filing for public benefits. Kaizen exists to fix that.

We build modern, AI-native software for local, state, and federal agencies. Today, our platform reaches 40 million residents across 50+ agencies in 17 states. Founded in 2022 and based in New York City, Kaizen has raised $35 million from NEA, a16z, Accel, 776, and Carpenter Capital. 

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek - a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
