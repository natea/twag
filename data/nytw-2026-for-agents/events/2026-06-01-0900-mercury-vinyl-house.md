## Description

Mercury’s Vinyl House at NY Tech Week 
June 1st, 2nd, and 3rd · 9 am–3 pm 
Port Sa'id · 88 King St, NYC

NY Tech Week moves fast. Mercury's Vinyl House is a place to slow down — just long enough to catch your breath, get inspired, and keep moving.

We're taking over Port Sa'id in SoHo for three days: part co-working hub, part vinyl listening lounge, all founder-first. Whether you're between meetings, hosting one, or just need somewhere to land, this is your home base.

What to expect:
☕ Coffee + light bites to keep you going 
🧑‍💻 Comfortable workspace to plug in and get things done 
🎧 Live vinyl DJ sets 
🎶 Listening booths + curated music moments 
🛍️ Limited edition merch
🤝 Meet members of the Mercury team from 12 pm-3 pm each day


Plus two expert sessions:

Live Pitch Jam — June 1 @ 10:30 am 
Want real, unfiltered feedback as you sharpen your ideas? In this interactive, fast-paced session, founders share their working pitches live with Brandon Bryant (Partner, Harlem Capital Partners) and Jenny Fielding (Co-founder & Managing Partner, Everywhere Ventures). Come ready to think on your feet — they've been known to flip the script just to see how you handle it.

How did you do that? — June 2 @ 10:30 am 
Everyone talks about strategy and outcomes. But what actually happened behind the scenes? Channing Allen (Co-founder, IndieHackers and Hatch) and Brandon Gell (COO, Every) break down the pivotal moments — what they did, what worked, what didn't, and what they'd do differently.

We'll wrap it all up with a Closing Brunch on June 3, 9–11 am.

Register now if you're planning to stop by.
