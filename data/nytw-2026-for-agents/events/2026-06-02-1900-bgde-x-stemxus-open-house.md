## Description

Join us for the BGDE x STEMXUS™ Open House in Brooklyn, hosted during #NYCTechWeek—a citywide series of events uniting founders, innovators, and the broader tech ecosystem.

This exclusive experience is focused on a live demonstration of STEMXUS™, our EdTech platform designed to prepare the next generation for the future of work. STEMXUS™ combines hands-on STEM learning with a scalable, tech-enabled LMS—featuring pathways in AI, data science, robotics, and emerging technologies.

During this open house, attendees will:

1. Experience a live demonstration of the STEMXUS™ platform

2. Explore how our learning model integrates in-person training with digital learning

3. Gain insight into our approach to building future-ready skills through technology

This is a focused opportunity for educators, parents, and partners to see our product in action and understand how BGDE and STEMXUS™ are leveraging technology to transform STEM education.

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
