Session Title: 
IEEE and the Future of AI - NYC Tech Week

Abstract: 
As artificial intelligence transitions from experimental hype to foundational infrastructure, the path forward requires a multidisciplinary approach that balances rapid innovation with global responsibility. This panel brings together a distinguished group of leaders to explore the next decade of the AI trajectory. We will examine the tech industry roadmaps and entrepreneurship strategies necessary to scale AI ventures, alongside the cutting-edge research and architectural breakthroughs emerging from world-renowned labs.

The discussion will bridge the gap between "Big Tech" implementation and the complex logistics and governance required for public sector integration. Crucially, the session will highlight the humanitarian aspects of technology, focusing on how IEEE standards and AI applications can be leveraged to solve the world’s most pressing social challenges. Join us for a comprehensive look at how these diverse sectors are intersecting to build an AI future that is as ethical and inclusive as it is technically advanced.
