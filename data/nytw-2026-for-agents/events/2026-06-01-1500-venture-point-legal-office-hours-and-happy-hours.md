## Description

Pull up a stool, grab a cocktail (or a cortado), and bring your hardest questions about startup law. Venture Point Legal partner Ellisha Blechynden is hosting an open Ask Me Anything session during New York Tech Week. Whether you're prepping for a seed round, navigating a Series A, or just trying to figure out what your lawyer is talking about, this is your chance to get real answers in a low-key setting.

On the table:
SAFEs, priced rounds, and everything in between
Cap table mechanics and dilution (the cocktail and non-cocktail kind)
Founder agreements, vesting, and co-founder dynamics
Delaware entity structuring
Investor negotiations and term sheet red flags
Whatever else you want to ask

The vibe: Small group, big questions. Cocktails, coffee, and non-alcoholic options on us. No panels, no slides, no pitch decks. Just conversation.

Who should come: Founders, operators thinking about starting something, and investors who want to talk shop.

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com
