## Description

​Generative Media wants to be free!

The best generative media models will be open. Open weights mean faster iteration, deeper customization, and real transparency about what's inside. Closed APIs can't offer that — and the market is starting to figure it out.

​Bria, fal, and LTX are building on this bet. Bria ships creative control and rights-clear models designed for commercial production. LTX builds open-source foundational video and image models pushing the frontier on quality and efficiency. fal makes them all run fast at scale. Together, we think this is what the generative media stack looks like going forward — open, fast, and commercially safe.

​Join us during NYC Tech Week for an evening of talks, a panel, and honest conversation about where open generative media is headed.
