## Description

A rare, fireside conversation with two architects of hypergrowth: Shuo Wang (Deel Co-Founder & CRO—who helped build the world's fastest growing company, surpassing $1B+ revenue in under six years) and Anish Acharya (a16z GP and Deel board member who backed Deel from the start).
