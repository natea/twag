## Description

​Hear from the investors shaping healthcare at one of North America’s fastest-growing healthtech communities.

Come for a afternoon of networking with the change-makers of healthcare (founders, operators & investors), and hear from a panel of investors investing in healthcare, across seed, Series A, and growth stages.

This conversation goes beyond fundraising theory. We will explore what investors actually look for in healthtech and life sciences companies, including how to think about timing, building relationships, communicating your story, preparing for diligence, and avoiding common fundraising mistakes. 

From timing the market and building investor relationships to metrics, storytelling, diligence, and common fundraising mistakes, this conversation offers practical insight into what different-stage investors look for and how founders can position themselves for a successful raise.
