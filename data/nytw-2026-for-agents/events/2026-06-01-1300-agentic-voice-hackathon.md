## Description

Agentic Voice Hackathon
Build the Next Generation of AI Voice Agents
Following the workshop, developers are invited to participate in an intensive Agentic Voice Hackathon, where teams will design and prototype innovative voice-powered AI agents.

Participants will use Cloudonix to build real-time voice applications capable of interacting with users, performing tasks, and integrating with modern AI systems.

Use Cloudonix with any of the following tools (or a combination of) to create something real and unique:
- Dograh
- Pipecat
- ⁠LiveKit
- ⁠ElevenLabs
- ⁠VAPI
- ⁠Retell
- ⁠Ultravox
- ⁠Dialog Flow
- ⁠Any LLM with Voice capabilities
- ⁠vCon Services

This short but high-energy hackathon is designed to encourage experimentation, creativity, and rapid prototyping in the emerging field of agentic voice technology.

The Challenge
Teams will build a working AI voice agent that demonstrates a useful or creative application of conversational AI. Possible examples include:
• AI customer service agents
 • Voice-based productivity assistants
 • AI receptionists or booking systems
 • Interactive voice games or educational tools
 • Voice interfaces for startups or services

We are looking for creative and innovative implementations in the following verticals:
- Real Time Voice Cloning for Contact Centers
- ⁠Real Time Multi-Party Transcription and Sentiment Analysis
- ⁠Real Time Multi Modal Sales Assistant
- ⁠Real Time Rejection Assistant

Hackathon Format
• Duration: 4 hours
• Teams: up to 3 participants
• Mentors available throughout the event
• Final demos presented to a panel of industry judges

Mentors will help teams navigate the technology stack, integrate voice systems with AI agents, and refine their prototypes.

Judging Criteria
Projects will be evaluated by industry experts based on:
• Creativity and originality
 • Technical implementation
 • Use of agentic voice capabilities
 • Real-world usefulness or startup potential

Prizes
• Winning team members will receive Amazon gift cards
• All participants receive three months of free Cloudonix service to continue building their projects

Who Should Participate
• Developers interested in AI agents
 • Students exploring startup ideas
 • Engineers interested in voice technologies
 • Builders who want to prototype new AI products quickly
