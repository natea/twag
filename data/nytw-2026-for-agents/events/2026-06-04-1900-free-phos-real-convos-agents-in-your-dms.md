Photon:
Photon is the easiest way to build and deploy AI agents inside the messaging apps people already use: iMessage, WhatsApp, Telegram, Slack, and more. Our open-source SDK Spectrum handles the infra so you can focus on building agents, not plumbing. 

QClaw:
QClaw is a free, local-first AI agent built on the OpenClaw ecosystem. From your computer or directly through messaging apps you already use, QClaw doesn't just chat — it acts. It files, schedules, drafts, and executes real tasks while you live the rest of your life. Endorsed by the OpenClaw community. Do less. Live more.
