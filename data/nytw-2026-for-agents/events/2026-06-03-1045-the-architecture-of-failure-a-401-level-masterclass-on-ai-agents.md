Everywhere you look, people claim to be deploying AI agents successfully and yet despite following all the “best practices,” agentic workflows keep failing. Why? Because when it comes to AI architecture, there is no silver bullet.

In this advanced, 401-level session, Google Founder Advocate and AI researcher John Alioto strips away the hype to reveal why smart teams hit a wall when building and deploying agents. This deep-dive 90 minute session unpacks the foundational principles that dictate how AI systems actually work and why they. We will explore foundational and advanced concepts in large language models and software architecture.
