## Description

Healthcare and Finance are the two hardest places to deploy AI. Highest stakes, tightest rules, smallest margin for error. We’re the founders building it anyway.

Theonix is the AI copilot for medicine. Generative AI, computer vision, and AR that trains clinicians and guides them through real procedures in real time.

Orquezta is doing it in financial infrastructure for a global ecosystem in the middle of massive reshuffling, giving SMBs, fintechs, credit unions, and their global equivalents the tools to compete against the giants and expand into new markets.

Come spend an evening with us on a Midtown Manhattan private rooftop. Honest conversation about what AI actually looks like in industries that can’t afford to ship and pray. Food, drinks, and the skyline.
