## Description

Pastor Poju Oyemade is a Nigerian pastor and thought leader.  He is known for connecting faith with leadership, business, and governance. 

As founder of The Covenant Nation, with church campuses across Africa, Europe, and North America; he has influenced both entrepreneurial and policy conversations across the world.

He is respected for a structured, systems-driven approach, emphasizing discipline, execution, and long-term thinking: equipping founders to build institutions that last.

For this session with Builders, Backers & Believers, he brings:
- Practical integration of faith and leadership
- Insight on building enduring careers & institutions
- A credible voice on Christian integrity and stewardship.
