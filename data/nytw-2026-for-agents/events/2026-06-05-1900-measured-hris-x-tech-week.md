## Description

Join us at the beautiful knitting loft of The Ragtrader and Bo Peep for an evening at the intersection of hospitality and high performance technology.

The Experience

Enjoy curated bites and drinks as we host a panel discussion featuring leading operators from the hospitality and tech industries. We will be diving into the evolution of the restaurant workforce and the realities of scaling in today's landscape.

About Measured HRIS

Measured HRIS is the workforce management platform built specifically for the complexity of multi location restaurant groups. While generic systems fall short, we provide a single, cohesive platform for:

Specialized Payroll: Tipped employees and multi entity compliance.

Streamlined Operations: Scheduling, time, labor, and benefits.

Intelligent Automation: AI powered HR tools and jurisdiction specific labor law compliance.

Come see how we are replacing manual workarounds with operational excellence.

Space is limited. Join us to connect with the founders and executives shaping the future of how we build for the people who feed our cities.

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com
