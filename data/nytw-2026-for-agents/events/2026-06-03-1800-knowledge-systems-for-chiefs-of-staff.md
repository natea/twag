## Description

How do the best Chiefs of Staff build systems that actually scale — not just for themselves, but for their entire org?

As a Chief of Staff, you’re often the connective tissue across teams — responsible for turning scattered information into something structured, accessible, and actionable. But what does it look like to build systems that don’t break as your company grows?

In this panel, we’ll explore how Chiefs of Staff design and manage knowledge systems that support fast-moving teams — from documentation and decision-making to cross-functional alignment. Expect practical insights, real examples, and a behind-the-scenes look at how operators are using tools like Notion to keep everything running smoothly. 

Join Notion and Ask a Chief of Staff during NY Tech Week as we dive into how high-performing operators design knowledge systems that keep teams aligned, informed, and moving fast.
