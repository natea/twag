## Description

Should you invest in an Anthropic or OpenAI SPV? A vibe-coded pre-seed app?

This is a curated event for AI investors: VCs, family offices, angels and institutions.

From the Good Investor Club.

AI is moving faster than investors can underwrite.

Funding rounds for the hottest companies are full before they even open... while new AI agents flood the pre-seed market.

What's defensible, and what's hype?

This event brings together experienced VCs, family offices, institutional investors and new investors to break down where AI dollars should be going.

We'll cover:
*Growing sectors and markets for AI applications
*Where AI will see sustained demand growth over the next 10 years
*The blind spots of AI majors like OpenAI, Anthropic, xAI, Google
*How to spot technical depth and IP - and how to do advanced due diligence
*Returns possibilities, exit options, liquidity

Join this expertly-curated event to connect with other angel, ultra-high-net-worth and VC investors and learn more about AI investing at VC and pre-seed. 

No sales pitches, no media.
