## Description

Join American Australian Association, Austrade and Tech Council of Australia for a full-day summit bringing together founders, investors, and enterprise leaders at the forefront of AI — with a focus on the US–Australia technology corridor.

​The program features candid conversations across five themed sessions: breaking into enterprise sales, building strategic partnerships, AI readiness from a global perspective, infrastructure and the future of AI at scale, and accessing capital as an AI company. The day also includes a VC reverse pitching session, giving startups direct exposure to investors, and is bookended by networking over breakfast and lunch.

​Whether you're an Australian company scaling into the US market or an American firm looking to deepen ties with the Australian ecosystem, this is a rare opportunity to connect with a curated group of peers and decision-makers.

​Stay tuned for announcements on this year's agenda on our LinkedIn or Instagram. 

​Last year's summit featured:

​- Keynote on AI's opportunity for Australia, delivered by Accenture

- ​Panels on selling to American enterprise clients (Mastercard, NVIDIA, Accenture), accessing US capital (Intel Capital, Antler, Necessary Ventures), and AI strategy from a CEO perspective

​- Fireside chats with leaders from Blackstone, Xero, and Artefact USA on the state of AI in corporate America and the investor outlook

​- A session on Agentic AI with Agentic Scale AI and MyState Bank

​- Perspectives from the New York Economic Development Corporation and the United States Studies Centre on policy and the NYC advantage

​- An executive session with Goldman Sachs

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
