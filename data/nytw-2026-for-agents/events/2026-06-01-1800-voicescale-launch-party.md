## Description

We built something. Now we’re celebrating it with the people who get it.

VoiceScale is the employee advocacy platform built for B2B marketing teams who are done watching their brand page reach disappear. We take one piece of company content and turn it into personalized LinkedIn posts for every person on your team, written in their voice, matched to their role. Your employees show up consistently. Your company surfaces in AI search. Your team does none of the writing.

We’re throwing a launch party during NY Tech Week to bring together the founders, marketing leaders, and operators who know that the next wave of B2B growth isn’t coming from ads. It’s coming from the people already inside the building.

Come out. Meet the team. Meet the people building what’s next.
