## Description

This isn’t another boring panel. It’s actually a fun one. Join us for an insightful conversation followed by drinks and bites on a rooftop with the DTC ecom crowd.

AI is changing how Meta ads work faster than most brands can keep up.

What used to be best practice just a year ago already feels outdated. This panel brings together operators and founders working hands-on with Meta ads to talk about what’s actually working right now.

We’ll cover:
• How campaign structures are changing in 2026
• What still matters and what doesn’t anymore
• How to think about testing, scaling, and consolidation
• The role of creative, data, and attribution in performance
• Real examples from brands actively spending on Meta

Our panelists:
• Yiqi Wu - Founder & CEO @ Meta
• Charles Tichenor - Founder @ Disrupter School
• Lee Bissonnette - CEO @ Brand X Commerce
