## Description

Japan x NYC Startup Mixer is hosted by Japanese founders and operators, and open to everyone in the NY Tech Week community who is curious about Japan and startups.

Whether you are a founder, investor, operator, builder, community organizer, or building community/event/social products like Partiful, this is a casual place to meet people across the Japan and New York startup ecosystems.

It is also for anyone exploring Japan as a market, partner ecosystem, source of talent, or source of ideas.

Jungle of Invention is a Brooklyn coworking space built as infrastructure for Japanese entrepreneurs in the U.S. We will use the coworking space and can flow upstairs to the rooftop, including one of Jungle's most memorable features: a rooftop sauna.

Expect relaxed introductions, small-group conversations, and a few informal founder/community updates rather than a conference-style panel.

Capacity is intentionally limited to keep the room useful and conversational. RSVP requests will be reviewed.
