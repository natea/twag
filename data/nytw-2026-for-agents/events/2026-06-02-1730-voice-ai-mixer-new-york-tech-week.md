## Description

Voice is now becoming a core part of how users interact with products - from support to automation to real-time engagement. 

​During NY Tech Week, Agora is hosting a private Voice AI Mixer bringing together founders, operators, and investors building voice-first AI applications. 

​Join us for live demos, practical discussions, and networking with teams putting Voice AI into production. 

​Sessions Schedule 
​Networking: 5:30 PM – 6:00 PM 
​Agora Presentation & Demo: 6:00 PM – 6:30 PM 
​Partner Demos: 6:30 PM – 7:15 PM 
​Panel Discussion (VCs, Builders, Operators): 7:15 PM – 7:45 PM 

​Happy Hour & Networking: 7:45 PM – 8:30 PM 

​What to Expect 
​Live Voice AI Demos 
​See how real-time voice applications combine speech, LLMs, and infrastructure to deliver low-latency, human-like interactions at scale. 

​Panel Discussion 
​Hear from VCs, founders, and operators on where Voice AI is heading - and what it takes to build and scale real-world deployments. 

​Curated Networking 
​Connect with founders, engineers, product leaders, and investors building and investing in voice-first applications. 

​Who Should Attend 
​Founders and startup teams 
​AI engineers and builders 
​Product leaders and operators 
​Voice, speech, and LLM developers 
​Investors exploring AI infrastructure and applications 
​Anyone building or exploring voice-first experiences 

​If you’re building or investing in Voice AI - this room is for you. 

​This event is part of Agora’s Convo AI World event series, designed to bring together the community shaping the future of Voice AI and real-time AI applications.

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
