## Description

In 2025, The Browser Company of New York (the team behind Arc and Dia) was acquired by Atlassian for over $600M.

At #NYTechWeek, come hear from Hursh Agrawal (linkedin.com/in/hurshagrawal) about his journey as an acquired founder.

Beyond hearing about the M&A process, Hursh will also share insights about Arc and Dia. This is your chance to get an inside look at the future of AI-powered browsers, where knowledge work happens.
