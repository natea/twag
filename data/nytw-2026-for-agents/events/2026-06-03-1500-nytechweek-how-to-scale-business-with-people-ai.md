## Description

Join NYC founders and GTM leaders for a practical NY Tech Week workshop on building a revenue engine that can scale with AI while keeping people, strategy, and execution at the center.

AI is changing how teams sell, market, hire, and work with outside partners. It can make strong systems stronger, but it will not fix unclear positioning, weak ICPs, disconnected sales and marketing, or teams that are not set up to execute well.

This session is for leaders who want to pressure-test their GTM approach, understand where AI can create real leverage, and think more clearly about the people and partners needed to support growth.

We’ll work through questions like:

• Is your GTM motion ready to scale, or is it creating more activity than pipeline?
• Where can AI improve the quality of sales and marketing work?
• What should stay human-led?
• What kind of team do you need for the next stage of growth?
• How should the agency/client relationship evolve as AI becomes part of execution?

✦ Workshop Circles
