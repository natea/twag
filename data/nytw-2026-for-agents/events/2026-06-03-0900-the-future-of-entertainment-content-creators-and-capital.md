## Description

As startups and investors rethink how technology reaches consumers — and how value is created at the intersection of hardware, AI, and media — the lines between distribution, creativity, and commerce are being rapidly redrawn.

Against that backdrop, the event will open with a 20-minute “fireside chat” with Manatt Partner Eric Bergner and Dan Glassman, Senior Director and Head of New Business Development for Samsung. The conversation will focus on the intersection of hardware, AI, consumer and immersive media — and how global tech companies are shaping the distribution layer of the future as well as the future of commerce and brand building. 

The “fireside chat” will be followed by a panel with leading voices across talent, venture capital, and emerging media to explore: 

- AI as creative collaborator vs. infrastructure layer
- The economics of the modern creators, the power of AI as a tool and its risks
- The role the new AI and creator economy plays in commerce and brand building
- How to leverage or stagnate investment theses in entertainment-tech compliance, content, consumer, regulatory and related challenges

Panelists for this event include: 
- Vejay Lalla, Manatt
- Avani Patel, The Ember Company
- Marshall Sandman, Animal Capital
- Sören Wrenn, TITLES
- Po Yi, Manatt

Expect a candid, off the record conversation with time for audience Q&A, followed by informal networking with fellow founders, operators, and investors across entertainment, advertising, hardware, and consumer tech. Refreshments will be provided.

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
