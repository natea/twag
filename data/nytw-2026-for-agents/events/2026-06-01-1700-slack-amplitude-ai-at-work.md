## Description

AI at Work: A Panel on Decisions, Context, and What's Next

Stating the obvious: AI has changed how we work. The less obvious part: the biggest outcomes aren't coming from the smartest models. They're coming from better decisions, grounded in real customer behavior, business context, and the conversations already happening across your team.

Join us for an evening of light bites, drinks, and an intimate panel with leaders from Slack, Amplitude, and Tightknit as we dig into the questions every operator is wrestling with right now:

* How is AI actually changing the way teams decide and act?
* What separates AI that feels smart from AI that understands your business?
* What does it look like when AI shows up where work already happens, instead of in yet another tab?
* What's coming in the next 12 months?

Hosted at Fabrik in NYC. Capped at 70 attendees.

Brought to you by Slack Community NYC, Amplitude, and Tightknit.

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
