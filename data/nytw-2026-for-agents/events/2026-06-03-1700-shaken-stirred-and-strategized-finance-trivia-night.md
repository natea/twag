## Description

Step into NYC’s iconic Raines Law Room for an exclusive speakeasy night with top finance leaders during #NYTechWeek. Expect sharp minds, strong drinks, and just the right amount of friendly competition in an evening designed for connection, conversation, and a little finance-fueled fun.

Throughout the night, guests can stop by our interactive Mixology Tutorial Station on the terrace, where an expert mixologist will guide attendees through the World of Martinis—featuring a curated selection of vodkas, gins, vermouths, and non-alcoholic options. Drop in at your leisure to learn, sip, and craft your own perfect cocktail.

As the evening picks up, we’ll transition into a fast-paced round of Finance & AI Trivia about halfway through the event. Test your market knowledge, compete with fellow finance operators and tech leaders, and see who comes out on top.
