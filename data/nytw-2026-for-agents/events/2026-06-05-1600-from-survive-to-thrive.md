## Description

A candid and insightful panel discussion with exceptional entrepreneurs who have successfully led their companies with inclusive leadership from small and scrappy to successful Series A and beyond. Networking to follow. Hosted by VC firm Astia, which invests in the future of Health, Climate and Prosperity into companies with women leaders.

Hosted by Lowenstein Sandler
With Osborne Clarke

Panel: 
Esther Dyson: Legendary tech investor pioneer, founding chair of ICANN, early investor in  SpaceX, Square, Flickr, bringing decades of unparalleled insight on scaling global innovation.


Maya Hardigan, CEO Mae Health: Former Pfizer digital innovation executive turned visionary CEO transforming the landscape of maternal healthcare equity.

Sumer Johal, CEO Full Harvest: MIT-educated pioneer and global AgTech leader with over 25 years of experience driving food sustainability through AI and open-source data infrastructure.

Victoria Pettibone, CIO & Managing Partner Astia: Former operator turned VC leveraging 15+ years of investment and board expertise to guide inclusive startups through the grueling transition from scrappy to scaled growth.
