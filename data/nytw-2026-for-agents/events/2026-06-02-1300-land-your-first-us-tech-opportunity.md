You have the skills. You have the degree. But the U.S. job market for international students is a maze — and most people are navigating it alone.
This workshop is for you.
Join us for an honest, practical conversation with people who live and breathe this world every day: a recruiter who places international talent at U.S. companies, an admissions expert who has helped hundreds of students build their legal foundation in America, and an immigrant founder who turned his own visa journey into a platform serving thousands.
