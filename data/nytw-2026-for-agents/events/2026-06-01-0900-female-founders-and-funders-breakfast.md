## Description

Kick off your morning with an exclusive breakfast gathering dedicated to New York’s ecosystem of female entrepreneurs and investors. This event brings together the city's brightest minds to connect, share resources, and build the relationships that drive venture success.

Designed to be high-impact and intentional, this gathering provides a dedicated space for founders to meet funders in an authentic, supportive environment.

Event Highlights
Curated Breakfast: Enjoy a seasonal breakfast while engaging in meaningful conversations with peers and potential partners in the heart of the city.

Founder-Funder Networking: A structured yet open environment designed to facilitate introductions between pre-seed/early-seed founders and active investors.

Strategic Connections: Exchange insights on the NYC tech landscape, share recent wins, and discuss the challenges and opportunities of scaling in today's market.

Who Should Attend?
We invite female founders across all industries and stages—with a specific focus on the pre-seed and seed community—and investors dedicated to backing women-led innovation.

Note: We are keeping this gathering intimate to ensure every attendee has the opportunity to make quality connections. Please RSVP early to secure your spot.
