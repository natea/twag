## Description

Vibe Space Mixer is a community-wide event that brings together founders, operators, and investors to connect, swap ideas, and share the kind of candid feedback that rarely fits into a formal deck or calendar slot.

Set against the backdrop of the NEXUS Club New York, one of the city’s most exclusive private venues, this mixer is designed for upscale networking that still feels relaxed and genuinely founder-friendly. Think polished ambiance, great drinks, and the kind of room where chance introductions can turn into your next round, your next hire, or your next big partnership.

This is your space to meet and discuss your AI projects with a couple hundred other like-minded members—from early-stage builders to seasoned operators and active investors who are actually writing checks. Here, no one will judge you for keeping your laptop half open running your AI agents while you finish your drink. Come here prepared for sketching out product ideas, sharing live demos, or even trading war stories from the frontier of AI.

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
