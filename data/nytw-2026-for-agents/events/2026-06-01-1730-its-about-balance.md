## Description

If you've ever had the Sunday scaries, this is for you. 

We live in an age of constant noise. Fractured attention, endless notifications, and mounting pressure have become the backdrop of modern life. We invite you to pause and take inventory.

Join us for an evening exploring the building blocks of a balanced life — weaving together physical, emotional, and spiritual well-being. 

Then we invite you into a live sound bath, light bites and the opportunity to experience the beta launch for Reframe.

This event is hosted as a part of the NYTechWeek in partnership with Latinas in Tech NYC.

This is where technology meets balance.
