## Description

Czech Tech Pitch & Networking Night

Join the Consulate General of the Czech Republic in New York and CzechInvest New York, together with SOSA, for an evening of startup pitches and networking with a delegation of Czech tech startups.

Tuesday, June 2, 2026 | 5:00–8:30 PM  
Bohemian National Hall, New York City

🕒 Run of Show
Open Doors & Welcome — 5:00–5:30 PM  
Guest arrival, check‑in.

Welcome & Startup Pitches — 5:30–6:30 PM  
Opening remarks followed by pitches from the Czech startup delegation.

Networking Reception — 6:30–8:30 PM  
Meet founders, investors, and members of the NYC tech and innovation community.

🚀 Participating Startups

Adbian — A software platform that uses 3D scanning and biomechanics to help arborists assess tree stability and make data‑driven decisions about tree care.

Chatlys — An AI platform that turns a company’s website and documents into a chatbot/assistant that provides accurate, on-brand answers and automates customer communication.

Eterny — A fintech platform that securely aggregates and manages personal finances, assets, and documents in one place while using AI to provide alerts, insights, and legacy planning.

Minds & Models — An AI‑driven retail technology that analyzes in‑store customer behavior in real time to deliver personalised advertising.

ValkaAI — A generative AI company building real‑time interactive digital avatars that enable personalized, responsive video and entertainment experiences.

VR Vitalis - A healthcare technology company using virtual reality to deliver interactive rehabilitation exercises and improve patient recovery outcomes.

Who should attend:

Investors, founders, startup operators, and anyone interested in tech, innovation, and cross‑border collaboration.

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com
