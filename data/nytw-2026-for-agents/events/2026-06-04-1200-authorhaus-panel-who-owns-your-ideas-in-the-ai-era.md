## Description

As technology changes how ideas are written, packaged, distributed, and remixed, ownership becomes one of the most important conversations of our time. This panel is for founders, creators, authors, and experts thinking seriously about the future of intellectual property, authorship, licensing, and legacy.

Inside the room, we’ll discuss what it means to protect your ideas, expand them, monetize them, and remain relevant in a world where creation is easier than ever. Some people are using AI for convenience. 

Others are using it to build empires. We’ll talk about the difference.
