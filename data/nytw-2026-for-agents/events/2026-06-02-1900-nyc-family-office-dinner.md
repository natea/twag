## Description

Come meet NYC's family offices and those visiting for NYC Tech Week at this dinner, hosted by O'Shaughnessy Ventures, Mike MacCombie of Generous Ventures, and Reitler. This is the third year of hosting this dinner during NYC tech week. It will be a standing gathering for the first half hour, a seated dinner for the attendees to meet each other, and then an optional gathering for a larger group of FOs to continue the conversation afterwards. It is strictly curated for members of families with active startup investing practices, or full time investors at family offices with venture strategies.

About the Hosts:
​Reitler Kailas & Rosenblatt LLP is a national law firm with offices in New York, Princeton and Los Angeles.  With a strong focus on providing sophisticated legal services across a range of practice areas, including venture capital, mergers and acquisitions, private equity, fund formation, taxation, intellectual property, privacy law, real estate, hospitality, entertainment & IP litigation and general commercial litigation, Reitler has established a reputation for excellence.

​Consistently ranked Top 10 in venture capital deal activity per Pitchbook, Reitler's dedicated attorneys employ an innovative approach and ability to close transactions quickly and efficiently at every stage of an organization’s lifespan, from seed funding through a liquidity event, IPO, or other exit.


This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
