## Description

As healthcare AI accelerates, investors are increasingly realizing that the next generation of successful healthcare companies will not be built by capital alone - they will be built alongside the physicians who understand clinical reality, adoption, workflow, and patient trust.

InvestDoc was created by Dr. Amad Khan and Diane Yoo, to bring together doctors, healthcare AI founders, venture investors, and family offices who believe healthcare investing requires true domain expertise.

Featuring:

- Leading neurosurgeons in the digital space
- CEOs of some of the hottest health AI and tech companies
- Venture capitalists and top tier investors
- Sir Petros Kattou KStWM; Chief Neuromodulation Officer – SOZΩ Brain Center, EU HQ Cyprus 
- Dr. Amad Khan, MD; Neurosurgeon 


Join us for the official New York Tech Week launch of InvestDoc.
We will have a private discussion on the growing role of physicians as investors, strategic advisors, and influential voices shaping the future of healthcare innovation.

Discussion topics include:
• Why clinical insight is becoming a competitive advantage in healthcare AI investing
• The growing role of physician-led diligence, trust, and distribution
• How doctors are moving beyond technology adoption into ownership and strategic influence
• Emerging healthcare AI sectors investors are watching closely
• The InvestDoc vision for building a high-signal doctor and investor network around healthcare AI

Curated audience of physicians, accredited investors, and healthcare leaders.

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com
