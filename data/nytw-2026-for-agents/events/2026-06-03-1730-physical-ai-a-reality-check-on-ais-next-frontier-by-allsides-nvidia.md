## Description

Artificial Intelligence is moving beyond the digital world and into the physical one. This shift, often referred to as Physical AI, is quickly becoming one of the most important frontiers in the field.

Leading AI labs, global technology companies, and a new generation of startups are building the foundations for this transition. But while progress is rapid, one question remains central: what does it actually take to make Physical AI work in practice?

NVIDIA is building the compute platforms and simulation tools. ALLSIDES is building the 3D data layer and infrastructure.

Join Franz Tschimben (CEO, ALLSIDES) and Adam Brasic (Senior Solution Architect, NVIDIA) for a fireside chat and reality check on where Physical AI stands today, what is already working, and where real value is emerging.

The conversation will explore how simulation, infrastructure, and AI come together in real-world applications, and what it takes to turn Physical AI into a viable business. It will touch on platforms such as Omniverse and Isaac Sim, the role of high-quality 3D data, and why data is increasingly becoming the bottleneck in this space.

This is a curated gathering of founders, investors, developers, and researchers working at the intersection of AI and the physical world.
