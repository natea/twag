## Description

You don't have a talent problem.
You have a clarity problem.

You've built your career through client work, word of mouth, and real experience. But when it comes to showing up online — explaining what you do, positioning your value, translating your work into a business that actually reflects what you know — it falls apart.

BUILT fixes that.

This is an invite-only, application-based working session where 5 featured builders use Char Pro — T8C's AI brand strategist, built specifically for beauty professionals, to build their brand system in real time. Not inspiration. Not notes. An actual output you leave with.

The session is structured around five builder types — because everyone in the room is at a different stage:

Starting Fresh — strong skills, no clear direction yet

Needs Refinement — has a brand but unclear positioning

Needs Structure — full presence online but the flow isn't converting

Ready to Scale — established and wants to expand or monetize

Multi-Skilled — does everything and needs to know what to lead with

You'll see your stage built live. Then you'll build it yourself.

In the Apply session, every attendee works through their own brand using guided prompts and Char Pro access — you leave with a real starting framework, not a to-do list.

This is also the public debut of Char Pro.

Builder Spots — $25 commitment fee

Limited to 5 featured spots. Application and 15-minute screening call required. The $25 fee is credited toward any Inner Circle Pro membership, Char Pro access, or T8C service purchased within 30 days of the event. No submission = no confirmed spot.

If more than 5 qualified applicants apply, everyone beyond the featured 5 will be offered a complimentary 1:1 consultation with Chardé — so applying is always worth it.

Observers are welcome and free — founders, investors, and operators who want to watch AI translate human expertise into real business structure in real time. 

This isn't AI replacing the human. It's AI amplifying what the human already knows — and building a brand that still centers around human connection.
