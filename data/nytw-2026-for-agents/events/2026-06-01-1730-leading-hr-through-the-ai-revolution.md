## Description

Together with Justworks and leaders from companies including Snappy, NICE, Amdocs, Compete, and more, we’ll discuss how AI is reshaping hiring, people management, leadership, and organizational culture.


AI is no longer just another productivity tool, it’s changing how companies hire, manage teams, build culture, and scale.

Join us for an evening bringing together founders, operators, HR leaders, and executives to discuss what leadership looks like in the age of AI. From AI-first organizations and leaner teams to talent strategy, employee trust, and the future role of HR — this conversation is about the real operational changes already happening inside modern companies.

In collaboration with Justworks and Bereisheet Tech.

Agenda

5:30 PM — Networking & Drinks

6:30 PM — Panel Discussion:

AI-First Companies: How Founders Must Rethink Hiring, Management & Culture

AI is fundamentally changing how modern companies are built. Teams are becoming leaner, workflows faster, and expectations from employees and managers are evolving in real time.

This conversation will explore what it actually means to build an “AI-first” organization today — from hiring and productivity to culture, trust, and leadership. The panel will discuss how founders and executives should rethink organizational structures, what skills will matter most in the next few years, and where human leadership becomes even more important as automation expands.

We’ll also discuss practical realities:

How AI is already changing recruiting and talent evaluation

What happens to middle management in AI-driven organizations

Whether startups will hire fewer people — or simply different people

How to maintain culture and employee engagement during rapid transformation

The balance between operational efficiency and human connection

What founders are getting wrong about internal AI adoption

The session will include audience Q&A and open discussion with founders and operators in the room.

Her work focuses on aligning business strategy with talent, leadership and the evolving impact of AI in HR.

Speakers:

7:00 PM — Fireside Chat:

Building and Leading Teams in the AI Era

Beyond the headlines and tools, AI is forcing leaders to rethink how they personally manage, communicate, prioritize, and execute inside their organizations.

In this fireside chat, we’ll move from theory to real operator experiences: how leadership changes when teams move faster, expectations increase, and employees themselves are adapting to entirely new ways of working.

The conversation will touch on:

How leadership styles are evolving in AI-driven companies

The changing expectations from employees and managers

Decision-making in a world of constant AI acceleration

Managing uncertainty, fear, and resistance internally

How operators personally use AI in their day-to-day work

What remains uniquely human in leadership

What the future workplace may realistically look like in 3–5 years

This will be an open and candid discussion designed for founders, executives, and leaders navigating these changes in real time.
