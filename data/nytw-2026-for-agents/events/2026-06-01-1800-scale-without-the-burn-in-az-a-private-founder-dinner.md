## Description

Arizona is aggressively supporting companies that choose to scale there. The Arizona advantage includes access to non-dilutive and dilutive capital, infrastructure and location, high-quality workforce, multiple testing opportunities and business-friendly policies. 

During New York Tech Week, Musa Capital and the Arizona Commerce Authority are hosting a private, invitation-only dinner for revenue-generating founders ready to expand to Arizona. This curated gathering is designed for founders with $1M+ in annual revenue who are evaluating expansion strategies and seriously considering Arizona as a possible location.

This curated gathering is designed for founders with $1M+ in annual revenue who are evaluating expansion strategies and want direct access to capital, incentives, and state-level partnership opportunities.

You’ll gain insight into:

●	Direct investment capital available to Arizona-based and relocating companies

●	Incentive programs designed to reduce operational burn

●	Lower cost-of-operations environments without sacrificing access to talent

●	Favorable regulatory frameworks built to accelerate growth

●	Enterprise and government partnership pathways and cutting-edge testing facilities

This dinner is intentionally small and strategic — built for operators making serious expansion decisions, not casual networking.

Eligibility requirement:
Companies must have a minimum of $1M in revenue and be open to exploring relocation or expansion opportunities.

Attendance is limited and subject to approval
