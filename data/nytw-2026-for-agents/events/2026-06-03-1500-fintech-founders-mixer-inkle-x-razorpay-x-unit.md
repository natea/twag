## Description

​Welcoming all fintech founders who are in New York, trying to crack the US market. 

​Hosted by Inkle, Razorpay and Unit. 

​This fun mixer is to bring together fintech founders & operators for an evening mixer in New York. Expect great conversations, dinner, and drinks. The kind of night that reminds you why you're building.  

​About the hosts:

Inkle (inkle.ai): is an AI based accounting and tax platform. Focus on building your startup—we’ll handle your bookkeeping, tax, and compliance. Founded by Anand Krishna (YC alum from S17), Inkle currently supports ~ 5% of all YC companies globally.

Razorpay (razorpay.com/us) is an 11-year old payments processing company (YCW15). We offer APIs to accept payments online for domestic US businesses with best-in-class pricing.

Unit (unit.co): the platform modern software companies use to launch and run financial products for their customers: accounts, cards, money movement, and capital, under their brand. Unit processes over $80B annually in money movement, serves 2M+ end users, and handles 15M+ API calls daily for leading platforms including Wix, Worldpay, Honeybook, and Relay.

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek, a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
