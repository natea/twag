## Description

Join us for an evening dedicated to meaningful global connection during a16z Tech Week.

This mixer is designed to create a thoughtful space for Global LPs — including family offices, endowment funds, institutional allocators, and high-net-worth individuals — who are actively deploying capital across borders. Together with GPs from around the world, we aim to exchange perspectives, share insights, and deepen understanding of today’s evolving investment landscape.

As technology continues to reshape global connectivity, we’re excited to explore how the next generation of globalization is unfolding — and how we, as investors, can thoughtfully allocate capital to empower innovation across markets.

Open to public registration. Space is limited.

We look forward to an evening of global dialogue and lasting connections.
