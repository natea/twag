## Description

When chatbots need to be most accurate—when people turn to them during breaking news to understand what’s going on in the world—that’s when they fail most often, and most egregiously. They spread false claims and even cite foreign propaganda.

At NewsGuard, arming news consumers with tools for assessing reliability is what we do.

Misinformation in AI is solvable, and it doesn't require exploiting the publishers whose work the LLMs are trying to source. And now we’ve proven it.

This event will announce and demo the tool that can do it.
