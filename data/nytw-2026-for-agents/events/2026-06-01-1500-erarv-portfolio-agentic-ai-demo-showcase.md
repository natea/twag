## Description

A Private Showcase by Entrepreneurs Roundtable Accelerator & Remarkable Ventures (Invitation Only)

Artificial intelligence is moving from models to systems.

The most important shift is not better outputs—it is autonomy. AI that doesn’t just assist, but acts.

This private showcase brings together a curated group of investors, operators, and enterprise decision-makers to experience how leading startups are deploying agentic AI in production environments today.

No slides. No abstractions. Live demos only.

What You’ll See

A selection of high-performing companies from ERA, Remarkable Ventures, and Remarkable Ventures Climate will demonstrate how agentic AI is transforming:

Enterprise workflows — automating complex, multi-step processes
Financial services — decisioning, underwriting, and execution
Healthcare & life sciences — intelligent coordination and analysis
Climate & energy systems — optimization, forecasting, and control
Sales, operations, and internal tools — AI agents embedded into daily work

Each demo is designed to answer one question:

What does this replace, and how much better is it?

Why Attend

This is not a typical demo day.

For investors: See where real product-market fit is emerging in agentic AI, and where it is not

For enterprise leaders: Identify deployable solutions that can drive immediate efficiency and advantage

For family offices and strategic buyers: Understand where durable value is forming as AI becomes operational infrastructure

You will leave with a clear view of which AI systems are actually working, not just being discussed.

Format

Curated set of live, product-driven demos
Short, high-signal presentations (no pitch decks)
Direct access to founders for follow-up conversations

Who Should Attend

Venture investors and growth equity firms
Family office principals and CIOs
Corporate innovation and strategy leaders
Operators responsible for deploying AI inside real businesses.
