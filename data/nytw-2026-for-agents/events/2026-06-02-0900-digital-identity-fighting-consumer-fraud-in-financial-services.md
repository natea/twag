## Description

Bankers, fintech leaders, and fraud fighters, join us for a deep dive into the rapidly evolving world of consumer identity fraud. Expect great networking, a hearty breakfast, and an expert panel exploring the latest trends shaping fraud detection and prevention.
 
The discussion will examine emerging fraud patterns, regulatory developments, and the future of identity verification in financial services. We’ll explore the impact of AI on fraud and financial services, with a focus on synthetic identity fraud, account takeover attacks, impersonation, and the growing sophistication of modern fraud networks.
 
Panelists:
Adv. Guy Lachmann, Co-Chair of High-Tech Practice
Idan Bar-Dov, Co-Founder & CEO, Heka Global
Megan Shkolyar, General Partner, Gorges Ventures
Dustin J. Eaton, Fraud Principal, Taktile
