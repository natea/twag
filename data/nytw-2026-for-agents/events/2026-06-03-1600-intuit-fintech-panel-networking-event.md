## Description

Join us for an intimate fireside chat and networking event with James Barrese, Senior Vice President of Fintech for Intuit Inc., as he shares his perspective on the evolving landscape of financial technology. 

With deep experience driving innovation at one of the world's most trusted financial platforms, James will explore how fintech is reshaping the way consumers and small businesses manage their financial lives — from embedded finance and AI-powered insights to the future of open banking and digital payments.
