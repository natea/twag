## Description

🚀 Successful exits: what founders should be doing now to get there.

Most early-stage founders focus on product, growth, and fundraising. The companies that consistently outperform are doing something else early, they're building the right people around them and structuring those relationships to actually drive outcomes. 

Join us for a high-impact morning designed for pre-seed to Series B founders who want to build smarter, scale faster, and unlock access to capital.

We will demystify advisory and governing boards and show you how they can accelerate growth, strengthen your investor narrative, and open doors to capital. We'll start with a short fireside on exits; what founders get wrong, how early decisions shape outcomes, and why the people around you matter more than most realize. Then we'll zoom out to the full playbook.

Advisory boards, governing boards, strategic advisors - these are the most underleveraged tools in a founder's arsenal, and experienced entrepreneurs never leave this value on the table.

Why? How to actually build, structure, compensate, and paper these relationships has been nearly impossible to find in one place. Until now.

🎓 You'll walk away knowing:
- the difference between advisory and governing boards and when to build each
- how the right board increases your fundability and investor confidence, practical strategies for structuring, compensating, and managing boards early
- how to identify and recruit high-impact members — and why diversity on your board leads to stronger decisions and better outcomes

💡 Boards should not be scary. They should be your rocket fuel.

Real-world lessons, common pitfalls, actionable frameworks. You'll hear from a VC, an M&A lawyer, and an operator-turned-founder on what the best companies do differently. Whether you're pre-seed or preparing for your next round, you'll leave with the clarity and tools to turn your board into a true strategic advantage, not the other way around.
