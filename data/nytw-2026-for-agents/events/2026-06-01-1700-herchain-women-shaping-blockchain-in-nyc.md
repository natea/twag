## Description

Bitcoin is big, but blockchain is even bigger. And women are shaping the industry’s expansion.

Join us for an evening of high-impact, differentiated conversation as we bring together a cross-sector panel of women sharing unique insights into blockchain in NYC.

📰 The Launch: We’re celebrating the official launch of babsNYC.news, your skimmable guide to decentralized NYC—from finance and tokenization to civil society and impact.

🎭 The Vibe: The event takes place at St. Mark's Comedy Club, the only TechWeek event opening with a stand-up act. 🤭 🎙️  Come for the laughs, stay for the inspo and insights.

🎤 THE LINEUP

Every industry needs guardrails, literacy, and a compelling narrative to go from niche to maturity. 

We’re honored and grateful to host a powerhouse lineup of women shaping the ethos infra for NYC blockchain:

Nora DeSimone: From early days with the NYC District Attorney cybercrime team, to Credit Suisse and JPMorgan, to overseeing global compliance for B2C2, a leading global liquidity provider, Nora brings a Brooklyn-native perspective (and some great stories) about blockchain’s journey from wild west to TradFi-credible. 

Maggie Love, Founder of the SheFi web3 education platform, in its 16th season with 21,000 followers and a global platform.

Jennifer Sanasie, Executive Producer & Senior Anchor at CoinDesk, with videos reaching millions of viewers per month. Jenn is also the host of Public Keys, CoinDesk's fantastic new show broadcasting live from the NYSE.

Hosted by:
Macy Kwok: Digital Assets Compliance expert and Founder of "Women Uninterrupted" comedy show.
Kala Philo: Founder & Senior Editor of babsNYC.news.

The future is all of us - we welcome people of all genders interested in this topic. 

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com
