## Description

Applied AI for the Modern Brand- 
Plug and Play NYC AI Nexus Batch 1 Program

​Join us for an energizing morning at the Plug and Play NYC Batch 1 Expo.

​This inaugural showcase features our first New York cohort, highlighting cutting-edge startups across Brand and Retail and Enterprise and AI. Meet the founders building innovative, real-world solutions as they share their progress, vision, and impact.

​Connect with investors, corporate leaders, and ecosystem partners while exploring how AI is transforming industries, from customer experience to enterprise efficiency. Be part of the momentum shaping the future of AI in New York City.

​NYC AI Nexus is designed to help AI startups scale, support small and medium-sized businesses in adopting AI tools, and encourage collaboration across industry, research, and the public sector. This event brings together founders, investors, corporate leaders, researchers, and ecosystem partners who are focused on using AI to solve real problems across sectors such as Brand & Retail, Food & Beverage, Professional Services (Legal & HR), Media & Advertising, Real Estate, and Travel & Hospitality.

Why Attend?
Discover how NYC AI Nexus advances applied AI and fosters inclusive growth across New York City.
Build connections with AI founders, investors, industry leaders, and partners developing and deploying AI solutions.
Learn about programs, partnerships, and opportunities to grow your startup, implement AI, or collaborate on pilot projects.
​
Who Should Attend?
• AI founders and entrepreneurs
• Investors and venture capitalists
• Industry leaders and small to mid-sized businesses exploring AI adoption
• Corporate partners, researchers, and ecosystem builders interested in applied AI

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
