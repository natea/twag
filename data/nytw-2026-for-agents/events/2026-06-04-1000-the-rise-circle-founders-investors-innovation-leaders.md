## Description

NYC Tech Week brings together some of the brightest minds in innovation. But the conversations that change trajectories happen in smaller, more intentional rooms.

The Rise Circle is bringing that room to New York.

This is a curated panel and networking experience designed to spark the kind of real, honest conversation that founders, especially female and minority founders, rarely get access to. No fluff. No surface-level networking. Just the people, insight, and connections that actually matter.

Our panel brings together founders, investors, and innovation leaders for an honest exchange about what it really takes to build, fund, and scale, and what the ecosystem needs to do better to support the builders who need it most.

Whether you are raising money, investing, scaling your start-up, or building your network, you will leave with real connections, real perspective, and real opportunities.

The Rise Circle — connecting bold leaders, fresh ideas, and opportunities that shape the future.
