## Description

Join us for an interactive evening bringing together women building in AI and women using AI.

This isn’t a typical pitch or networking event. It’s a hands-on showcase where companies and creators can demo their AI products directly to a room full of real women users—getting immediate feedback, onboarding new users, and seeing how their products land in real life.

Attendees will have the opportunity to explore a curated selection of AI-powered apps and tools designed with women in mind. Meet the founders, try the products, and share your thoughts in real time.

Whether you’re building, exploring, or just curious about how AI is shaping everyday life, this is a space to connect, discover, and support one another in a welcoming, community-driven environment.

✨ What to expect:

•  Live product demos at dedicated showcase tables
•  Direct access to founders and builders
•  Real-time feedback and user conversations
•  Opportunities to discover and try new female-focused AI products
•  Food, drinks, and a relaxed, social atmosphere

✨ Who should attend:

•  Women building or working in AI
•  Founders and teams creating AI products for women
•  Early adopters and curious explorers of new tech
•  Anyone interested in the future of AI for real life
