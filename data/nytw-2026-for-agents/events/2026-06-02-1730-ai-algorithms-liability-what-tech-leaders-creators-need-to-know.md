## Description

AI governance has moved from the IT department to the boardroom with mounting legal exposure. 

This session unpacks the enterprise liability landscape for tech companies, founders, and creative professionals navigating algorithm-driven products, data privacy mandates, and evolving regulatory enforcement. We'll examine what boards are demanding on AI risk and where compliance gaps are creating real legal vulnerability.

For business owners and founders operating in spaces that intersect with the creator economy, we'll dive into how AI use for creative is adding a new layer of exposure to product, content, and partnership decisions. 

Panel led by Co-Managing Partner Shaliz Romano and Entertainment Partner & Chair Novika Ishar, moderated by Founder & Managing Partner Domenic Romano. Learn more about our panelists and moderator here: https://www.romanolaw.com/the-firm/team/
