## Description

AI governance has moved from the IT department to the boardroom, and the legal exposure is mounting. 

This session unpacks the enterprise liability landscape for tech companies and founders navigating algorithm-driven products, data privacy mandates, and evolving regulatory enforcement. We'll examine what boards are demanding on AI risk, where compliance gaps are creating real legal vulnerability, and how platform liability is being redefined in real time. 

For business owners and founders operating in spaces that intersect with the creator economy, we'll also touch on how AI-generated content, voice cloning, and licensing disputes are adding a new layer of risk to product and partnership decisions.

Panel led by Founder & Managing Partner Domenic Romano, alongside Entertainment Partner & Chair Novika Ishar. See more from Domenic and Novika here: https://www.romanolaw.com/the-firm/team/
