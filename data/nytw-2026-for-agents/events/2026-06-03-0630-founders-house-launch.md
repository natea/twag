## Description

Join us for the official launch of Founders House. Come meet other founders, operators, advisors and cowork for the day in our space.  ​

Founders House (www.foundershouse.nyc) is a members-only home base in Manahattan where founders work, recover, and build alongside others going through the same thing.  

​It's a workspace (phone booths, conference room and hot desks), a wellness club, and a community in one - designed around how founders actually spend their days.
