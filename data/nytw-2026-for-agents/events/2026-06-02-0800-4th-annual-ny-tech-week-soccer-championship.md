## Description

Investors. Founders. Operators. One Pitch.

Back for the 4th year running, the NY Tech Week Soccer Championship brings together the startup ecosystem’s most competitive (and collaborative) investors, founders, and operators for a morning of fast‑paced soccer, real connections, and post‑game community.

This is a fun & casual game that's well‑organized and  high‑energy designed to spark offline relationships that actually last beyond NY Tech Week.

Whether you’re a former college player, weekend league regular, or just competitive enough to lace up boots for good people and a great cause, this is your chance to play with the ecosystem, not just network around it.

👥 Who’s Playing

--> VCs & Angels
--> Startup Founders
--> Operators across Finance, HR, Ops, Product, and GTM
--> All skill levels welcome (we organize accordingly...see below)


🧩 Team & Match Format

Teams: ~6–8 players per team (small‑sided for high engagement)

Organization:

--> Players will be grouped by role (Investor / Founder / Operator)
--> And by self‑reported skill level to keep games competitive and inclusive

This ensures:

--> Balanced teams
--> High‑quality play
--> No one stuck defending a former D1 striker all afternoon (unless they want to)

👟 What to Bring
Please come ready to play:

--> Soccer or turf shoes (no metal cleats)
--> Athletic wear
--> Water (we’ll have limited extras, but bring your own to be safe)
--> Fun energy + good sportsmanship (no jerks, no slide tackling, no hard shots to someone's face)

🤝 After the Whistle: Stay Connected
The game is just the beginning.

All players will be invited to:

--> World Cup Community Days...in the spirit of the tournament coming this summer to the US where we'll have games, co-working, and watch parties...all in the name of the beautiful game
--> Private WhatsApp Group... for follow‑ups, pickup games, intros, and future matches across NYC and beyond

This is where conversations continue, partnerships form, and next year’s rivalries begin.

✅ Why Play

Real relationships > surface‑level networking
Shared experiences beat small talk
You’ll leave with sore legs, new friends, and actual follow‑ups scheduled

Spots are limited to keep teams tight, RSVP early to secure your place on the pitch.
See you out there. ⚽

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek, a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
