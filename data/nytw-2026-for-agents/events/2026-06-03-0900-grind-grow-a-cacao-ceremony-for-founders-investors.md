## Description

An intimate cacao ceremony for Seed to Series B founders and Partner-level VCs who value real connection over surface-level networking.

In the middle of Tech Week, this is your reset.

With ~20 highly curated attendees, you’ll gather over ceremonial cacao — a natural, sugar-free, non-alcoholic drink known to support focus, openness, and calm — for a guided experience designed to spark clarity, trust, and meaningful conversations.

What to expect:
- Curated, high-signal room with Seed to Series B founders and Partner-level VCs
- Elevated, thoughtfully designed venue
- Ceremonial cacao from women-led Guatemalan collectives

Step off the grind and grow by osmosis. Come talk with founders who are a few steps ahead of you and VC partners with multiple successful exits — in a space built for intentional conversations.
