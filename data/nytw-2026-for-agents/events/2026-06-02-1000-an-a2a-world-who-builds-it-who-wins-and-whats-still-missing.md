## Description

99% of the most useful agents, tools, and services haven't been built yet. Not because the models aren't good enough but because most people still can't picture what a real agent-native world looks like. More and more people are building agents and building products that serves agents. But we are still multiple steps a way from a true A2A economy

Dedalus Labs is bringing together a panel of founders working to close that gap and building the missing parts for where the future of AI agents is headed.

In this session, we examine the state of AI agents now, discuss the A2A future, and breakdown what is stopping a world where agents communicate, coordinate, transact, and be evaluated in the real world. Ending with live Q&A segment with the audience.
