## Description

Compete to present your startup's story on a Broadway stage.  The winner will share the Gammarama main stage with Malcolm Gladwell, Bobbi Brown, and more big names! 

Gammarama is our star-studded storytelling show. One wildcard slot is held for the winner of our pitch competition. This lucky founder gets to tell their startup's story alongside our celebrity storytellers, in front of an audience of 2,000.  

Here's how it works: 

- Interested in competing? Apply here: https://gamma.app/events/gammarama/pitch-competition
- Our judges will choose 10 finalists to pitch live on June 3rd.
- The winner will get to present alongside our star-studded lineup on the Gammarama main stage that evening (and receive a Gamma subscription for life) .

In your RSVP, indicate if you'd like to compete or are coming to watch the 10 finalists live and network. Learn more at the link above.
