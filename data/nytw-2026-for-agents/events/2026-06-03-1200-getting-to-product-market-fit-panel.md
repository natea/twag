## Description

You’ve built your initial product or MVP, launched your website, and maybe landed your first few customers through personal outreach or a handful of experiments.  How do you know if you’ve achieved product-market fit (PMF)?

In this panel discussion, we’ll hear from various points of views on how to get to the elusive product-market fit. We cover questions such as:

- What data and insights should you pull to understand your customers?
- What are investors like Mucker looking for from their founders when sharing that insight and data?
- What do we see as strong signals from other Seed/Series A startups who are validating PMF?

Panelists:

- Cliff Bersani - Investor at Mucker Capital
- Chris Sheng - Founder & CEO of LeadrPro, Growth Advisor at Mucker
- Alex Song - Founder & CEO of Proxima
- Tony Yang - Moderator
