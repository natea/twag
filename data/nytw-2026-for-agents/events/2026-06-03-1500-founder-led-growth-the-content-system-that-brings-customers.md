## Description

Most founders know they should be creating content, but very few know how to turn content into actual customer acquisition.

In this workshop, we will break down how founder-led growth actually works: how to turn insights, product perspective, and company narrative into content that builds trust, reaches the right people, and creates customer demand. We will cover positioning, content angles, distribution, and how to turn founder visibility into pipeline instead of vanity metrics.

Designed for founders and operators who want a practical content system that brings customers.
