## Description

Event Description

The global relocation and moving industry—worth over $100B annually—remains one of the most fragmented, opaque, and operationally inefficient markets today.

We’re bringing together a curated group of founders, operators, and investors to explore how AI, embedded finance, and marketplace infrastructure are transforming physical relocation into a scalable, data-driven category.

Hosted by MoveSwitcher, this private session will dive into the intersection of:

AI-powered logistics and dynamic pricing
Marketplace infrastructure for labor and fleet coordination
Embedded finance: payments, escrow, and insurance in relocation
Data as the foundation for next-generation mobility platforms

The evening will include a short, focused discussion followed by a live product demo of MoveSwitcher—an emerging platform designed to become the operating system for global relocation.

This is not a traditional networking event. Attendance is limited to ensure high-signal conversations and meaningful connections among participants building and backing the next generation of marketplaces and infrastructure companies.
