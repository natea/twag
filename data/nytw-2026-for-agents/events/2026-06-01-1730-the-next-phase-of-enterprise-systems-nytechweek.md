## Description

As organizations push past basic automation, a new model is taking shape: systems that can make decisions and take action independently. This fireside chat will explore how companies are shifting from fixed, rules-based workflows to more adaptive, self-directed operations - and what that transformation looks like in practice. You’ll hear from David Robinson, President, North America and Americas Customer Success Functions @ SAP,  alongside Insight Partners' Managing Director, George Mathew, on where these capabilities are already creating impact, how to approach risk and governance, and what lies ahead.

Following the discussion, we’ll host a startup showcase featuring three companies. Each will present their business, take part in a live conversation with leaders from SAP and Insight Partners, and then open the floor for audience Q&A.

Startups presenting:
• Parker Mitchell, Founder & CEO at Valence 
• Ken Naumann, Founder & CEO at Strike48 and Devo 
• Krishna Gade, Founder & CEO at Fiddler

 
Hors d'oeuvres and drinks will be provided. Looking forward to seeing you there!
