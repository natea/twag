## Description

Tech is getting personal again. People are building cyberdecks, switching to dumbphones, vibe coding their own apps, obsessing over their Notion and homescreen setups, and bringing back Frutiger Aero. Call it a reaction to AI fatigue, digital burnout, or just being tired of using the same stuff as everyone else, there's a real movement toward making your devices, your software, and your workflows feel like they actually belong to you. This is a night for the people in that movement. Come see what others are building, learn what's driving it, and walk away with something new to try.
