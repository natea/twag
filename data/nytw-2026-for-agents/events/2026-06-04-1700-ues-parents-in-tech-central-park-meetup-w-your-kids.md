## Description

For parents in tech who find it hard to make the most of NY Tech Week, this event is for you. 

If you're a founder, investor, or senior operator in tech, we're hosting you and your families for a casual meetup in Central Park in the UES (90s) after school pickup. Treats will be provided and kids are invited! Families from all neighborhoods are welcome, but we are highly preferencing those with children or caregiving responsibilities where typical networking opportunities can be a burden.
