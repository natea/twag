## Description

Master VC Empanadas is a small-group cooking experience for founders, VCs, and operators during NY Tech Week. Hands in the dough, a glass of wine in the other, and the kind of meaningful conversations that only happen when no one's pitching anything.

No decks, no panels. Just an evening of learning to make Argentinian empanadas alongside other people who are building, investing, and operating across the ecosystem.

📌 Limited to 20 selected guests. Invite-only.
