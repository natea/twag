## Description

Empanadas Master VC is a private culinary experience where founders, investors, and ecosystem leaders come together to learn the art of making empanadas — cooking side by side, sharing wine, and building meaningful connections in a relaxed setting.

Designed as an intimate icebreaker during New York Tech Week, the experience transforms cooking into a shared space for conversation, collaboration, and authentic networking beyond the traditional event format.

An invite-only evening created to foster genuine relationships and high-value interactions among some of the most relevant voices in the ecosystem.

Limited to 25 selected guests.
