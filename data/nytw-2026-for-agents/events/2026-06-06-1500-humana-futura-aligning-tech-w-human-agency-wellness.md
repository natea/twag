## Description

Join the big-ideas gathering of NY Tech Week hosted by OneDay MBA alongside other founders, investors, and visionaries as we explore how frontier tech and business incentives can be aligned to keep human agency and wellbeing at the center of an inspiring future.

From AI, biotech, and neurotech to quantum computing and more, the next wave of frontier technologies is reshaping work, psychology, connection, culture, and beyond. We’ll navigate these macro-dynamics to better position ourselves to thrive.

We’ll hear from thought-provoking speakers, contribute ideas in an interactive ideation session, share meaningful conversations over healthy snacks and drinks, and close the evening with an afterparty experience featuring live music.
