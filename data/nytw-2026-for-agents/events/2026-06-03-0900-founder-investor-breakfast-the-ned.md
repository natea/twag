## Description

The Ned's Club Nomad in partnership with the Well Network, Ramp, The Lew Firm and friends, is pleased to host a breakfast with founders and investors as part of NY TechWeek on June 3, 9-11am.

Special guests will share about their role in NYC's innovation ecosystem.   Attendance is limited to Ned's Club members and guests and requires advanced approval.

This event is a part of #NYTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.

By registering or attending the event, the participant consents to receiving emails from the organizers The Ned's Club, The Well Network and partners.  

The Ned’s Club NoMad is a place for diverse professionals to meet, work and have a good time. Members get access to all Club spaces, from a Rooftop restaurant to The Atrium for live music. Members have benefits at our restaurants and bedrooms, and priority access to The Ned NoMad. Plus, exclusive member events, perks and early bookings for special events.  Learn more at: https://www.thened.com/nomad/membership

Ramp is a finance operations platform built to help emerging brands operate efficiently as they scale. Ramp combines corporate cards, expense management, bill pay/AP, and procurement workflows with real-time controls so teams can manage inventory buys, retail/field spend, marketing and influencer expenses, and vendor payments in one place—while giving finance clean books, faster close, and tighter visibility around spend. They're working with companies from David's to Simple Mills and Spindrift. Learn more at: https://ramp.com/partners/the-well-network

The Lew Firm represents an eclectic variety of top-tier clients ranging from entrepreneurs, boxers, musicians, actors, restaurateurs, medical professionals, fine liquor and spirits, and magazine publications. Our legal services included contract drafting/reviewing, corporate transactional law, athlete and artist representation, employment litigation, ABC permitting, and trademarks.  Learn more at:  https://www.thelewfirm.com/

The Well Network is a membership-based organization established in NYC which is committed to empowering the Consumer Packaged Goods (CPG) in food, beverage, consumer tech and wellness communities. The Well Network’s mission is to build a dynamic, engaging, curated ecosystem where members prosper through intentional introductions and the ability to leverage the necessary resources for business advancement.  Learn more at:  https://thewellnetwork.com
