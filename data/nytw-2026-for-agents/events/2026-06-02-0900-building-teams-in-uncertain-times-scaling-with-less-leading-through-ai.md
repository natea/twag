## Description

Join us for a conversation on how founders are building and leading teams in today’s constrained capital environment. We’ll explore how leaders are approaching hiring, culture, and workforce planning amid rapid change, including the growing influence of AI. At the center of the discussion: how to scale thoughtfully.

Featuring insights from Vanessa Liu, CEO, Sugarwork & Board Chair, Appen, the conversation will offer a candid look at what’s working, what’s not, and what’s next for building modern organizations in an era of uncertainty.

Join moderator, Anna Lenhardt, Founder & CEO, Lenhardt Partners, for an intimate, high-level conversation on what it takes to scale organizations with intention - without losing the human element that drives performance, culture, and long-term success.
