## Description

Ship It Boston is a full-day build event during Boston Tech Week. You show up with an idea. You leave with something deployed. That's the whole format.

​Teams of 2 to 4 pick a problem in the morning and spend the day building a real product using Vercel V0, Cursor, Lovable, etc. Each candidate gets $100 coding credits and dedicated mentors. Not a scheduled drop-in. Someone who actually sits with you, works through blockers with you, and pushes you when you're stuck.

​At 4pm, every team pitches live. Five minutes to demo, five minutes of questions from a panel of VCs and investors. The Q&A opens to the whole room after pitches, so even teams that don't place walk away with real feedback from people who fund startups.

​Top three teams win gift cards, platform credits and sponsor swag.

​The day

​9:30am — Breakfast and check-in
10:00am — Build
12:30pm — Lunch
4:00pm — Pitches
5:30pm — Investor panel and open Q&A
6:00pm — Awards and reception

​Spots are limited. We're building a curated mix of founders, engineers, and builders who are serious about shipping. If that's you, apply.

This event is a part of #BOSTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
