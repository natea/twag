## Description

AI agents are moving fast, but most of the conversation is still happening inside the tech world. This event is for the people who run real businesses and want to understand what AI can actually do for them today.

We’ll start with a practical conversation on how AI agents are being used in construction, home services, and other real-world industries. We’ll cover the tradeoffs business owners should understand before adopting AI tools: reliability, security, cost, control, and how to know whether a vertical AI product is actually useful or just hype.

Leading the discussion is Jordan Hayashi, founder of Bizzen and former executive at Kensho, the AI company whose S&P Global acquisition in 2018 dethroned DeepMind as the largest AI acquisition in history. Wan Li Zhu of Suffolk Technologies, the innovation and venture capital arm of Suffolk Construction, one of the 20 largest builders in the country and the largest in Massachusetts, will share what he sees as the most exciting AI agents emerging in the built world.

Then the room flips into a hands-on mini-hackathon where you'll build a simple AI agent for your own workflow, with Bizzen's AI engineers supporting you the whole way. No coding experience required, just a problem worth solving.

This event is a part of #BosTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com
