## Description

As AI accelerates software development, many teams are shipping faster than they can properly validate. Testing at the Speed of AI is an evening panel discussion co-hosted by Venture Lane and Software Velocity Corp. for founders, CEOs, CTOs, and engineering leaders on the new realities of software quality, validation, and risk in the age of AI-driven development. The event will feature experienced technology leaders, followed by networking over appetizers and drinks.

This event is a part of #BOSTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
