## Description

Behind the Hustle: How Personal Finances Make or Break Startups
Everyone talks about building a startup. Almost no one talks about what it costs you personally.

Behind every bold founder story is a more honest one; the savings drained, the salary skipped, the money decisions made under pressure. This panel pulls back the curtain on the financial realities founders rarely say out loud and investors rarely ask about.

Whether you're pre-seed or post-exit, this one's for every founder who has wondered if the sacrifices are adding up in the right direction.
What we'll cover

The unspoken financial toll of building and how it compounds over time
How your money relationship shapes your decisions, leadership, and risk appetite

What financial resilience actually looks like as a founder. 

Light drinks & appetizers 

Panelists: 

Stanley Rameau — Founder, Renavest
A platform bridging emotional wellness and financial growth through personalized financial therapy.

Darrel Frater — Founder & Managing Partner, TGN Ventures
