## Description

Ever notice how most demo-days don’t actually have any demos? We’re ditching the deck to showcase the game-changing AI products startups are building. This is about AI show, not tell!

Ditch the Deck is a social meet-up curated for founders, technologists, tinkers, investors, and connoisseurs. Come watch 5 demos and mingle across the startup community!

Over the course of a few hours, we'll enjoy an evening filled with networking, live product demos, and absolutely zero pitch decks or slides, just pure innovation💡

Demos have already been selected for this event, come watch them! 

Thank you to our partners at Gunderson Dettmer, the go-to startup attorney and Mercury, the go-to startup banker! 

This event is a part of #BOSTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
