## Description

Most B2B companies are running outbound the same way they were five years ago: SDRs combing through LinkedIn, manually building lists, writing sequences one contact at a time, and logging everything by hand. It is slow, inconsistent, and it breaks every time someone on the team leaves.
The result is a pipeline problem that most revenue teams have accepted as inevitable. CAC keeps rising. Response rates keep falling. And the board keeps asking why the pipeline coverage ratio is not where it needs to be.
It does not have to work this way.
At Tech Week Boston 2026, Hamlet Azarian, Founder and CEO of Azarian Growth Agency, will demo, live and in real time, the autonomous agent system his team has built and deployed for B2B companies. Not a concept. Not a recorded walkthrough. A working system, running in the session, building a real pipeline from a real ICP.
The system is built on three agents that operate in sequence. The first agent maps your total addressable market and scores each one against live intent signals to identify who is actively looking for a solution right now. 
The second agent identifies the exact decision makers to target at each company: title, seniority, department, contact. No SDR hours. No manual research.
The third agent builds a full persona for each decision maker and generates a personalized outreach sequence: not a template, a message written specifically for that person at that company.
Together, these agents do what an SDR team spends most of their time doing, and they do it faster, at higher volume, and with more consistency than any human workflow can produce at scale.
Azarian Growth Agency has built and deployed 91 agents across client engagements. This session is the proof. You will see the TAM builder identify companies that match your criteria. You will see the decision maker finder pull the right contacts in seconds. You will see a fully personalized outreach sequence generated, ready to send, before the session ends.
This session is designed so that by the end of the hour, you have a clear answer to one question: what would my pipeline look like next quarter if this system were running for my company?
If you are a VC-backed founder spending money on SDRs and not hitting pipeline targets, a VP of Sales watching outbound response rates collapse, or a PE or VC operator evaluating GTM efficiency across portfolio companies, this session was built for you.
