## Description

Swan × Link Ventures · Boston Tech Week
Wednesday, May 28 · 5:15 PM onwards
1 Kendall Square, Building 200, 2nd Floor · Cambridge

Here's what we believe: in a world where AI can do almost anything, the thing that can't be replicated is you - your judgment, your context, your relationships, your taste, your instinct for what's real and what's noise.

We built an AI GTM Engineer to carry the execution so you can carry the judgment. We want to show you what that feels like.

The format is simple. You show up. You tell us your messiest GTM challenge, the one that's been bugging you all quarter. We solve it live, in front of the room, in about 5 minutes. You walk away with a free Swan account already loaded with context about YOUR company.

No slides. No panels. No name tags with corporate titles. Just drinks, founders, operators, investors, and a live demo that either impresses you or doesn't. We're betting it does.

Who we are: Swan is a 3-person AI startup. Our CEO flew in from Israel. Our Chief Evangelist flew in from Sri Lanka and our VP of Autonomous GTM umm took the train. Our AI GTM Engineer didn't need a flight, it's already researched your company.

Come find out what it knows.

🍸 Drinks and food on us
💻 Bring your laptop if you want to build during the session
🦢 Walk away with a Swan account personalized to your business
