## Description

The Consulate General of Mexico in Boston, the Carnegie Mellon University (CMU) Hispanic-Latino Alumni Association (HLAA) of Mexico and Boston, along with Sundai LATAM, invite you to a special event as part of the first edition of Tech Week in Boston.

Come discover how the Mexican & LATAM tech community more broadly is riding the AI wave, explore cross-border collaborations, and connect with innovators who dream in algorithms. Whether you're training models or just trying to understand what a transformer is (the AI kind, not the robots), this is your spot to geek out about artificial intelligence between Mexico, LATAM, and Boston. 🤖🇲🇽
