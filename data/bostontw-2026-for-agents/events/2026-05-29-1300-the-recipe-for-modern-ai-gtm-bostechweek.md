## Description

The Recipe for Modern GTM is a practical session for founders, operators, and GTM leaders who want to understand how modern go-to-market actually works. This is not a generic AI panel or a sales pitch. It is a real-world conversation about how teams connect AI, data, signals, and workflows to turn ideas into execution.

Most teams already have ingredients. They have tools, meetings, channels, data, and ideas. What they are missing is the recipe and the operating rhythm to make those ingredients actually produce revenue. This session brings together three different operator perspectives on what modern GTM looks like when you connect better inputs, better systems, and better execution.

What to expect:

- Practical insight from three different GTM and operator perspectives
- A live GTM teardown and audience Q&A
- Clear takeaways you can bring back to your team right away
