## Description

The org chart is breaking. AI agents are replacing early hires, and most founders are scaling without a playbook for what their team should actually look like.

This is a panel + hands-on workshop for pre-seed to Series B founders and you leave with a redrawn org map and a 30-day action list.

We'll cover:

1) Why the old org chart fails at 15 people (and how to fix it)
2) Real org structure cases by stage: pre-seed through Series B
3) Dynamic Teaming, a framework for building a team that evolves as your company does
4) How to use fractional generalists, AI agents, and Northeastern co-op talent to go further without burning cash

Hosted by Britt Gage, founder of of All Trades, and Nancy Tarr, Senior Co-op Faculty at Northeastern whose co-op program places students who work as true team members, not interns.

For founders who want to stop guessing at org design and actually build for what's next.

Please bring your computer to be able to participate! 

This event is a part of #BOSTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
