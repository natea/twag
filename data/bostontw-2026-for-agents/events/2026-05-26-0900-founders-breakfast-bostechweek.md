## Description

Join us for a Founders Breakfast co-hosted by @Fidelity Private Shares, @HSBC Innovation Banking, and @Puzzle.

Over coffee and breakfast, we’ll chat about fundraising, building great teams, finding product–market fit, and the real challenges that come with scaling a company.

Expect thoughtful conversations, meaningful connections, and a room full of builders who truly understand the journey.

Who should attend
• Venture-backed founders
• Startup CEOs and cofounders actively building and scaling

What to expect
• Casual, high-quality networking over breakfast
• Honest, founder-to-founder conversations
Spots are limited—we’d love to have you join us.

Fidelity Private Shares is committed to supporting the startup & venture capital community. Fidelity helps founders prepare for their next round of funding with our cap table management and data room platform, events that give you access to capital, and premier support to generate, execute & store legal documents.  No matter where you are in your journey - from inception to IPO - Fidelity has the expertise to support you.

Puzzle’s accounting software is built for growing startups that need real-time financial insights without the hassles of monthly bookkeeping. Its AI-powered platform automates transaction categorization, reconciliation, and daily reporting for accurate numbers you can trust. The result is faster decisions, stronger growth, and peace of mind.

​HSBC Innovation Banking brings HSBC’s expansive product suite and unmatched international capabilities to support startups and their VCs in the innovation ecosystem at every stage of their journey. Our mission is to provide founders and their teams with exceptional service, tailored banking and venture debt facilities, and value-add connections to our extensive global network of investors and corporate clients.

This event is a part of #BosTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
