## Description

​Join Standard Metrics and Juniper Square for a morning of pickleball, networking, and conversation during Boston Tech Week. We’re bringing together a group of VC finance, operations, and platform leaders for a casual gathering on Friday, May 29th from 10:30 AM – 1:30 PM ET. Whether you’re looking to rally on the court, connect with peers in the ecosystem, or simply enjoy a laid-back morning with great people, we’d love to have you join us. Light refreshments, open play, and good company included.

Juniper Square provides connected software and fund administration solutions for private markets GPs. Firms including Felicis, Greystar, and Satori Capital use Juniper Square to streamline fundraising, investor onboarding, reporting, fund operations, and the overall LP experience through a unified platform.

​Standard Metrics is an AI-driven portfolio monitoring and reporting platform built for venture capital and private equity firms. Firms like Bessemer Venture Partners, General Catalyst, Accel, and Salesforce Ventures use Standard Metrics to automate data collection, streamline LP reporting and portfolio reviews, benchmark company performance, and centralize investment data in a single source of truth.

This event is a part of #BosTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com
