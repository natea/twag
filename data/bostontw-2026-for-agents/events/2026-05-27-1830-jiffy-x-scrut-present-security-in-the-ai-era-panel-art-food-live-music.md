## Description

Jiffy and Scrut Automation are teaming up for an evening with Boston's security community, hosted in a modern art gallery. Expect great conversation, food, drinks, and a chance to connect with practitioners across every level of the field.

AI is changing what skills matter, what roles look like, and how hiring decisions get made, but nobody's talking straight about it. Our moderator will sit down with a panel of cybersecurity practitioners from CISO to SOC analyst, working at hyperscalers, financial institutions and security startups, for a candid 45-minute panel on what it actually takes to break in, level up, and stand out in the new era of hiring.

After the panel: live music, catered food and drinks, and open networking for the rest of the night, surrounded by unique art pieces that AI could never generate. Stay for the kind of off-the-record  conversations that make these events worth showing up for.
