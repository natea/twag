## Description

There’s no shortage of ideas in EdTech - but very few are actually working at scale.

Join us for a focused conversation with founders, operators, and education leaders building across K-12 learning and postsecondary pathways, sharing what’s truly driving outcomes today.

We’ll run two short, high-signal panels:

Panel 1
Fixing Learning Early: K–12 to Higher Ed
What’s actually improving how students learn, from personalization to real engagement across the journey.

Featuring:

Ray Batra - A leading voice in education industry and Cofounder @ Future Of Higher Ed (FOHE)

Ryan Patenaude - Founder & Managing Director of RP Impact Partners and a K–12 EdTech Investor and Advisor with 17+ years of experience

Narayani
A Harvard education graduate bringing a thoughtful lens on learning design, student outcomes, equity, and how modern education systems can better support learners from early stages through long-term growth.

Panel 2
From Learning to Outcomes
How education connects to real-world paths : college, careers, and beyond.

Followed by curated networking with founders, investors, educators, and builders shaping the future of learning.

No fluff. No generic “future of education” talk.
Just what’s actually working - and what’s coming next.

This event is part of #BosTechWeek - a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
