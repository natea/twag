## Description

A rolling lightning-talk + office-hours style discussion during #BostonTechWeek exploring how AI, structured data, marketing effort, and HCP/patient behavior are reshaping #healthcarecommunication and #betterdecision-making.

Featuring:
•	Katie Manty, MIT Sloan MBA and healthcare marketing/data strategy leader with experience across analytics, omnichannel engagement, and regulated industries
•	Umbereen S. Nehal, physician executive, board-certified clinical informaticist, and MIT-trained healthcare AI and systems strategist

Drop in for 20 minutes and Explore With Us
•	Which communication channels people actually trust for health information
•	Why AI readiness depends on data structure — not just new tools
•	How healthcare organizations can move beyond vanity metrics toward systems-level insight

Designed for a curious #BostonTechWeek audience of technologists, founders, healthcare innovators, marketers, and systems thinkers. We love patient perspectives - No healthcare experience required.

Come prepared to vote, discuss, and rethink how healthcare data flows through real-world systems.
