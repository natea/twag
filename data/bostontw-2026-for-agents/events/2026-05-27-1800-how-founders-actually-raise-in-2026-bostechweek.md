## Description

Fundraising advice is usually either too theoretical or too spammy. This session is a practical workshop for founders who want to build real investor momentum in 2026. We’ll break down the modern fundraising operating system: tightening your story, mapping warm intro paths, sequencing follow-up, and using AI to stay consistent without sounding robotic. Expect tactical frameworks, live examples, and curated connections with founders and capital partners.
