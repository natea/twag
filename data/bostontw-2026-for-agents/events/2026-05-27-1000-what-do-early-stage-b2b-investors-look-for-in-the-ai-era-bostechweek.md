## Description

If you're a founder raising money, this is an event for you to hear real VC perspectives! If you are an early-stage VC, join to meet founders.

Panel discussion with:
 * Kent Bennett, Bessemer
 * David Beisel, NextView Ventures
 * Alex Harstrick, J2 Ventures
 * Maia Heymann, ConvergeVC
Co-hosted with Foley Hoag

This session will explore how these investors think about market potential, founders, traction, and what ultimately drives conviction (or hesitation) in backing a startup.
*️ The “must-haves” before a VC invests 
*️ Weighing the Team vs. the Idea
*️ Pattern Recognition vs. Pattern Breaking
*️ Blending Intuition, Metrics, and Market Research in early-stage Investments

This event is a part of #BOSTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
