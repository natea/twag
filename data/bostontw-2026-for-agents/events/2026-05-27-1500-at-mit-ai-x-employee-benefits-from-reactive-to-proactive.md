## Description

How AI Is Transforming Employee Benefits: From Reactive to Proactive Healthcare

Join Avenir AI at MIT for an inside look at how AI agents are revolutionizing the way organizations uncover cost drivers in employee benefits and shift healthcare strategy from reactive to proactive.

The Problem
Every year, HR leaders, CFOs, total rewards teams, benefits leaders, and consultants spend hundreds of hours manually sifting through claims, pharmacy, and utilization data to identify what's actually driving plan costs. Despite the effort, critical insights still slip through the cracks—and rising costs continue to outpace strategy.

What You'll Discover
In this session, we'll explore how Avenir AI's intelligent agents are transforming that process, turning complex benefits data into instant, decision-ready analysis that pinpoints cost drivers with precision and speed. You'll see live examples of how AI surfaces insights in minutes that would take analysts weeks to uncover.

Who Should Attend
Employee benefits and HR leaders
CFOs and finance leaders
Brokers and benefits consultants
Total rewards professionals

Whether you're managing a plan, advising clients, or rethinking your benefits strategy, come see how AI is reshaping the way we uncover what's really behind rising costs.
