## Description

​Join us during Boston Tech Week for an evening of connection, conversation, and a little friendly competition.

Gesmer Updegrove LLP is bringing together founders, operators, investors, and builders for a high-energy mixer at Spin Boston—where networking doesn’t feel like networking.

​Skip the awkward small talk and connect over ping pong, drinks, and real conversations with people actively building and investing in the tech ecosystem.

​Whether you’re scaling a company, exploring your next venture, or looking to expand your network, this is a space to meet the right people—organically.

​What to expect:

​Open networking with curated attendees

​Ping pong play throughout the night (no skill required)

​Drinks and light bites

​A relaxed, social atmosphere designed for meaningful connections

​Who should attend:
Founders, startup operators, investors, and members of the Boston tech community.

​Spots are limited so make sure to RSVP!

This event is a part of #BOSTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
