## Description

BY POPULAR DEMAND!! An inside look at the surf park and wave pool industry.

1. Why are billions of dollars being poured into wave pools?
2. Why did Doja Cat, Adam Neumann, and Mark Zuckerberg get on board?
3. How are surf parks redefining wellness, the experience economy, and real estate?

Currently growing 80% year over year with over $1B invested last year alone, the surf park sector is poised to reshape how people experience surfing, wellness, and leisure.

By the end of this event you will understand:
- the financial state of the global surf park market
- technologies powering the wave pools and operations
- what's missing from the industry & how you can fill the gaps

================================

Val S. K., founder of Surflytics, will share primary research across the global surf park industry including concrete data and detailed insights from behind the scenes interviews with surf park executives from her forthcoming book Surf Parks & Wave Pools Around the World.

(Use code BOSTECHWEEK for 30% off)

A former software engineer, Val built and maintains Surf Park DB--the world's most comprehensive free public database on surf parks covering technology trends, market growth, real estate models, and the new category of recreation-driven developments.

Ideal for founders, investors, developers, and anyone curious about where surf, tech, and real estate intersect.
