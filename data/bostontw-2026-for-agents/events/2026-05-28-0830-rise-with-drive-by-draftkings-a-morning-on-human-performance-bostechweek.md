## Description

Most people know they are leaving performance on the table.

In a world that rewards sustained output, the edge increasingly comes from how well you recover, focus, and adapt over time. Sleep, recovery, and resilience are not just wellness priorities. They are foundational inputs into both peak performance and long-term health.

The science has been clear for years. Now, technology and AI are accelerating how quickly data becomes insight, and how quickly insight turns into action.
RISE is a breakfast gathering for founders, operators, and investors who want to go deeper on what high performance actually requires, not just in moments, but over time.

Over breakfast, we will explore the inputs that compound quietly: starting with sleep, moving into recovery and inflammation, and expanding into the tools making performance and longevity more measurable and actionable than ever.

Join us for a thoughtful conversation, practical insights, and a chance to connect with others building at the intersection of performance, health, and technology.

Speakers to date:
