## Description

AI is changing what one person can build.

In the past, launching a serious product, community, media brand, or service business often required a small team across product, engineering, design, marketing, research, content, and operations. Today, AI tools and agentic workflows are making it possible for solo founders and small teams to move faster with far fewer resources.

This virtual coffee chat is for solo founders, indie hackers, one-person company builders, creators, operators, and anyone curious about how AI is reshaping the way we build.

Instead of a formal webinar or lecture, participants will be randomly matched into small breakout groups to introduce themselves, share what they are building, discuss how they are using AI in their work, and meet others building in a similar direction.

Expect a relaxed, practical networking session focused on real workflows, honest challenges, shared learnings, and meaningful connections among people building in the AI era.

OPC Community is the global community for AI-era solo founders and one-person companies. OPC stands for One-Person Company — a real, scalable business operated by a single founder using AI tools, automation, software, and global distribution leverage. It is built for people who are creating products, communities, media brands, agencies, SaaS tools, consulting practices, and independent businesses without a traditional full-time team.

With the belief of “Build solo, not alone,” OPC Community helps solo founders, indie hackers, AI builders, creators, consultants, and high-leverage individuals connect, grow, and build together through community, resources, events, opportunities, AI tools, and founder support.
