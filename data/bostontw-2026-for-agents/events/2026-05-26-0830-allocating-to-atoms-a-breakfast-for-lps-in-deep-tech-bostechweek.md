## Description

Join First Star Ventures for a Boston Tech Week breakfast briefing designed specifically for limited partners evaluating deep tech exposure in their portfolios.

Software VC playbooks don't map cleanly onto synthetic biology, energy, or robotics but these are precisely the categories where the next wave of massive, durable, defensible outcomes is being built. Technical risk profiles, capital intensity, time-to-revenue, and exit dynamics all look different in deep tech, and LPs allocating here need frameworks to reflect that.

Over breakfast in Kendall Square (the densest square mile of deep tech innovation in the world), we'll cover:
* Capital intensity and dilution in deep tech startups: truths and myths
* Milestones that matter in early-stage, frontier technologies
* Current signal from the Boston/Cambridge ecosystem across chips, bio, and energy

Whether you're already allocating or still only evaluating, join us in Kendall Square to trade notes on what's working, what isn't, and what we're seeing across the deep tech ecosystem in Boston and beyond. Hosted by First Star Ventures.

This event is a part of #BOSTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
