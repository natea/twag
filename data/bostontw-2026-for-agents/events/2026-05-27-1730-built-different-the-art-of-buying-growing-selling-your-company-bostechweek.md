## Description

The best exits don't happen by accident. Neither do the best acquisitions.

Built Different brings together a panel of experienced operators, investors, and dealmakers who have lived every stage of the M&A journey — from building a company with intention, to navigating an acquisition, to executing a successful exit. This is the conversation that happens after the deal closes, brought to the room before you need it.
We'll cover the full arc: how to grow a company that's positioned for success, what it actually takes to buy or sell, and how to set yourself up to win at every stage of the process. 

Then stick around for happy hour and connect with the dealmakers, operators, and investors in the room and keep the conversation going over drinks.

This event is for: Leaders building a company with an eye toward what's next · Executives exploring acquisitions as a growth strategy · Operators and investors who want an honest look at what M&A really looks like · Anyone who has been through a deal and wants to do the next one better

This event is a part of #BOSTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
