## Description

The biggest barriers to AI in manufacturing aren’t technical—they’re human.

In this fireside chat, Ben Armstrong (Executive Director, MIT Industrial Performance Center), Liz Asai (Global Head of AI & Connectivity at Hologic), Pete Gowac (Sr. Director of U.S. Operations at Insulet), Alvin Clark (Sr. AI Engineer at NVIDIA), and Daniel Cummins (Fellow/VP, ISG Edge Solutions Platforms at Dell Technologies) explore the often-overlooked layer of AI adoption: trust, workforce dynamics, and the realities of implementing change inside complex manufacturing environments.

From frontline skepticism to leadership misalignment, we’ll unpack why even well-built systems fail to gain traction—and what successful teams are doing differently to bring their people along.

This is a discussion for those responsible for making AI real: plant leaders, operations executives, and investors looking to understand where value is actually created—or lost.

This event is a part of #BOSTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
