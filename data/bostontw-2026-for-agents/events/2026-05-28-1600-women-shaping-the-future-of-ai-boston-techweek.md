## Description

Find Women X at: https://womenx.foundation/

Women Shaping the Future of AI is a gathering by WomenX that brings together women working across both the tech industry and academia.

As AI continues to transform industries and research, women are already shaping the future of this field through leadership, innovation, and technical expertise. This event creates a space for women in AI to share their experiences, exchange ideas, and build meaningful connections within a supportive and ambitious community.

At this event, we will invite women from companies such as Meta, NVIDIA, and Red Hat. They will share how they use AI in their daily work and how they see AI evolving across different industries and research areas.

After the talks, there will be a networking session where speakers and attendees can connect more freely, continue conversations, and get to know one another.

To keep the community thoughtful and relevant, attendance will be curated. We ask applicants to share materials such as a resume, GitHub, personal website, or social media that reflects their work or interests.

Given the curated nature of the event, it may take 1–3 business days to review and confirm applications. Thank you for your patience!

We’re building a space where women in AI can learn from one another, support each other, and grow together as builders, researchers, founders, and leaders.

This event is a part of #BostonTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
