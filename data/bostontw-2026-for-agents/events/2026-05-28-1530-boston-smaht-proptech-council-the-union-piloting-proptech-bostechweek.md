## Description

Most technology pilots start with a handshake and end in a drawer. How do you make sure yours actually scales?

Join the Boston Smaht chapter of Building Intelligence Group and the US PropTech Council for a brutally honest panel on what it really takes to get a building technology pilot off the ground — and across the finish line.

We're bringing together owners, vendors, and advisors to have the conversation the industry usually avoids:

• Why do owners insist on pilots — and is it really about the technology?
• What does a pilot actually cost a vendor that never shows up on any invoice?
• Should pilots be paid — and what does your answer say about how serious you are?
• Who's really responsible when a pilot succeeds but the rollout fails?
• How do you define success before you start, not after?

This isn't a product showcase. It's a room full of people who've been on both sides of the table — and are willing to say what they actually think.

Come for the insights. Stay for the best networking in Boston's PropTech scene.

This event is a part of #BosTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
