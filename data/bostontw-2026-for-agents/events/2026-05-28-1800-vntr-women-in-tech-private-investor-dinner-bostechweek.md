## Description

For Investors Only

"Women in Tech: Private Investor Dinner"

Join us in Boston for an intimate dinner bringing together a select group of women shaping the future of venture capital; partners, principals, and decision-makers actively investing across the tech landscape.
This gathering is designed for genuine connection and candid exchange. Over a long-table dinner, we'll explore how technology is reshaping every industry, where capital is flowing next, and how investment strategies are evolving across cycles. The room will be small by design (15 to 18 guests) to create space for the kinds of conversations and connections that don't happen at larger industry events. This dinner is the first in what we hope will be an ongoing series.

Format: Private dining, single long table

Agenda
6:00 — Cocktails and arrivals
6:45 — Welcome and introduction from VNTR, Citizens Bank and G19 Studio
7:00 — Guest introductions
7:30 — Dinner and discussion
9:00 — Closing toast

What to expect: 
•⁠  ⁠An intentionally curated room of women in decision roles across Boston's VC ecosystem
•⁠  ⁠Roundtable introductions and guided discussion on the topics shaping the next investment cycle
•⁠  ⁠A closing "ask and offer" round: leave with specific ways to help and be helped
•⁠  ⁠An optional group to stay in touch with fellow attendees after the dinner

This event is a part of #BosTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com
