## Description

Customer acquisition costs are climbing across every channel. CPMs are up, attribution is gutted, and the old playbook of spending more on Meta and Google is producing diminishing returns. Brands are paying more per customer and converting fewer of them once they arrive.

Join us on a beautiful rooftop in Fenway for a panel and networking event bringing together the founders building the next generation of ecomm tools and the agencies implementing them.

We'll cover both sides of the equation: how brands are driving traffic without burning cash, and how they're converting that traffic at higher rates once it lands.
