## Description

Women who build Boston - from mentorship to sponsorship is a Boston Tech Week breakfast gathering hosted at PwC, convening women executives and senior leaders shaping the region’s tech ecosystem. Join us for a thoughtfully curated morning of meaningful conversation, peer connection, and a candid panel discussion—designed to spark new relationships, share hard-won insights, and celebrate the women building what’s next in Boston.   The event will consist of a key note opening speaker Kim Walsh, GTM Leader, Lovable, followed by a panel consisting of Jennifer DiRico, CFO, PTC Inc.; Rebecca Schechter, Ind. Board Director, Advisor to PE, CEO, Optum/Liberty Mutual; Carol Meyers, Ind. Board Director, Former CMO, Rapid 7; Kate Prouty, CIO, Akami Technologies; Kristin Francisco, US Chief Operating Officer, Assurance, PwC; Shara Slattery,  Assucance Partner, PwC; Heather Lombardo, Assurance Partner, PwC.

This event is a part of #BOSTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
