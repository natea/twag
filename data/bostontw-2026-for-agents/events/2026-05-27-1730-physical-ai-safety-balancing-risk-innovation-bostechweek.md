## Description

The robotics industry is at an inflection point. Systems are getting more autonomous and more capable, but how do you move fast on innovation while actually shipping systems that won't fail or be compromised?

We're excited to gather a community of people who are building, operating, and actively deploying physical AI systems. We'll dig into conformance, observability, and the infrastructure that lets you expand what your systems can do without creating liability or safety nightmares.

Join us for a high-energy and informative panel with people actually deploying Physical AI in the field, to learn how to balance moving fast with managing real risk in physical systems.

Followed by an Open Mic and light networking.
