## Description

Pillar VC is hosting an evening for future founders in Boston. Come meet a co-founder, build your founder network, and share your breakthrough ideas.

Attendees will include engineers, researchers, business and product leaders who want to build something new. Some attendees will be in the process of starting ventures now, while others will be many years out from departing their current roles. You do not need to have an idea or a clear path to starting something today, but rather that you have dreams of one day building something new and novel from scratch.

The evening will be a mix of introductions, open networking, and 1:1 match chats with a highly curated group.

Apply to attend. Space is limited.

This event is a part of #BOSTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
