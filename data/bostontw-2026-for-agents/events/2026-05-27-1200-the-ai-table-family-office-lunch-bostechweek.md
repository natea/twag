## Description

By application only. Capacity: 20.

Everyone throws around the phrase “AI is the next Industrial Revolution” to point of it being a shallow meaningless cliche. But it’s not wrong to say that!

The problem is everyone is talking about the AI itself. Few are talking about the industries around it in a meaningfully coherent way. What are the inputs/outputs? What massive changes will we see there?

Like the first Industrial Revolution…we need to be prepared for how AI is going to fundamentally transform our economy and industries in ways we probably can’t easily predict.

Mineral scarcity? How do we fix that?
Energy transformation? How do we fix that?
Massive labor displacement? How do we fix that?
Sudden and accelerating wealth inequality? How do we fix that?
Water? How do we fix that?
On and on and on…

The AI Table is a closed-door lunch during the first-ever Boston Tech Week, convening the founders building it, the investors funding it, and the operators inside the institutions that have to absorb it.

One question goes to the table. Everyone answers.

No panels. No decks. No pitching. What happens in the room stays in the room.

Convened by Quantum Alliance, the talent and capital network across the full AI stack. Co-hosted with the Massachusetts AI Hub, the Commonwealth's flagship AI initiative connecting Boston's research institutions, innovation ecosystem, and private capital.

Every seat is individually reviewed and confirmed. Approved guests will receive the venue address and guest list preview 48 hours before the event.

This event is a part of #BOSTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
