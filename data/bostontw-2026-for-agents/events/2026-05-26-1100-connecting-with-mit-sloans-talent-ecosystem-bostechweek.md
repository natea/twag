## Description

As Boston’s innovation ecosystem continues to scale, access to top-tier talent remains a real constraint. MIT Sloan’s Career Development Office will host a Boston TechWeek session for venture capitalists, early‑stage founders, and established tech leaders focused on practical, efficient ways to engage MIT Sloan’s global network of alumni and students—operators and leaders with experience across startup, growth, and enterprise environments.

The session will share concrete models, real examples, and clear pathways investors and companies can use to build earlier, more strategic relationships with talent—supporting venture formation, scaling, and long-term impact across the Boston tech ecosystem.

This event is a part of #BOSTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
