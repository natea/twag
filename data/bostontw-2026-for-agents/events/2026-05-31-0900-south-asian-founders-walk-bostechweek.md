## Description

Join us for a curated morning walk along Boston’s Harborwalk, bringing together South Asian founders, builders, operators, investors, and professionals in town for Boston Tech Week.

We’ll walk from Christopher Columbus Waterfront Park to Fan Pier along the waterfront, passing public art, open harbor views, and some of the city’s best skyline backdrops. No panels, no formal agenda, just a relaxed setting to meet good people, have real conversations, and start the day strong.
