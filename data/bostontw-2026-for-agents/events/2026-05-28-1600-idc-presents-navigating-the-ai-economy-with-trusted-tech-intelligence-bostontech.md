## Description

Boston has always been where the future gets built. IDC, a leading tech intelligence and AI firm, was founded in the Boston area 60+ years ago and has recently returned its global headquarters to downtown Boston. This May, IDC is proud to host an exclusive event as part of Boston Tech Week.

Join IDC for an afternoon of expert briefings, sharp analysis, and candid conversation on the forces reshaping the technology industry. The program features three focused sessions with Q&A, followed by cocktails and networking.

The Next Trillion-Dollar Opportunity 
Meredith Whalen, Chief Product, Research & Delivery Officer 
Where is the next massive wave of technology-driven value being created — and how do you position your business to capture it? Meredith draws on IDC's global research to map the opportunity landscape ahead.

AI Agents: The Next Shift 
Rick Villars, Group Vice President, Worldwide Research 
Agentic AI is moving from experimentation to enterprise reality. Rick breaks down what this shift means for technology strategy, infrastructure, and competitive positioning – and what actions you need to be taking now.

Marketing Your Company in an AI World 
Sudhir Rajagopal, Research Director, CMO Advisory Service 
When every company claims to be an AI company, how do you stand out? Sudhir offers a practical framework for marketing leaders navigating differentiation, messaging, and growth in an AI-saturated market.

The briefings run a total of 90 minutes, followed by Q&A and then a cocktail reception. Attendees will have time to connect with IDC analysts and fellow technology leaders from across the Boston ecosystem.


This event is a part of #BostonTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
