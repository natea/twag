## Description

Early-stage teams obsess over tech debt and ignore the trust debt that kills them first. In this session, a startup founder turned product manager and a marketing leader break down trust debt into two dimensions — security trust (compliance, reliability, safety) and market trust (positioning, promises, buyer belief) — and give you a practical scorecard and 90-day playbook to close the gaps blocking your revenue and fundraising.

Notable speakers/guests:
Amber Bennoui, brings 15+ years in  AI and cybersecurity product leadership (DataRobot, Chainguard) and has founded 2 startups.

Beatriz Datangel Rodgers, brings 15+ years in marketing leadership (Root, Paypal, Resilience)

Together they'll deliver key learnings and a concrete playbook founders can use immediately.

This event is a part of #BOSTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
