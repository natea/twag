## Description

Join us for an intimate breakfast during Boston Tech Week, bringing together a curated group of founders, investors, and operators building at the intersection of Construction and AI.

Hosted with Enlaye and the Harvard Innovation Labs.

Construction is one of the world’s largest yet most fragmented industries. This conversation will explore how AI-native platforms are transforming scattered project data into actionable insights—unlocking better decisions, earlier risk detection, and more efficient workflows across the build lifecycle.

The morning will feature a conversation with:

Philippe Rival: Co-Founder & CEO of Enlaye
David Morczinek: Co-Founder & CEO of Airworks
Noah Sadhwani: Senior Analyst at Suffolk Tech
Skye Zhou: Co-Founder and Head of Operation of Jobotics 

Moderated by: Dr. Stamati Liapis, Co-Founder & CTO of Enlaye

Expect a relaxed, off-the-record gathering designed for thoughtful dialogue and meaningful connections within the construction and AI ecosystem.

What to expect

• Founder perspectives on building AI for construction
• Conversations around data, workflows, and operational intelligence
• A curated room of investors, operators, and innovators in the built environment
• Breakfast, coffee, and high-signal networking

Good coffee. Smart people. Meaningful conversations.



This event is a part of #BosTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
