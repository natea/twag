## Description

Brian Halligan (HubSpot) x Eric Paley (State of MA) x 7AI x Lovable x Blitzy — all on one stage at Klaviyo HQ to kick off Boston Tech Week.

Flybridge presents an afternoon of real talk about Boston's AI moment. The ecosystem that gave us HubSpot, Klaviyo, and the research behind the biggest AI breakthroughs has always had the talent and the institutions. Now the question is: what happens when the best builders decide to stay and build here?

THE TITANS
Brian Halligan (CEO, HubSpot) + Eric Paley (Sec. of Economic Dev, Commonwealth of MA) in conversation with Flybridge GP Jeff Bussgang — on where Boston tech has been, where it's going, and what it takes to turn this moment into a boom.

TITANS OF TOMORROW
Lior Div (CEO, 7AI) + Kim Walsh (GTM, Lovable) + Brian Elliott (CEO, Blitzy) with Flybridge GP Chip Hazard — three AI-native companies building from Boston. Lovable just chose to HQ here. 7AI is changing the game in cybersecurity. Blitzy is one of the fastest-growing dev tool cos in the country. They'll tell you why they're here and why you should be too.

Talks kick-off at 11AM followed by lunch and an early happy hour on the deck - the perfect place to be before the kickoff party with A16Z.

For founders, future founders, CTOs, and senior operators who might just be plotting their own thing.

Co-hosted by Goodwin with support from Carta, Citrin Cooperman, and Rho.

This event is a part of #BOSTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
