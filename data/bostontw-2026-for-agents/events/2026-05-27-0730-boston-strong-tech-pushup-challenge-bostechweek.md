## Description

We're taking the term Boston Strong to a new level for the first Boston Tech Week to answer one question: how many pushups can we do as a tech community before the day gets started?

If 100 people do 100 pushups, that's 10k. Can we hit 20k? 50k? 100k?!

We'll meet at the Fielder Dock on the Espanade on the Charles River in Boston from 730am-9am. See the link bellow for an exact location.

This is a casual event to bring out the tech community in Boston hosted by Subconscious (AI infrastructure, has nothing to do with pushups) and HYBRD (personalized training app, has a lot to do with pushups!). Come in your casual clothes, meet great founders, engineers, and investors, and start warming up those arms.

We'll have a white board to track how many pushups we can do as a Boston Tech community. Let's aim high this year, and aim to beat it next year too!
