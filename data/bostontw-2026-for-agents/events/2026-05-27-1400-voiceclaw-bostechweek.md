## Description

Limited to the first 50 registrants!

Join VoiceRun, Link Ventures, and ClawComp for an action-packed build day during #BOSTechWeek. In one afternoon, you’ll go from zero to a live, standalone voice agent using OpenClaw, VoiceRun, and a fully code-first workflow.

This event has two tracks:

Corporate Track
For engineers and technical teams at companies exploring voice agents. Work alongside VoiceRun experts to design, build, deploy, and test a real voice agent for your business use case.

Hacker Track
For Boston-based hackers who want to push the limits of AI-assisted development. Build a voice agent from scratch, but with one rule: no self-configuration. Every setup, configuration, code change, and deployment step must be done through your AI coding agent, whether that’s OpenClaw, Claude Code, Codex, or Cursor.

Come ready to build, ship, and see what AI agents can do when they’re asked to build other agents.

This event is a part of #BOSTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
