## Description

AI is transforming GTM faster than any shift in the past decade. 

Outreach is automated. Content is generated instantly. Entire workflows are being rebuilt with AI at the core.

But one thing hasn’t changed: humans still make buying decisions.

The companies pulling ahead aren’t replacing sales teams. They’re redefining them. They’re building AI-enabled revenue engines while doubling down on the human skills that actually close deals: judgment, trust, positioning, and execution.

In this conversation, moderated by JR Butler, CEO of Shift Group, we're bringing together both old school and new school sales leaders:

- Chris Ward, CRO, Jellyfish
- Dino DiMarino, CRO, Tenable 
- John Hanlon, Experienced Sales Leader (Presidio, EMC)
- Brooke Duffy, Fractional CRO
- Brett Johnson, COO, Gauntlet AI 

Together, they've led sales organizations across every era of GTM – from the pre-SaaS playbook to the AI-native stack.

Join us to learn what’s changing, what still works, and how the best revenue teams are combining proven sales instincts with AI to scale faster.
