## Description

Many successful careers often don’t go straight.

Join us for a fireside with the founder of a scaled tech/media company, the founder of a fast-growing early stage startup, and a tech creator - each with a non-linear path across roles and industries, with bets taken that didn’t obviously connect at the time.

We’ll drill down into the decisions behind their paths: when to explore vs. double down, how to navigate moments with no clear next step, and how seemingly unrelated experiences compound over time.

Moderated by a physician-operator with a similarly diverse background across startups, consulting, and the tech ecosystem. 

This event is a part of #BOSTechWeek - a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
