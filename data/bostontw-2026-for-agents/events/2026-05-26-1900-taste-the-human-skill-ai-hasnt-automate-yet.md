## Description

"Taste" is everywhere right now — founder threads, investor memos, job descriptions asking for it like it's a software skill. But nobody agrees on what it actually means, or whether you can learn it. Is it design intuition? Pattern recognition from years of building? A million AI-generated logos, landing pages, and product concepts hit the internet today, and most of them are mid. The difference between what ships and what sticks is someone in the room who can tell the difference.

Wix Ventures and C House are hosting an intimate dinner at C House in Cambridge to get into it. Builders and creatives, breaking down design, curation, product instinct, and the human skills that can't be prompted. Dinner is on us (and yes, it's going to be a tasty one!).
