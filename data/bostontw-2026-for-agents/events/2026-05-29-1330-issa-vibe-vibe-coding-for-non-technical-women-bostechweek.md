## Description

Issa Vibe is an interactive, hands-on vibe coding workshop for non-technical women in Boston.

*Women focused but all are welcome!

Join Ore Segal from Base44 and Alexa Hall from Wildseed Capital (ex-Google Cloud and Gemini teams, investor in OpenAI and Anthropic) for an exclusive event at a16z's inaugural Boston Tech Week!

Agenda
1:30-2pm Arrive and find a workstation (BRING FULLY CHARGED LAPTOPS)
2-4pm Hands-On Workshop with Prizes from Base44
4-5pm Networking reception 

Participants will learn how Base44's platform and intuitive tooling makes software creation more accessible — turning simple prompts into functional apps and websites without traditional coding experience. Attendees will leave with tangible skills, builder credits from Base44, and the confidence to improve their prototypes.

Hosted in a gorgeous downtown Boston venue - the event is designed for students to late-career professionals.

These accomplished leaders bring strong domain expertise as a foundation to become tech founders — yet many have not yet seen how quickly vibe coding can lower the barrier to building and launching ideas.

The event is capped at 100 attendees to ensure relevance and personalized support. Our previous event at SF Tech Week filled within 24 hours!

This event is a part of #BosTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com
