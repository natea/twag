## Description

Calling all CFOs (Chief Foodie Officers)! Join Hercules Capital and Andreessen Horowitz for an intimate evening of oysters and candid conversation on the state of venture debt. As AI reshapes how companies scale—and how much capital that scaling demands—non-dilutive financing has become one of the most important levers in the modern CFO toolkit. We'll dig into where the debt markets are heading, what's working in today's structures, and how leading finance teams are funding growth without giving up the room on the cap table. Want to get a pulse on the private credit market? Join us!

This event is a part of #BOSTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
