## Description

As AI surges forward and new products enter the market every day, the real challenge is no longer innovation; it is impact. What actually makes an AI product stick inside the enterprise, scale, and deliver lasting value?

Join Glasswing Ventures, Goodwin, HSBC, and Techstars for Defensible AI, a conversation focused on how enterprise leaders are evaluating AI today and what truly separates durable solutions from passing momentum.

We will explore how decisions are being made around adoption, what signals matter most from early traction to enterprise scale, and how the next generation of innovators and investors are approaching defensibility with intention.

9:00 AM | Breakfast and registration

10:10 AM | Panel: Abha Dawesar (HSBC), Jonathan Jeffrey (WHOOP), Eireann Connolly (Black Kite), moderated by Steve Charkoudian (Goodwin)
11:00 AM | Networking

If you are an early-stage founder building to transform enterprise B2B markets, we hope you will join us.
