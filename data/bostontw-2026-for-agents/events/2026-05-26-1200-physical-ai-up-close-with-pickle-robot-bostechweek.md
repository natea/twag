## Description

Join Pickle Robot for an exclusive behind-the-scenes look at the company building Physical AI for supply chain automation. Founded by three MIT engineers, Pickle Robot is tackling the $100B global challenge of truck unloading with intelligent, collaborative robots that eliminate backbreaking manual labor.

This 90-minute session begins with an executive meet-and-greet at Pickle Robot HQ in Charlestown, where you’ll enjoy a light lunch, connect with the leadership team, and hear the founding story, company vision, and what’s next for Physical AI in supply chains.

You’ll then tour the office and innovation lab, stepping onto the warehouse floor for a live demo of Pickle’s robotic unloading system as AI-guided robots work at better-than-human speed. The experience wraps with an open Q&A and networking - perfect for making connections and getting your questions answered directly by the team building it.

This event is a part of #BOSTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
