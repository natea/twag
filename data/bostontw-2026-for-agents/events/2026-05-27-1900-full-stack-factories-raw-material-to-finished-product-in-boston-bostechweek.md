## Description

A new generation of founders is rebuilding American manufacturing — owning the whole stack from software to machines to factory floor. Join us inside Parallel Fluidics' facility for a fireside chat with founders building vertically integrated factories from raw material to finished product:

 • Josh Gomes (Founder/CEO, Parallel Fluidics)
 • Alex Zimmer (Founder/CEO, Topologic)
 • Dr. Ali Forsyth (Founder/CEO, Alloy Enterprises)
 • Dr. Ashley Beckwith (Founder/CEO, Foray Bioscience)

Expect candid conversation on the unit economics of software-enabled factories, hiring engineers fluent in both bits and atoms, and why Boston is quietly becoming the capital of modern manufacturing. Drinks, food, and facility tours before and after.

This event is a part of #BOSTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
