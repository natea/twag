## Description

Join Foley Hoag, MassRobotics, MA AI Hub, Aethos, The Outpost and Outpost Ventures for an unforgettable evening celebrating the vibrant communities shaping Boston's technology landscape.

As robotics and artificial intelligence continue to redefine what is possible, the bridges between disciplines, industries, and people have never been more important. This Block Party brings together the best and brightest from across the Boston ecosystem — founders, engineers, investors, policymakers, and visionaries — for a night of connection, conversation, and celebration in the heart of the Seaport.

Whether you are building the next breakthrough in AI, pushing the boundaries of robotics, or simply passionate about the future of innovation, this is your chance to be part of something bigger. Great communities are not built in isolation; they are built at the intersections. Come meet the people and organizations bridging ideas, capital, and talent to propel Boston's tech scene forward.

Good company. Great conversation. One incredible night. We look forward to seeing you there.

This event is a part of #BOSTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
