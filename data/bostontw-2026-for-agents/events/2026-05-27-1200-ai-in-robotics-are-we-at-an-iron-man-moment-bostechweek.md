## Description

AI in Robotics: Are We at an Iron Man Moment?

​Sponsored by PTC & SVB, join us for our next fireside chat event with early-stage founders, top investors, and engineers shaping the future of robotics.

​​Hear from Jon Hirschtick, Chief Evangelist at PTC and Founder of Onshape and SOLIDWORKS, and Marc Raibert, Founder and Executive Chairman of Robotics and AI Institute, and Founder of Boston Dynamics, as they explore how AI is transforming robotics and hardtech innovation. 

In this fireside chat, Jon and Marc will share perspectives from building and scaling world‑class hardware and software companies, discuss the role of AI in modern robotics product development, and reflect on entrepreneurship at the intersection of engineering and AI. Expect practical insights, real‑world examples, and a forward‑looking discussion on what’s next for AI‑driven robotics and hardware startups.

​If you're building your breakthrough product, you won't want to miss this! Make sure to RSVP as space is limited.

Hosted and sponsored by both PTC & SVB, this event is a part of #BOSTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
