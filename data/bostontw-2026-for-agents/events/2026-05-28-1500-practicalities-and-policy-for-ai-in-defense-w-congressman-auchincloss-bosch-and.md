## Description

This session features Congressman Jake Auchincloss alongside a panel of experts, Adam Jackson, Partner from Bosch Ventures, Procurement Sciences’ CEO, Christian Ferreira, and VIA’s CEO, Colin Gounden, to discuss how AI is reshaping the landscape of federal operations, particularly defense.

Attendees will hear the debate of AI industry speed versus government policy and procurement.

Following the panel, please join us for a networking happy hour to connect with other leaders in defense, policy, and venture capital. We invite you to enjoy New England seafood paired with fantastic bubbly as we celebrate the close of Boston Tech Week.

Why Attend?
