## Description

Two hours on the clock. Four challenges. One grand champion.

Wayfair, Subconscious, and BaseTen are running a hackathon-sprint during Boston Tech Week. We'll have great templates to get started, so you'll have 2 *pure* hours to hack hack hack.

Now, Cloudflare will also be supporting the event with killer tooling for cloud sandboxing, storage, and more agent infrastructure.

The schedule:
- 5:00pm: Doors open at Wayfair. Meet other great Boston enigneers.
- 5:30pm: Quick intros from Subconscious, Wayfair, and BaseTen.
- 5:45pm: HACK. Clock starts.
- 7:45pm: Submit.
- 8:00pm: Top Teams Present.
- 8:30pm: Track winners and grand champion crowned.

Teams will need to show a live demo or video. If it runs, it runs in front of everyone!

What you'll get
- Starter repos for every track so you're ready to jump right in.
- API access and free credits from Subconscious and BaseTen
- Support from our teams to unblock you quickly!

Pick one of four tracks to build an agent. Each track will have a champion, with one overall grand champion named at the end of the night.

1. Browser agents for shopping. Build agents that browse, compare, or purchase furniture on a live site.
2. Agents for supply chain ops. Resolve exceptions on a mock logistics dashboard.
3. Agents for finance operations & customer service. Resolve customer complaints, fight fraud, read invoices, and more.

How you win:
- Speed to submission. The earlier before 7:45 you submit, the more points you bank.
- Completeness. Does it actually work?
- Usefulness. Does it solve a real problem?
- Creativity. How far out of the box did you think?

Come if you are:
- A software engineer who wants to experiment with agent infrastructure
- An early-career developer who builds on nights and weekends
- A non-technical person who knows how to wield claude code

Bring a laptop. We'll have a killer group of hackers. The clock doesn't wait!
