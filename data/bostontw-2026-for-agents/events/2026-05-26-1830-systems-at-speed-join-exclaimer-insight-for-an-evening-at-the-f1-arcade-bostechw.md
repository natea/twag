## Description

Every CTO and CIO knows that the hardest part of transformation isn't finding the right technology — it's making the whole system move together. Visibility breaks down at the seams between tools. 

Governance erodes where ownership is unclear. And trust in your stack comes not from any single vendor, but from how everything holds under pressure.
Insight helps technology leaders navigate exactly that complexity — bringing visibility, governance, and integration discipline to environments that have outgrown their original design. 

As a leading Solutions Integrator with 35+ years of expertise and 30,000+ global clients, we design, build, and manage environments that scale without accumulating risk.

Exclaimer solves a specific, stubborn digital brand governance and compliance problem: email signatures. Every message leaving your organization carries brand, legal, and compliance weight — and most orgs have zero control over it. Together, we represent something CTOs and CIOs rarely get from their vendors: a complete solution to a real enterprise problem.

No pitch. No deck. Just a small group of senior technology leaders, a competitive evening on the F1 simulators, and honest conversation about building systems that actually hold.

Exclaimer
Exclaimer is the global leader in email signature management for Microsoft 365 and Google Workspace, trusted by 9 million users across 75,000 organizations worldwide — including Sony, Bank of America, the BBC, and the Academy Awards. Its cloud platform helps IT, Marketing, and Compliance teams centrally manage and automate email signatures and video meeting branding, ensuring consistent corporate identity, brand governance, and regulatory compliance across everyday business communications. Learn more at exclaimer.com or follow on LinkedIn, Facebook, and X.

Insight Enterprises
Insight Enterprises is a leading Solutions Integrator that helps clients solve technology challenges by combining the right hardware, software, and services. We're a global Fortune 500 technology company with a network of over 6,000 partners and experts around the world who provide access to end-to-end IT capabilities. For more than 35 years, we have delivered and optimized technology solutions for our clients efficiently, effectively, and safely. Discover more at insight.com.
