## Description

Where Boston Tech Week comes to life & we turn the week’s momentum into real connection.

During Boston Tech Week 2026, CIC will host CIC Discovery Day: a high-energy, open-format exhibition spotlighting the innovators, builders, and problem-solvers within the CIC community.

Designed as an interactive afternoon experience, Discovery Day will invite attendees to explore the products, services, and solutions being developed by CIC members while strengthening connections across the broader Cambridge–Boston tech ecosystem.

Rather than a traditional vendor showcase, CIC Discovery Day is positioned as a celebration of innovation in action aligned with the visibility, momentum, and city-wide engagement of Boston Tech Week.

This event reinforces CIC’s role as a hub where innovation is built, supported, and scaled.
