## Description

Thinking about expanding beyond the US? Join us at BOSTechWeek as the British Consulate gathers founders, scalers, and investors at Northeastern University for a panel discussion and networking.

Hosted by the British Consulate, in partnership with Northeastern University, Osborne Clarke, MCS Group, and Vistra, our goal is to showcase the UK opportunity and provide you with the knowledge and resources to make it your reality. If you’re considering the UK to start, scale, or invest, this event is for you.

The panel discussion will be followed by audience Q&A and networking over breakfast, offering both strategic insights and practical guidance. Topics will range from tax to talent sourcing, including:

Why the UK is a compelling market for international expansion

Strengths in high-growth sectors such as AI, life sciences, and deep tech

Access to talent, capital, and global markets

Key considerations for setting up and scaling in the UK

Your hosts:

•Northeastern University is a global R-1 research institution, widely recognized for its leadership in education innovation that fuels partnership-aligned talent pipelines and pathways in the U.S., UK, and abroad.

•Osborne Clarke is a leading international law firm, headquartered in the UK with offices across Europe, specialising in working with technology companies at every stage of growth. From establishing a UK entity to protecting IP, we offer practical, business-minded advice tailored to the needs of fast-growing tech companies.

•MCS is a specialist recruitment and talent strategy based in the US and UK. With extensive experience in global expansion, MCS has enabled clients to simplify strategy and access key talent quickly to build, grow, and scale teams globally.

•Vistra is a global leader in helping companies grow, expand, and operate around the world. Vistra's services streamline complex functions like entity management, tax, accounting, payroll and HR, and international expansion, enabling clients to scale with confidence and compliance.

Featuring EdenBase, a London-based frontier technology investment platform and ecosystem focused on AI, quantum computing, fintech, and other emerging technologies. Founded by the team behind Level39 and Tech Nation, it backs early-stage startups while also building innovation communities and industry partnerships to accelerate adoption of deep tech solutions.  QBase is EdenBase’s dedicated Quantum Applications Hub, designed to connect startups, corporates, investors, and researchers working in quantum and AI. Based in London and launched in partnership with Northeastern University, QBase helps companies commercialise quantum technologies through access to funding, expertise, and industry networks.

This event is a part of #BOSTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
