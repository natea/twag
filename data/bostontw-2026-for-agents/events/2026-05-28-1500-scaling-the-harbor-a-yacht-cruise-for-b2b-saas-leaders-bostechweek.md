## Description

All aboard the good ship ARR! Join us for an afternoon on Boston Harbor during the inaugural Boston Tech Week. 

After a day of panels and back-to-back sessions across the city, CIBC and York IE are inviting you to step away from the crowd and unwind on the water.

This one's simple: no speakers, no agenda, no moderated Q&A. Just founders, investors and operators building B2B SaaS companies sharing a boat, good conversation and some of the best views in the city. Whether you're navigating Series B waters or just trying to keep your burn rate from going overboard – this cruise is your port of call. 

Drinks, light bites and skyline views included. Seasickness and cap table advice at your own risk.

This event is a part of #BOSTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
