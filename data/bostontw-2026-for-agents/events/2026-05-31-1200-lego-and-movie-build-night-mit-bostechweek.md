## Description

LEGO AND MOVIE NIGHT @ MIT 🧱🍕🎬

WE ARE GIVING AWAY 100 FREE LEGO SETS! 

Want to destress after finals or celebrate with friends and family during commencement week? Join us for an all-day LEGO build/hackathon-style hangout at MIT during Boston Tech Week, designed especially for technical students who want to take a break from coding, research, and building startups to make something fun with their hands.

Hosted in partnership with a16z, Maritime.sh, Sundai Club, and the Massachusetts AI Institute.
Come by on Sunday, May 31 starting at 12 PM to build with us (not your startup for once!)

You don’t have to follow the instructions. Build the set, mix pieces, trade with people, and make something completely original. 

In the evening, we’ll put on a movie, order pizza, and keep building.

Open to students from MIT and nearby universities.

Spots are limited to 100 people because we only have so many LEGOs and so much pizza.

Come build legos, chill, meet people, and make something fun.

Yes, you can take your legos home.

This event is a part of #BOSTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
