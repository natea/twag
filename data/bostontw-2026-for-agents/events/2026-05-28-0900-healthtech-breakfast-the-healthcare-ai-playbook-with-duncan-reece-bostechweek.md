## Description

A must-attend breakfast for healthtech builders, this session features a candid Q&A with Duncan Reece, part of the founding team at Iora Health (acquired by Amazon for $2B) and now building Liza Health, an AI-native primary care company.

This conversation squarely confronts the role AI will play in transforming American healthcare and how startups need to build differently over the next decade to drive meaningful patient outcomes.

In a fast-paced discussion, we'll cover:
- What healthcare-focused AI can and cannot solve
- How Duncan is rethinking and rebuilding primary care at Liza Health
- The realities of building in healthcare and where founders get it wrong
- How financing needs to be approached for healthcare-specific businesses

Expect a direct, no-fluff conversation grounded in real experience and hard-earned lessons.
