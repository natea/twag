## Description

​Are you a woman founder building the next breakthrough in AI?
Are you an investor seeking the next wave of visionary AI startups?

​Join Oasis Collective, SVB, and Sisters at Microsoft NERD Center on Friday, May 29th for the inaugural Spring 2026 Boston Women in AI Pitch Competition—an inspiring evening of pitches, curated networking, and celebration spotlighting top women-led startups and artificial intelligence.

​For the first time of many, we are bringing our esteemed pitch competition from SF to Boston to convene trailblazing founders, top investors, technologists, and community champions to connect, showcase transformative ideas, and uplift the next generation of AI leadership.

- Applications are open to AI startups with at least 50% female founders

​EVENING HIGHLIGHTS:

​- Live pitch competition featuring early to growth-stage AI startups
- Thoughtful feedback and Q&A from an elite judging panel
- Networking and vibrant conversation with investors and technologists
- Elegant, thoughtfully curated bites and sips
- Closing reception and celebration with the finalists!

​We are proud to make this competition open to all fundraising stages, removing traditional barriers that often exclude women founders and underrepresented innovators.
