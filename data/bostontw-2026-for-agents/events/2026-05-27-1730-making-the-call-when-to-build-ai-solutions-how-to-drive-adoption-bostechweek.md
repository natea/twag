## Description

Making the Call: When to Build AI Solutions & How to Drive Adoption

We are still in the early innings of the AI era. Across consumer and B2B products, the expectations to leverage AI are massive, but is it always the right fit?

A core responsibility of a Product Manager is identifying the right solution based on user feedback and problem-fit, rather than simply chase the "shiny new" AI idea.

Our panel of experts will share real-world examples of:

* The decisions behind their AI roadmaps: why they moved forward (or didn't).
* How they successfully gained user adoption and generated measurable value.
* Lessons learned - what didn't work and what I would do differently next time.
* And more!

Speakers:

* Craig Daniel, SVP & GM at Toast
* Shailja Dedakia, Head of Product at Capsule
* Krishna Kannan, Head of Product at Jellyfish
* Jamie Conroy, Senior Principal Product Manager at CMT
* Harriet Brown, Chief Product Officer at Mobi
