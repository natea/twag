## Description

Join The Boston DAO (Boston's Web3 & Crypto Community) for networking, drinks, and snacks as we take over an MIT fraternity brownstone near Kenmore Square! 

This isn't your average college frat house - it's a 6-story brownstone with a roof deck that used to be the Governor's mansion. We're excited to open our May monthly meetup to the greater Boston Tech Week community.  So, whether you're a Boston DAO member or just want to see what's up with Boston crypto scene and meet some of the best builders in the industry, join us!

Don't worry, for anyone missing those old college vibes, we'll have red cups on hand.

This event is a part of #BosTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
