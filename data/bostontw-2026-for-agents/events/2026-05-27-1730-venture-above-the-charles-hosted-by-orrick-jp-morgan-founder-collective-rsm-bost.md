## Description

We’re back for year three. Join us for Venture Above the Charles, our summer soirée during a16z Boston Tech Week, hosted by Orrick, J.P. Morgan, Founder Collective and RSM.

We’re gathering a sharp mix of entrepreneurs and investors, perched right above the Charles, for new connections and a chance to step out of the day-to-day grind with people who are building what’s next.
