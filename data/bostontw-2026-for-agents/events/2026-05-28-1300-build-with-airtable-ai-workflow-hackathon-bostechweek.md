## Description

AI is transforming how companies operate, but most teams are still figuring out how to embed AI directly into their workflows.

Join other Builders and founders using Airtable for an interactive Airtable AI Hackathon, where participants will design and prototype AI-powered workflows in Airtable.

Teams will build solutions that automate decisions, streamline operations, and power new workflows across product, marketing, operations, and startups.

At the end of the session, teams will demo what they built and share their ideas with the group.

Whether you're an operator, builder, founder, or Airtable enthusiast, this event is designed to spark new ideas, practical applications of AI, and connections across the builder community.

To get the most out of this event, make sure you have an Airtable account, and have experimented with Airtable AI. Need to get up to speed? Check out Airtable Academy: https://academy.airtable.com/

This event is a part of #BOSTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
