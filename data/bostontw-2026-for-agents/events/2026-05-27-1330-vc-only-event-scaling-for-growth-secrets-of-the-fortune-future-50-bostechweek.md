## Description

Startups are becoming unicorns faster than every, reaching a $1 billion valuations driven by AI, lean teams, and high-agency founders.  The key isn’t just how fast you can scale, but how successfully you can create durable, profitable growth for stakeholders over time. 

If you are a venture capitalist, join Workday and SemperVirens VC at Tech Week Boston to hear from the Boston Consulting Group, B Capital, SemperVirens, Stage 2 Capital, and Workday Ventures as they unpack the scaling strategies of the Fortune Future 50 and top-performing startups, followed by an exclusive fireside chat with Nayya CEO Sarah Liebel on she has scaled the company backed by Workday Ventures and SemperVirens VC. See the technology in action through a live Workday GO demo and leave with practical ideas and strategic next steps you can bring back to your own business.

This event is designed for VCs only, and is part of #BOSTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
