## Description

Join us for an intimate gathering of women building at the frontier of Robotics and Physical AI.

​During Boston Robotics Tech Week, we’re hosting Breathing / Room: Human Override at Remedy Place. Step out of systems, and back into yourself.

​This is not just another networking event. Breathing / Room is a curated experience for female founders, operators, and investors working in robotics and emerging technologies. We aim to create a rare opportunity to take a step back from constant execution, iteration, and output.

​Throughout the evening, guests will have the freedom to explore Remedy Place’s signature social wellness offerings at their own pace, from contrast therapy and ice baths to infrared saunas and recovery treatments.

​Come and go between conversations and experiences. Reset in your own way.

​Expect an intentional group and the kind of conversations that don’t happen on panels or in passing.

​In a week centered around machines, this is a space for the humans behind them.

​Co-hosted by Matter Venture Partners and Cybernetix Ventures.

​Spots are limited.

This event is a part of #BosTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com
