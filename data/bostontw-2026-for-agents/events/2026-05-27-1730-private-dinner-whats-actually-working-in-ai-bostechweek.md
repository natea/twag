## Description

​Want to know how other leading orgs are moving from prototypes to scalable production deployments for agentic systems?

Arcade.dev and Tavily.com are bringing together senior AI leaders in Boston from the largest enterprises to discuss the challenges we’re all facing in moving AI agents from proof of concept into production. This event will take place in Boston.

Featured Speakers
​- Sam Partee - Founder & CTO, Arcade.dev
- Maya Sivan - Lead of AI Engineering - Tavily

​​This invite-only event is designed for AI leaders and executives who are past experimentation and focused on shipping durable, production-ready AI systems at scale.

No slide decks, no sales pitches. This evening will be designed to share our biggest challenges and insights with our closest peers to come up with actionable strategies for improving the impact of AI within our orgs off the record.

Please reach out to marketing@arcade.dev for more information.

This event is a part of #BosTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
