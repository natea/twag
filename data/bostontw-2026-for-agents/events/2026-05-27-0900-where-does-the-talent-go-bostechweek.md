## Description

Where Does the Talent Go?
Making Sense of Tech Careers in the AI Shakeup

Every week there's a new LinkedIn post from someone who says they cut their team from 50 to 2 using AI. The comments go wild. Half the replies are "this is the future" and the other half are "this is short sighted". Nobody's actually unpacking what's true, what's noise, and what it means for the people building careers in tech right now.

That's what this conversation is about.

We're bringing together operators, founders, and investors who are close enough to the shift to see what's actually happening. Not the PR version and certainly not the engagement-bait on social version. Who's getting hired (and why), who's getting cut, what teams actually look like on the other side, and how experienced tech professionals can prep themselves to win in this new world.

We're bringing together a small, sharp panel followed by real conversation.

Meet the panel: 
Mike Gutner, Chief People Officer at Toast, Inc.
Sharon Pollard, VP of People & Culture at CloudZero
Kelley Morse, Chief People Officer at Bullhorn
Peter Zotto, CEO & Founder at Trove 

Come with questions you'd ask a friend in the industry over coffee, not the ones you'd ask on a webinar.

Hosted by Trove: the home for your real career story in a post-AI world (thetrove.io).
