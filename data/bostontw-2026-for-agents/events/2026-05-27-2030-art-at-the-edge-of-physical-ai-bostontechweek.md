## Description

What if the hardest test for physical intelligence isn't a surgical suite — but performative art?

Art at the Edge of Physical AI is a fireside exploring what physical AI can truly do through the lenses of art: the dexterity, improvisation, and hardware it would take for a robot to co-create a room-scale botanical sculpture live on stage — and how those same frontier capabilities map directly to what's being built for industries. 

About the host: Peter is a physical AI investor at Climactic and an artist creating sculptures that are alive. His large-scale botanical works have been shown at the San Francisco Asian Art Museum, City Hall, and Fort Mason. He's developing a 2027 stage performance with robots; this fireside is where the thinking happens out loud.

This event is a part of #BostonTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
