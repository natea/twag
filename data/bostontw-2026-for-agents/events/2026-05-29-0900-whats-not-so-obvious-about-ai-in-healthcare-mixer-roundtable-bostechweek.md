## Description

This event explores how AI is transforming the healthcare sector beyond the headlines. The session focuses on real-world applications across care delivery and operations—where value is actually being created, where adoption breaks down, and what healthcare providers and stakeholders need to get right to move from experimentation to measurable clinical and operational impact. Grounded in practical experience rather than hype, the conversation brings together healthcare leaders, clinicians, operators, and innovators to examine how AI fits into existing systems, workflows, and real-world constraints shaping the future of healthcare. 

This event is a part of #BOSTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
