## Description

Join AI practitioners, researchers, academics, and artists to look at AI from a societal perspective and speak your own desires for AI into existence. 

Amidst multidisciplinary artworks inspired by AI, the creators will talk through the why behind each piece and where they each see AI heading, building bridges across disciplines and walks of life for a breadth of insight rarely found at a typical tech industry event. The reception is when that mix of makers and visitors is most dense, so come ready to bring your own questions and views into the room. Refreshments provided.

Reception capacity is limited. RSVP required.

This event is a part of #BOSTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.

*About Ship It!

A pop-up installation during Boston's inaugural Tech Week, Ship It! is a collaboration between AI practitioners, researchers, and creative technologists that invites participants to:

Witness the gap between the world we want and the world AI seems to be creating.
Ponder the accountability structure of bots, their designers, and their users.
Speak your desires for the Intelligence Age into existence.
