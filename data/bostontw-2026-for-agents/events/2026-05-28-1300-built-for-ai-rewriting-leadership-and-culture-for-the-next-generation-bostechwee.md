## Description

Harvard Business School Rock Center for Entrepreneurship presents....

Built for AI: Rewriting Leadership and Culture for the Next Generation

AI is no longer a feature. It is reshaping how companies are led, staffed, and scaled. This session brings together founders from both established companies and newer ventures to explore how leadership and culture must evolve as AI becomes core to decision-making and execution. Panelists will share candid lessons on what legacy assumptions break down, how teams and incentives are being redesigned, and what leaders must intentionally unlearn to build organizations that can thrive in an AI-driven future.

Moderator: Jeffrey Bussgang (Senior Lecturer, Harvard Business School; Co-Founder & GP, Flybridge Capital)

Panelists:
•	Mike Lloyd (MBA 2015)
Founder & CEO, AACI Group
AI-native property & casualty insurance platform

•	Yinka Ogunbiyi (MBA 2023)
Founder, Halo Braid
First automated hair-braiding tool, reducing hair braiding time from six hours to minutes, enabling stylists to scale their business

•	Madeleine Smith (MBA 2023)
Co-founder & CEO, Civic Roundtable
AI platform helping government teams collaborate on complex, cross-sector challenges
