Space is limited and registration is free; secure your spot today!

Join the athenahealth AI for Healthcare Hackathon.
 
athenahealth invites developers and emerging technical talent of all levels to a full-day, in-person hackathon focused on building innovative healthcare solutions using our platform, APIs, and AI technologies.

• Collaborate in teams to create real-world healthcare applications
• Hands-on sessions with athenahealth technology and expert support
