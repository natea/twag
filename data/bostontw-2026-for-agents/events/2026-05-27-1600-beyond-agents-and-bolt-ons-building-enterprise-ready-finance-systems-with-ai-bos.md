## Description

Enterprise finance teams are under pressure to automate but most AI approaches force a bad tradeoff. Lightweight copilots deliver incremental gains. Fully autonomous agents introduce risk and governance problems that finance leaders can't accept.

A third path is emerging: AI as the architect, designing structured, auditable workflows tailored to how your operations actually work, while execution stays deterministic and transparent.

Hear from an expert panel actively driving AI implementation and system scaling to match. 

Here's what you can expect to gain:
 
💡How to drive strategic and tactical alignment.

💡Top considerations for change management in complex, enterprise organizations. 

💡AI as an accelerator versus the fault line on how to account for for controls and governance
