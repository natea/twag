## Description

Co-founder Matchmaking x ScaleUp Labs & CIC is a high-energy networking event built to help founders meet the right people to build with. Whether you're searching for a technical co-founder, a commercial leader, or an early founding team member, this session creates a focused environment for meaningful connections and high-quality conversations. Meant to share what you’re building, meet ambitious operators and builders looking to join early-stage startups, and connect with others who are serious about turning ideas into real companies. If you’re building—or ready to join a startup at the ground floor—this is where the next partnership can start.

This event is a part of #BOSTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
