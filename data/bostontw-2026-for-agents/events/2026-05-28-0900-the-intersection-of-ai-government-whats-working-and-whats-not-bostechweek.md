## Description

AI has permeated government systems, from Medicaid operations, national defense, and local infrastructure and public safety, but the path from pilot to production is still uneven, opaque, and often misunderstood.

This panel brings together founders, operators, and investors working directly at the intersection of AI and the public sector to unpack what’s happening on the ground. 

The conversation will focus on how AI is being adopted across different levels of government; how procurement and decision-making processes are evolving; and what it really takes for companies to sell, deploy, and scale within these systems.

We’ll cover where AI is gaining traction today, where deals stall, and what founders consistently get wrong when approaching government customers. The goal is a clear-eyed view into a market that is large, complex, and increasingly central to how AI reaches real-world impact.

This event is a part of #BOSTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
