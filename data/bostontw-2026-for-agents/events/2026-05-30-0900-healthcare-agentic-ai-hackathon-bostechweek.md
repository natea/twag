Join the Harvard Innovation Labs and its healthcare and life sciences ventures in the Launch Lab X accelerator for a one-day hackathon focused on building the next generation of AI-powered solutions in health care and life sciences.

The Healthcare Agentic AI Hackathon brings together physicians, health care experts, scientists, engineers, and builders to tackle real-world challenges using agentic AI. Whether you’re coming from the clinical side or the technical side, this is a chance to collaborate, experiment, and create, building upon OpenClaw and NemoClaw.

Whether you’re here to build, learn, or find collaborators, you’ll leave with new ideas, new connections, and a clearer sense of how agentic AI can shape the future of health care.

Prizes will be provided by NVIDIA.

This event is a part of #BOSTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem.
