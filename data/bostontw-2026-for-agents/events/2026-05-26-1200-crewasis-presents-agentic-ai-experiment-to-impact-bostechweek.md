## Description

CREWASIS <https://crewasis.ai/>  presents a thoughtful panel from Enterprise leaders in Agentic AI and Marketing.  CREWASIS is a Techstars company.

Agentic AI in Enterprise: From Experiment to Impact

Most AI Agents work in demos. Very few survive real customers.

This panel breaks down what actually happens when Agentic AI moves from pilot to production inside enterprise companies and why so many teams stall at scale. 

What you'll learn:
How do you move from AI to Agentic.

What does an AI Agent really do in Enterprise companies. 

Why most enterprise Agentic AI initiatives fail after the pilot phase
How leading companies are actually selling Agentic AI internally and externally

What it takes to move from experimentation to repeatable impact

Where human judgment still outperforms AI agents and why it matters

Who should attend:

Founders building AI products for enterprise customers
Operators responsible for deploying AI inside large organizations
Investors evaluating real vs. hype in AI companies
