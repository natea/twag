## Description

Healthcare makes billion-dollar bets on what people say they'll do. Real-world behavior tells a different story.

Digital twins are rewriting how industries make decisions. In healthcare, that shift is just starting. The next wave isn't just simulating biology. It's simulating how patients decide, where clinicians disengage, and why campaigns fail before they launch.

Join us for a one-hour panel with practitioners building at this frontier, and hang around after for networking. 

Jeff Gombala | Co-Founder and CEO, Evidexa
20+ years building products and platforms across healthcare and regulated industries.

Maitri Patel | Co-Founder and Chief Behavioral Scientist, Evidexa
Board-certified psychiatrist and founder of Progressive Therapeutics. Industry expert in behavioral science and AI systems.

Ahmed Albaiti | Principal, ZS Associates
Former CEO of Medullan. Healthcare technologist with deep experience commercializing digital health at scale.

Jon McBee | Head of AI in Biotech, Anthropic Hackathon Winner
Builder of award-winning LLM solutions at the intersection of AI and life sciences.

Our panelists will share perspectives from the front lines, including where AI and simulation are creating real leverage in healthcare decisions, what it takes to build and validate behavioral models in regulated environments, and why the gap between what people say and what they do is the most expensive problem in health innovation today.

This event brings together founders, investors, health system leaders, and life sciences innovators for an honest conversation about what it means to build at the edge of AI and human behavior in healthcare.
