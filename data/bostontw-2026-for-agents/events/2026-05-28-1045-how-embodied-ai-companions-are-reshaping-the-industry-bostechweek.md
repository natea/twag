## Description

Screens are winning the war for children's attention and the solutions so far have been more screens.  

SamSam is an AI companion initiative involving people from Harvard Graduate School of Education and MIT that seeks to answer one of the most pressing parental challenges of our time: how do we raise curious, engaged children in a world designed to distract them? 

Event takes the form of an interactive discussion and demonstration where we dig into the research, opportunities, and the ethics of putting AI in a child's hands.
