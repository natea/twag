## Description

The next generation of iconic companies may not start with big teams. They may start with one person armed with the right tools.

AI is fundamentally changing what early-stage founders can build before hiring a team, raising a large round, or scaling headcount. Solo founders and lean teams are moving faster than ever, using modern tooling and AI leverage to launch products, acquire customers, and build real businesses with fewer people.

Solo is a gathering for the builders doing exactly that.

Whether you're a solo founder, part of a small startup team, an operator thinking about taking the leap, or a student experimenting with ideas, this event is designed for people building in the trenches.

The evening will feature:
- A presentation on emerging trends around solo founders and AI-enabled company building
- A candid panel discussion with founders building high-growth companies with lean teams, featuring: John Rogers, CEO of Haddy, Barbara Jones-Brown, CEO of Freeing Returns, & Monty Kothiwale, CEO of QASolve AI
- Networking with founders, operators, and early-stage investors across the Boston tech ecosystem

More than anything, this is a room for ambitious people building at the edge of what's now possible with small teams.

Come to learn how the next wave of startups is being built. Stay for the people you'll want in your corner afterward.

Thank you to our partner Supabase for supporting the next generation of solo founders and builders.

This event is part of #BOSTechWeek — a week of events hosted by VCs and startups bringing together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com
