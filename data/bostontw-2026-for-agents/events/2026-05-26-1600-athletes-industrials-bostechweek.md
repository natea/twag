## Description

What do elite athletes and industrial operators have in common? They both perform under pressure, optimize in real time, and leave nothing on the field.

Join Nexxa.ai and Construct Capital at Boston Tech Week for an event that brings together the people building the future of industrial technology. 

What to expect:

- A punchy panel featuring operators and investors at the frontier of industrial AI, covering where the real deployment challenges are, what's separating hype from working systems, and why heavy industry is the next major AI battleground
- Candid conversation on what it takes to build and scale AI in environments where failure isn't an option 
- A curated crowd of founders, engineers, and capital allocators who are serious about what's next for industrial AI

Moderator:
Michael Hacker, Partner, Gunderson Dettmer

Speakers:
Philipp Wehn, CEO, Nexxa.ai
Camila Saruhashi, Early Stage Ventures, Construct Capital
Ramses Alcaide, CEO, Neurable

This event is a part of #BOSTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
