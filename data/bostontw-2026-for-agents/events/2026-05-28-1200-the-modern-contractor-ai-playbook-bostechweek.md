## Description

Most contractors are not looking for more software. They are looking for simpler ways to save time, stay organized, follow up faster, and run a more profitable business.

The Modern Contractor Playbook is a practical lunch and learn built for contractors, builders, remodelers, and small construction teams who want to understand how AI can help in the real world. Instead of broad AI theory, this session focuses on a few practical use cases that matter most to contractor businesses, including quotes and proposals, customer communication, follow-up, and lead organization.

Attendees will see how AI can help reduce office chaos, improve follow-through, and make day-to-day work easier without needing to become tech experts. The goal is to leave with practical examples, simple tools, and next steps that can be applied right away.

What Attendees Will Leave With:
- Practical examples they can use right away
- A simpler understanding of how AI fits into a contractor business
- Useful prompts and tools they can use on their phone or laptop
- Clear next steps for improving follow-up, communication, and workflow
- A QR code to resources they can use after the session
