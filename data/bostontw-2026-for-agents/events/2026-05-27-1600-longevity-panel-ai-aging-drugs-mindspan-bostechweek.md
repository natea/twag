## Description

Join us for an evening of learning & connection. Researchers, clinicians, practitioners, & enthusiasts across a variety of disciplines – ranging from biomedical engineering to geriatrics – will convene to discuss emerging trends in the field of longevity. 

The event will consist of panels with researchers from leading longevity labs in Boston (e.g., Gladyshev, Sinclair, etc.), AI drug discovery experts from both academia & industry, as well as authors of scientifically-backed books & protocols for optimal brain health (e.g., Preston Estep, The Mindspan Diet), along with countless opportunities for serendipitous connections !
