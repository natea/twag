## Description

Join us for an evening of live AI demos and networking during Boston Tech Week.

Hosted by MassBio, together with a16z and Lilly, this event brings together the people and platforms shaping the future of drug discovery.

Lilly TuneLab, a first-of-its-kind, collaborative AI/ML drug discovery platform, alongside a select group of a16z portfolio companies will take the stage to showcase how cutting-edge AI tools, including advanced bio models and emerging agentic systems, are accelerating science from early research to drug development.

Attendees will connect with founders, innovators, AI researchers and industry leaders at the frontier of biotech and AI. This is a curated, invitation-only gathering for  leaders across biotech, tech, and life sciences.

This event is a part of #BOSTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
