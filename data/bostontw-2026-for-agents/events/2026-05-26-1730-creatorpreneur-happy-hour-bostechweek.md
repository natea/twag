## Description

Building a business while building your brand? Join us for an honest conversation about the reality of being a creatorpreneur in 2026.

This event is all about creating space for content creators, coaches, consultants, and solo entrepreneurs across tech and creative media to connect authentically about what it actually takes to turn your creative energy and focus into a sustainable business.

Whether you're monetizing your side hustle, scaling your business, or figuring out how to balance content creation with client work, this happy hour is for you.

What to expect:
- Real talk about entrepreneurship & being authentic as a creator
- Community connections with fellow Boston-area creatorpreneurs
- Practical insights on building sustainable work without burning out
- A judgment-free zone to discuss revenue, boundaries, and what's actually working (or not)

This event is perfect for: Content creators, coaches, consultants, freelancers, and anyone building a business around their personal brand in the Boston area.

This event is a part of #BOSTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
