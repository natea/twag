## Description

Women Applying AI is a non-profit organization committed to helping women learn, connect, and leading with AI. We've grown to over 1,000 members around the world in just 6 months. 

Join this curated gathering to celebrate the momentum and see how women are shaping the many ways AI gets applied across industries and roles. 

The Program: 
The evening features Charlene Li and Katia Walsh who will present a short keynote based on their new book, "Winning with AI: The 90-Day Blueprint for Success".  All event attendees will receive a complimentary copy of the book!

The event includes:

7:00pm: Charlene and Katia's remarks and moderated Q&A
7:30pm: Book signing
