## Description

Generational Perspectives on Early Stage Investing, co-Hosted by Fenwick and the Mass AI Coalition

How do different generations of investors approach early-stage investing and what can founders learn from those differences? As venture continues to evolve, perspectives shaped by experience, market cycles, and emerging trends are influencing how capital is deployed at the earliest stages.

Join a panel of investors and industry leaders for a dynamic discussion on how generational viewpoints impact investment strategy, risk tolerance, and founder relationships. From seasoned investors who have navigated multiple cycles to newer voices shaping the next era of venture, this conversation will unpack where perspectives align and where they diverge.

David Horne, Partner at Fenwick, will moderate a panel including: 
- Giuseppe Stuto, General Partner at Offscript
- Jules Deplanck, Partner at Genesis Fund 
- Devon Triplett, Co-Founder & General Partner at 021T 
- Ellen Chisa,  Partner at Boldstart Ventures
- Dave Frankel, Partner at Founder Collective
