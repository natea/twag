"Trials in Motion"  is an event dedicated to everyone who has an interest in Pharma and Clinical Operations. Join us and you will be able to interact and network with Leaders and Experts in Pharma and Tech!

10:00-10:20 am | Welcome Networking Reception led by Raviv Pryluk, PhaseV's CEO
