## Description

The best conversations happen at 7:30am with your running shoes on. 

Start your morning with a 5k group run along the Charles River, alongside people building interesting things with AI. Hosted by Formation Bio, all paces welcome. 

NOTE: Photo ID is required to enter the building. 

We'll finish at our Back Bay office for breakfast, coffee, and a chance to connect before the rest of Tech Week has even poured their first cup. Meet the engineers and scientists building AI for drug development, and find out about the unique problems they get to solve everyday. Come if you're an engineer, scientist, or builder, grad student, work in AI or healthcare, or are just curious about it. Formation's CBO will be there to welcome and host. 

Not a runner? Join us at 8:15 for coffee and breakfast. You’ll leave energized, connected, and with Formation Bio swag.

We’ll run rain or shine, but in the event of severe weather, we'll go straight to breakfast at the office. You'll get a heads-up the morning of.

See you out there! 

This event is a part of #BOSTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
