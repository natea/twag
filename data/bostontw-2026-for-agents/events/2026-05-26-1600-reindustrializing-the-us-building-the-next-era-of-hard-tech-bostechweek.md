## Description

For decades, the biggest tech breakthroughs lived mainly on our screens. As global supply chains shift, data center buildouts consume massive amounts of capital and energy, and AI accelerates physical engineering, the next massive wave of innovation isn't just in software - it’s in hard tech.

Join us during Boston Tech Week for a high-impact panel discussion and networking session focused on the resurgence of American hard tech and advanced manufacturing. We are bringing together the minds, the capital, and the scale required to grow our high-tech industrial base. Panelists include Miles Arnone, CEO of Re: Build Manufacturing, Nick Myers, CEO of Phoenix Tailings, and Lie Shi, CEO of AM Batteries.

Founders & Builders: Connect with the capital funding the next generation of American manufacturing and learn how to scale physical operations in a digitized world.

VCs & Investors: Look beyond digital innovations to uncover high-growth, defensible opportunities in vanguard industries like manufacturing, critical minerals, energy, robotics, aerospace, defense and industrial automation.

Corporate Leaders: Find agile startup partners to drive supply chain resilience, pilot advanced manufacturing tech, and accelerate your industrial growth.

This event is a part of #BOSTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.

The organizers wish to thank our event sponsor Wolf Greenfield, an IP law firm committed to supporting innovators at every stage of their journey.
