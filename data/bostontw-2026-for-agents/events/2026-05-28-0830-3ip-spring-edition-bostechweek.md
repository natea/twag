## Description

Join us for the next 3IP gathering on Thursday, May 28th at the outdoor plaza in front of Tatte, 125 Summer Street. This is a relaxed, high-signal coffee meetup with people across many parts of the Boston startup ecosystem.

What's 3IP? "Three interesting people." You're encouraged to share this event with three people you'd love to see there — and usually at least one of them comes. It's a great way for us to bring fresh faces to the event, and for you to have someone there that you're excited to catch up with.

No presentations. No sponsors. Just great coffee, thoughtful conversation, and the chance to connect with people who are quite interesting.

Come for 20 minutes or stay longer. Start your day with energy, ideas, and new connections.

In the event of rain, we move over to High Steet Place, the nearby food hall, which has more indoor space than Tatte.

This event is a part of #BOSTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
