## Description

A decade and a half after the original "Software is eating the world” thesis, the question feels newly open.

As part of the inaugural Boston Tech Week, Innospark Ventures and Two Lanterns Venture Capital invite you to join for a rooftop happy hour to revisit this statement for 2026. Now, AI is reshaping how software is built, sold, and, in some cases, whether it remains the product at all.

Join founders, operators, and investors to connect and trade perspectives on where things are heading.

No panels—just good people, drinks, and a shared question: what comes next?

This event is a part of #BosTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com
