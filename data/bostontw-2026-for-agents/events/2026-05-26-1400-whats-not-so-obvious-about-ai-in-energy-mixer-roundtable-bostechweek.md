## Description

This event explores how AI is transforming the energy sector beyond the headlines. The session focuses on real-world applications across operations—where value is actually being created, where adoption breaks down, and what energy operators and stakeholders need to get right to move from experimentation to measurable impact. Grounded in practical experience rather than hype, the conversation brings together industry leaders, operators, and innovators to examine how AI fits into existing systems, workflows, and business realities shaping the future of energy.
