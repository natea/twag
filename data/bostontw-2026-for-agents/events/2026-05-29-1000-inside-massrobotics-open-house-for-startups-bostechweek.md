## Description

Join us at MassRobotics during Boston Tech Week for an exclusive information session on Friday, May 29th from 10:00 - noon.  

Whether you're building your first prototype or scaling a robotics venture, this is your chance to step inside one of the world's leading robotics innovation hubs. Meet with the MassRobotics team, meet current residents, and learn how a MassRobotics residency can accelerate your startup with access to shared labs, testing facilities, industry connections, and a vibrant community of robotics founders and engineers. Come see what it's like to build alongside 100+ robotics and automation companies — and find out if MassRobotics is the right home for your next stage of growth.

Please note that this event is for robotics startups only. 

This event is a part of #BOSTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
