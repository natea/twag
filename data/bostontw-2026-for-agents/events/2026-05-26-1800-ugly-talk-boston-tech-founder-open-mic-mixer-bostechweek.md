## Description

You've never been to a mixer like this one.

Forget the highlight reel. Forget the rehearsed pitch and the superhero LinkedIn post. For one night, we're trading all of that for the good, the bad, and the ugly.

Boston founders get on stage and share the pivoting moment — the night the deal fell through, the morning they realized the product was wrong, the quiet decision to start over.

7 minutes each. Vulnerable. A little embarrassing. Funnier than you'd expect. Every story is a lesson somebody paid full price for.

You sit back. You laugh. Maybe wince. Then catch yourself nodding. By the time the mixer kicks in, the networking dance is dead. You're just catching up with people you actually want to know.

🎯 Why You Should Attend
Hear the version founders don't save for the panel circuit. Get the lesson without paying full tuition. Feel the room loosen up — once one person tells the truth, everybody stops auditioning.

🙋 Who's Getting In This Room
Founders actively building. Not circling the idea. People at a pivoting moment, ready to make a real move. Boston founders plus visiting builders in town for Tech Week.

📋 Run of Show
30 min · Check-in — Arrive, grab a drink

15 min · Transition — Let the room reset
