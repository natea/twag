## Description

Legendary Ventures, VC Village, Glasswing, Underscore, and Alpha Partners are hosting an exclusive shindig for investors, founders, and entrepreneurs to share, learn, and engage each other in the VC ecosystem.

Legendary Ventures is and early-stage fund that focuses on consumer, retail, and technology companies led by consumer-centric management teams with clear visions of their businesses, technically efficient products, and super passionate customers that cuts across three primary sectors: Consumer Internet, Enterprise (BaaS, PaaS, SaaS), Deep Tech, and PropTech.

VC Village is an exclusive investor focused community that brings together early stage investors around the world.

Glasswing Ventures is a first-capital-in venture firm investing in early-stage AI-native and frontier technology companies across enterprise B2B and cybersecurity markets. We back founders who are building the infrastructure, applications, and security layer for the AI era.

Alpha Partners is a $400M growth equity fund that co-invests alongside the world's top early-stage VCs, sharing economics to back their highest-conviction companies at scale.

Underscore VC is a Boston-based venture capital firm backing bold founders at Pre-Seed and Seed. Underscore pioneered an “open source” investing model, sharing investment returns with a vibrant, curated, expert ecosystem that sources and supports the startups Underscore backs.

​Sponsered by Gunderson Dettmer, the preeminent international law firm with an exclusive focus on the innovation economy. The firm serves market-leading venture capital and growth equity investors and pioneering companies through inception, growth and maturity, as well as groundbreaking public companies that result from the global venture capital ecosystem. The firm’s focus and technical skill enables an accelerated pace and unmatched efficiency, delivering best-in-class value at each phase of a client’s business.​

Sponsored by Leader Bank. ​Leader Bank's Startup Banking team helps founders and entrepreneurs achieve their growth goals. We offer innovative banking products, investor + LP networking opportunities, and partner with Fidelity to offer free 409A valuations

This event is a part of #BOSTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com
