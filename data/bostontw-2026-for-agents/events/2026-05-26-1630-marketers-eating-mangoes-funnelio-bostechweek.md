## Description

A small, curated gathering for people who actually run growth during Boston Tech Week.

This is not a networking event. It’s 10–15 operators - paid acquisition, lifecycle, analytics - trading notes on what’s working and what isn’t, without the usual surface-level conversations.

No panels. No pitches. No presentations.

Instead, the anchor is something simple but rare: Alphonso mangoes, widely considered the best mangoes in the world and only available for a short window each year (April–July).

We’ll be hosting this at the beautiful offices of Funnel.io - an ideal setting for a small, high-signal room.

The idea is straightforward: put the right people in a room, remove the noise, and let the conversation do the work.

This event is a part of #BosTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com
