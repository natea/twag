## Description

While AI and layoffs get the headlines, 3x productivity increases and shifting hiring strategies are the real story. The best companies aren't just adopting AI. They're re-engineering their talent equation to embrace the speed, flexibility, and specialization AI requires.
                                                                                                                    
Join Human Cloud, the human layer to the agentic workforce, Topcoder, the leading AI development platform, and the US Department of Defense's agentic collaboration platform, for an afternoon of expert-led sessions and hands-on building. You'll hear from Tony Buffum (former CHRO, FLIR), General Mike McGinley (Director of DoW's America's Talent Arsenal Initiative), and Matthew Mottola (CEO, Human Cloud) before rolling up your sleeves to build and deploy your own workforce agents.

This couldn't happen in a better environment, Babson College, the #1 school for entrepreneurship 29 consecutive years running. 

What you'll walk away with:
  - A tangible understanding of how AI has reshaped how companies should hire
  - At least one agent you built and deployed during the session                                                    
  - Feedback from experts during the closing pitch and awards   
                                                                                                                    
Best for: Executives with significant talent budgets, talent leaders driving innovation and alternative work models, and founders building workforce tech. 

This event is a part of #BosTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com
