## Description

Decision Drag is the hidden leadership bottleneck slowing many high-growth companies. It happens when decisions appear to be made but never fully land—creating loops of re-discussion, misalignment, and invisible operational friction that drains momentum across teams.

In this session, executive decision strategist Kim Groshek introduces the concept of Decision Drag and the leadership operating model behind it: UN-DECISION → Decision Containment → Calm Momentum. Leaders will learn how unresolved authority, unclear ownership, and open decision loops quietly stall execution—even in fast-moving organizations.

Through real leadership scenarios and practical frameworks, founders, operators, and senior leaders will walk away with a simple way to identify decision drag inside their own companies and a method for containing decisions so momentum can return to the work that actually moves the business forward. 🚀
