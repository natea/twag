## Description

Building hardware is hard—long cycles, real-world constraints, and no room for shortcuts. So how do great teams actually make it work?

Join founders behind some of Boston’s most exciting physical product companies—Formlabs alumni now building the next generation of hardware startups—as they share what it really takes to go from idea to shipped product.

Featuring:
Christian Reed (REEKON)
Luke Winston (Reservoir)
Matthias Hofmann (Eyebot)
Max Lobovsky (Formlabs)

Moderated by: Eric Paley, Massachusetts Secretary of Economic Development

After the panel, stick around for drinks, networking, and a chance to try Formlabs’ latest 3D printers yourself.

This event is a part of #BOSTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
