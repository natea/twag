## Description

Hosted by J2, Link Ventures, Fenwick, and Rho, a mixer is a simple way to get good people in one room. Bringing together founders, investors, and friends in the Boston ecosystem for a night featuring drinks and light bites.

​​Meet the hosts:
J2 Ventures — J2 Ventures is an early-stage VC focused on dual-use technology critical to national security and private sector advancement, backed by world-class limited partners including JP Morgan and MetLife.

Link Ventures — Link Ventures is a Cambridge-based VC firm managing over $1 billion in capital, backing the next generation of AI entrepreneurs through deep ties to MIT's ecosystem and a team that has co-founded more than 50 companies.

Fenwick — Fenwick is a leading U.S. law firm serving technology and life sciences companies at every stage of growth, with deep expertise across venture capital, M&A, intellectual property, and capital markets.

Rho — Rho is the all-in-one banking platform built for high-growth startups, offering fast account setup, cashback cards, and powerful tools for managing spend, paying bills, and closing the books.

​​​​Rho is a fintech company, not a bank. Checking and card services are provided by Webster Bank, N.A., member FDIC; savings account services are provided by American Deposit Management Co. and its partner banks.

This event is a part of #BOSTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
