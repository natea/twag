## Description

As AI reshapes industries at breakneck speed, founders and investors alike are navigating one of the most volatile fundraising landscapes in decades. This session brings together leading venture investors, operators, and innovators to break down what it really takes to raise capital in today’s rapidly evolving environment and how AI is a catalyst to change in both fundraising and finance organizations. Our keynote, Aman Govil, Founder of Building AI for CMOs, will showcase real AI insights from the market, followed by our panel which includes David Borecky, Partner, Venture, a16z; Laura Stoffel, Partner, Cooley; Aaron Goldsmid, SVP of Product and Strategy, Deel; Shankar Vellal, Product Leader, Stripe; Ries McQuillan, Director, Startup Banking, SVB; Robert Ballard, Deal Manager, DFIN; and Sidd Bhattacharay, Partner Life Sciences AI Leader, PwC . Come for discussion, networking and cocktails.

This event is a part of #BOSTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
