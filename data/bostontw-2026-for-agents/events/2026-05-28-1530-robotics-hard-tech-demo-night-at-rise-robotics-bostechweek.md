## Description

Join RISE Robotics for an evening showcasing some of the most exciting robotics and hard-tech innovation being built in the Boston area.

Hosted at the RISE facility in Somerville during Boston Tech Week, this open-house-style event will bring together robotics, hardware, and deep-tech companies for live demos, networking, and a chance to see real machines and systems up close.

Attendees will have the opportunity to explore emerging technologies, meet founders and engineers, and connect with others working across robotics, electrification, AI-enabled machines, and industrial innovation.

The event will also include access to the RISE lab and demonstrations of Beltdraulic™ technology alongside other participating companies.

Time: Doors open at 3:30 PM, demos begin at 4:00 PM 
Location: RISE Robotics, 1 Union Square Somerville, MA

This event is a part of #BosTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
