## Description

AI has made it easier than ever to build.

Which means more people are building fast… and building the wrong things.

This session is for founders who don’t want to waste months building something no one actually needs.

In this interactive Problem Discovery Sprint, we’ll focus on what matters before any code is written: identifying real, high-impact problems worth solving.

You’ll work through a structured process to:

Break down real-world workflows and inefficiencies across industries like AI, healthcare, climate, and productivity
Identify where time, money, and effort are being wasted today
Separate “interesting ideas” from problems people will actually pay to solve

This is not a panel or a lecture. It’s a working session designed to help you think clearly and move forward.

By the end of the session, you’ll leave with:

A clearly defined problem statement
A specific target customer
A concrete next step to validate your idea

If you’ve been thinking about starting something but aren’t sure what’s actually worth building, this session will give you clarity.

Spots are limited to keep this session highly interactive.

This event is a part of #BOSTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
