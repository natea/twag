## Description

An in-person gathering for investors, founders, operators, and ocean‑curious builders to explore opportunities and challenges in maritime shipping technology. 

We’ll share an overview of the market, highlight success stories from local maritime startups, review resources available in Boston, and then break into a panel discussion and networking session to spark new collaborations and companies.

This event is organized by Hash (x1025.com | hash39.com), a former merchant marine officer driven by a love of ships and a passion for technology, and co-hosted by SeaAhead (sea-ahead.com), a bluetech venture builder that brings together acceleration programs, capital, and a powerful network to scale startups.

The Engine (engine.xyz), a non-profit accelerator and incubator built by MIT to support Tough Tech startups will be hosting the event.

This event is a part of #BOSTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.

If you're interested in sponsoring, please reach out at h6239@alum.mit.edu.
