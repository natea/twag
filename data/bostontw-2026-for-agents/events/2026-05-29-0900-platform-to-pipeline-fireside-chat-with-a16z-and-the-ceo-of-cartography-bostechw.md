## Description

From Platform to Pipeline: A Fireside Chat with a16z and the CEO of Cartography Biosciences

Building a platform is one thing, translating it into a clinical pipeline is another. Fenwick Partner, Heidi Erlacher, will moderate a conversation with Kevin Parker, CEO of Cartography Biosciences, and Jorge Conde, Partner at Andreessen Horowitz, for an inside look at how Cartography evolved from a technology platform into advancing its first programs into the clinic.

In this candid fireside chat, they’ll explore the strategic, scientific, and operational decisions required to make that leap. Topics from prioritizing targets and building a pipeline, to aligning investors and preparing for clinical development.
