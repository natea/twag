## Description

The leaders building Boston's hardest tech aren't meeting at conferences—they're meeting over dinner. A curated table of CTOs, VPEs, and CPTOs from companies like Whoop, CloudZero, and Markforged, wrestling with the question nobody's fully answered yet: where does product end and engineering begin when AI rewrites the rules? 

AI isn't just changing what your teams build—it's dismantling the walls between *who* builds and *who* decides what gets built. Product and engineering are bleeding into each other faster than anyone predicted, and the leaders who figure out the new shape first will pull ahead of everyone else.

We'll dig in with thoughtful discussion: Are integrated builder teams the future or a fantasy? How are you reworking your SDLC? What org design changes are you making right now—what are you theorizing about?

This is a seated dinner at Committee in the Seaport—an intentional room of leaders from ambitious Boston tech companies. No panels. No keynotes. Just sharp people and honest conversation.

Presented by VPE Forum, a community of senior engineering leaders across Boston startups, growth-stage, and scaled companies
