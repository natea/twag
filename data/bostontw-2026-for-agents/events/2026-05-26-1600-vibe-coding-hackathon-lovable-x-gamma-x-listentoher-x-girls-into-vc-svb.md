## Description

Join a high-energy evening for women building the future of tech and venture. Meet founders, aspiring VCs, and operators for a night of building, founder pitches, investor feedback, networking, and a free Gammatcha cart ☕ during Boston Tech Week.

Hosted by Girls Into VC, a 10K+ member 501(c)(3) nonprofit increasing female representation in venture capital, this event is designed for ambitious women looking to build, invest, and connect.

🏆 Hackathon Awards
The top 3 founder pitches will receive free Lovable Pro accounts + API credits to continue building after the event.

Agenda

• 4:00–5:30 PM: Sprint Hackathon
Build a quick MVP or concept, individually or in teams. Prepare your pitch with Gamma (the Gamma team will be present to advise). Apply to be one of 8 selected women founders/builders participating in the sprint: https://ts4hwh867cg.typeform.com/to/WfbcvIRp
• 5:40–6:30 PM: Founder Pitches + VC Judge Feedback
Selected teams pitch their ideas with Gamma and receive live feedback from investors. Following pitches, investors will break down how they evaluate companies, what makes a strong pitch, and answer audience questions on venture capital and founder fundraising.
• 6:30–7:00 PM: Networking + Winners Announced
Connect with founders, investors, and operators while hackathon winners are announced.

Apply by May 16 @11:59 pm ET🚨

About the Partners:

Girls Into VC (GIVC) is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit using the power of community to increase female representation in venture capital. With 10,000+ members across 75+ countries and 25+ college chapters, GIVC provides education, mentorship, events, and career opportunities for the next generation of women in venture.

ListenToHer is an AI-powered platform founded by Eraina Ferguson (in beta!) connecting women to speaking opportunities and helping more female voices take the stage.

Gamma is an AI-powered platform for creating presentations, websites, and documents — no design skills needed. Millions of people use Gamma, with over 1 million gammas created daily.

Lovable helps founders and builders turn ideas into products faster, enabling users to build apps and MVPs with minimal technical experience.

SVB, a division of First Citizens Bank, supports startups, founders, and investors through specialized banking and deep connections across the innovation ecosystem.

This event is part of #BosTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups bringing together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
