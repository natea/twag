## Description

Experience the most innovative square mile on the planet on this guided walking tour of Kendall Square. We will start our journey through four centuries of world-changing tech breakthroughs at the Kendall/MIT Red Line stop at 300 Main Street. 

With 105 Nobel laureates hailing from this ecosystem, Kendall Square is where you will find the global hub of biotech, plus MIT, Microsoft, Google, and an ever-increasing number of startup and emerging companies in tech of all kinds. This tour will give you a sense of the past, present, and future of technology–from the Polaroid instant camera to Zipcar, the Human Genome Project, COVID19 vaccines, AI, and quantum–it’s all here.  

The tour will land you at The Engine for a 3 pm panel discussion on Quantum Future followed by networking and a happy hour at The Engine, home of tough tech.

This event is a part of #BOSTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
