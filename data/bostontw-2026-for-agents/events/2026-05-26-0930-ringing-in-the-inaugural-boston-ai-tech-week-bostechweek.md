## Description

The Healey-Driscoll Administration is officially ringing the bell on the inaugural Boston Tech Week — and just like the bells that have echoed across this Commonwealth for centuries, this one calls everyone to action. 

Let’s Build!

Hosted by The Open Accelerator — the joint initiative from MA AI Hub, Red Hat, and IBM dedicated to helping AI startups start, grow, scale, and stay in Massachusetts — this Kickoff is proof that Boston's AI future isn't coming. It's already here.

Whether you're building, investing, or just obsessed with what's next — this is where history starts. Again.

This event is a part of #BOSTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
