## Description

Most AI is theater.

Join us for a candid, behind-the-scenes conversation with leaders across product, sales. IT, venture, and academia on what’s actually happening inside AI adoption—and what’s just hype.

From real-world implementation challenges—like data quality, permissions, and model reliability—to broader questions around “AI-powered” products and autonomy, this session explores what it actually takes to make these systems work in practice.

Featured speakers include:
• Daniel Jaenicke, Director of B2B Product Strategy at MacPaw
• Maura Brady, Chief Sales Officer at a leading data and AI company
• Dr. Salem Othman, Associate Professor of Computer Science in the School of Computing and Data Science at Wentworth Institute of Technology

This event is a part of #BosTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
