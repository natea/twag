## Description

Every tech scene you admire has someone running a weekly event. Your city probably doesn't. That person could be you.

Tech Wednesdays is Edmonton's longest-running weekly tech event. 157 weeks. Three years without missing a week. Thousands of attendees. Founders who met at the bar and raised rounds together. Engineers who found their next job between talks. Senior government officials in the same room as first-time builders. No venue of its own. No paid staff. No sponsor deck.

Just a Wednesday, every week, for three years.

This session is the full playbook. How to start one from zero. How to fill the room. How to keep it alive past the honeymoon phase when most community events quietly die. How to turn a recurring event into the thing your city organizes itself around, and what that does for your career, your network, and the place you live.

You leave with a one-page checklist, a date on the calendar, and no excuses.

For anyone who has ever looked at their city and thought someone should run an event like this here, and wondered if that someone could be them.

It can.
