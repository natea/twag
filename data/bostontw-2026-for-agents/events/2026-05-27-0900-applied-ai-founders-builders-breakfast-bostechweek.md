## Description

Inviting Massachusetts-based Applied AI founders and company leaders who are actively building to join for breakfast with the MA AI Coalition, Alex Grant @ Foray, and Kristen Craft @ Fidelity Private Shares.

Some of the most important AI work right now is happening where software meets the physical world. Across biology, materials, energy, robotics, infrastructure, manufacturing, and beyond, AI is opening entirely new frontiers. Massachusetts is the best place in the world for this work.

We’re gathering a intimate group of people currently building Applied AI companies for breakfast and conversation about what’s emerging, what’s working, and where opportunities are forming across the ecosystem.

​Fidelity Private Shares is committed to supporting the startup & venture capital community. Fidelity helps founders prepare for their next round of funding with our cap table management and data room platform, events that give you access to capital, and premier support to generate, execute & store legal documents.  No matter where you are in your journey - from inception to IPO - Fidelity has the expertise to support you.

​Foray makes it possible to build plant products and seeds from single cells. By assembling from the cell up, we're creating predictable access and resilient supplies without the harvest. We're making this future possible by integrating AI and Plant Biology. Human abundance shouldn’t have to come at the expense of the natural world. And now it doesn’t have to.

​The Massachusetts AI Coalition is a private-sector initiative to unite the founders, operators, students, investors, supporters, and builders that will define the next era of AI in Massachusetts.

This event is a part of #BOSTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
