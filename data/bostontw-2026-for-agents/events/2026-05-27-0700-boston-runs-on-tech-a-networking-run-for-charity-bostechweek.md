## Description

Join RemoFirst and Running Booth for a morning 5K along the iconic Emerald Necklace — starting and ending at the Dicks House of Sport. Whether you run or walk, come meet Boston's tech community one stride at a time. 

Via PD will also be providing water, coffee, and post-run fuel so you can head into the rest of your day and Boston Tech Week energized and ready.

The run will be approximately 3 miles with a shorter walk option.

For every runner that joins us, we will be making a donation to Still I Run. Still I Run is a running-based mental health nonprofit focused on supporting, inspiring, and uniting people in their journey toward better mental health through running.

This event is a part of #BosTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com
