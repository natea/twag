## Description

Join us for an interactive breakfast session built for the real challenges leaders face right now. JD Pincus, PhD, neuroscientist and author of The Emotionally Agile Brain: Mastering the 12 Emotional Needs that Drive Us, will present a thought provoking new approach to leadership.

You’ll have the opportunity to tap into your own leadership via a 3-minute neuroscience based AgileBrain Exercise and explore your results with AgileBrain AI chat. We will break into small groups to share our leadership challenges and how to take advantage of AgileBrain thinking to drive positive change.

John Penrose, the CEO of AgileBrain, and Kham Inthirath, CEO of Compound Effect, will share practical experiences from their work with leaders building their EI and AI EQ. You will walk away with new insights about what drives human behavior and a copy of JD’s award winning book.

You will also receive one month of free access to the AgileBrain leadership platform to support your leadership journey and a complimentary follow up debrief session.
