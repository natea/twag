## Description

Sponsored by Orrick, Herrington & Sutcliffe LLP

Join the Arizona Commerce Authority, Musa Capital, and other leaders for a private, curated dinner designed exclusively for growth-stage startups generating $1M+ in annual revenue and looking to scale operations in Arizona.

Why Arizona?

Founders relocating or expanding into Arizona gain access to:

●	Direct capital and tax incentive programs for Arizona-based companies

●	Lower operating costs, testing opportunities, soft landing

●	Business-friendly, relaxed regulatory frameworks 

●	Proximity to major markets without coastal burn rates

●	A rapidly growing tech ecosystem with real institutional support

This is not a networking mixer — it’s a strategic working dinner for operators making real expansion decisions.

Limited seats available. Attendance subject to approval.
