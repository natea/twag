## Description

AI adoption requires a CULTURAL shift — not the best models. And the people most critical to building the CULTURE of AI are HR, Comms, and Marketing leaders who own and shape the internal communication and knowledge flow.

The company Intranet is no longer the stale old link-farm or news portal. It's role has been elevated as the "control surface" and "front-door" employee experience for organizational AI.

Here's what we'll cover in this 90-minute roundtable-style workshop designed for executives who are done waiting for clarity:
→ A clear picture of where your organization actually stands on AI readiness — not a guess, a structured assessment
→ A shared language for the conversation you need to have with your leadership team
→ A prioritized starting point that's specific to your organization — not a generic roadmap

This is not AI 101. It's not a vendor pitch. It's a planning session for leaders who need to move — and need to move in the right direction.

We're keeping it small — 15 to 20 seats — focused on real conversation.
