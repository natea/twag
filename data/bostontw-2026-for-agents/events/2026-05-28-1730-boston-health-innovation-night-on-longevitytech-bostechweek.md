## Description

In honor of Boston Tech Week swing by for a drink between 5:30p and 8:30p at the Liberty Hotel, 2nd floor lobby bar in downtown Boston.

Guest of Honor Zoe Andreeva of Welltory, the wellness and longevity app, will make opening remarks on "Measuring biomarkers to extend healthspan."

Join the evening's hosts Kate Brigham (AgeTech Connect), Shilpi Gupta (Qyn Labs), Danielle Duplin (AgeTech Connect), Josh Berg (Vincere Bio), Justin Taylor (Longevity Global Boston), Ki-Youn Jung (EON.HEALTH), and Steven Wardell (Wardell Advisors, DigitalHealth InvestorTalk).

The theme of the evening is tech for longevity.

We are bringing together innovators and investors in longevity. Stay for a short visit or as long as you like. Complimentary hors d'oeuvres and cash bar. Taking the T is easiest; if you are driving you may find the Mass Eye & Ear Parking to be the most reasonable.

This event is a part of #BOSTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
