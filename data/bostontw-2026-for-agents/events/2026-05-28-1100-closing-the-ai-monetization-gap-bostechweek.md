About the Event:
As technology organizations transition from typical subscription models to dynamic, agent-led operating models, the traditional frameworks for monetization, revenue attribution, and accounting are breaking down. 

Join Workday, Deloitte and Zuora at Tech Week Boston as we go explore how modern enterprises can capitalize on "Agentic ROI", moving beyond simple seat-based licensing toward value-based monetization.  You’ll hear from finance leaders on how they are capitalizing on new value opportunities being created by today’s AI economy and get practical advice on how to prepare your finance teams for what comes next.

This event is a part of #BOSTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
