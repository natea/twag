## Description

AI Won’t Solve This: Leading Through Uncertainty in Tech Organizations
#BOSTechWeek

- Why are your teams busy but you don't always seem to move in the same direction? 
- Are you spending more time managing stakeholders than driving execution?

The hardest problems aren’t technical. Learn how to lead when AI isn’t the answer.

Tech organizations today operate in a constant state of disruption: scaling faster than their structures can sustain, integrating AI into core workflows, and managing teams where humans and AI agents increasingly work side by side. Markets shift quickly, talent expectations evolve, and pressure to deliver never slows down.

Yet many of the most critical challenges leaders face are not technical. They are adaptive: they require shifts in mindset, behavior, and ways of working across teams, learning how to delegate and collaborate even when you disagree.

In this interactive workshop, Ashveena Gajeelee (Founder, The Adaptive Leader; Harvard-trained leadership educator) introduces practical tools that will allow participants to work through real-world tech scenarios and:
- Diagnose the hidden challenges behind rapid growth and scaling
- Navigate the tension between AI adoption and human judgment
- Manage resistance, misalignment, and competing priorities

This session is designed for leaders who recognize that in the age of AI, the hardest problems are not solved by better tools, but by better leadership.

This event is a part of #BosTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com
