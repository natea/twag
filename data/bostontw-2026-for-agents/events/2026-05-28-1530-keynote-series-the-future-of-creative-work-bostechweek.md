## Description

Creative work is being reshaped by new tools, workflows, and expectations, changing how ideas move from concept through execution. This session brings together a group of creative leaders to share how their practices are adapting in real time, with each talk offering a focused perspective grounded in current work. Speakers include:

ERICA KUNG
— Head of Production and Creative Operations,Squarespace

IAN SIGMON
— Group Creative Director, Duolingo

ALEX CHEN
— Creative Director, Google Creative Lab

MEREDITH BINNETTE
— Creative Director, Black Math


Hosted by Black Math and AIGA, this event reflects an ongoing conversation around where creative work is headed and what it requires from the people building it. Expect a series of short keynotes followed by time to connect with others working across design, technology, and storytelling.
