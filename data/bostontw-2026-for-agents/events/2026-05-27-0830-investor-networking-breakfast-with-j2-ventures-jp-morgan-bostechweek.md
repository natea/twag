## Description

Join J2 Ventures and J.P. Morgan in bringing together Boston's investor and founder community for an invite-only breakfast. This inaugural Boston Tech Week is an opportunity to reinforce the robust ecosystem we have in our backyard through meaningful connections, conversations, and of course, coffee. 

Members of the J.P. Morgan team will also be onsite and available for one-on-one consultations around financial planning, startup banking,  and other questions founders may have on their minds.

This is an invite-only event. We're intentionally curating attendees to keep the morning casual, but high-quality. Please RSVP for approval.

This event is a part of #BOSTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
