## Description

VIRTUAL EVENT: Google Event link can be found above.

In this session, Hardware Velocity brings together founders building in B2B hardware to share what breaks between initial pilots and production. The discussion will focus on how early decisions around customer validation, product scope, and business model shape what’s possible later - often in ways founders don’t anticipate.

What to expect
- How to validate real demand before committing to product and engineering decisions
- Where teams get stuck moving from pilots to repeatable sales
- Tradeoffs between custom builds vs scalable product approaches (make vs buy)
- How to think about unit economics and GTM early, before they become constraints
- Interactive discussion with time for Q&A

Speakers
Eric Dahlseng - Co-founder & CPO, Empo Health
Anuj Khandelwal - Co-founder and CEO, Empo Health

About Hardware Velocity
Hardware Velocity works with early-stage hardware companies to align product, engineering, and go-to-market decisions before they become expensive to change.
