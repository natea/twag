## Description

This event explores how AI is transforming manufacturing beyond the headlines. The session focuses on the real ways AI is being applied across operations. Where value is actually being created, where adoption breaks down, and what manufacturers need to get right to move from experimentation to impact. By grounding the conversation in practical experience rather than hype, the event brings together industry leaders, operators, and innovators to examine how AI fits into existing systems, workflows, and business realities shaping the future of manufacturing.

This event is a part of #BostonTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
