## Description

AI is rewriting the way we see work. There's not a day that goes by where the media isn't blasted with news of mass layoffs, meanwhile leaders are struggling to stay ahead - redefining roles, performance and recruitment. 

As a result we are seeing a rise in AI driven hiring, AI job application tools for candidates and a tough job market. 

This fireside chat brings both sides into one conversation. Whether you're running an organization or running your own career we will be covering the following areas: 
- which roles and industries are growing or shrinking
- how recruitment is evolving
- what AI adoption looks like or where to start as an organization
- what leaders and individuals should be doing right now to stay ahead.

Join Lucy Schott, Andrea Henderson and Christopher Hudson - all working at the intersection of talent and AI  for an interactive discussion, followed by networking and drinks.

Lucy Schott -  Founder of a self-service AI Adoption Platform, AI & Workforce Strategy Consultant, and AI instructor to leaders at General Assembly. 10+ years in global HR, Culture & Capability at Fortune 500 companies. Cornell MILR; started her career as a games designer.

Andrea Henderson - Partner at DHR Global (top 6 executive search firm), Perplexity Fellow, and Angel Investor. 25+ years matching talent to value across life sciences, healthcare, and tech. Columbia MBA.

Christopher Hudson- SHRM-SCP & Associate CIPD, is the Founder of HR Lift Hill Advisory. With 20+ years of HR leadership experience across North America, EMEA, and APAC, he helps organizations build people strategies needed to navigate AI transformation responsibly.
