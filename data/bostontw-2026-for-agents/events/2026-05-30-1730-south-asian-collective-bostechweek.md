## Description

South Asian Collective is an evening gathering bringing together South Asian founders, technologists, investors, operators, and leaders in Boston.

This edition follows the ASAN AI Hackathon, where select teams will present what they built earlier in the day. Join us to engage with the builders, experience live demos, and connect with others shaping the next wave of technology and entrepreneurship.

Designed as a relaxed but intentional space, the evening brings together individuals across stages and disciplines for thoughtful conversations, meaningful connections, and shared context within the South Asian ecosystem.

**Who Should Attend:

- South Asian founders, builders, and technologists
- Investors and operators across startups and tech
- Individuals engaged in or around Boston Tech   Week
- Those looking to connect within a high-signal, community-driven environment

**What to Expect:

- A curated, drop-in evening gathering
- Live demos and conversations from the hackathon
- Cross-disciplinary connections across tech, startups, and investing
- A community-first environment with strong, intentional conversations
- No formal agenda, just meaningful interactions
