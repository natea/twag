## Description

Join us for an intimate evening of Radical Collaboration hosted by The Open Accelerator—a joint initiative between Red Hat, IBM, and the Commonwealth of Massachusetts (MA AI Hub).
We are hosting an energetic, high-impact gathering to celebrate and fuel the future of AI in Massachusetts. To ensure meaningful connections and high-quality conversations, this curated event is strictly limited to 120 guests, uniting the absolute forefront of the local AI innovation ecosystem under one roof.
Who will be in the room:
Pioneering Founders: The next generation of entrepreneurs building bold AI solutions.
Top-Tier Investors: Venture capitalists ready to collaborate, de-risk the journey, and fund the future together.
Enterprise Experts & Customers: Industry partners actively looking to skip the build-versus-buy dilemma and engage with vetted, enterprise-ready AI innovation.
Community Leaders: Top academic minds, non-profit partners, and talent from across the Commonwealth.
This is your chance to connect side-by-side with the key players who are on the front lines of the industry every day. Come share your vision, spark new partnerships, and help us bridge the gap between AI prototypes and enterprise-scale solutions.
Where enterprise experience meets startup innovation.



This event is a part of #BOSTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
