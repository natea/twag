## Description

Cosmic Playground is a social, technical, and philosophical event where we explore space, time, and reality through conversation, agentic coding, and playful experimentation.


Event Format
10:00 - Arrival + social warmup
10:30 - StarTalk-style Discussion with invited speakers: conversational, fun, slightly philosophical, interactive with audience
12:00 - Creative exploration
2:00 - Projects showcase + closing discussion


Experiment with ideas like:
planets with different physics laws,
alien intelligence  (Project Hail Mary) ,
reusable rockets,
quantum entanglement,
Artemis mission analysis,
aging across space missions,
Cosmic GeoGuessr,
realism of films like Interstellar,
or generate visuals of how you imagine the future, black holes, aliens, spacecraft, alternate universes, and more.

We will have refreshments!  🪸


For those who love https://www.youtube.com/@StarTalk!
Come chill with us :)
