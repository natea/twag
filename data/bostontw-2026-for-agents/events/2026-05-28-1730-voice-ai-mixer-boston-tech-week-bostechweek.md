## Description

Voice is becoming the default interface for AI - and the teams building real-world systems are leading the shift. 

​During Boston Tech Week, Agora is hosting a private Voice AI Mixer bringing together founders, leaders, and investors building and scaling voice-first AI. 

​Join us for an evening of demos, discussions, and networking with those shaping the next generation of Voice AI systems. 

​Sessions Schedule:
​Networking: 5:30 PM – 6:00 PM

​Happy Hour & Networking: 7:45 PM – 8:30 PM

​Live Voice AI Demos 
​See how real-time voice applications combine speech, LLMs, and infrastructure to deliver human-like interactions at scale.
