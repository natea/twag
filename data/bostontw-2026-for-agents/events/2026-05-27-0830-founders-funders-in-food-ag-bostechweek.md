## Description

Start your day with the people building and backing the future of food!

This event is hosted in partnership with Insperity, supporting growing companies with HR solutions that scale.

Join fellow:
- VCs and angel investors actively deploying capital 
- Food & agtech founders building across the value chain in: 
Consumer Products
Food is Medicine
Sustainable Packaging
Functional Ingredients
Climate-Smart Agriculture
Food Security
Biomanufacturing
Food Waste/Upcycling

Coffee, breakfast, and thoughtful conversation provided ☕️
