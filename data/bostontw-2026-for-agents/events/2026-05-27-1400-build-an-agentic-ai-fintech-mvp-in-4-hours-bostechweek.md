## Description

Join QuantUniversity for a hands-on Boston Tech Week workshop where teams rapidly prototype AI-native fintech ideas. Build practical applications in areas like research, risk, compliance, and decision support, then demo your concept to a panel of judges from fintech, AI, and financial services.

What you’ll do:
- Join a team or form your own
- Pick a fintech challenge area
- Prototype an AI-native finance workflow or product
- Demo your concept live
- Get feedback from judges and peers

Sample challenge themes:
- Agentic AI in finance
- AI for research and market intelligence
- Compliance and regulatory workflows
- Risk and governance tooling
- AI copilots for financial services teams

Who should come:
- Fintech founders and aspiring founders
- Engineers and technical builders
- Product managers and operators
- Financial services professionals exploring AI
- Students and enthusiasts with a strong interest in fintech

What to bring:
- Laptop
- Charger
- Curiosity
- An idea, or willingness to join a team and build

Format / run of show:
Opening remarks and challenge kickoff
Team formation
Guided build sprint
Mentor check-ins
Final demos and judging
Networking

Judging criteria:
- Strength of the problem/use case
- Quality and originality of the prototype
- Relevance to financial services
- Practicality and potential impact
- Thoughtfulness around trust and responsible AI
