## Description

Most founders don’t fail because they can’t build. They fail because they build…in a vacuum. This event fixes that.

We’re bringing together some of Boston’s earliest-stage startups for a night of real-time product testing, feedback, and beta user growth.

This is not a presentation or panel: it’s a startup showcase. 

Founders will be set up at dedicated stations around the room, where they’ll demo what they’ve built, ask for specific feedback, and invite attendees to sign up as early users for their MVP or Beta List.

You’ll walk around, try products, talk directly to founders, and (if you like it) become an early user.

Think: part startup showcase, part user research lab.

What to expect:
- 15 early-stage startups demoing live products + prototypes
- Founders actively asking for feedback (not just pitching)
- A room full of operators, builders, and curious users
- Opportunities to join beta lists and get early access

For founders: This is your chance to pressure test your product, refine your messaging, and hear what people actually think, before you scale it. 

For attendees: You’ll get a first look at what’s being built across Boston and a chance to shape it. Your feedback isn’t just welcomed, it’s the point.

This is NOT a pitch competition, it’s where products get better.

This event is a part of #BOSTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
