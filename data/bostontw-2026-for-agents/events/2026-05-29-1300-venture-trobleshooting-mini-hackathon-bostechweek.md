## Description

For 2-5y old startups! (Early Stage are Welcome!)

Did you raise a little but now you feel stuck? Have a brilliant MVP but has a GTM/PMF problem?
If so, this hackathon is for you and your team!

90% of startups fail; 75% of funded startups fail; See a pattern? This Venture Troubleshooting Hackathon is designed to tackle the failure rate! We deliver resilience building tools for founders to troubleshoot in a trial and error environment that tests the core of their ventures! Founders will develop a "north" and take home effective tools they can continue to build on!

Hack Your Own Venture!

This event is a part of #BosTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
