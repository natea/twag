## Description

As founders race to build and scale their companies, tax decisions made on day one can quietly shape outcomes for years to come. This session brings together PwC leaders in venture capital and corporate tax to unpack the tax considerations every tech founder should understand - from entity formation and early-stage structuring to how venture capital is taxed and what investors care about. We’ll cut through the jargon to connect tax strategy to growth, fundraising, and exits. Join us for practical insights, open discussion and networking.

This event is a part of #BOSTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
