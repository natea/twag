## Description

Co-hosted by Darshana & Get on Board

What does it actually take to build a global tech career today?

Join Darshana and Get on Board for a practical conversation on international hiring, AI-driven recruitment, credential visibility, and the changing realities of global work.

We’ll share data, frameworks, and tools around how professionals can better navigate global opportunities and build stronger, more portable professional profiles in an increasingly AI-mediated job market.

Topics include:

— What companies actually look for in global talent
— How AI is changing hiring and candidate visibility
— Why credentials and verifiable experience matter more than ever
— Common barriers in cross-border hiring
— Strategies for building a globally competitive career profile

Open discussion + live Q&A for students, professionals, founders, and anyone thinking beyond local career paths.

This event is part of #BOSTechWeek — a week of events hosted by VCs and startups bringing together the global tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
