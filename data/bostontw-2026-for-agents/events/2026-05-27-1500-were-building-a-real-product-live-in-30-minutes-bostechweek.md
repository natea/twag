## Description

Ever wondered what you can actually build with AI, from idea to live app?

We use rapid AI-assisted builds to test ideas with real users before investing in a full product cycle. It's how we validate fast, skip the guesswork, and get to something tangible before anyone writes a line of production code.

We're pulling back the curtain on that process, live, in front of you.
Alec and Mark are the founders of VisualBoston, a full-service design and development agency. They work with teams solving real, complex problems, the kind where getting it right actually matters, and help them use AI to move faster, think more creatively, and build things that actually work.

They'll take a product idea, yours if you bring one, from sketch to working deployed app in 30 minutes. Design and engineering running at the same time, the way modern teams actually ship.

This is a fun way to see what's possible and peek under the hood.

What you'll see:

- A product idea turned into a PRD in minutes with AI
- Design and engineering running simultaneously, not sequentially
- Real imagery and UI come together into a working, deployed app-
