## Description

AI is creating wealth faster than any technology in history — and concentrating it in fewer hands. This is a curated conversation about what fulfilling lives look like when frontier tech can do more of what used to require us, and who's responsible for that future. Moderated by Chris Russo (CEO, Savas Labs) with a panel bringing three distinct angles: a venture-backed builder on abundance and acceleration, a founder reshaping creative economies and ownership in Black communities, and an economist whose work on racial wealth and federal policy has shaped how we think about redistribution at scale. Audience questions accepted in advance and live.


This event is a part of #BosTechWeek — a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
