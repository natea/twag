## Description

Connect Smarter at Tech Week!

Have you ever attended a large event with hundreds—or even thousands—of people, and felt unsure about who you should talk to?

We're solving that problem.

Instead of leaving it all to chance, your personal AI agent will first connect with other participants' agents, helping you discover the most relevant people for you to meet. By the time you walk into the room, you'll already know who your best matches are.

Here's how it works:
Now – May 25, 6:00 PM → Sign-up is open. Create your Digital Twin by signing up and let it know what you're looking for at Tech Week.

May 26, 8:00 AM → Agents Meet begins. Your AI twin starts networking with other participants' agents—having conversations, finding common ground, and identifying your best matches while you go about your day.

May 26, 9:00 AM → Your twin wraps up and sends you a personalized email summarizing who you should meet and why, based on the conversations it had on your behalf.

May 26, 2:00 – 4:00 PM → Join us for an in-person networking session at a cafe in Boston, where you can meet your top matches face-to-face.
We look forward to seeing you there!

This event is a part of #BosTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
