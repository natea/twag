Join us for the presentations of the Applied AI x Bio Hackathon, where top teams take the stage to present their solutions at the intersection of artificial intelligence and the life sciences. After an intense build sprint, founders, scientists, and engineers will showcase prototypes tackling critical challenges across drug discovery, diagnostics, clinical operations, and bioindustrial innovation.

This event is cohosted by C10 Labs, Evolved Technology, Bayer Co.Lab, Nebius, and LabCentral as a part of #BosTechWeek

This event brings together investors, operators, researchers, and ecosystem leaders for an evening of high-impact pitches, live demos, and curated networking. Attendees will get a first look at emerging ideas and talent, while teams will receive feedback from a panel of expert judges spanning AI, biotech, and venture.

Winning teams will be recognized for innovation, technical execution, and real-world impact,

Come discover what happens when top builders collide across AI and biology—and meet the teams shaping what’s next.

This event is a part of #BOSTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
