## Description

THE EXPERIENCE
Boston’s cybersecurity community is coming together for an evening to officially kick off the summer. From practitioners to CISOs, this event brings together the people operating, building, and leading security teams.

WHAT TO EXPECT
Live Panels & Full Venue Takeover
- Why Boston, Why Now: AI Security in Production: Boston is emerging as a hub for next-generation cybersecurity, where AI is moving from concept to critical infrastructure. Hear from Benjamin Dulieu, CISO & CIO of Duck Creek Technologies, Joel Miller, Principal Software Engineer at DraftKings, and Debashis Das, Principal, Office of the CISO at AWS on how they’re building and deploying AI in production to secure modern systems.
- F1 Racing Simulators: 3+ hours of unlimited racing. The F1® Arcade experience is accessible to all skill levels and designed for casual competition and fun between conversations.
- Food & Drinks: Hosted food and drinks throughout the evening to refuel and recharge.
- Networking: Build new connections, reconnect with familiar faces, and learn from practitioners and leaders shaping the Boston cybersecurity community.

WHO SHOULD ATTEND
Attendance is reserved for cybersecurity professionals and subject to confirmation.
