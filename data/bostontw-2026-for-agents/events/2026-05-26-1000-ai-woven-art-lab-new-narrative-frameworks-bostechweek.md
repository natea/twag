## Description

**OPEN TO ALL ARTISTS / CREATIVES**

This session offers a window into the future of collaboration as 50 creators build one experience within a collaborative system, reflecting how brands and agencies can work with AI in practice.‍

Black Math will pre-build the creative and technical foundation using ComfyUI. The system is designed to support contributions from people with different skill sets, tools, and levels of familiarity, all working within a shared framework.‍

The visual language, constraints, and pathways for contribution are defined in advance, allowing participants to focus fully on creating. Brand managers, Creative Directors, Tech Directors, AI Creators and first-time AI users alike will work in parallel, each contributing from their own perspective while shaping a single outcome together.‍

The result of the AI-Woven Art Lab will be a unified high-quality art piece, developed collectively with a clear and intentional point of view. It will premiered and discussed on Thursday, May 28th at Black Math’s keynote series: “The Future of Creative Work.”‍
