## Description

Join us at RapDev’s headquarters during Boston Tech Week for a networking happy hour and conversation on building the AI-native enterprise. Focused on AI-driven service operations, RapDev will lead a conversation on how companies are rebuilding systems end to end—and what role humans continue to play in the loop. A panel of leaders in automation and industrial systems will offer perspectives to stimulate conversations for open networking.

This event is hosted by RapDev and is co hosted by Investissement Québec and the Québec Government Office in Boston in honor of a delegation of leading Québec based automation and operations technology companies visiting Boston.

Speakers
• Welcoming Remarks: Tameem Hourani, Founder & Principal, RapDev
• Moderator: Camille Labat, Synechron 
• Patrick Khamis, TELUS Digital
• Nick Plathe, Grantek
• Michael Scalia, RapDev

Why Attend
• Hear from leaders building enterprise systems around AI as a core operating layer
• Understand how AI moves from workflows into real operational execution
• Connect with operators bridging digital platforms and physical systems
• Engage with Boston and Québec leaders shaping next generation enterprise operations

Format
• Welcome remarks + short panel
• Networking, drinks, and light bites

This event is a part of #BosTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
