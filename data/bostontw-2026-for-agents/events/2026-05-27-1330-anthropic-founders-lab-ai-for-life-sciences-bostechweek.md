## Description

Join us for the Anthropic Founders' Lab: AI for Life Sciences on Wednesday, May 27, 2026, from 1:30–5PM ET in Boston during Tech Week!

This exclusive, curated gathering brings together founders and leaders from the life sciences startup ecosystem for an afternoon of inspiration, connection, and hands-on exploration. Attendees can expect an engaging founders forum featuring top voices in life sciences, opportunities to network with other founders, and 2 interactive experiences - The Lab (live demos of Claude for life sciences use cases) and The Bench (work directly with our engineers and AI experts).

Whether you're already building with Claude or just getting started, this is your chance to connect with peers and see firsthand how AI is transforming life sciences.

Featuring Guest Speakers
●Kexin Huang, Co-Founder & CEO @ Phylo
