## Description

A curated breakfast session for senior leaders exploring how to design, deploy, and scale AI systems within their organizations.

This two-hour session brings together experts from the MIT ecosystem to translate cutting-edge AI developments into practical applications for corporations. The focus is on enabling decision makers to move from exploration to implementation  with clarity on strategy, security, and system design.

Participants will join one focused training track, followed by a speaker panel discussing the future of AI-driven organizations.

What to expect
->Hands-on exposure to applied AI use cases
->Practical frameworks for implementation
->Direct access to leading experts in AI systems and strategy

Training Tracks (choose one)
1. AI strategy - workflow Automation & Agents: Agents for corporations, autonomous workflows, and operational efficiency
2. Security & Safe Deployment: Designing secure systems, responsible pilots, and risk-aware implementation

Speakers
-> Paul Cheek — MIT professor and expert in AI-driven companies. Author of "No One Works Here: How AI-Driven Enterprises Are Dramatically Redefining Business, Leadership, and Competition"
-> Isabella Loayza — MIT Sloan researcher, expert in the future of work
