## Description

Join Reframe Systems for an exclusive look at how software-driven manufacturing is transforming urban development. Based in Andover, MA, Reframe is a vertically integrated modular manufacturer redefining how we build for the modern city.

Our process leverages a unique blend of proprietary software and advanced robotics/automation to deliver turnkey housing solutions—ranging from ADUs and single-family homes to high-density infill projects like the iconic triple-decker and large-scale multifamily developments. Join us at the factory to see how we are removing the traditional barriers of construction to make high-quality housing faster, more efficient, and more scalable for everyone.

This event is a part of #BOSTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
