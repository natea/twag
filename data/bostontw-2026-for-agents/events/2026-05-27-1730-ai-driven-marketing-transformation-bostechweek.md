## Description

AI isn't coming for marketing. It's already here, and it's rewriting the rules faster than most organizations can keep up.

The organizations getting ahead aren't just experimenting with AI tools. They're redesigning how marketing works: automating workflows to make teams more efficient, using AI to personalize at scale, and rebuilding revenue operations from the ground up. Everyone in the org is now a content producer, and marketing is being asked to lead the structure around that. Meanwhile, your CEO and board have made AI adoption a mandate, not a suggestion.

Demand Spring and Brandlight are bringing together a small group of senior marketing leaders in Boston for an evening of real conversation. You'll hear briefly from our AI and revenue marketing experts, then spend the rest of the night in the kind of candid, peer-to-peer conversations that are hard to find anywhere else. Brandlight will also share how leading brands are staying visible as buyers shift to AI tools first in their research process.

Come ready to share, not just listen. Space is limited.

Drinks, good food, and the best views in Boston included.
