## Description

Hey there! 👋
The world is awash with cookie-cutter happy hours in a too-loud bar. This is a backyard sauna hangout at Chris Tolles' house in West Cambridge during Boston Tech Week. Come hang!

What? 🧖
Join us for a chill evening in the backyard with sauna for those inclined to partake. No pitches, no sponsors, just genuine conversation and good vibes with climate homies. 

The sauna holds four people, six if you are friendly, and will be available all evening. If you plan to sauna (AND YOU SHOULD), it’s easiest if you wear your bathing suit to the event, but there is a private bathroom inside the house if you need. 

Who? 👪
You! Climate founders, investors, operators, and fans. The yard is only so big, so attendance will be capped and climate people prioritized. 

When? 🗓️
We'll get started at 7PM on Thursday, May 28 and go until ~10PM (or as long as sauna party vibes persist).

Where? 🗺️
Chris lives in West Cambridge near Fresh Pond. It's an easy Uber or you can take the bus direct from Harvard Sq. The address will be shared once you're approved to attend. There's free street parking which technically requires a parking pass but it's rarely enforced.

There will be bathrooms inside the house for changing, but if you wear your bathing suit from the start it’ll reduce demand on the bathroom.

By registering for this event, you agree to share your registration information with the organizers of Boston Tech Week.

This event is a part of #BosTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
