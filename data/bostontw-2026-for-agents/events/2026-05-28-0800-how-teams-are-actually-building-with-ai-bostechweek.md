## Description

Join Klaviyo and the MA AI Coalition for a Boston Tech Week breakfast centered on a candid conversation about how teams are actually building with AI today.

This session will feature a fireside-style panel with Cody Patnaude (Engineering, AI Enablement at Klaviyo), Frank Fodera (Senior Director, Cloud & Developer Experience at CarGurus), Javier Garza (Staff Developer Advocate), and Andrew Sail (Forward Deployed Engineer at Cursor). Together, they’ll share how their teams are approaching AI in practice - what’s working, where they’re still figuring things out, and the real tradeoffs of moving quickly in an evolving landscape. The conversation will be moderated and interactive, designed to surface honest perspectives and practical insights you can take back to your own work.

We’ll leave time before and after to connect over coffee and breakfast with other founders, engineers, and operators in the room.

This event is part of #BOSTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
