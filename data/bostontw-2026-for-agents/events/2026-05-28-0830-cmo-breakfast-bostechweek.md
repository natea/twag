## Description

AI in marketing isn't coming... it's already here, and the gap between early movers and everyone else is widening fast.

Join Zapier, Acquia, and VentureFizz for a morning of candid, peer-led conversation on AI and what's actually working today.

This invite-only breakfast is for marketing leaders (CMOs and VP/Head of Marketing).

Hear from two curated panels: one focused on Enterprise Scale and the other on High-Velocity Growth. 

You’ll have the chance to trade notes with peers facing similar challenges and walk away knowing exactly where you stand... and what to do about it.

No vendor pitches. No hype. Just real leaders sharing what they're deploying, what's delivering ROI, and what's coming next.
