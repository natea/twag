## Description

Join Toyota Ventures for an evening of networking and connection as we bring together robotics investors and scientists, Toyota Ventures portfolio companies, and emerging startups. We’re creating a space for the builders and backers of the next generation of automation to share insights and explore new opportunities.

Our goal is to foster dialogue and connections between those funding what’s next in robotics and the innovative founders and researchers who are transitioning from the lab to venture-backed startups launching commercial collaborations. We invite you to connect with these leaders through an evening of relaxed, collaborative networking.

The event venue is conveniently located about 15 minutes walking distance from the Boston Convention Center. ​Registration approval is required for each guest. Substitutions / +1s are not allowed.

​About Toyota Ventures ( https://toyota.ventures ) 

​As Toyota's early-stage venture capital arm, Toyota Ventures invests in deep tech and climate tech solutions across a variety of areas, including AI, autonomy, mobility, robotics, space, carbon capture, renewable energy, batteries and energy storage, and sustainability.

This event is a part of #BOSTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
