## Description

America is short 5 million homes. 

Meanwhile, half of all rental inquiries go unanswered, and property teams are stretched thin. The real bottleneck is operational infrastructure that was never designed to scale.

EliseAI is rebuilding that infrastructure with AI. Tony Stoyanov, Co-Founder & CTO, and Justin Kahl, Partner at a16z, will get into what it actually takes to build and scale a vertical AI company in one of the largest, most underserved industries in the country — from the technology decisions to the go-to-market motion to what it looks like when customers experience AI for the very first time.

Come if you love working on problems that show up in the real world.

Format: Fireside chat + Q&A + networking 
For:  GTM pros, customer success teams, and engineers who are building in vertical AI

This event is a part of #BOSTechWeek — a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem.
