## Description

Join a conversation on how advertising is changing in the era of near-unlimited content creation.  In an age where brands are vying for mere seconds of consumers’ attention, and when humans are increasingly delegating their agency to AI systems to decide and purchase products and services, what brands advertise and how they advertise are changing drastically. Come hear from Extuitive on what future awaits us and how we can predict performance of digital ads ahead of time.
