## Description

Innovation gets stronger when the bridges are real. ALPFA Puerto Rico and ALPFA Boston are linking up with IncentivesPRO to bring Roundtrip Capital to Boston, an event series putting Latino founders, investors, and operators in one room with the people, capital, and diaspora networks already moving between the U.S. mainland, San Juan, and the rest of Latin America.

The night runs across two panels.

The ALPFA Boston panel, Building Wealth and Influence Across Emerging Markets, brings together Boston investors, entrepreneurs, and global strategists getting honest about what it takes to win in the world's fastest-growing economies. Expect real conversations on geopolitical risk, high-growth sectors, local partnerships, and building influence where the opportunity is. If you're looking to invest, expand, or compete globally, this panel is worth your time.

The ALPFA Puerto Rico panel, Latino Founders Building Back Home, brings together founders building companies in the U.S. and investing back into Puerto Rico and the rest of Latin America, alongside professionals from the diaspora plugging into that work, with a working breakdown of tax incentives across jurisdictions, what's real and what's noise, from R&D credits to exports, manufacturing, and creative industries.

We close with a networking happy hour with salsa, plena, and merengue, because we do business and we dance.

The pipelines between Puerto Rico, Boston, and the rest of the ecosystem are already there. We're making them visible.

This event is part of #BOSTechWeek, a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem, and runs alongside @a16z Tech Weeks: Boston May 26 to 31, New York June 1 to 7. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
