## Description

MachineTrader.io is excited to launch its new dedicated algorithmic trading site as part of Tech Week - AI Velocity Trading.  While MachineTrader offers its users a platform for writing proprietary trading algorithms using the visual programming environment Node-RED, AI Velocity offers pre-built, backtested strategies running in live and paper trading accounts.

AI Velocity Trading is a high-performance execution platform offering retail traders the infrastructure,  sophisticated, and pre-configured algorithms reserved for hedge funds.  Users have Immediate access to professional-grade, multi-leg options strategies—including Iron Condors, Iron Butterflies, and Bull Call Spreads—alongside momentum, mean reversion, and statistical arbitrage models.

AI Velocity’s scale is our speed. Unlike legacy institutions, we iterate on market signals in seconds, not weeks, maintaining a market-neutral posture through pure algorithmic rigor.

Evidence-Based Trading
Every strategy is supported by exhaustive backtesting data and transparent daily performance reports, ensuring your edge is backed by numbers, not intuition.

Seamless Alpaca Integration
AI Velocity is not a broker-dealer.  Your trades are executed throughout the top-rated, commission-free Alpaca Trading API. Their state-of-the-art trading architecture handles the heavy lifting of execution so you can focus on the signals.

Risk-Free Iteration
In this session, we will show you validate your approach with the ability to run simulations in up to three concurrent paper trading accounts before putting real capital at risk.  Move from sandbox to live trading with a single toggle when your data confirms the edge.

What You’ll Do
Bring your laptop and leave with running strategies deployed to your Alpaca paper account —without writing code.  We’ll take you through some of the strategies and explore the thinking behind them.
