## Description

Join us for facilitated panel and discussion on the future of women’s health innovation, featuring Tufts faculty and leaders from Boston’s tech, digital health, venture capital, and life sciences communities.

This robust discussion is being co-hosted by the Friedman School of Nutrition Science and Policy and the Tufts University School of Medicine, and will feature several leaders within the Tufts University nutrition + women's health ecosystem, including:

- Dr. Christina Economos PhD |  Dean of the Friedman School of Nutrition Science and Policy
- Dr. Megan Evans, MD, MPH | OB-GYN, Tufts Medicine and  Associate Professor, Tufts University School of Medicine 
- Dr. Sameer Sonkusale PhD | Professor, Tufts University School of Engineering
- Katie Stebbins, MS |  Executive Director of the Food and Nutrition Innovation Institute at Tufts
- Asli Dirik, PhD Candidate | Tufts University School of Engineering
And more!!
