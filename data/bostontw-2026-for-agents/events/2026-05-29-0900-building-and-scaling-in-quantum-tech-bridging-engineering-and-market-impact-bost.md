## Description

The conversation will explore the challenges and opportunities of commercializing quantum technologies — spanning computing, sensing, and quantum algorithms. The discussion will weave together the investor perspective, the realities of building early-stage companies , and what exits, and strategic partnerships look like in the space 


Moderator: Norma Padrón, J.P. Morgan Innovation Economy
Panelists:
•	Chris Sklarin, Managing Partner, Alumni Ventures
•	Bharath Kannan, Founder of Atlantic Quantum (Acquired by Google Quantum)
•	Brandon Severin, CEO & Founder, Conductor Quantum
•	Stephen Buchanan, Founder, Bandelier Technologies 



About the Hosts:

​J.P. Morgan Innovation Economy Banking is dedicated to supporting the next generation of builders — bringing together founders, investors, startups, and high-growth companies.
