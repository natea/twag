## Description

AI is changing how we work faster than most people can adapt.

Entry-level hiring is down 35%, and the IMF estimates 60% of roles are highly exposed to AI. Companies like Ford, Salesforce, and Amazon are already forecasting major shifts in how work gets done.

The people who will succeed are not the ones who automate the most. They are the ones who can think clearly under pressure and make better decisions in uncertain environments.

That ability is not a personality trait. It is a trainable neurological state, and most high performers operate at only a fraction of it.

In this 60-minute workshop, you will learn a practical system for staying sharp as work continues to evolve, combining AI workforce research, performance neuroscience, and a live somatic protocol.

You’ll leave with:
- A clear understanding of how AI is actually impacting your career, not just the headlines
- A simple nervous system reset you can use before high-stakes decisions
- A personalized AI workflow, including what to automate, what to protect, and how to stay sharp daily

Who this is for:
Professionals, founders, and creatives navigating rapid change and looking to work smarter with AI without burning out or losing their edge.
