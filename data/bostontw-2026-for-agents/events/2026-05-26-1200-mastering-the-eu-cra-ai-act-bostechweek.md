## Description

Hosted by Larry Veino, Joao Mendes, and Kham Inthirath, this practical lunch and learn is built for product, engineering, security, and AI leaders who need a clearer way to prepare for the EU Cyber Resilience Act and EU AI Act without slowing innovation. Instead of broad theory or legal jargon, the session translates regulation into real product decisions around scope, classification, conformity paths, documentation, SBOMs, vulnerability handling, and operational readiness. Attendees will leave with a practical compliance roadmap, a product classification checklist, and clear next steps their teams can begin acting on now.
