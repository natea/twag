## Description

Come join MongoDB during Boston's First Tech Week for an evening of food, drinks & networking! We’re hosting a happy hour at Swingers, the city’s brand-new "crazy golf" club.

If you haven’t been yet, Swingers is a self proclaimed "Crazy Gold Club" that reimagines mini-golf within a stunning, 1920s-style English country house setting. It’s the perfect spot to grab a cocktail, enjoy some gourmet street food, and get competitive on the green. Whether you're here to talk tech or just to play a round, we can't wait to see you there!

This event is a part of #BOSTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
