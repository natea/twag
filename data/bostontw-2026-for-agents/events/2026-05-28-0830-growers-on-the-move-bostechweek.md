## Description

What?
Join a group of Boston-area builders working across food, agriculture, forestry, biology, climate, and other “grown things for a short run or walk, stay for coffee, or just stop by to meet others working in the space.

Who?
Whether you're building a company, working in research, deploying technology in the field, supporting this ecosystem, or simply exploring how you might contribute, you’re welcome here.

Where?
We’ll meet in front of Area Four in Tech Square in Cambridge. We will break into walk and run groups respectively and take off from there. We will meet back at Area Four for coffee and pastries.

When?
We will start our run/walk at 8:30am we’ll return back at Area Four around 9:15am. We will mingle over coffee and pastries in the back room of Area Four.

This event is a part of #BOSTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
