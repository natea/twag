## Description

A lunchtime virtual showcase of student research and builds powered by Allium's onchain data, hosted during Boston Tech Week.
On the lineup:
• Credence Protocol: Winner of BNB Chain Hack College Edition Consensus 2026: Onchain credit infrastructure by Justin Wender.
• Liquidity Load Layer (L³): Cashu mint risk scoring and auto-migration tool, MIT Bitcoin Hackathon 3rd place. Ayaan Imtiaz, Jackson Zheng, Jiawei Zhao.
• Master's Thesis from Norwegian University of Science and Technology: Price discovery in CEX and DEXs perpetual Future Markets. Nicolai Kleppe Baadstø and Noah Langum du Plessis.
• Cornell Blockchain Research on CEXs and DEXs.
• Uniswap (UNI) valuation analysis, 1st place at Bentley FinTech Day. Justin Wender, Northeastern University Blockchain
• Stablecoin research from the Allium team. Ryan Hawkos, Allium
Each presenter walks through their question, their data, and what they found. Expect short talks, real numbers, and time for Q&A.
Who should come: students, researchers, builders, and investors interested in onchain finance, stablecoins, DeFi, and market structure. No prior blockchain background required.
Every project on the lineup was built using Allium Academia, our free data platform access for qualifying students and researchers. Apply at https://www.allium.so/team/academia 
Part of #BosTechWeek, a week of programming hosted by Boston-area VCs.
