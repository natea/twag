The Session will Start at 5:30PM. See more at seventrainventures.com/quantum-event

Cambridge is one of the densest quantum innovation center in the world. The startups, spinouts, and government contracts generated here are reshaping defense, computing, and cryptography.

But where is quantum today, really? The researchers know what's possible. The industry knows what's deployable. The investors know what's fundable.

SevenTrain Ventures is hosting this moderated fireside chat and private reception to let you in on where those overlap, and connect industry leaders on the deployment, research and funding side.
