## Description

Founder × Investor Roundtable: First-Time Founders — What Nobody Tells You

EchoHer and Epipelagic Ventures are hosting a curated gathering of founders and investors in Cambridge.

We’ll start with a focused roundtable conversation on what’s actually happening in the first 24 months of building — what’s working, what’s not, and where founders get stuck.

The second half features lightning pitches from selected founders, followed by direct feedback and open networking.

Who should join
• Early-stage founders
• Investors and operators working with startups
• Builders looking for clear feedback and strong connections

This event is a part of #BosTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com
