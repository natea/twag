## Description

Join Flex, Companyon Ventures, Suffolk Tech in the Seaport for an evening bringing together the best of the Boston tech community — founders, operators, and investors — for a happy hour during Boston Tech Week.

​NOTE: This event is 21+, photo ID required for entrance (passports for our international friends).

Our Hosts: 

Flex is a financial operating platform built for growing businesses -- think corporate cards, bill pay, expense management, and working capital all in one place, designed for companies that are scaling and need their finances to actually keep up. Interested in learning more? Start your application today at flex.one.

Companyon Ventures is an early-stage venture capital firm investing in high-growth B2B software and AI startups with $1M–$5M in ARR. We back audacious founders with the vision and drive to build category-defining software enterprises. Learn more at Companyon.vc.

Suffolk Technologies is a venture capital platform investing in ConTech, PropTech, and InfraTech startups transforming the $12T built environment. Since 2019, we’ve supported 60+ high-growth companies across AI, automation, robotics, and sustainability, helping them validate, scale, and commercialize solutions through Suffolk’s $10B construction business and industry network.


This event is a part of #BOSTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
