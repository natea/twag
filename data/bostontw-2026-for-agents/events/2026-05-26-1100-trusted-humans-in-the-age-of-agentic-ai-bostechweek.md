## Description

In the future we envision workflows comprised of multiple agents working within and across organizations on behalf of humans - this means multi-agent collaborations and negotiations that could occur simultaneously not just sequentially. In this context understanding who are trusted humans (behind agents), what are loyal agents and how do we build ai systems that put humans not just in the loop but in the center is key.  It goes beyond identification and authentication and biometrics to understanding how agents can be trusted to act for humans especially in contexts that require nuance, compromise, optimization, or a firm no.   

Join us for a thoughtful discussion of the challenges, opportunities and what people are building now with Beth Porter of C10 Labs and the Loyal Agents project, David Chang making VIA AI to rapidly find critical, trusted humans in your network, and Gabi Zijderveld of Blue Tulip Ventures on what it means to create Human-Centric AI. 

Moderated by Jennifer Jordan, Operating Partner at iGlobe Partners who works on AI trust and transparency with Foundation for Agentic Networks (FAN) and the MIT-born Project NANDA.

This event is a part of #BOSTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
