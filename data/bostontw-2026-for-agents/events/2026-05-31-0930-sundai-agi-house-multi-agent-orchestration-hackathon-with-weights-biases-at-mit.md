## Description

Come and hack with the largest hacker club in Boston, born at MIT and Harvard. We build, we learn the latest AI tools, and we hack.
Build an agent, win a robot dog!

Held at The Engine in Cambridge, this is the multi-agent hackathon to watch. Have the most fun with agents you'll have this year. The highlight of the hack is multi-agent orchestration. Explore, get familiar with, and hack on the latest techniques.

​We bring: Good food, expert judges, a robot dog that yells at you.

​You bring: Your ideas, a readiness to actually show up & crank out code, good vibes

Agenda:
09:30 - 10:00 Doors open, coffee + networking

11:00 - 11:30   Idea pitching & team formation
11:30 - 13:00  Building starts

18:00 - 18:30  Dinner
19:00 - 20:00 Finalist selection (if >20 projects)

Cohosts and event partners include:

AGI House is a frontier AI community, applied research lab, event space, and venture firm based in Hillsborough, CA. We bring together engineers, researchers, and founders building at the edge of AI — through build days, research dinners, and curated community events. Past speakers include Sergey Brin, Andrej Karpathy, Sebastian Thrun, Jimmy Ba, and Joe Palermo. agihouse.org

SundAI Club is an MIT/Harvard-born hacker club building & launching AI prototypes every Sunday. www.sundai.club

E14 Fund is the early-stage venture fund officially affiliated with MIT. E14 invests in AI and deep tech startups from MIT and beyond, while also donating a share of profits back to MIT. www.e14.vc

This event is a part of #BosTechWeek,a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com
