## Description

Regulation vs. Acceleration: A Debate on Governing the AI Future.

Join GovLab (Harvard's GovTech Startup Incubator), Madeleine Smith (Founder & CEO of Civic Roundtable), Santiago Garces (CIO of Boston), Stephen Brakow (Technology Partnerships  at Anthropic) and Prat Mallick (Principal at Xfund) for a debate on the future of AI governance.

The event commences with a conversation between panelists, followed by a live audience debate on the following topics:
- Should the US government take steps to ensure that the dividends of AI benefit all Americans?
- Does open source AI drive innovation or threaten public and national safety?
- Should frontier labs be allowed to self-regulate?
- Should US defense and intelligence agencies be given unchecked access to new model releases?
-Should the US implement universal basic income? If so, at what point?

This event is a part of #BOSTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
