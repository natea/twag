## Description

Your phone number is still one of the easiest ways into your digital life, and most people just don’t realize how exposed they are.

At Doxxing & Drinks, we’ll show you.

Join Cloaked for an interactive Tech Week event at SPIN, the iconic ping pong bar, where we’re mixing privacy, play, and a little bit of paranoia (the useful kind). We’ll put your digital footprint to the test with real-world examples of how personal data gets exposed, how attackers connect the dots, and where you might be more vulnerable than you think.

This isn’t a lecture. It’s a live, hands-on look at modern privacy risks, paired with drinks, food, ping pong, and a room full of people building (and protecting) the future of tech.


Come for the drinks. Stay to get Cloaked. Stay longer for ping pong.

This event is a part of #BosTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com
