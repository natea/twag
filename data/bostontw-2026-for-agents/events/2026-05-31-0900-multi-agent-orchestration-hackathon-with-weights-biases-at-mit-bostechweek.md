## Description

Build an agent, win a robot dog! 

Held at The Engine in Cambridge, this is the multi-agent hackathon to watch. Have the most fun with agents you'll have this year. The highlight of the hack is multi-agent orchestration. Explore, get familiar with, and hack on the latest techniques.

​We bring: Good food, expert judges, a robot dog that yells at you.

​You bring: Your ideas, a readiness to actually show up & crank out code, good vibes

Agenda:
9:30 -- Doors open, coffee + networking
10:00 - 11:00 -- Keynote, panel, & AI News
11:00 -- Idea pitching & team formation
11:30 -- Building starts
1:00 - 2:00 pm -- Lunch & demos
4:30pm -- Project check-ins
6pm -- Dinner 
7pm -- Finalist selection (if >20 projects)
8-9:30pm -- Finalist presentations, judging, & awards
