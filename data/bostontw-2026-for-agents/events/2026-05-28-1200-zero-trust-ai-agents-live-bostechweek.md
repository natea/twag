## Description

AI agents are racing into HR, finance, sales, and ops, but most “agent platforms” have one fatal flaw: once an agent is live, almost no one can explain or control what it can actually touch. This workshop shows how Karini’s agentic AI foundation builds a real zero trust perimeter around agents so they can safely operate across the entire enterprise without blowing up security, compliance, or reputational risk.

You will see live, no‑BS examples of multi team agent meshes, HR and payroll automations, and CRM sales copilots that inherit the user’s identity, enforce least privilege, and log every decision. We break down inbound control (who can invoke which agents, with which roles) and outbound control (what those agents can see and do) using identity propagation, role based access to tools and data, per user OAuth scopes, and human checkpoints where it matters.

Investors will see why secure, governable agent meshes are the next defensible layer in the AI stack, not just another feature. Business leaders will walk out with a mental model and a checklist they can take to their CISO and board. Builders will leave with patterns they can implement now, and a clear view of why Karini is the only platform that treats security, governance, and speed as a single product, not a tradeoff.
