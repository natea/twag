## Description

ScaleUp Labs Demo Day 2026 is bringing together some of the most exciting founders, investors, and operators shaping the future of tech and AI.

We’re especially excited to welcome keynote speaker Jake Ledbetter from Silicon Valley, Principal of Research, who will open the evening with an inside look at the current state of the innovation economy and the future of venture in AI. His keynote will cover where capital is moving across the market, the evolving VC landscape, fundraising dynamics, valuation and growth benchmarks, and new data on how venture backed companies are investing in AI across sectors including fintech, healthcare, and consumer tech. Jake will also share insights into what makes the Boston and New England startup ecosystem uniquely positioned in the next wave of innovation.

The evening will also feature a curated lineup of top startups from the ScaleUp Labs ecosystem presenting live pitches and demos to an audience of investors, founders, and industry leaders. Expect bold ideas, real traction, and high signal conversations with the people building what’s next.

Whether you’re looking to invest, partner, hire, or discover the next breakout company, Demo Day is designed to create meaningful connections across the innovation ecosystem.

This event is part of #BOSTechWeek, a citywide series of events hosted by VCs and startups bringing together the Boston tech community. Learn more at [www.tech-week.com](http://www.tech-week.com).
