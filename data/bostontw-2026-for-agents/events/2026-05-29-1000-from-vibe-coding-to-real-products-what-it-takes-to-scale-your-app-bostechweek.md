## Description

Everyone can build an app these days with AI and vibe coding. But scaling and monetizing an app? That's the hard part. 

Join us for a breakout session over coffee where Setapp by MacPaw, our partners from ByDesign, and iOS developer Jesse Waites will explore what it takes to turn an app idea into a sustainable product.

The event will feature a discussion on different parts of the app development lifecycle, including diverse perspectives of app growth, AI-assisted app development, and opportunities for indie devs. Speakers include:
- Pavlo Haidamak, Product Manager at Setapp – on developer relations, platform strategy, and app monetization
- Arthur Emma and Chirali Jain, founders of ByDesign – on building and scaling an app as a growing product team and Setapp partner 
- Jesse Waites, Founder of JTPCK.com – on the realities of indie iOS development and navigating the app landscape

Attendees can have 1:1 talks with each of our speakers after the discussion concludes, or simply stick around, work from the space, and connect with others over coffee and breakfast.

Come hang, meet other builders, and get practical insights on what it actually takes to go from idea → product → revenue.
