## Description

Are you building a startup outside the U.S. and considering the American market?

Do you have traction abroad and want to pilot, sell, or scale in the U.S.?

Are you looking for the right network, strategy, and partners to enter the U.S. well?

This curated Tech Week meetup brings together international founders with real traction in their home markets, alongside investors, operators, and expert partners who can help them enter and grow in the U.S. more successfully.

The U.S. can be a major growth opportunity, but it can also get complicated quickly. Go-to-market strategy, positioning, partnerships, banking, legal setup, and the right local network all matter early.

We’ll host a short, practical conversation on what founders need to get right for U.S. market entry, followed by a mixer designed to spark useful connections and real relationships.

Who this is for

International founders building outside the U.S.
Startups with traction, revenue, or institutional backing in their home markets
Founders actively exploring U.S. expansion
Companies preparing to launch, sell, hire, fundraise, or build partnerships in the U.S.
Investors, operators, and expert partners who can help international startups enter the U.S. successfully

Expect

A curated international founder meetup during Tech Week
