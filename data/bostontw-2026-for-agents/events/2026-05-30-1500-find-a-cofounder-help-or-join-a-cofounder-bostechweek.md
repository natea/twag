## Description

Welcome to Startup Oasis! #BOSTechWeek   

If you are a startup founder, investor, developer, marketer, or anyone else in the tech ecosystem looking to start, join, or help a tech startup, this event is for you! Startup founders pitch their startups to the attendees; then, you can join a breakout room/area (that we curate) to talk to the founders, ask questions, and give feedback.   

If you would like to pitch, RSVP here, and you can sign up on the day of the event. This event is free to attend. You can also support us with a donation.   

Schedule for the Day:  
3:00 🚪 - Doors Open for networking. Sign up at the check-in table if you want to pitch your startup 
4:00 🎤 Founders Pitch  
4:30💡- Founders break off into break-out groups, and attendees can move around and help one for the remainder of the time, similar to a hackathon   The audience will vote for a winner after the last pitch, who will be eligible to compete in the championship at the end of the year.

This event is a part of #BOSTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
