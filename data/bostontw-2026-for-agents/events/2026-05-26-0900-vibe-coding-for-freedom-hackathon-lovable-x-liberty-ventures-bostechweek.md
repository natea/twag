## Description

The same AI revolution that puts a Fortune 500 company's capabilities in your pocket is also being used by authoritarian regimes to build pre-crime models, deploy real-time facial recognition, surveil ethnic minorities, and extract data from the phones of dissidents. The question is not whether AI will shape the future of freedom — it's who will shape AI.

Vibe Coding for Freedom is a full-day hackathon designed to answer that question practically, and put tools directly in the hands of the people who need them most.

What you'll walk away with:
You'll leave this day knowing how to use vibe coding tools to build the digital assets your organization actually needs — and understanding how AI can be both the threat and the solution when it comes to human freedom.

The agenda covers two tracks:
AI as a tool of repression — how regimes are using pre-crime modeling, facial recognition, ethnic minority tracking, and phone surveillance to build stronger police states, and what freedom advocates need to know.
AI as a tool of liberation — how open-source, privacy-protecting agents, organizational scaling tools, activist-focused super-prompts, and freedom tech infrastructure (including Bitcoin and nostr) can give nonprofits and individuals inside dictatorships access to the world's best intelligence — safely and securely.

This event is hands-on. You will try vibe coding yourself. No technical background required — that's the point.

Who should attend:
Anyone working at the intersection of technology and human freedom who wants to understand and use what's available right now.
Space is limited. Attendance is free but requires approval.

This event is a part of #BOSTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
