## Description

This event is a part of #BosTechWeek— a week of events bringing together the startup and tech ecosystem. The roadmap changes overnight. The data is incomplete. The stakes are high. You make the call anyway.

Join engineering leaders from Klaviyo, Net Health, and beyond for a candid conversation on leading through ambiguity in modern R&D — from scaling systems and teams to navigating the rapid shift AI is creating across how we build, ship, and lead.

This conversation goes beyond polished success stories. We’ll unpack the hard calls: what shipped, what broke, what changed minds, and how strong engineering leaders make decisions when there’s no perfect answer.

Topics include:
- Leading technical teams through uncertainty and rapid change
- Balancing speed, quality, security, and team trust
- How AI is reshaping engineering leadership and execution
- Lessons from decisions that worked — and the ones that didn’t

Featuring:
- Surabhi Gupta, CTO at Klaviyo
- Keavy Murphy, VP of Security at Net Health
- Tacita Morway, VP of Engineering at Applause

The evening will also include interactive networking designed for builders — including facilitated roundtables, collaborative games, and smaller group discussions that create space for real conversation, shared problem-solving, and new connections.
