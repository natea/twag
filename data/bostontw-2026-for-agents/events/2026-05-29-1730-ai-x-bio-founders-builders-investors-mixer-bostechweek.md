## Description

Join us for an evening at the intersection of AI and biotechnology as part of Boston Tech Week. This event brings together founders, investors, scientists, and operators building the next generation of companies in AI for science and drug discovery.

We’ll feature short presentations from emerging startups working on cutting-edge applications of AI in biology, followed by a curated discussion on where the space is heading. The evening will conclude with a 30-minute mixer, giving attendees the opportunity to connect, exchange ideas, and explore potential collaborations.
