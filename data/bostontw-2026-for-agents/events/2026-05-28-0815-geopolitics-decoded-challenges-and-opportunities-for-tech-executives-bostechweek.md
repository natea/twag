## Description

Geopolitics for Tech Executives brings tech leaders together for a timely, executive-level conversation on how global political and economic shifts are reshaping strategy and risk. Join us for insights and practical discussion with a geopolitical mastermind Craig Stronberg on what today’s geopolitical landscape means for product and market decisions, supply chains and operations, data and security, regulatory exposure, and long-term growth—plus time to connect with peers facing the same challenges.

Craig Stronberg brings a rare combination of geopolitical mastery and real‑world national security experience, giving audiences unmatched clarity on how global risks truly shape business decisions. Recognized by Fast Company as one of the “100 Most Creative People in Business,” he translates decades of high‑stakes intelligence leadership into compelling insights that leaders can act on immediately.

This event is a part of #BOSTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
