## Description

Join us for an evening of learning & connection. Researchers, clinicians, practitioners, & enthusiasts across a variety of disciplines – ranging from biomedical engineering to surgical oncology – will convene to discuss emerging trends in precision medicine for cancer and in biomedical AI. Open to anyone interested in healthcare & enthusiastic about biotech in Boston !

This event is hosted by the alumni networks of the Dana-Farber Cancer Institute / Harvard Cancer Center (DFCI / HCC)’s Continuing Umbrella of Research Experiences (CURE) Program and the Biomedical Science Careers Program (BSCP), founded by Dr. Joan Y. Reede, Dean for Culture and Community Engagement at Harvard Medical School. 

Networking, good vibes, and good fun – guaranteed !
