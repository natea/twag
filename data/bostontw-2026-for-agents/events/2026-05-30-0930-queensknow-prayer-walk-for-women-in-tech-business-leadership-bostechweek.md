## Description

Join QueensKnow for a faith-filled morning of reflection, connection, and community during Tech Week Boston.

This prayer walk is for women in tech, business, leadership, and creative industries who want to begin the day grounded, intentional, and connected. We’ll gather at Harbor Shore Drive in Seaport for a peaceful walk, meaningful conversation, and a moment to reset before heading into the day.

After the walk, we’ll continue with Coffee & Conversations for Women Building in Boston, a casual space to connect with inspiring women building purpose-driven lives, brands, businesses, and careers.

Come as you are. Leave refreshed.

What to Expect
• Guided prayer walk
• Meaningful community
• Faith-centered reflection
• Coffee and conversation after
• A welcoming space for women building in Boston

Who It’s For
Women in tech, business, startups, leadership, and creative industries

What to Bring
• Comfortable walking shoes
• Water
• An open heart and mind
• Business cards optional 

This event is a part of #BOSTechWeek - a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. https://www.tech-week.com/
