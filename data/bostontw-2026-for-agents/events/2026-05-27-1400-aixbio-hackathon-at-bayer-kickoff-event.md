## Description

Join us for a high-impact, cross-disciplinary hackathon bringing together founders, scientists, engineers, and domain experts at the intersection of AI and the life sciences. 

Participants will have 24 hours and $100 in credits to the Nebius Token Factory to promote rapid innovation in AI for healthcare and life sciences. 

Whether you’re an AI engineer, biologist, clinician, or entrepreneur, this is your chance to build, learn, and launch at the edge of AI x Bio!
