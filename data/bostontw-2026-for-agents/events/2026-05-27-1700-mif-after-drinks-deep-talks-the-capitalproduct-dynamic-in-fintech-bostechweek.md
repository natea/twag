## Description

Chill vibes, great drinks, and real talk about money and products—come hang and dive deep with us!

Money In Focus invites a select group of founders, investors, and operators to a casual, off-the-record conversation exploring one central theme:

Where does capital truly meet product in fintech?

THE FORMAT

This is not a panel.
This is not a stage.

This is a free-flowing, informal conversation — a curated circle designed for:

Real perspectives (not rehearsed answers)
Open exchange between builders and capital allocators
Spontaneous insights in a relaxed setting
Hosted during Boston Tech Week, and designed as an intimate and informal experience, the gathering aligns with a week that brings together founders, investors, and operators shaping the future of technology and capital.

LOCATION

We will gather at Society on High — a refined yet inviting space in the Financial District.

Led by award-winning Chef and Owner Ian Just (formerly of Les Zygomates), the restaurant offers a contemporary American bistro experience with local favorites and eclectic cuisine.

FORMAT DETAILS

Cash bar (guests order and pay individually)
Light, informal flow — arrive, connect, engage
No fixed seating — designed for movement and conversation
WHO SHOULD JOIN

Fintech founders
Investors (VC, angel, private capital)
Product leaders
Operators at the intersection of finance and technology
LIMITED SPOTS: This is a small, curated circle.

Spots are intentionally limited to preserve the quality of the room.

JOIN US: Join this curated happy hour conversation.
