## Description

Is AI moving faster than humanity can handle?

You are cordially invited to Four Course Debate.

The best conversation you've had in years.

An interactive series for the questions worth lingering over, the things we're not supposed to say out loud. A space for new faces, real stories, and honest conversation.

Curiosity required.

Doors open at 7:00pm.
***Phones are collected at the start and returned at the end.

-->The Experience
This Boston Tech Week edition of Four Course Debate is an open discussion with David Lee, co-founder of Vanka AI, over the course of a multi-course dinner.

David Lee believes AI isn't moving fast enough. Your host disagrees. You'll decide where you stand.

We follow a simple arc: Story → Debate → Participation → Reflection

The evening opens with David's story and the perspectives that shape his work. Each course brings a new provocation — a question designed to be sat with, pushed back on, opened up. Guests join the conversation directly, in dialogue with the host and David, then move into guided table discussion. We close with reflection.

-->Food and Atmosphere
Wine and a light multi-course meal are served throughout the evening, designed to elevate attention and slow the pace of conversation.
If you enjoy bringing something to share, a bottle of wine is welcome.
