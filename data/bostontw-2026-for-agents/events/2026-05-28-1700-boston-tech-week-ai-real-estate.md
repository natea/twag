## Description

AI is changing real estate. Join top agents, brokers, and developers from across Greater Boston for a look at what's shaping the future.

Whether it's finding off-market deals, automating lead generation, creating stunning property renderings, enhancing listing photos and videos, streamlining client management, or writing marketing copy in seconds, AI is already reshaping how the best in the business operate. The professionals adopting early are pulling ahead. This is your chance to see how.

Hosted by Cityside Construction, William Raveis, and DOCA, this intimate early-evening session brings together active agents, brokers, builders, and developers for a no-fluff conversation. No lectures, no sales pitches. Just real dialogue, practical takeaways, and the kind of candid exchange that only happens in a small room with serious professionals.

This event is a part of #BOSTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
