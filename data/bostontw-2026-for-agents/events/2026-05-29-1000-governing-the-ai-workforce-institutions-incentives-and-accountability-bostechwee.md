## Description

Reign Dragon Lab is hosting a small research roundtable on the emerging governance problem of the AI workforce.

As AI agents move from tools into organizational actors, they will increasingly coordinate, delegate, compete, represent users, and make decisions under institutional incentives. This roundtable will discuss how multi-agent systems behave inside companies, markets, platforms, and governance environments — and what kinds of accountability, trust, incentive design, and institutional safety mechanisms may be needed.

This is an intimate, discussion-first gathering for people thinking seriously about agentic AI, AI safety, enterprise AI, governance, and the future of AI labor.

Learn more about Reign Dragon Lab: reigndragon.ai
