## Description

TNT is Boston's premier community and accelerator for technical founders out of Harvard and MIT, bringing together the best engineers, researchers, and builders who are starting companies. Our accelerator brings together the 20 best teams across MIT and Harvard.

Join us for the Spring 2026 Cohort Demo Day, featuring live demos from our latest cohort, showcasing some of the most promising founders in building at the frontier of technology. Teams will take the stage to showcase what they've been building across AI, developer tools, hardware, and more.
