## Description

"How do we turn AI into measurable EBITDA?"

Join us for an exclusive panel and networking mixer with leading Private Equity Operating Partners focused on translating AI from experimentation into real operating leverage across the portfolio. 

Featured speakers include:
• Emily Chien: Executive Fellow at Harvard Business School and former IBM AI Global Offering & Partnerships Leader
• Ritwick Rane, Chief Transformation Officer at PDC Brands (a CVC portfolio company)
• Janet Michel: VP, Global Parts Operations & Supplier Partnerships at PartsSource (a Bain Capital portfolio company)

This is a small, closed-door session for Operating Partners to get specific which initiatives that are moving the EBITDA bridge, where implementation is breaking down post-close, and how AI is showing up in exit diligence conversations.

This event is a part of #BOSTechWeek, a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
