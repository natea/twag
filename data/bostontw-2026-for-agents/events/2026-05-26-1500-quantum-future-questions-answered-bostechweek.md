## Description

Wondering how a quantum computer is different than a classical computer? Want to understand the relationship between quantum and AI?  Thinking about how your industry will be impacted by quantum? Join us on May 26th for a  lively discussion on the future of quantum.

Our speakers include:

Joshua Baktay, XPRIZE Quantum Applications - a global competition designed to generate practical, impactful quantum computing algorithms that address real-world challenges.

Victor Kerros, Alice & Bob - a French deep tech company building a fault-tolerant, universal quantum computer incorporating "cat qubits"—superconducting circuits with built-in error correction.

Shayne Gilbert (moderator) - Convergence Center for Applied Quantum, a community of quantum scientists, technologists and researchers.

Send in your questions when registering for a lively and engaged discussion about our quantum future. Most interesting questions will receive a special gift at the event.
