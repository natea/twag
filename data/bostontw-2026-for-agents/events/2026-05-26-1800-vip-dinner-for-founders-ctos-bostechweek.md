## Description

Let's kick-off #BOSTechWeek by setting the table! Join us for dinner at the best Brazilian Steakhouse in Boston along with other Founders and CTOs navigating how AI is reshaping the way teams are built and scaled.

​Honest conversations about talent strategy, what's actually working, where things break down, who you're really hiring for in 2026, rarely happen in public. This is a space for that.

​We'll sit down with a handful of peers already rethinking their engineering org: how they're balancing speed with quality, where nearshore fits (or doesn't), and how AI is changing expectations on both sides of the hire.
