## Description

Dependencies are now replaced with building it yourself, inside the product, alongside engineering. This hands-on workshop will teach you how. Join us for a morning of tools, practical workflows, and vibe-coding.

The future of software development means everyone builds and moves faster together. The teams that figured this out first are seeing ~5x faster time-to-market — and it's more accessible than you think.

🧑‍💻 We’ll be working hands-on, so make sure to bring your laptop and charger.

The Lineup:
👉 Keren Fanan (Co-Founder & CEO, Myop — ex CRO at Gett) — how vibe-coding helps non-technical teams ship faster: workflows, tools, and the technical vocabulary you actually need 

👉 Hadar Geva (Co-Founder & CTO, Myop — ex Wix) — hands-on session: take a real feature design or PRD, build its frontend step by step, from first prompt to a working, production-ready component 

Who is this for:
👉 PMs and product designers who want to ship UI without waiting on a dev cycle
👉 Growth and marketing people building on top of an existing product
👉 Anyone on a product team who's non-technical but ready to start building

The event is supported by Venture Guides.
