## Description

We love all things Boston: The weather, runs along the Charles, the Citgo sign, Dunks, and especially our vibrant start-up community.

At TBD, we pride ourselves on the strength and diversity of our community, the depth of knowledge our operator-investors bring to each interaction, and the urgency that we bring to each deal. Like our hometown, we bring expertise and insight to all sectors - from biotech and SAAS, to deep and clean tech, consumer and fintech. 

And we get stuff done. Our typical deal takes 4-6 weeks to close, and founders get an active roster of operators that have built in Boston to make connections, give advice, and smooth their path.

Come join us during Boston Tech Week on Thursday, May 28th from 4 - 7 pm at Orrick’s beautiful Back Bay offices for light refreshments and great conversation. No panels or formal talks, just a bunch of founders, operators, and investors coming together to celebrate Boston Tech Week.

This event is a part of #BOSTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
