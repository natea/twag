## Description

Can you beat Dispatchino?

Dispatchino is building the AI dispatcher for freight — an agent that calls brokers, negotiates loads, tracks updates, and keeps humans in control.

For Boston Tech Week, we’re doing what most AI companies avoid: putting the product in front of a room full of the smartest people and letting them try to break it.

We’ll start with a fast founder pre-game on what it actually takes to build production-grade voice AI apps: latency, interruptions, negotiation, evals, live calls, messy humans, and all the edge cases that make polished demos fall apart.

Then we open the arena.

Competitors will get a live call with Dispatchino’s AI dispatcher. Your job is simple:

Negotiate against it.
Pressure-test it.
Find a real failure mode.
Make it accept a bad deal.
Get it to violate a business rule.
Expose an edge case we have not seen before.

If you’re good, you make the leaderboard.

If you're really good and beat Dispatchino, you win a 2026 World Cup ticket.

Founders, investors, operators, AI builders, logistics people, auto transport people, and media are welcome.

Most AI events are people talking about the future.

This one is the future getting heckled on speakerphone.

Expect founder war stories, live calls, music, light bites, a leaderboard, and a room full of people trying to prove they are smarter than an AI model.


This event is a part of #BOSTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
