## Description

Partnering with Biotech Corporations for Strategic Investment 

Corporate venture capital can offer more than funding. It can unlock strategic partnerships, validation, and pathways to scale. Working with large biotech companies requires a different strategy.

Ian Goldstein, Partner at Fenwick will moderate a panel to include: 
- Olga Danilchanka, Partner at MRL Ventures at Merck
- Christine Brennan, Partner at Johnson & Johnson Development Corp.
- Jason Hafler, Managing Director at Sanofi Ventures

Join corporate VC leaders from top biotech organizations for a candid discussion on how life sciences founders can successfully raise capital and build meaningful partnerships.
