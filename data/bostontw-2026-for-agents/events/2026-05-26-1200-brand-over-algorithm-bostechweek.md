## Description

Shopline × AcceleratorCON Present

Brand over Algorithm:
The playbook that built the last wave of DTC brands is broken. CACs are through the roof, attribution is a guessing game, and the algorithm decides who sees your product. So how do the best consumer and e-commerce brands actually win?

This panel brings together founders and operators behind physical product brands sold online — the ones building real brand moats, owning their customer relationships, and growing beyond paid channels. We're talking about the shift from performance dependency to brand durability: how to build owned channels that convert, leverage retail as a growth engine, create community around physical products, and future-proof your commerce strategy in a post-cookie world.

If you're building a consumer product company, selling on e-commerce, or scaling a physical goods brand — this conversation is built for you.
