## Description

Join Driver AI for an exclusive evening reception during Boston Tech Week, bringing together AI founders, investors, and engineering leaders working on the hardest problems in agentic development.

Robust context engineering is as important as model reasoning in agentic coding. When critical context is missed, no amount of downstream reasoning can recover the lost signal, and existing retrieval-based solutions weren't designed to solve this.

Featured Session
Daniel Hensley, CTO and Co-founder of Driver AI, will explain why a compiler-based architecture is the right solution to address the inevitable context chasm that AI coding agents face. By pre-computing exhaustive, high signal-to-noise context, Driver AI transforms agentic coding from a probabilistic guess into a reliable, deterministic process.

About Your Host
Backed by YC and Google Ventures, Driver is the rigorous, pre-compiled context layer that brings deterministic performance to agentic coding at scale.  Learn more at driver.ai.
