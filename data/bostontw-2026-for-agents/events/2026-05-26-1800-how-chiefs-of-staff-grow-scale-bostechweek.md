## Description

What does it actually look like for Chiefs of Staff to grow with their role — and scale their impact as the company evolves?

As a Chief of Staff, your scope rarely stays the same. One moment you’re focused on execution, the next you’re driving strategic initiatives, building processes, and supporting leaders through periods of rapid growth. So how do you adapt, level up, and continue to create leverage as the organization scales?

In this panel, we’ll explore how Chiefs of Staff grow alongside their companies — from expanding their scope and influence to building the systems and relationships needed to operate at scale. Expect practical insights, real examples, and honest conversations about what it takes to succeed in the role at different stages.

Join Industrious and Ask a Chief of Staff during Tech Week as we bring together a group of operators to share how they’ve navigated growth, scaled their impact, and evolved with their organizations.

Gina is a Chief of Staff and GTM operator who thrives on turning ambitious growth goals into reality. She’s led revenue strategy, operating cadence, and post-sale scale at high-growth SaaS companies, most recently helping Goldcast improve retention and pipeline performance during a period of rapid growth and acquisition. She’s especially passionate about the behind-the-scenes systems—cadence, forecasting, and org design—that make companies actually run.

✨ Becca Hammond — Chief of Staff at Bevi
With over 15 years of leadership in the technology sector, Becca has spearheaded strategic initiatives across high-growth startups Enpocket, Nexage, and HqO as well as major corporations like TripAdvisor and Drizly (an Uber company). She specializes in establishing Program Management Offices and orchestrating Go-To-Market campaigns that bridge the gap between technical and business teams. During her tenure at Drizly (an Uber company), she launched a company-wide strategic framework and streamlined technical roadmapping across eight engineering squads. Since September 2024, Becca has served as the Chief of Staff at Bevi, where she continues to leverage her expertise in product operations and operational efficiency.

✨ Sarah McCarthy — Director, Chief of Staff at Autodesk
Sarah McCarthy is a Chief of Staff at Autodesk, driving strategic operations and organizational execution for product development teams behind flagship products including AutoCAD and Civil 3D. Autodesk software is used globally by architects, engineers, designers, and infrastructure teams to design and make the built world.

Sarah leads cross-functional initiatives spanning product, engineering, and executive leadership, with a focus on organizational alignment and execution at scale across global teams. She holds an MBA from Boston College and brings a systems-level approach to leadership, strategy, and organizational effectiveness.

✨ Lilly Wilkie, PhD — Strategic Operations Director and Chief of Staff Vertex Pharmaceuticals
Lilly is a Strategic Operations Director and Chief of Staff for Cell & Gene Therapy Clinical Development, where she partners with leadership to drive organizational effectiveness, governance, and high-impact initiatives across clinical development. With a Ph.D. in Pharmaceutical Sciences and over 13 years in the industry, she brings a rare combination of deep scientific expertise and operational leadership to the Chief of Staff role.
