## Description

BASE-X, in partnership with J.P. Morgan and CSC Leasing, will kick off a 3-part event series focused on the future of dual-use technology during the inaugural a16z Boston Tech Week.

​This panel will examine the opposing forces shaping the pace of deployment for human-out-of-the-loop autonomy in dual-use technologies, from accelerants like adversarial escalation and latency-driven competition to key constraints like governance frameworks and computational limits in real-world environments.
