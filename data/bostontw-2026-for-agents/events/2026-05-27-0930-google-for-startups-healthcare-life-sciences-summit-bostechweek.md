## Description

Accelerate the Future of Health Tech!

Join Google for Startups during Boston Tech Week for a highly curated, invite-only summit.

Built specifically for founders, this exclusive gathering converges high-potential Healthcare and Life Sciences (HCLS) startups with top-tier venture capitalists and leading academic researchers.

This summit is designed to dive straight into the commercial and technical realities of scaling health tech today. We will explore how to bridge the gap between academic innovation and industry scale, apply advanced AI to highly specific problems, and position your infrastructure for enterprise success.

This event is a part of #BOSTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
