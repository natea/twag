## Description

Join us during Boston Tech Week for a curated happy hour bringing together founders, investors, operators, and industry leaders shaping the future of the built environment.

Hosted in partnership with Enlaye, Fidelity Private Shares, and a few other leading ecosystem partners, this gathering is designed to connect the people building the next generation of ConstructionTech, PropTech, infrastructure, and industrial innovation.

Construction and the built world are entering a new era—where AI, data, and automation are transforming how projects are designed, managed, financed, and delivered. This event creates space for thoughtful, off-the-record conversations with the founders and investors driving that change.

What to expect:
• A curated room of BuiltWorld founders, investors, and operators
• Meaningful conversations on AI, construction, and infrastructure innovation
• Opportunities for partnerships, hiring, and collaboration
• Relaxed networking with drinks and great people

Whether you’re building software for construction, investing in industrial transformation, or operating in the built environment, this is a chance to meet others shaping what’s next.

Good people. Strong ideas. Real connections.


This event is a part of #BOSTechWeek—a week of events hosted by VCs and startups to bring together the tech ecosystem. Learn more at www.tech-week.com.
